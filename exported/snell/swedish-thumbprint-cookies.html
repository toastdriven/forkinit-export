Forkinit: Swedish Thumbprint Cookies

By Sarah Marie
this recipe for Swedish Thumbprint Cookies are wonderful! they are soo good. you can make them big or small.

Ingredients

For the ingredient:

¼ c. shorting
¼ c. butter
¼ c. brown butter
1 egg ~ separate the yoke for cookies
½ teaspoon of vanilla
1 c. flour
¼ teaspoon salt
¾ c. of chopped walnuts
Jelly

Instructions

Mix everything except egg whites, jelly and nuts. Roll into balls. Then roll in slightly beaten egg whites and finally roll in nuts. Put on a greased baking sheet and bake for 5 minutes at 375˚ or 350˚ take out and make a thumbprint very carefully. Bake for 5-10 minutes more(usually 5 minutes is enough. ;) when cool put jelly in thumbprint hole.

Source: From My Swedish Grandma
