Forkinit: Blueberry Lemon Cheesecake Muffins

By Sarah Marie

Ingredients

1 cup old fashioned rolled oats
1 cup buttermilk
1 pkg. (8 oz.)Neufchatel Cheese, softened
1/4 cup granulated sugar
*zest from 1 lemon
1 cup whole wheat flour
3/4 cup packed brown sugar
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1 egg, beaten lightly
1/4 cup butter, melted and cooled
1 tsp. vanilla extract
1 cup frozen blueberries

Instructions

Heat the oven to 350ÂºF.
In a medium sized mixing bowl,combine the buttermilk and oats.
Mix together and let stand for 10 minutes.
Meanwhile in a small bowl, mix well cream cheese,granulated sugar and lemon zest and set aside.
In a Kitchen Aid mixer, mix flour, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in large bowl.
Add the egg, vanilla and butter to oat and buttermilk mixture stir until well blended.
Add this wet mixture to the dry ingredients in the Kitchen Aid mixer and stir just until moistened. Gently fold in the blueberries.
Put 18 paper liners in muffin pans.
Spoon half of the batter evenly into 18 muffin tins, filling each liner about half full.
Top each with 2 Tbsp. of the lemon/cheese filling. Cover with remaining batter, smoothing the batter to ensure an even muffin.
Bake the muffins 22 to 26 minutes-depending on your oven or until toothpick inserted in centers comes out clean.
Let cool in muffin pan 5 to 10 minutes, then remove to a cooling rack.

Enjoy! Sarah
