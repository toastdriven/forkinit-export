Forkinit: Rhubarb crunch

By Sarah Marie
a family favorite recipe

Ingredients

1 cup flour
1 teaspoon cinnamon
3/4 cup quick oats, uncooked
3/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 cup melted butter

4 cups diced rhubarb

Sauce:
1/2-3/4 cup sugar
1 cup water
2 tablespoon cornstarch
1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

Mix flour, cinnamon, oats, brown sugar, and melted butter until crumbly.
Put 1/2 of the mixture into a 9x9 inch greased pan.
Cover with 4 cups diced rhubarb.
Cook sugar, water, cornstarch and vanilla, making a sauce. Cook until thick.
Pour over rhubarb. Put remaning crumb mixture on top.
Bake at 350Ëš for 1 hour.
Cut in squares and serve with whipped cream.
