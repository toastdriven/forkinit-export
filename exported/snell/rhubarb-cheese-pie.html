Forkinit: Rhubarb Cheese Pie

By Sarah Marie
Rhubarb, Cream Cheese anad Sour Cream

Ingredients

3 cups of fresh rhubarb cut into 1/2-inch pieces
1 cup, plus 2 tablespoons of sugar, divdied
1 tablespoon of flour
1 10-inch prepared graham cracker crust your own or the stores
8 ounces of cream cheese, softened
1 1/2 cups of cup (12 ounces) sour cream, divided
2 eggs
1 tablespoon, of plus 1 teaspoon vanilla, divided

Instructions

preheat oven to 350ËšF. Combine rhubarb, 1/2 cup of sugar and flour in a non-stick skillet. cook over medium het until sugar melts. pour into bottom of prepared pie crust.

meanwhile, beat together cream cheese, 1/2 cup of sour cream and 1/2 cup of sugar until fluffy. add egg's one at a time, and 1 tablespoon vanilla until blended.pour over rhubarb layer. bake for 30 minutes. or until puffed and golden.

combine remaining sour cream, sugar and vanilla; spread over hot pie. set on a wire rack to cool slightly; cover and refrigerate before serving.

Yields: serves 8
