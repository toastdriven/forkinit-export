Forkinit: fresh pear upside down cake

By Sarah Marie
pears, a great recipe and soo easy

Ingredients

4 fresh pears or more
3/4 cup butter
1/4 cup brown sugar
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 and 2/3 cup flour
1/2 cup milk

Instructions

Pare, halve and core the pears. Set aside. Put 1/4 cup of butter into a 9x13 pan. Place into oven as it preheats to melt. Sprinkle brown sugar evenly over melted butter. Arrange pears over brown sugar, cut side toward pan. Cream the remaning 1/2 cup butter with the sugar, egg and vanilla. Blend in the baking powder and salt. Add the flour alternately with the milk, blending well. Pour mixture over pears evenly. bake at 350Ëš degrees for 45 minutes or until center of cake tests done. Invert onto serving plate. Serve warm with whipping cream if desired.
Enjoy~have fun!
