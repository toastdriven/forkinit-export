Forkinit: Chocolate Chip Banana Bread

By Sarah Marie
Yum! Great warm and great cold!

Ingredients

1) Cream together in mixer bowl:
1/3 c. butter
¾ c. brown sugar

2) Add and beat until light:
2 eggs
1 c. bananas crushed (2-3 bananas)

3) Combine and add alternately with milk. Beat after every addition until smooth:
1 ½ c. flour
1 tsp. soda
1 tsp. salt
½ c. milk

Instructions

Grease loaf pan(s). Bake at 350 degrees for 65 minutes or until done. Cool in pan for 10-12 minutes then remove. Cool slightly then wrap tightly in plastic bag or wrap.

Mini loaves: 50-55 minutes

Muffins: Makes 18--Bake 400 degrees for 15 minutes
I like to make three at a time because then I can use 2 sticks of butter (1 cup) and it's easy to make extra for the freezer since I'm making the mess any way!

Just add 1 cup chocolate chips to the batter once it is in the pan. Don't mix it in.

Yields: 1 loaf
Prep time: About 20-25 minutes
