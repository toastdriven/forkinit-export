Forkinit: Lasagna

By karinalynn20
from my youth pastor's wife

Ingredients

9 lasagna noodles

1 pound of ground beef

1 c. cottage cheese

1/4 c. sour cream

1/4 c. parmesan cheese (optional)

8 oz. mozzarella cheese, shredded

2 Tbsps. vegetable oil

2 pound jar spaghetti sauce

salt, pepper, oregano- to taste

Instructions

Cook noodles according to package directions. Drain. Pour oil over noodles and stir to coat-prevents them from sticking together. Brown ground beef with salt and pepper ( I cook with onions), drain. After draining meat, I stir in oregano. Combine cottage cheese and sour cream ( I add a little milk to thin mixture). In 9x13 baking dish, cover bottom with 1/3 of noodles. Layer 1/2 meat, 1/2 cottage cheese/sour cream mixture, 1/2 parmesan cheese (if using), 1/3 mozzarella cheese, and 1/3 sauce. Repeat with 1/3 noodles, remaining meat, cottage cheese, and parmesan cheese, 1/3 mozzarella cheese, and 1/3 sauce. Finish with rest of noodles and top with sauce. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top.



Bake at 350* for 40-50 minutes.

Yields: 6 servings (2 pieces per person
Prep time: 15 mins prep/40-50 mins bake time
