Forkinit: Chicken Potato Salad Ole'

By karinalynn20
Forget you idea of what potato salad is. This non-traditional potato salad has a Mexican twist that will leave you wanting more.

Ingredients

1 large ripe tomato (chopped)
3/4 c. chopped green onions (I use the green part)
1/4 c. chopped fresh cilantro
1 tsp. of salt
1 c. Hellman's Mayonnaise
3 tbsp. lime juice
1 tsp. ground cumin
1 pound small red potatoes, cooked and sliced 1/4 in. thick
2 cups cubed cooked chicken
1 large yellow or red bell pepper, diced
Tortilla chips (serve with salad)

Lime slices, whole chili peppers and cilantro sprigs for garnish (optional)

Instructions

-Boil potatoes until soft
-Boil chicken until tender
-In medium bowl combine mayonnaise, lime juice, cumin and 1 teaspoon of salt; set aside.
-In a large bowl combine tomatos, green onions, chopped cilantro, and bell pepper. Then add chicken and potatoes.
-Combine mayonnaise mixture to salad; coat well.
-Cover; chill. To serve, spoon salad onto lettuce-lined platter.
-If desired, garnish with tortilla chips, lime slices, and cilantro sprigs. ENJOY!!!!!!!

Yields: 8-10
