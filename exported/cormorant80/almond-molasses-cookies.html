Forkinit: Almond molasses cookies

By cormorant80
Quick and easy cookies

Ingredients

1/4 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup molasses
1/2 cup milk
1 teasponn bicarb soda
1 teaspoon vanilla
1 cup chopped toasted almonds
1 cup wholemeal flour
1/4 cup rye flour

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350'F. In a saucepan bring butter, sugar, molasses and milk to a rolling boil.
Add bicarb soda and take off the heat.
Mix in the almonds and vanilla then add the flour and mix until combined.
Drop spoonfuls of the mixture on a baking tray lined with wax paper - they will spread a lot so leave them plenty of room.
Bake 8-10 minutes. Let cool fully before eating.

Yields: 20
Prep time: 20 minutes
