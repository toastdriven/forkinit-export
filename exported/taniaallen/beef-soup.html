Forkinit: Beef Soup

By Tygher
Very simple soup of beef, carrots, celery and a tomato base, with very small pasta.

Ingredients

1-2 lbs stew beef
1-2 lbs carrots, peeled and sliced
2 small cans tomato paste
1 bunch celery, washed and sliced
6-8 oz ancini di pepe pasta (orzo and ditalini also work .. anything very small)

Instructions

1. Put stew beef in bottom of a large stew/stock pot. Fill pot with water to a few inches over beef.
2. Cook on med-high heat until beef is cooked ~ about an hour. (Watch out for boil-over. Skim off rendered fat from top periodically.)
3. When beef is cooked through, add tomato paste and allow to dissolve fully.
4. Add carrots and celery. Cook until carrots are tender.
5. Add pasta and cook or 6-8 minutes, depending on pasta used. (If necessary, add more water. I usually heat the water first to keep from having to re-boil.)
6. Add salt/pepper to taste, and enjoy! (Hubby also adds Parmesan cheese when I make it for him, though I prefer it without.)

Prep time: 2-3 hours
