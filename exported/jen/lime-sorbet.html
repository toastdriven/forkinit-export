Forkinit: Lime Sorbet

By Jenny jen@blacker.me.uk
Quick and simple, very tasty!

Ingredients

250g Caster Sugar
Zest of 3 limes
200ml Water

Instructions

Bring all ingredients to the boil to disolve the sugar. Simmer, skimming the surface for about 10 minutes. Cool. Blend. Freeze.

Prep time: 30 mins
