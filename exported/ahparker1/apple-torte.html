Forkinit: Apple Torte

By Arline Parker
This apple dessert doesn't require many ingredients and is quick and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

1 Cup diced Apples
1/2 Cup Flour
3/4 Cup Sugar
1 Tsp. Baking Powder
1 Egg (slightly beaten)
1/2 Cup chopped Walnuts (Optional)

Instructions

Stir all together until ingredients are moist.
Spread into slightly greased pie plate.

Bake at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes, until slightly browned.

Serve warm or cold....with whipped cream or ice cream if desired.

Yields: 6 Servings
Prep time: It takes me about 20 minutes, but I am old.
