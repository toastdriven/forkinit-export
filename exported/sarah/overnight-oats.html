Forkinit: Overnight Oats

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1/2 c. Old fashioned oats
1/2 c. Milk
1/4 c. Plain yogurt (Greek or regular)
1 T. Chia seeds
1 T. Honey or maple syrup (optional)
1/4 t. Vanilla

Instructions

Mix all together and let set overnight in refrigerator.
Keeps 5 days.
Add toppings (fruit, nuts, etc.) when ready to eat.

Yields: 1
