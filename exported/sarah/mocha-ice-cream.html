Forkinit: Mocha Ice Cream

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1/4 cup cocoa powder
1/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup sucanat
pinch salt
1 cup whole milk
2 cups heavy cream
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1 tsp. coffee extract

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients together.
Whisk or beat wet ingredients into dry until completely dissolved.
Refrigerate at least 1 hour before freezing in ice cream maker.
