Forkinit: Mom's Sugar Cookies

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 cups white sugar
1 cup shortening (w replace 1/2 cup with butter) note-I don't use shortening so I substitute all butter... probably not exactly the same result
3 egg yolks
1 1/4 cup buttermilk
3-4 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp almond extract

Instructions

Do not double recipe!! Make one batch at a time.
Cream sugar and shortening well.
Add egg yolks and flavoring
Add buttermilk
Sift dry ingredients into a bowl.
Add batter to it, mixing well.
Cover tightly and let stand overnight in fridge
Roll out thick onfloured board.
Sprinkle flour on top as needed to roll (lots)
Cut
Sprinkle with powdered sugar (optional)
Bake 8 minutes at 450

Prep time: overnight
Source: Guzzetta family recipe
