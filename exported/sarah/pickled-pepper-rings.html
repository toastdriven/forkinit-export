Forkinit: Pickled Pepper Rings

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

Hot banana peppers
Mustard seed
Garlic
Brine:
9 cups cider vinegar
3 cups water
3 T. salt

Instructions

Wash and "gut" the peppers
I then run the peppers through the food processor with the slicing attachment to make rings.
I strongly suggest that you wear rubber gloves while you handle the peppers.
Stuff them into pints or quarts with a clove or two of garlic and 1 teas. mustard seed per quart. Pack the peppers in as tightly as possible, they will "cook down" a lot.
Pour boiling brine over peppers and run a butter knife or around the edge to dislodge any bubbles. Top brine up to 1/4 head space.
Process pints 10 min, quarts 15 min. at full boil.

Yields: About 1 canner load.
