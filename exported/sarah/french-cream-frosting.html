Forkinit: French Cream Frosting

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 T. flour
1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. sugar
1 t. vanilla

Instructions

Combine flour and milk in a small saucepan and cook 3 min. until thickened.
Cool completely.
Beat butter and sugar until fluffy.
Combine flour and sugar mixtures and add vanilla.

Yields: Frosts 1 pan cake or one layer.
