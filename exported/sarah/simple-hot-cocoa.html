Forkinit: Simple Hot Cocoa

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1/2 gal. Milk
1/4 cup cocoa powder
3/4 cup sucanat
1/2 tsp. vanilla
Sprinkle cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

Gently heat milk with cocoa and sucanat to drinking temperature while stirring with a whisk.
Remove from heat and stir in other flavorings.
Serve with marshmallows, squirty whipped cream, peppermint stir stick, or etc.

Yields: 8 - 8oz. Servings
