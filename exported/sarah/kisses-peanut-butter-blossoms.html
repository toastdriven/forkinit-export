Forkinit: Kisses Peanut Butter Blossoms

By Sarah Potter
Make without the kisses for regular peanut butter cookies.

Ingredients

48 Hershey Kisses Chocolates
1/2 Cup Shortening (I use butter, but you can also do 1/4 Cup shortening and 1/4 cup butter)
3/4 Cup creamy peanut butter (I also like using 1/2 of this amount with crunchy peanut butter and 1/2 creamy, but not necessary)
1/3 Cup granulated sugar
1/3 Cup packed light brown sugar

1 egg
2 TBLS milk
1 tsp vanilla extract

1 -1/2 Cups flour
1 tsp baking soda (I use baking powder if you are going to chill the dough and make cookies later)
1/2 tsp salt

Granulated sugar (for rolling in)

Instructions

Preheat oven 375Â° Remove Foil from Kisses (this needs to be done ahead because you have to quickly put the kisses into hot cookies)

Beat shortening and peanut butter until well blended.
Add granulated sugar and brown sugar; beat until fluffy.
Add egg, milk and vanilla; beat well.

Stir together flour, soda and salt; gradually beat into peanut butter mixture.

Shape dough into 1-inch balls. Roll in granulated sugar; place on un-greased cookie sheets (I use parchment paper)

Bake 8-10 minutes or until lightly browned. IMMEDIATELY press a chocolate into center of each cookie; cookie will crack around edges.
Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack and allow to cool completely.

Yields: 4 dozen
