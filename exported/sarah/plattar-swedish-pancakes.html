Forkinit: Plättar - Swedish Pancakes

By Sarah Potter
A basic crêpe - the recipe my Swedish grandmother used to make.

Ingredients

2 eggs
4 c. milk
4 T. melted butter
1/2 t. salt
1 T. sugar
2 1/2 c. flour

Instructions

beat eggs
add milk
sift dry ingredients and add
beat until smooth
add melted butter and beat
let stand for 1 hour
beat well and pour enough for 1 pancake onto middle of hot buttered skillet.
tilt skillet to achieve uniform thickness - THIN!
brown and flip and serve

Each person fills and rolls their own pancake - eat hot.

Filling options:
butter and syrup
butter and cinnamon sugar
applesauce and cinnamon sugar
sour cream and powdered sugar (my favorite!)
peanut butter and honey
jam or fruit syrup
custard
yogurt
whatever sounds good...
