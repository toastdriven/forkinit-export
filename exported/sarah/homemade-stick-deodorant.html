Forkinit: Homemade Stick Deodorant

By Sarah Potter
Still tweaking... I like it so far, but it may get too soft in summer.

Ingredients

1/2 c. coconut oil
2 T. shea butter or cocoa butter
1/2 T. beeswax (antiperspirant and keeps the stick more solid)
3/8 c. arrowroot powder or cornstarch
3/8 c. baking soda
3/8 c. bentonite clay
20-40 drops Plant Therapy brand Deodorizing Synergy EO

Instructions

Melt first three ingredients in double boiler.
Stir in dry ingredients.
When cool enough to keep your finger in easily, add EO.
Press into empty deodorant stick container or jar.

Notes: can be simplified to use ingredients on hand. This blog post helps decide which are right for you. http://amyrogershays.com/2013/07/16/homemade-deodorant-for-sensitive-skin-bentonite-clay-and-baking-soda/
If it is too melty in hot weather, increase beeswax or Shea or Cocoa butter and decrease coconut oil accordingly.
Tea tree EO is really effective for smells prevention, so, if not using the deodorizing blend, use Tea Tree EO plus whatever smells you enjoy!

Yields: 3 small sticks
