Forkinit: Mineral supplement

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

4 parts Potassium Citrate powder (Now)
4 parts Magnesium Malate powder
2 parts Dolomite powder (Now)
2 parts MSM powder (Now) (adults only)
4 parts Acerola powder (Mr Ros)
2 parts Ascorbic Acid powder
1 part Baking Soda
4 parts unrefined salt

Instructions

Parts are determined by volume, not weight.
Mix all together and store airtight.
Mix with water to serve.

Adult dose 1 heaping tsp 1-2 times daily
Child dose 1/4 - 1/2 tsp 1-2 times daily

As this is a supplement use with the same caution you would any other supplement.
