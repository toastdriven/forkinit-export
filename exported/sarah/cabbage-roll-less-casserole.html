Forkinit: Cabbage Roll-less Casserole

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 head cabbage chopped into 2 inch cubes
28 oz. can diced tomato
1 small can tomato paste
1 8 oz. can diced green chili peppers
1 medium onion
1 green pepper
1 1/2 lb. hot pork sausage
1 cup uncooked rice (cook with about 1 1/2 cup water)
salt and pepper to taste
mozzarella cheese (optional)

Instructions

Cook rice.
Saute onion and green pepper.
Mix onions and peppers with tomato and paste.
Fry sausage.
Spread a tiny bit of tomato sauce onto the bottom of the pan to reduce sticking.
Layer chopped cabbage, sausage, rice, tomato mixture, salt.
Repeat.
End with cabbage
Top with cheese if desired.
It tastes better the next day.

Yields: 9x13 casserole
