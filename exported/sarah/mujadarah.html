Forkinit: Mujadarah

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

Layer 1:
1 cup Lentils
2 cups rice
1 tsp cumin powder
2 cups hot water
Salt and pepper to taste

Layer 2:
2 large onions, diced
1 small eggplant, cubed
2 Tbs coconut oil

Layer 3:
2 large cucumbers, cubed
2 large tomatoes, cubed
1/4 tsp ground ginger or to taste

Layer 4:
2-4 cups Greek yogurt

Tomato chutney:
2 cups tomato
1 onion, minced
3 Tbs lemon juice
2 Tbs apple cider vinegar
1 Tbs sugar
Salt and pepper to taste
1/4 tsp ginger if serving over chicken.

Chicken (optional)

Instructions

Combine lentils with water to cover. Cook 7 minutes or until half done.
Add remaining layer 1 ingredients and cook covered until tender and rice is done.
Fry Layer 2 ingredients in a frying pan.
Toss Layer 3 ingredients together.
Prepare chutney the day before in food processor, or finely chopped.
To serve, layer ingredients on individual plates.
If serving without chicken, serve chutney as a side dish.
If serving with chicken, season chicken with ginger and salt before cooking and add ginger to the chutney. Serve chutney on top of piece of chicken.
