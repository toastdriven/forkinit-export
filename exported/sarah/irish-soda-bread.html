Forkinit: Irish Soda Bread

By Sarah Potter
A moist, but heavy bread. Great toasted!

Ingredients

3 3/4 c. fresh ground flour
1 t. salt
1/2 t. baking soda
1 - 2 T. honey
1 1/2 c. buttermilk

Instructions

Combine dry ingredients.
Add honey and buttermilk and knead slightly.
Shape into a round about 1 1/2 inches thick.
Slash X across the top.
Bake on greased cookie sheet or pie pan at 375 until done (about 45 minutes)

Yields: 1 loaf
Prep time: 1 hour
