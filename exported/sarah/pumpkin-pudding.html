Forkinit: Pumpkin Pudding

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 cups pumpkin puree
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 t cinnamon
1/2 t nutmeg
1/2 t. ginger OR
1 t. fresh grated ginger
1/2 t. salt
3 eggs, slightly beaten
1 c heavy cream
3/4 cup milk

Instructions

Blend all ingredients
Pour into a buttered 1 1/2 quart casserole.
Bake at 350 for 55 to 65 minutes or until a knife inserted near enter comes out clean.
Cool and garnish with whipped cream.
