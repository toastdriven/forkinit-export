Forkinit: Double Chocolate Bread

By Sarah Potter
Not overly sweet, chocolatey yeast bread.

Ingredients

1 c warm milk
1 1/2 t. vanilla
1 egg
1 T. melted butter
3.4 t. salt
3 T. (heaping) Sucanat
4 t. unsweetened cocoa
3 c. fresh ground hard wheat flour
1 t. yeast
2/3 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1/2 t. cinnamon (optional)

Instructions

Combine milk, vanilla egg, butter and salt.
Add sucanat and cocoa and mix well.
Stir in half the flour and yeast until well mixed.
Add remaining flour to make a soft dough.
Add chocolate chips and cinnamon last and mix just until well blended.
Pour into greased loaf pans and smooth top.
Let rise for several hours. This bread rises very slowly!
Bake at 350 for 20-30 min.

Yields: 2 loaves
Prep time: 4+ hours
