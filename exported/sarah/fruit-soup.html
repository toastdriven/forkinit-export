Forkinit: Fruit Soup

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

5 1/3 c. water
1/4 c. pearl tapioca
1/2 c. raisins
1/2 c. prunes
1/2 c. dried apricots
1/2 c. sugar
1 sm. lemon, sliced thin
1 sm. stick cinnamon
Cream

Instructions

In a large kettle, combine water and tapioca; set aside for 2 hours to soak. Add raisins, prunes, apricots, sugar, lemon slices and cinnamon stick and cook 1 1/2 hours, stirring constantly. Remove the cinnamon stick. Can serve warm or cold. Drizzle cream on top of individual bowls. Will keep refrigerated for 2 or 3 weeks.
