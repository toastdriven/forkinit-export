Forkinit: Seasoning Salt

By Sarah Potter
Lawry's copycat

Ingredients

Fast and easy variety:
2 tablespoons salt
2 teaspoons sugar
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1/4 teaspoon turmeric
1/4 teaspoon onion powder
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon cornstarch
More complete variety:

2/3 cup salt
2 tablespoons superfine sugar (plus 1 teaspoon superfine sugar)
1 tablespoon cornstarch

HERBS AND SPICES
3 tablespoons celery salt (Tradewinds Ground Celery Salt)
2 tablespoons paprika
1 tablespoon onion powder
2 teaspoons garlic powder
2 teaspoons ground turmeric
1/2 teaspoon ground mace
1/2 teaspoon ground oregano
1/4 teaspoon ground thyme
1/8 teaspoon ground cumin
1 pinch ground cinnamon
1 pinch ground cloves

Instructions

Mix ingredients together and store in spice jars.
