Forkinit: Fresh Strawberry Pie

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
1 quart (or more) fresh strawberries
3/4 cup white sugar
3 tablespoons cornstarch
1 cup water
1 cup heavy whipping cream

Instructions

Arrange half of strawberries in baked pastry shell.
Mash remaining berries and combine with sugar, cornstarch, and water in a medium saucepan.
Place saucepan over medium heat and bring to a boil, stirring frequently. Boil 2-3 minutes.
Pour mixture over berries in pastry shell. Chill for several hours before serving.
In a small bowl, whip cream until soft peaks form. Serve each slice of pie with a dollop of whipped cream.

Yields: 1 pie
