Forkinit: Baked French Toast

By Sarah Potter
A french toast you can prepare the night before and bake in the morning.

Ingredients

1 loaf french bread (or any kind of bread you like)
8 eggs
2 c. milk
1 1/2 c. cream
2 t. vanilla
1/4 t. cinnamon

Topping:
3/4 c. butter
1 1/3 c. brown sugar
3 T. maple syrup

Instructions

Butter a 9x13 baking dish.
Arrange the bread to cover the bottom.
In a large bowl, beat together eggs, milk, cream, vanilla, cinnamon.
Pour over bread, cover and refrigerate overnight.
Measure topping ingredients into a small saucepan, cover and set on stove.

Next morning preheat oven to 350.
Heat topping ingredients until bubbling. Pour over bread and egg.
Bake uncovered about 40 minutes.

Yields: 12 servings
