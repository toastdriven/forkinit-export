Forkinit: Special K Cookies (Flossenschnoole)

By Sarah Potter
These cookies always make me think of my Great Aunt Floss (hence the silly alternate name). We always had them around for Christmas.

Ingredients

2 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/3 C sugar
2 eggs
1 C butter, softened
1 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla
6 C Special K Cereal

Instructions

Blend butter, sugar , vanilla.
Add eggs, beat well.
Add dry ingredients and TWO cups of Special K cereal.
Roll teaspoons full of dough in remaining cereal.
Bake 325 degrees 10-15 minutes until lightly browned

Yields: 50-60 cookies
Source: Aunt Floss
