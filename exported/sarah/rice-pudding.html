Forkinit: Rice Pudding

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 large eggs
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
3 cups milk
2 cups cooked rice
3/4 cups raisins

Instructions

Beat slightly; eggs, sugar, salt vanilla, and nutmeg.
Scald milk and pour into egg mixture.
Pour this mixture over rice
Bake at 350 degrees about one hour.
Serve hot or cold.

Yields: 9x13 pan or 3qt casserole
