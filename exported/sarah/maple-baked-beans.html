Forkinit: Maple Baked Beans

By Sarah Potter
These baked beans are slow-cooked in a crockpot for about 24 hours. As the sap slowly cooks down it turns into a sweet, maple-flavored gravy.

Ingredients

4 cups dried navy beans (or any bean)
10 cups fresh maple sap
1 Tbsp salt
2 medium onions, minced

Instructions

Soak beans in water overnight.
In the morning drain, put beans in a crockpot and add 10 cups fresh maple sap.
Turn crock pot on high and cook all day until beans are soft and tender.
Add diced onions and a little more maple sap if they seem a bit dry or low on liquid at this point.
Continue to cook with the lid off of the crockpot overnight.
