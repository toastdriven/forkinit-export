Forkinit: Cheese Cake

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

Crust:
7 whole double graham crackers
1/4 c. butter
2 T. sugar

Filling:
16 oz. cream cheese
2 eggs
1/2 t. vanilla
1/2 c. sugar

Topping:
1 c. sour cream
2 T. sugar
1/2 t. vanilla

Instructions

Crust:
Crush graham crackers.
Mix with sugar.
Add melted butter and press into 9" pie pan

Filling:
Beat together till smooth.
Bake 20-30 minutes, until set, at 375
Cool slightly.

Topping:
Beat together till smooth.
Spread on top of slightly cooled cake.
Bake 5 minutes at 375

Yields: 9" pie
