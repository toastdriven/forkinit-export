Forkinit: Sourdough Sour Cream Waffles

By Sarah Potter
quick and simple, making use of "extra" sourdough starter that builds up exponentially (if you keep a sourdough starter, you know what I'm talking about ;)

Ingredients

2 cups discarded sourdough starter
1 tablespoon vanilla extract
⅔ cup sour cream
2 tablespoons melted butter
1 tablespoon honey
1 teaspoon baking powder
¼ teaspoon sea salt
½ teaspoon baking soda

Instructions

Preheat waffle maker as directed by the manufacturer.
In a medium bowl, beat eggs.
Whisk in starter, vanilla, sour cream, melted butter, and honey.
In a separate small bowl stir together baking powder, sea salt, and baking soda. Add the dry ingredients to the wet and stir just until combined.
Grease or butter waffle maker, if necessary, and pour in batter and cook according to the manufacturer’s instructions.
