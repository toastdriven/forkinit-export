Forkinit: Almond Crescent (Nussbaumkinder)

By Sarah Potter
My grandma always made these for Christmas. She never put almonds in them, maybe because of the cost difference? So neither do we. Still they are one of my favorites!

Ingredients

1 cup lard or shortening
1 cup butter
2/3 cup sugar
2 tsp almond extract
4 cups sifted flour
1 1/3 cups ground walnuts
1 cup chocolate chips (heaping)
or
Powdered sugar

Instructions

Cream first 4 ingredients.
Add flour, then nuts.
Refrigerate.
Form into small crescents (about 1 heaping tsp of dough yields around 7 dozen)
Bake 325° Until light brown. About 15 minutes.
Let cool completely before spooning melted chocolate to one end. Don’t dip because they will stick to the tray.
Chill to set.
Alternatively, they can be rolled in powdered sugar.

Yields: 7-8 dozen
Source: Grandma Helen
