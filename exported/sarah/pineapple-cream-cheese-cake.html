Forkinit: Pineapple Cream Cheese Cake

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 box Jiffy yellow cake mix or recipe to fit an 8x8 pan
1 box instant pineapple or lemon pudding
8 oz. cream cheese - softened
2 c. cold milk (or use drained pineapple juice + milk to equal 2 cups liquid)
1 #2 can drained crushed pineapple
1 large cool whip OR
1 pt. whipping cream and sugar

Instructions

Prepare cake mix. Bake in 9x13 pan.
While baking, whip cream cheese.
Add milk and pudding mix and beat until thick.
Spread on top of cooled cake.
Spread drained pineapple over this.
If using cool whip, spread on top.
Cover and refrigerate overnight.
If using whipping cream, just cover and refrigerate overnight.
Whip cream when ready to serve and spread on top.

Yields: 9x13 cake
Prep time: overnight
