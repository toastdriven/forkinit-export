Forkinit: Apple Turnovers

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 c. flour
1/2 t. salt
1/3 c. butter
3 oz. cream cheese

Filling:
1 1/2 c. finely chopped apples
1/4 c. sugar
1/4 t. cinnamon
dash nutmeg

Instructions

Combine first 4 ingredients.
Make into 4-6 little balls. Chill.
Mix filling and spoon into rolled dough to make individual turnovers. Seal edges with a fork.
Bake at 400 20 min.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar (optional)

Yields: 4-6 turnovers
