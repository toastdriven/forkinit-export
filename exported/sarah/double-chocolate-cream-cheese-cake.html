Forkinit: Double Chocolate Cream Cheese Cake

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

CAKE:
3 c. flour
2 c. sugar
2 c. cocoa
2 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. hot coffee
2/3 c. oil
2 tbsp. vinegar
2 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
FILLING:
1/3 c. sugar
8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 egg
1/2 c. chocolate chips
1 c. finely chopped nuts
1/4 c. sugar

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 degrees.
In large bowl combine all cake ingredients. Beat at low speed until moistened.
Beat 1 minute at medium speed.
Pour into greased and floured 9"x13" pan.
In small bowl, cream 1/3 cup sugar, cream cheese, 1/2 teaspoon vanilla and 1 egg until fluffy.
Fold in chocolate chips and nuts.
Spoon teaspoonfuls of filling evenly over batter;
sprinkle with 1/4 cup sugar.
Bake for 45 to 60 minutes.

Yields: 9x13 cake
