Forkinit: Everlasting Fruit Salad

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 box small noodles 8 ounces.
2 eggs beaten
1 cup sugar
2 cans crushed pineapple #2 can (20oz.) drained and save juice
2 Tablespoons flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 or 2 cans fruit cocktail #2 can drained ( don't save juice) chill
2 cups marshmallows
1 pt whipping cream
Can add coconut or bananas ( but bananas turn brown)

Instructions

Cook noodle rings, drain, and cool.
Cook eggs, sugar,flour and salt in top of double boiler
Add pineapple juice and cook until slightly thick.
Poor over noodles and mix and set in refrigerator overnight , covered.
Next day fold in whipped cream (whip it), marshmallows, and cooled fruit and serve...with a smile.
