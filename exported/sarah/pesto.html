Forkinit: Pesto

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 1/3 c. oliver oil
9 cloves garlic, pressed
3 t. salt
1 1/2 t. pepper
1 1/2 c. parmesan cheese
1 c. walnuts or minenuts or almonds
2 c. fresh basil

Instructions

Mix together in a food processor or blender.
Serve over pasta, pitas, etc.
