Forkinit: Ganache

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 cup heavy cream
9 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
1 tablespoon dark rum (optional)

Instructions

Heat cream just to a boil.
Whisk in chocolate until smooth.
Stir in rum if desired.
Allow to cool slightly before pouring over a cake.
For a fluffy frosting or chocolate filling, allow to cool until thick, then whip with a whisk until light and fluffy.

Yields: 2 cups
