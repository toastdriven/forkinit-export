Forkinit: Lime Curd

By Sarah Potter
Easy, made with whole eggs rather than the usual yolks only.

Ingredients

6 large eggs (or 12 yolks)
1 1/2 cup cane juice crystals
Pinch of salt
1 cup fresh lime juice (or bottled Key Lime juice)
Zest of 2 large or 4 small limes (or Key limes) (optional)
8 tablespoons unsalted butter, diced

Instructions

Place eggs, sugar, salt, juice, and zest in a medium saucepan. Do not put it over the heat yet. Whisk the ingredients together until smooth.
Place over low heat. Stir constantly with a wooden spoon until the mixture thickens, about 4-5 minutes. Turn all the way to low and add the butter. Stir until smooth.
Remove from heat and pour into jar(s). Makes just shy of 4 cups.

Yields: 1 quart
