Forkinit: Broccolli Salad

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients


Chop really fine:
medium fresh broccoli head
2 hard boiled eggs
small onion
green olives with pimento to taste

Add:
few T. mayonaise
few squirts lemon juice
garlic salt to taste

Instructions

Refrigerate overnight for better flavor.
