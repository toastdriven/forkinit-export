Forkinit: Chicken rice casserole

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 cups dry rice
4 cups chicken broth
1 1/2 cups sour cream
1 heaping tsp. salt
Black pepper to taste
Broccoli
Chicken breasts cut to equal one serving

Instructions

Combine first five ingredients in bottom of a 9x13 casserole pan.
Layer on broccoli chunks.
Sear salted chicken in pan with oil.
Layer on chicken pieces.
Salt and pepper to taste and cover with foil.
Bake 350Â° about 1 hour or until done.
