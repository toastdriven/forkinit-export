Forkinit: Honey Glaze

By Sarah Potter
Simple glaze I use on cinnamon rolls.

Ingredients

2/3 c. honey
1/4 c. brown sugar
1/3 cup butter
1 t. pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Melt honey sugar and butter and boil a short time so it will be slightly thick when it cools. Stir constantly.
Stir in vanilla.
Drizzle.

Yields: 1 cup
Prep time: 5 min.
