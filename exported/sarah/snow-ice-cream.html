Ingredients

2 eggs
2 c. milk or cream
1 1/2 c. sugar
3 T. vanilla

Instructions

Beat eggs in a large bowl.
Add cream, sugar and vanilla.
Mix together and add enough clean snow to create the desired consistency.
Refrigerate for a while to get a smoother consistency.
Can be stored in the freezer.

Prep time: 10 min.
