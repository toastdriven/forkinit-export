Forkinit: Spicy Vinegar Pickles

By Sarah Potter
The oven processing keeps the pickles nice and crisp. These pickles can also be hot water bath 10 minutes, but a grape leaf should be added to retain more crispness. Or you can just stick them in the fridge without canning and let them sit for a month before eating.

Ingredients

cucumbers
1 t. mustard seed
1 large head dill
1-4 garlic cloves
1-5 chilies

Brine:
4 1/2 c. water
4 c. vinegar
6T salt

Instructions

Sterilize jars.
Pack quarts with cucumbers and spices.
Boil brine and fill jars.
Set jars into a couple inches hot water in ovenproof pan.
Bake jars 1 hour at 250. (I need to double check the time, now I'm second guessing my memory...)

Yields: approx. 5 qts. Depends on how tightly the jars are packed.
