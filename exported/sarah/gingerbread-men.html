Forkinit: Gingerbread Men

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1/2 cup lard or butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup molasses
1/4 cup water
2 1/2 cups flour
3/4 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. ginger
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. allspice

Instructions

Cream butter and sugar.
Combine dry ingredients.
Combine all ingredients.
Chill 3-4 hours.
Roll 1/4 inch thick.
Bake 370 for 9 minutes.
Remove from baking sheet immediately.
