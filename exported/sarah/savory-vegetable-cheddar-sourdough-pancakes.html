Forkinit: Savory Vegetable-Cheddar Sourdough Pancakes

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 large carrots
 1/4 medium head of cabbage
 1/2 large onion
 1/2 cup packed grated cheddar cheese
 3 eggs
 3 cups active, bubbling sourdough starter (that has been fed in the past 4 to 6 hours)
 2 teaspoons salt
 1/2 teaspoon pepper
 1 tablespoon garlic powder
1 teaspoon smoked paprika powder
 Lard, tallow, or coconut oil for frying

Instructions

In a large bowl beat eggs. Use a box grater to grate carrots, cabbage, onion, and cheese right over eggs. Add salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika and mix well.
2. Pour starter over contents of bowl and mix just to combine. Let stand five minutes.
3. Meanwhile, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add 3 tablespoons of cooking fat
and allow to melt and heat up.
4. Once skillet is well-heated, ladle 1/3 cup portions into hot fat to fry pancakes. Fry for
3 to 5 minutes or until they are browned and can flip easily. Cook through on the other side. Serve with salsa, a creamy, tart sauce, and a salad.
