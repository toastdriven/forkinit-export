Forkinit: Scouring Powder

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

2 parts baking soda
1 part salt
1 part borax powder

Instructions

To make:
Pour ingredients into jar or bowl.
Mix or stir until well mixed.
If you want to make a shaker, put in a pint or quart mason jar and poke some holes in the top (carefully) with a sharp serrated knife. Or use an empty Parmesan cheese shaker.

To Use:
Lightly wet surface with water or undiluted white vinegar (for really bad messes)
Sprinkle powder on and let sit 5 minutes
Scrub with sturdy brush until clean
Rinse with water or vinegar (for really bad messes)
