Forkinit: Tapioca Pudding

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 c. honey or other sweetener
3/4 c. tapioca pearls
4 egg
11-12 c. milk
2 T. cornstarch (optional)
4 t. vanilla

Instructions

Mix honey, tapioca and egg in pan.
If using cornstarch mix it into 1/2 c. milk prior to adding.
Add milk and cornstarch mixture and let set for 10 minutes.
Bring slowly to a full boil.
Remove from heat and stir in vanilla.
Serve warm or cool in serving bowl or individual cups.

Yields: about 12 cups
Prep time: 45 min.
