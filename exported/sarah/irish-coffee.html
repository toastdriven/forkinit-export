Forkinit: Irish Coffee

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 cup Heavy Cream
3 Tablespoons Bailey's Irish Creme
1/2 cup Irish Whiskey (or Any Whiskey)
4 Tablespoons Brown Sugar
4 cups Strong Hot Coffee

Instructions

With an electric mixer, whip cream and Bailey's together until stiff. Set aside.

Into each of the four large coffee mugs, add 2 tablespoons whiskey, 1 tablespoon of brown sugar, and 1 cup coffee and stir. Top with a very generous layer of the Bailey's whipped cream. (Almost a third of the cup should be taken up by the whipped cream.)

Drink the coffee through the cream! Delicious.

Yields: 4
