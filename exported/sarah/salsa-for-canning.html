Forkinit: Salsa for canning

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 red onion, chopped
1 white onion, chopped
1 yellow onion, chopped
1-2 bulbs garlic, minced
6 pounds fresh tomatoes, chopped and drained
2 banana peppers, chopped
3 green bell peppers, chopped
3 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
1/4 cup lime juice
1 1/2 tablespoons salt
1 tablespoon cayenne pepper or to taste
Fresh cilantro

Instructions

Put all veggies through a food processor, but not too long as you want some chunkiness.
As you do each load of tomatoes, let strain through a colander for a minute or two before putting into cooking pot.
Combine all ingredients in a stock pot and cook until thick.
Can at 10 pounds pressure for 15 minutes.

Yields: 8 pints
