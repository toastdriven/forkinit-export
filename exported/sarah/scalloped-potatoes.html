Forkinit: Scalloped Potatoes

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

6 or more potatoes
1 large onion
6 Tbsp. butter
1/2 cup flour
1 tsp. salt (more to taste)
3 cup cream
2 cup milk plus more
Pepper
ham (optional)

Instructions

Thinly slice potatoes into a greased 9x13 casserole pan (it might boil out of a cake pan) alternating with thinly sliced onions and ham if desired until pan is slightly over half full.
In saucepan, melt butter.
Stir in salt and flour and cook till bubbly.
Add 2 cups cream and 2 cups milk. Heat till beginning to thicken.
Add some pepper and pour over potatoes in pan. Pour additional 1 cup cream over potatoes and add milk so most of the potatoes are covered.
Grind a bit of pepper over the top if desired.
Bake at 400 1 1/2 hours or until potatoes are tender.

Yields: 9x13 casserole
Prep time: 2 1/2 hours
