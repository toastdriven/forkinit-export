Forkinit: Sweet Yeast Dough

By Sarah Potter
For use in sweet rolls, cinnamon, rolls, nut rolls, poppyseed rolls, Russian style pie, etc.

Ingredients

Proof:
1 package active dry yeast (2 1/4 tsp.)
2 tablespoons sugar
1/2 cup warm water
Scald:
1 1/2 cups milk
Add to milk:
4 ounces butter (8 Tbs.) (butter needs to melt in the hot milk)
1/2 cup sugar
Beat till thick:
3 large whole eggs
3 large egg yolks (use the whites for nut mixture in nut roll)
Remaining ingredients:
1 teaspoon vanilla
Zest of 1 lemon or orange (one tsp. powdered dry zest can be substituted)
1/2 teaspoon salt
6 cups (or more as needed) all-purpose flour

Instructions

To beaten eggs, add vanilla, zest, lukewarm milk mixture, and yeast mixture. Beat well.
Add salt and two cups flour. Beat well.
Switch to dough hook, or stir in, remaining flour. Dough will remain soft and sticky at this point.
Let rest 15 minutes, and knead about 5 minutes in machine.
Let rise 1.5 hours.
Shape and fill. Will make two rolls.
Let rise and bake according to the shape you made.
Dough can be refrigerated 24 hours.
For cinnamon rolls:
Melt two sticks butter, spread sugar over rolled out dough, drizzle melted butter, sprinkle with cinnamon, roll and cut. Be sure to drizzle any butter/sugar that oozed out during rolling and cutting over the tops of the pre-baked rolls on the pan! Bake about 20-30 minutes at 375Â°
For nut roll:
Beat egg whites till fluffy and add finely chopped walnuts. Roll dough as you would for cinnamon roll and spread nut mixture, sugar, melted butter (1 stick), bit of cinnamon, and roll as you would a cinnamon roll to form a log. Do not cut, but bake as it is. Slice after cooled.
