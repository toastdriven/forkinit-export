Forkinit: Corn Bread

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1 cup milk
1/4 cup butter, melted
1 egg
1 1/4 cup cornmeal
1 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar or 1/3 cup honey
1 Tbsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
sprinkle nutmeg

Instructions

Heat oven to 375 degrees.
Melt butter in pan in pre-heating oven. 9 inch round pan or 8x8 square pan. I use a cast iron skillet
Beat milk, melted butter and egg in large bowl.
Stir in remaining ingredients all at once just until lour is moistened (batter will be lumpy).
Pour into pan and bake 15 or more minutes, until set. Golden brown and toothpick inserted into center comes out clean.

Yields: 9" skillet
