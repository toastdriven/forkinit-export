Forkinit: Goop

By Sarah Potter
WARNING: Not for consumption!

Ingredients

8 oz. white school glue
6 oz. water
1 tsp. borax
1/2 c. water
Food coloring

Instructions

Combine glue and 6 oz. water in large bowl.
Combine borax, 1/2 cup water and food coloring in separate container.
Pour borax mixture into glue mixture and mix with hands until all liquid is absorbed and mixture is smooth.
