Forkinit: Mouthwash

By Sarah Potter
Makes a great easy mouthwash and can also be used on your scalp before washing hair to help with dandruff issues.

Ingredients

1 cup water (filtered or boiled is best since it can sit at room temperature)
1 tsp baking soda
2-3 drops peppermint essential oil
1 drop tea tree essential oil (optional)

Instructions

Mix in a bottle.
Shake before use as the baking soda settles.
Swish or apply for at least a couple minutes.

Yields: 8 oz.
Prep time: 2 minutes
