Forkinit: Molasses Cookies

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

3/4 c. butter
1 c. sugar
4 T. molasses
1 egg
2 c. flour
1 t. cinnamon
1/2 t. cloves
1/2 t. ginger
2 t. baking soda

Instructions

Combine ingredients.
Form into ball and roll in sugar.
Press with a fork.
Bake at 375.

Source: Linda Potter (family recipe)
