Forkinit: Grandma's Pancakes

By Sarah Potter
The buttermilk pancakes my grandma used to make.

Ingredients

2 1/2 c. buttermilk
2 eggs (beaten well)
2 c. flour
1 1/2 t. baking soda
1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
2 heaping T. melted butter

Instructions

Start butter melting.
Beat eggs in medium bowl.
Add buttermilk and mix.
Sift remaining ingredients together into bowl.
Mix as little as possible.
Mix in butter.
Bake on ungreased griddle.

Yields: serves 4 people a large pancake breakfast
