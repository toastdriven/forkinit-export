Forkinit: Crumpets

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

1.5 cups semi-skimmed milk
1 lb flour
1 tsp yeast
2 tsp sugar
1.5 cups lukewarm water
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
Lard or butter for cooking

Instructions

Whisk together milk, flour, yeast, sugar.
Add half the water and beat into the batter. Continue adding water until the batter is thick and smooth. Stop adding water once the consistency of thick cream.
Cover and let sit in a draft free location until foaming (1-2 hours)
Whisk in the salt and baking powder.
Lightly grease a heavy pan and 3"x1.5" crumpet rings (sitting in the pan) to hot, but not smoking.
Fill rings with batter to just below the top.
Cook approximately 5 minutes, until there are many tiny holes on the surface and the crumpet is setting. Flip and cook additional 2-3 minutes. Repeat.

Note: If the batter seeps under the ring, whisk in more flour. If the crumpet is heavy and doesn't form holes, add more water.

Yields: 24 crumpets
Prep time: 2-3 hours
