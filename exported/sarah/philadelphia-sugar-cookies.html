Forkinit: Philadelphia Sugar Cookies

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

8 oz. cream cheese (softened)
1 c. butter
2/3 cup sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla
2 cups flour
colored sugar or decorations

Instructions

Beat cream cheese, butter, sugar and vanilla until well blended.
Add flour and mix well.
Refrigerate several hours or overnight.
Roll 1/4 inch thick on floured surface.
Decorate.
Bake 350 12-15 minutes.

Prep time: overnight
