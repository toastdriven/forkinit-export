Forkinit: Lotion

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

3/4 cup oil (olive, avocado or coconut are great choices*)
3 tablespoons beeswax, grated
1 cup water, rose water, or herbal tea â€“ room temp or slightly warmer
essential oil for fragrance (optional)
Vitamin E oil (optional - to enhance shelf life)

Instructions

Melt 3/4 cup oil and beeswax in double boiler.
Pour tea into food processer or blender. While blender is running, very, very slowly drizzle warmed oil into the tea.
Add vitamin E oil and essential oils.
