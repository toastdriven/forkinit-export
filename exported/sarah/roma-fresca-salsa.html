Forkinit: Roma Fresca - Salsa

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

5 large roma tomatoes, skinned, seeded and chopped
1 medium sweet or yellow onion, diced
1/2 bunch of cilantro, chopped
2 cloves of garlic, minced
hot peppers to taste, chopped
juice of 1 small lime
4 Tbs. whey
1/2-1 Tbs. sea salt

Instructions

Mix all together and put in quart jar.
Leave at room temperature overnight.
Lasts 2 months in cold storage.

Yields: 1 quart
