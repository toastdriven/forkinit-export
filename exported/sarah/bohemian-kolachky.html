Ingredients

2 cup flour
2 T sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup butter (softened)
8 oz cream cheese (softened)
2 medium eggs (well beaten)
1/2 c. cherry or apricot preserves

Instructions

Stir together flour, salt, sugar, baking powder in large bowl.
In large bowl cream butter and cream cheese
stir in flour mixture.
Add eggs, mixing to form soft dough
Chill until firm enough to roll out (several hours - overnight)
Roll out 1/4 of the dough at a time ona floured surface, flour rolling pin as well, to 1/4 inch thickness.
Keep remainder chilled and use lots of flour to roll out.
cut rounds 1 3/4 inch. re-roll scraps.
With floured finger make deep indentation in centers
Fill with 1/4 tsp preserves (too much will boil over)
bake 1 inch apart on ungreased baking sheet
bake 375 15 minutes, until golden brown
best if eaten within a week.

Yields: 78 cookies
Prep time: overnight
Source: Yedinak family recipe
