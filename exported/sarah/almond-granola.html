Forkinit: Almond Granola

By Sarah Potter

Ingredients

7 c. rolled oats
1/4 t. salt
1/4 t. stevia powder
1/4 c. brewers yeast
1/2 c. coconut
1 c. almond slivers mixed with 1/4 t. almond extract
1 c. Mixed dried fruit (I add half before baking and half after)

Melt in a pan:
1/2 - 1 stick butter
1/2 c. water
1/4 c. sucanat
1/4 c. cane juice crystals

Instructions

Mix the dry ingredients.
Add the wet ingredients and mix.
Bake in roaster pan 350Â° 20 minutes.
Stir every 10 minutes.
