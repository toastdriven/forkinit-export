Forkinit: Bee Sugar Syrup

By Sarah Potter
this really is for feeding bees, but it smells delicious!

Ingredients

8 cups white sugar (do not substitute)
3 cups almost boiling water
1 cup chamomile tea
2 Tbs of your own honey
1/2 tsp fresh squeezed lemon juice
A healthy pinch of sea salt
3-4 drops lemongrass essential oil

Instructions

Mix sugar and water until completely dissolved.
Mix in remaining ingredients leaving out the lemongrass EO.
Mix in lemongrass EO when cooled to room temperature.

I have heard mint can also be beneficial to bees to keep trachea mites and hive beetle in check. I might try adding mint either into the tea or as an EO.
