Forkinit: Sanina's Sauerkraut

By Sarah Potter
Served hot.

Ingredients

1 bag sauerkraut
1 Tbs. Caraway seeds
1 Tbs. Dill seeds
4-6 strips of bacon
1 onion
1 potato

Instructions

Rinse and drain kraut.
Cover with water and add seeds.
In a separate pan fry bite sized bacon pieces with onion.
Finely grate potato.
Cook all together for 1/2 hour on low heat in non-stainless steel pan.
