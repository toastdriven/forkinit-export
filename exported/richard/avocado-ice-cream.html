Forkinit: Avocado Ice Cream

By Richard Bronosky
In many parts of the world the Avocado is used in a sweet context. Here in America the avocado is primarily enjoyed in a savory context. That makes this treat even more intriguing.

Ingredients

2 cups skim milk
1/2 cup sugar
3 avocados

Instructions

These instructions and measurements are perfect for 1.5 quart counter top ice cream makers.

Chill avocados for several hours
Add milk and sugar to blender
Blend 1 minute starting on high speed*
Remove blender jar and place in the freezer while you prep the avocados (do not freeze)
Half, pit, and peel the avocados using this technique: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PAAMx_R523o&feature=related
Add avocado to blender jar and blend 2 minutes starting on high speed
Pour into ice cream maker
Run ice cream maker 20 minutes
Scoop soft ice cream into a bowl with air tight lid and place into freezer to harden

* start on low and work your way to high

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 25 minutes
Source: inspired by Krishna Patel's avocado smoothie
