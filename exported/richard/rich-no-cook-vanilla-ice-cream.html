Forkinit: Rich No-Cook Vanilla Ice Cream

By Richard Bronosky
I wasn't making any friends by making ice cream out of heavy cream.

Ingredients

4 cups Half & Half
1 cup Skim Milk
3/4 cup Sugar
1 tbs Vanilla Extract
3 tbs Vanilla Pudding Mix

Instructions

Combine wet ingredients and sugar.
Whisk vigorously.
(Optional) Place in freezer until ice forms on top. (about 2 hours)
Add Vanilla Pudding Mix.
Whisk vigorously.
Whisk vigorously.
Whisk vigorously. Seriously. The more air in the mixture the better.
Pour mixture into ice cream maker and churn.

Yields: 6 Southern Adult Servings
Prep time: 5 minutes
