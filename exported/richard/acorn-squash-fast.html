Forkinit: Acorn Squash - Fast

By Richard Bronosky
Microwaving an acorn squash is a perfectly acceptable preparation. Don't feel like you are sacrificing quality for speed.

Ingredients

1 acorn squash
2 tablespoons butter
2 tablespoons brown sugar
1/8 teaspoon salt & pepper

Instructions

Split acorn squash in half stem to blossom.
Remove seeds with a spoon.
Place acorn squash halves cut side down in a casserole dish.
Fill casserole dish with 1.5 inches (4 cm) of cold filtered water. (most people's hot water tastes like calcium)
Tilt dish steeply to releas air bubbles from squash cavities.
Microwave for 20 minutes*.

* Tested in a 900 watt microwave. More powerful microwaves will require less time. Water will boil for the last few minutes.

Yields: 2
Prep time: 25 minutes
