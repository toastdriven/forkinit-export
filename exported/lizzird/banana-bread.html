Forkinit: Banana Bread

By lizzird

Ingredients

2 c. all-purpose flour
¾ tsp baking soda
½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
1 c sugar
½ c canola oil
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
½ c buttermilk
3 med bananas, mashed
1/2 c chopped walnuts

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a 9x5x3 loaf pan. In small bowl, combine flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
2. In mid bowl, beat together sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla. Alternate adding dry ingredients and buttermilk. Fold in bananas and nuts.
3. Pour batter into loaf pan. Bake for 45-50 minutes. Let bread cool, then turn out onto wire rack.
