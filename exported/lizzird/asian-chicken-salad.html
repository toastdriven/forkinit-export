Forkinit: Asian chicken salad

By lizzird
A light, fresh, crispy salad for summer

Ingredients

3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
1 bag mixed salad greens (I buy them from Trader Joe’s, so they’re not much more expensive than a head of lettuce; if they’re too pricey at your local grocery store, just use whatever lettuce you like)
1 can (8 oz) mandarin oranges, drained
Almond slices (optional)
Sesame seeds (optional)
Crispy noodle bits (I like the crispy chow mein noodles in the can, but I’ve been known to use Ramen in a pinch)
Sesame salad dressing

Instructions

Cook up the chicken breast however you’d like (I like to grill mine with some lemon pepper), and shred it into bite-size bits. Pour salad greens, oranges, almonds, and crispy noodle bits into a large bowl. Toss. Add chicken on top, and drizzle with a sesame dressing. Top with sesame seeds, if you’d like.

Yields: Feeds 4-5
