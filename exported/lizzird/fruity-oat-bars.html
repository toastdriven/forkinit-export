Forkinit: Fruity Oat Bars

By lizzird

Ingredients

1 1/2 c. oats
1 c. brown sugar
1 1/2 c. flour
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1 3/4 sticks butter (at room temperature)
1 small jar preserves of your choice

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 325. Grease a 9x9 baking dish and set aside. In a large bowl mix together the oats, sugar, flour, salt and baking powder. Mix in the softened butter as thoroughly as you can. Pour about half the oat mixture in the baking dish and press down with your fingers, packing it in a bit.

Spread the jar of preserves evenly over the oat mixture, then top with the remaining oat mixture, again pressing it down with your fingers. Bake 25-30 minutes or until the top is golden brown. Cut into squares once it's cooled
