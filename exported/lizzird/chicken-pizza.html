Forkinit: Chicken Pizza

By lizzird

Ingredients

2 lg pizza crusts
1 roasted chicken (or 2 cans white chicken)
1 can green chilies, diced
2 cups shredded cheese (jack, cheddar, mozzarella)
1 handful chopped cilantro
Mixture 1/2 mayo/smart beat, 1/2 plain yogurt

Instructions

Mix all ingredients to consistency of chicken salad. Spread on crusts.

Bake at 350F for 20-30 minutes, until warm and bubbly.
