Forkinit: Chris Chun's Lemonade Scones

By lizzird

Ingredients

6 Cups (780g) Self Raising Flour
500ml (2 cups) cream (heavy cream)
500ml (2 cups) Sprite or other lemon-lime soda

Instructions

Mix with knife lightly until just combined. Roll out gently, cut into scones with round shaped cutter then bake in moderate oven 180C (350F) for 15 minutes.

Note: Depending on how large you make your scones, this recipe could feed many, many people. Other options: quartering it and making small bite-sized scones, or halving it and making larger scones.
