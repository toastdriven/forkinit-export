Forkinit: Zucchini Noodles

By Kyle Stern

Ingredients

Zucchini
Garlic
Onion
Olive Oil
Salt and Black Pepper, to taste

Instructions

Peel zucchini into strips resembling wide noodles. Place 'noodles' in colander and top with salt. Let zucchini drain for 30 minutes. Rinse noodles with water so the dish doesn't turn out too salty then pat dry with towel before cooking.
Heat oil in pan, saut√© onion until slightly tender. Add garlic and cook mixture for about 2 minutes. Add zucchini noodles and heat through. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Variations:
This dish is really meant to be built up to suit your needs. The zucchini noodles are a great stand-in for pasta if you're looking for a gluten-free solution or just want to integrate more fresh vegetables in a larger meal.

Prep time: 40 minutes
