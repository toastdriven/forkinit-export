Forkinit: Berry Cobbler-Pudding

By Katie D
This is not a true cobbler --it's almost pudding-like-- but super yummy and easy. The variations on this are endless. It also allows for substitutions-- whole wheat or sprouted flour and Sucanat work great and make for a more healthy version. You might want to double or quintuple this recipe-- it goes fast!

Ingredients

1/2 cup melted butter
1 cup milk
3/4 - 1 1/4 cup sugar (depending on preferred sweetness)
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup flour
1 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 cups fresh or frozen berries or stone fruit.

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 and grease a shallow, 2 qt. baking dish. Whisk together all ingredients except the fruit. Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Spread the fruit evenly over the top of the batter-- the fruit will sink in as it bakes. Bake for 1 hour. Try not to burn your mouth when you can't stand to let it cool before you taste.

Prep time: 5-10 minutes
