Forkinit: Knock-Off Emergen-C

By Katie D
Vitamin C drink similar to Emergen-C (note that this does not contain B vitamins like Emergen-C does)

Ingredients

1 part ascorbic acid
1 part lemon or orange peel powder
1/2 part dolomite powder (the fine kind like Kal brand)
1/8 part baking soda (more/less to adjust the amount of fizz)
stevia powder to taste

Instructions

Mix powders thoroughly and store in an airtight container. Keep dry.

To make a drink, stir 1/2 teaspoon of the mix into a glass of juice or water. Sweeten to taste with honey, maple syrup, stevia, etc.
1/2 teaspoon of the mixture should contain approximately 1,000 milligrams of vitamin C.
