Forkinit: Cranberry Relish

By johnjmonaghaniii
Adapted from several on the net

Ingredients

1 Orange
2 Apples
2 C (1 bagish) of cranberries
4 ribs Celery (minced)
2 Tbsp Brandy
1 C white sugar

Instructions

Wash ingredients
Rough chop apples and orange
Run Orange, Apples, and Cranberries through meat grinder (largest grind)
Stir in minced celery, brandy and sugar (to taste. Usually less than a cup)
Leave to mellow. A day is best, but it's tasty in a few hours.
Stir before serving.
