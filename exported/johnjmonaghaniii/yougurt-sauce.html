Forkinit: Yougurt Sauce

By johnjmonaghaniii

Ingredients

1 (6-ounce) container plain yogurt
1/3 cup mayonnaise
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
1 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
Salt and pepper to taste
Sugar to taste

Instructions

Whisk together yogurt and next 3 ingredients; add salt, pepper, and sugar to taste.
