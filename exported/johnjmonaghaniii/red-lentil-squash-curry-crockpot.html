Forkinit: Red Lentil Squash Curry Crockpot

By johnjmonaghaniii

Ingredients

3 Tablespoons Patak's Madras Curry Paste or similar curry paste
Chili paste to taste/heat
6 cups veg broth
1 yellow onion, chopped
2 cups red lentils, rinsed
3 stalks celery, sliced
3 large carrots, peeled and sliced
1 pound (3 cups) butternut squash, peeled and cubed
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup Frozen peas
Optionally add sauteed cubed chicken or beef

Instructions

Mix the curry and chili pastes into a bit of broth and add to the crockpot.
Add all other ingredients
Top up with broth
Cook on high for 4 or low for 6hours.
Adjust seasoning with salt, additional curry paste
Optionally serve with rice.

Yields: 8+
Prep time: 10min prep, 4-6hours cook
