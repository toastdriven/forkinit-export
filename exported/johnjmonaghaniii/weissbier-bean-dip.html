Forkinit: Weissbier Bean Dip

By johnjmonaghaniii

Ingredients

1 1/2 C Black Beans (rinsed)
1/3 C Carrot (grated)
1/3 C Celery (minced)
1/4 C Red Bell Pepper (minced)
1 T Garlic (minced)
1/2 t Coriander(ground)
1 t Cumin(ground)
1/4 t salt
1/2 t Chipotle Pepper Powder
1/4 C Wheat beer (Preferably a Hefeweissen)
1/2 C Sour Cream

Instructions

In a mixing bowl, roughly mash half of the beans with a fork. Stir in all other ingredients, cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving. Stir and serve with tortilla chips.
