Forkinit: Indian Turkey Bowl

By johnjmonaghaniii
Quick, healthy Indian fix with no need for rice.

Ingredients

3 Tbsp oil
1 lb ground turkey
1 chopped onion
1 tsp Salt or to taste
1-inch piece of peeled ginger, grated (or 1/4-1/2 tsp ground ginger)
2 minced garlic cloves
1/2 cup water
1 Tbsp garam masala
1 tsp turmeric
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp ground coriander
1 large Sweet potato (or 2 Yukon Gold), peeled and cut into 1-inch chunks
1 can chickpeas
2-4 Roma/plum tomatoes, diced
1 cup fresh or frozen peas
1/2 cup chopped cilantro

Instructions

Oil pan and heat over medium high
Brown Turkey (Hint: spread it out by pressing into pan and don't stir it until it begins to brown)
Add Onion and saute for 5 minutes
Sprinkle with 1 tsp Salt
Add Ginger and Garlic and saute 2 minutes
Add Water, Spices and Potatoes. Stir to combine and cover. Turn heat down to medium-low and simmer for 10-15 minutes, or until potatoes are tender.
Stir in Chickpeas, Tomatoes, and Peas, cover and continue to simmer 3 minutes
Serve topped with fresh Cilantro


Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 30 minutes
