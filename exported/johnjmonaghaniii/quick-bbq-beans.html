Forkinit: Quick BBQ Beans

By johnjmonaghaniii
This is quick in that it uses canned beans and can be made in about 30 minutes, but cooking it for an hour or two does produce a much better dish.

Ingredients

6-10 strips Thick Cut Bacon, diced
1 large Yellow Onion, diced
1 Green Bell Pepper, diced
2 Jalapeno, diced
6oz Beer (Optional), cheap yellow beer is just fine
2 16oz cans Pinto Beans, rinsed
1 16oz can Kidney Beans, rinsed
1 16oz can White Beans, rinsed
1.5 cups Favorite BBQ sauce ( I use Sweet Baby Rays)

Instructions

Heat a pot over medium
Add bacon and cook until near done, not crispy (4 minutes)
Add onion, and bell pepper and cook until tender (5 minutes)
Add Jalapeno and cook for 1-2 minutes
Deglaze pan with beer and cook for another minute
Add beans and BBQ sauce
Bring to a boil
Cover and reduce heat to simmer and cook for 20 minutes
Reduce heat to low and cook for 1 - 1.5 hours (optional)
