Forkinit: Asian Meatball and Glass Noodle StirFry

By johnjmonaghaniii
Asian Fusion Meatballs (usually served as an appetizer) are partnered with mung bean thread noodles, sugar snap peas, carrots, and red bell pepper in a zesty sauce.

Ingredients

Asian Fusion Meatballs
Recipe: http://forkinit.com/johnjmonaghaniii/recipes/asian-fusion-meatball-hors-duvres/

Stir Fry
oil (high temp)
1 large Carrot (julienned)
1 large Red Bell Pepper (julienned)
1C Sugar Snap Peas
Mung Bean Noodles (Soaked in hot water to hydrate, then drained)

Sauce
1C Chicken Broth
4Tbs Soy Sauce (I use Gluten free Tamari)
2Tbs Orange Mustard
1/4tsp Red Pepper flakes
1Tbs Fresh Ginger (grated)
1Tbs Sweet Chili Sauce

Instructions

Better Instructions soon.

Make meatballs according to recipe, but instead of cooking in a pan, just put them on a lightly greased baking sheet and put them in the oven at 425 until browned and cooked through (about 30min)

Add all sauce ingredients to a small pot and bring to a boil. Reduce heat to simmer, whisk in cornstarch/water mixture and simmer until thickened (about 15min)

Heat oil on high in wok or frying pan
add vegetables and keep them moving for a few minutes (the goal is to leave them with some bite to them. They should be crisp)
Remove from heat and add noodles to pan.
Toss with vegetables for 30 seconds.
Add sauce.
Toss and transfer to a plate to cool.

Serve noodle mixture with meatballs on side/top.



Yields: 6
Prep time: 1hr
