Forkinit: Turkey Burgers over Baby Greens with Avocado Dressing

By johnjmonaghaniii
I like these served over salad greens, but they can easily be served on a bun with lettuce, and slices of red onion.

Ingredients

Burgers:
1 C Corn, Fresh or frozen
1/2 C Red Onion, chopped
1 Small Red Bell Pepper, chopped
2 Jalapeno Peppers, seeded and minced
2 Tsp Olive Oil
2 Tbsp Fresh Lime Juice
2 Garlic Cloves, minced
1/2 Tsp Salt
1/2 Tsp Ground Cumin
1/4 Tsp Chili Powder
1/8 Tsp Dried Oregano
1 lb Ground Turkey

SAUCE:
1 Medium Avocado, peeled
1/2 C Sour Cream
2 Tbsp minced fresh cilantro
2 Tsp Fresh Lime Juice
1 Garlic Clove, minced
1/8 tsp Salt

Salad:
Fresh Spring Mix Salad greens
Red Onion, thinly sliced
Tomatoes
Black Olives
Shredded Cheese

Salad Dressing:
Olive Oil
Lime Juice
Lime Zest
Salt Pepper
Minced Garlic

Instructions

Burgers:
In a large skillet, saute the corn, onion and peppers in oil until crisp-tender. Stir in the lime juice, garlic, salt, cumin, chili powder and oregano; cook 1 minute longer. Transfer to a large bowl and cool slightly. Crumble turkey over mixture and mix well. Shape into four burgers. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Grill burgers, covered, over medium heat or broil 4 in. from the heat for 5-7 minutes on each side or until a meat thermometer reads 165Â° and juices run clear.

Sauce:
In a small bowl, mash avocado with the sour cream, cilantro, lime juice, garlic and salt. Refrigerate until serving.


Salad:
Toss all ingredients accept cheese in a salad bowl with dressing

Serve:
Plate salad, then top with a burger, and about 1/4 cup of sauce. Sprinkle with cilantro and shredded cheese.
