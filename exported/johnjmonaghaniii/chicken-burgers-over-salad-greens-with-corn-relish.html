Forkinit: Chicken Burgers over Salad Greens with Corn Relish

By johnjmonaghaniii

Ingredients

1 (11-ounce) can Mexican-style Corn, drained
1 (4.5-ounce) can Chopped Green Chiles, drained
1/2 cup Breadcrumbs
1 large Egg
1 lb Ground chicken
1/3 cup Roasted Red Peppers, drained and chopped
1/3 cup Red Onion, chopped
1/4 cup Mayonnaise
2 1/2 Tsp Dijon Mustard
2 Tsp salt-free herb-and-spice seasoning
2 teaspoons chopped fresh basil
2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
2 1/2 tablespoons olive oil

Instructions

Burgers:
Remove 1/4 cup corn, and place in a large bowl, reserving remaining corn in can for Tomato, Basil, and Corn Relish.
Remove 1 teaspoon chiles, and place in bowl with corn, reserving remaining chiles in can for Tomato, Basil, and Corn Relish.
Add breadcrumbs, egg, chicken, and next 7 ingredients to corn and chiles in bowl.
Combine chicken mixture until well blended; shape mixture into 4 patties.
Grill patties over medium high heat.

Relish:
Combine remaining corn, Chilies, and Roasted Red Peppers with Red Onion, Basil, Cilantro

Arrange patties on salad, top with Relish, and drizzle with Yogurt Sauce.
