Forkinit: Seed Bars

By johnjmonaghaniii
Gluten free, dairy free, seed, nut, and fruit bars

Ingredients

1 cup raw sunflower seeds
1/2 cup raw pumpkin seeds
1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
1/3 cup raisins, craisins, or other dried fruit (apricots, banana chips, apples)
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1/3 cup dry roasted almonds, pistachios, or mixture of both coarsely chopped
2 tbsp ground flax
1/4 cup brown rice syrup
3/4 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp salt

Optional additions/substitutes:
puffed rice
gluten free rolled oats
cashews
semi sweet chocolate chips
m&m's
Cocoa Powder
pumpkin pie spice

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325
Toast coconut, and any raw seeds in the oven. (around 10 minutes. coconut may take less)
Add to a bowl with all other dry ingredients.
Warm syrup in the microwave
Drizzle syrup over mixture while mixing with your hand.
Line a pan or cookie sheet with wax paper and press mixture into pan. Mixture should be less than 3/8" thick once pressed.
Bake for 8 minutes or until browning begins.
Cool on a rack and then break into chunks or bars.
Keep refrigerated for up to one week or frozen for up to 2 months.
