Forkinit: Chili

By johnjmonaghaniii

Ingredients

1lb Ground Beef
2 Garlic Cloves (minced)
1/2 an Onion (diced)
1-2 Jalapenos (diced)
1 can Tomato Sauce
8oz Stout/Porter
1T Chili Powder
2 Beef Bullion Cubes
2t Cumin
1/4t italian seasoning
1/4t coriander
1/2t unsweetened cocoa

Instructions

Brown Beef and Onions
Add Garlic and Jalapenos
Deglaze with Beer
Add Tomato Sauce, Bullion and all seasonings
Bring to a boil, cover and lower to simmer
Cook till done
