Ingredients

2-3 Large Tomatoes
1 bunch Cilantro
1 bunch Chopped Green Onions
1-2 cloves of Garlic
1-2 Jalepenos or Serranos (depending on your preference for spiciness)
1/2 Lemon
Salt

Instructions

Tear cilantro leaves from stems, and add to large bowl.
Add chopped tomatoes, green onions,garlic, and Jalepenos, and combine all ingredients.
Squeeze in lemon juice and add salt to taste

Yields: 4-5
Prep time: 30 minutes
