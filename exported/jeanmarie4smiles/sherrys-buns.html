Forkinit: Sherry’s Buns

By Mary Jo Kurka
Buns made using a bread maker

Ingredients

1 ¼ cup warm whole milk (powdered milk)
2 eggs
¼ cup melted butter (or oil)
4 cups flour (½ whole wheat ½ white)
½ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. baking soda
½ tsp. baking powder
½ cup sugar (or ¼ cup honey)
1 Tbsp. yeast
1 Tbsp. gluten

Instructions

Stir ingredients in breads pan before it started mixing
Put on bread dough cycle
Make into buns and let rise (double)
Bake at 350⁰ for 10 minutes on middle rack
