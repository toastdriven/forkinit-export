Forkinit: Karen DeGraff's Blueberry Muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka
Delicious with all kinds of fruit

Ingredients

1 ½ cup flour
½ cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
Cut in ¼ cup butter
Combine ½ cup mild and 1 egg and ½ tsp lemon extract
Add wet ingridients to dry ingrideints mix until moist
Fold in ½ - ¾ cup blueberries

Instructions

Put in muffin tins, Bake at 400⁰ for 15-20 minutes
