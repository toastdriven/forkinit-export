Forkinit: Helen's Christmas Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka
Also known as vanoca

Ingredients

Mix in blender:
1 ½ cup scaled milk
1 cup sugar
2 tsp salt
½ tsp mace
2 tsp lemon rind
Let cool until lukewarm
Meanwhile ½ cup lukewarm water and 2 packages dry yeast, let stand 5 minutes and put into above mixture which you have now transferred to a large bowl
Add:
1 cup melted butter
2 eggs
1 cup raisins
½ cup chopped nuts
Now add about 8 cups flour, mixing like you do for bread

Instructions

Let double in size by rising
Can make 2 big braids or 4 small.
Let rise again 30 minute or longer
Bake 325⁰ for 20- 25 minutes less for smaller braids
Cool and frost with powdered sugar frosting
