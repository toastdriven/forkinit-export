Forkinit: Sally’s Hamburger Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ½ pounds lean ground beef
3 qts water
1 lg can tomatoes chopped
3 medium onions chopped
4 carrots, sliced
½ cup checkup
½ pound fresh mushrooms sliced
4 bouillon cubes
¼ tsp garlic powder
4tsp Lawrey’s seasoned salt
2 tsp basil crushed
2 Bay leaves (remove before serving)
½ tsp mixed Italian herb
½ cup barley

Instructions

Brown meat and drain fat
Add all ingredients
Simmer 3-4 hours
Serve with Parmesan cheese
