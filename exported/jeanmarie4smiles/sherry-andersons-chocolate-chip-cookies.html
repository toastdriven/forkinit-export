Forkinit: Sherry Anderson’s Chocolate chip Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

⅔ cup crisco
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 ½ cup flour
½ tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
6 oz chocolate chips OR 2 cups M&M’s

Instructions

Bake at 350० on a greased cookie sheet for 8-10 minutes
Leave on cookie sheet for a few minutes
