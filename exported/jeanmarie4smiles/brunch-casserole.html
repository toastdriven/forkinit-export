Forkinit: Brunch Casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb bacon or ham cubed
8 slices bread
8 eggs slightly beaten
3 cups milk
½ tsp salt
½ tsp dried mustard
2 cups American cheese
Onions
Peppers
Mushrooms

Instructions

Grease 9x13 inch pan
Tear up bread
Beat eggs and milk together
Add remaining ingredients to egg/milk mixture
Put bread in 9x13 pan and pour mixture over bread
Cover w/ foil and refrigerate overnight
Bake at 350⁰ 1 hour let set 10 minutes
