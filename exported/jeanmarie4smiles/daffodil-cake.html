Forkinit: Daffodil Cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup egg whites
1 ¼ cup sugar
½ tsp salt
1 tsp cream of tarter
6 egg yolks
½ cup cake flour (white part)
½ tsp vanilla white part)
2/3 cup cake flour (yellow part)
½ tsp orange extract in yellow part

P

Instructions

ut egg whites in large bowl. Beat until foamy and salt and cream of tarter. Beat until stiff. Fold in sugar and divide into two parts

First part: white
add ½ cup cake flour and ½ tsp vanilla

Part 2 yellow
Add 6 beaten egg yolks and 2/3 cup cake flour, and orange extract

Alternate spoonfuls in angel food cake pan. Bake at 325⁰ for 1 hour
