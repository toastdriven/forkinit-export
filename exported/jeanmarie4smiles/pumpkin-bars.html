Forkinit: Pumpkin Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 eggs
1cup oil
2 cups sugar
1 cup 15oz pumpkin
Add
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp ginger
½ tsp ground cloves

Cream cheese Frosting
8 oz cream cheese softened
½ cup butter softened
1 tsp vanilla
2 ¼ cups powdered sugar

Instructions

Mix eggs, oil, sugar, pumpkin
Add rest of ingredients
Bake at 350⁰ 20-25 minutes in a jelly roll pan
Mix frosting and frost when cake has cooled
