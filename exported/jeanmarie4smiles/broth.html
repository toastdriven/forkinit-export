Forkinit: Broth

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 gallon distilled water
1 tsp blackstrap
1 tsp ash
2 Tbsp olive oil
2 carrots – chopped
3 stalks celery –chopped
½ rutabaga or turnip
2 small zucchini
1 can white hominy with juice
1 can asparagus drained
1 can green beans
1 can corn

Instructions

Add above ingredients
Add later:
handful of fresh spinach and parsley
Cayenne pepper
Simmer slowly for 6 hours (add more water as necessary)
Last hour of cooking, add handful of fresh spinach and parsley
Cayenne pepper to taste
