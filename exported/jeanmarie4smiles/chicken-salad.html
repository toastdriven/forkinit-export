Forkinit: Chicken salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 cups diced cooked chicken breasts
1 cup chopped celery
2 cups seedless grapes (cut in half)
1 cup pineapple chunks
3/4cup chopped macadamia nuts
½ cup sliced water chestnuts
1 ¼ cups plain yogurt
1 Tbsp curry powder
1 Tbsp soy sauce
1 Tbsp lemon juice
½ cup coconut

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine chicken, celery, grapes, water chestnuts, pineapple and 1/2cup nuts
Mix yogurt, curry powder, soy and lemon juice and stir into vegetables
Chill serve on a bed of lettuce and garnish with coconut and reserved nuts
