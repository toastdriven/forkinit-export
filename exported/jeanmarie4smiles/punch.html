Forkinit: Punch

By Mary Jo Kurka
Used for a 25th anniversary

Ingredients

8-10 packages lime kool-aid
2 large frozen orange juice
3 large frozen lemonade
2 large cans pineapple juice
2 large cans grapefruit juice
10 lb sugar
2 bottles of squirt, 7 up or ginger ale
1 large container of lime sherbet

Instructions

Mix together and serve chilled

Yields: Serves 125
