Forkinit: Pumpkin Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka
from Pam Zal

Ingredients

2 cup brown sugar
1 cup oil
2 cup white sugar
4 cup pumpkin
1 tsp cloves
2 ½ tsp cinnamon
4 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
5 cup flour
1 cup chopped nuts
1 1/3 c raisins

Instructions

Mix in order given
Bake in ungreased coffee cans (5 one lb cans)
Top with toothpick.
Cool on racks.
Refrigerate or freeze
Best after aging a few days
Bake at 350⁰ for 1 hour
