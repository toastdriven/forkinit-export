Forkinit: 4 Bean Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

One can kidney beans
garbanzo beans (or dried and cooked)
wax beans (yellow)
Green beans (frozen and cooked)
1 onion chopped
1 green pepper chopped
Celery chopped
1 cup oil
1 ½ cup vinegar
½ cup sugar
Black pepper
Celery salt

Instructions

Add any combination of beans that you like
I usually keep adding until an ice cream pail is ½ to 1/3 full
(One can of wax beans is plenty. The rest I add mainly for color. You could add lima beans if you like them)
Blend oil, vinegar, sugar, pepper and salt
(I usually end up adding more vinegar and sugar)
Better after it sits for a while
Keeps quite a while
