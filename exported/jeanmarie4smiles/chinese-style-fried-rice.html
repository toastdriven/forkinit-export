Forkinit: Chinese Style Fried Rice

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 1/3 cup water
1 1/3 cup Minute Rice
3 Tbsp butter
Whole mushrooms
1 egg beaten 1/3 cup chopped onion
2 Â½ Tbsp soy sauce
Bacon bits

Instructions

Boil 1 cup water, add rice, removed and cover
Let stand 5 minutes
Cook egg in butter until set
Add onion and rice
Cook and stir until rice is lightly browned about 5 minutes
Combine 1/3 cup water and soy sauce
Add to rice
Add mushrooms and fried bacon bits
