Forkinit: Cinnamon almond braid

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 tube (8oz) refrigerated crescent rolls
2 Tbsp + ¼ cup sugar divided
1 tsp cinnamon
½ cup chopped slivered almonds
1 Tbsp butter melted
¼ tsp almond extract
Icing:
½ cup powdered sugar
¼ tsp almond extract
1 ½-2 tsp milk
¼ tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Line a 15x10 inch baking pan with waxed paper
Unroll crescent dough into prepared pan
Seal seams and perforations
Combine 2 Tbsp sugar and cinnamon
Sprinkle over dough
Combine the almonds, butter, extract and remaining sugar,
Spread lengthwise down the center of dough
On each long side, cut 1 inch wide strips about 2 ½ inches into center
Staring at one end, fold alternating strips at an angle across filling
Pinch ends to seal
Bake at 375⁰ for 10-15minutes or until golden brown
Cool for 10 minutes, remove to a wire rack
For icing,
Combine powdered sugar, extract and enough milk to achieve desired consistency
Drizzle over braid
Sprinkle with cinnamon
Serve warm

Yields: Yields 10 slices
