Forkinit: Child’s Play Dough

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 cups flour
3 Tbsp powdered alum
2 ¼ cups boiling water
1 ½ cups salt
3 Tbsp cooking oil
Food coloring

Instructions

Mix food coloring with water
Mix dry ingredients and add to water mixture
Let cool then knead 5 minutes
Keep in an airtight container for best results
