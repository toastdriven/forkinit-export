Forkinit: Mixed Berry Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

¼ cup orange juice
1 Tbsp salad oil
2 tsp Dijon style mustard
1 tsp salt
4 cups torn lettuce
1 ½ cups fresh blueberries, raspberries, quartered
Strawberries, and/or mandarin orange sections, drained
1 Tbsp shelled sunflower seeds

Instructions

For dressing, in a screw-top jar combine orange juice, oil, mustard, sugar, and salt
Cover and shake until combined
Place lettuce in medium bowl
Drizzle with dressing, toss to coat
Divide lettuce among 4 plates
Arrange fruit on lettuce
Sprinkle with seeds
Serve immediately
