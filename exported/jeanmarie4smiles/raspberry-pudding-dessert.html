Forkinit: Raspberry Pudding Dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
⅓ cup butter softened
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 cup milk
2-3 cups raspberries
2 cups boiling water
½ -1 cup sugar depending on sweetness of berries

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350०
cream sugar with butter until fluffy
add flour, baking powder, salt, and milk
pour into glass baking dish (about 2 ½ qt size)
Pour berries on top of batter
boil water and add sugar
stir until dissolved and pour over berries
bake about 50 minutes or until top is golden brown
allow to cool
