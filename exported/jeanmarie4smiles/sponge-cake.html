Forkinit: Sponge Cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sifted cake flour
1 cup white sugar
6 eggs ½ tsp cream of tarter
¼ tsp salt
1 Tbsp water
1 Tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

Mix ¼ cup sugar and flour together.
Separate eggs, whites into large bowl and yolks into a small bowl.
Add cream of tarter and salt to whites.
Beat until stiff, add 1/3 cup sugar and beat.
Set aside.
Add lemon juice and water to yolks and beat until thick.
Add rest and sugar and beat until thicker.
Combine whites and yolks by folding.
Fold in flour ¼ cup at a time.
Make 1 ½ recipes
Bake at 350⁰ for 30-40 minutes
