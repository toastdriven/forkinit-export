Forkinit: Crunchy chicken hot dish

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups of crushed whole wheat snack crackers
2 cups diced cooked chicken (or more)
½ cup Mayonnaise
2 cans Mayonnaise
2 cans cream of chicken soup
2 10oz cans asparagus –drained
1 8 oz can water chestnuts, drained and sliced
2- 2 ½ oz cans sliced mushrooms –drained
4 Tbsp melted butter

Instructions

Put half of crushed crackers on bottom of 8x10 pan or casserole dish
Layer Mayonnaise mixed into soup,
Then mix chicken, water chestnuts, mushrooms, asparagus and put about ½ of that on top of soup
Then put another layer of soup mix and then rest of chicken mix
Top with rest of the crackers
Pour melted butter over crackers
Bake covered for 30 minutes at 350◦ then 15 more minutes uncovered
