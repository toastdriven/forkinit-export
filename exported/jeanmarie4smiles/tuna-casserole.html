Forkinit: Tuna Casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb potatoes, thinly sliced
1 onion, thinly sliced
Salt and pepper
2 (6 oz) cans tuna
1 can sweet corn
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup half and half

Instructions

Grease large casserole
Layer ½ the onions and potatoes and seasoining
Top with tuna, sweet corn, and half cheese
Top with remaining potatoes and onion and seasoning
Pour half and half over all
Top with cheese, cover and cook at 375⁰for 1 hour
Then uncover and cook another 15 minute until potatoes are tender
