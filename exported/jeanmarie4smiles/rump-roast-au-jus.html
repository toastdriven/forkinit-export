Forkinit: Rump Roast Au Jus

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 Tbsp black pepper
1 Tbsp paprika
2 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp celery salt
1/ tsp ground cayenne pepper
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp mustard powder
1 (4 lb ) rump roast
1/2 cup water

Instructions

In a small bowl, mix together all herbs and spices
Rub mixture over the surface of the meat
Place roast in a slow cooker, and add water
Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours
When meat is tender and well done, transfer roast to a serving platter
Skim fat from juices, and strain
Serve juices with meat
