Forkinit: Mabel’s Dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka
raw egg

Ingredients

¾ pound vanilla wafers
1 cup butter
3 cups powdered sugar
4 eggs
1 qt sliced strawberries
1 pint cream (to be whipped)
bananas

Instructions

Crush wafers fine and put more than ½ of them in cake pan
cream butter well and add sugar
add 1 egg at a time and beat well
spread over crumbs in pan
cover with strawberries add bananas
Spread whipped cream on top and put remaining crumbs on top
place in refrigerator and let stand overnight
