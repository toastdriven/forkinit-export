Forkinit: Banana Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka
Marilyn Darsett

Ingredients

½ cup butter
1 ½ cup sugar
2 large eggs
2 cups flour
1 ½ tsp soda
1 ¼ tsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
1 ¼ cup mashed bananas
½ cup buttermilk
½ cup nuts

Frosting
½ cup oleo-margarine
8 oz cream cheese
½ tsp vanilla
3 ½ to 4 cups powdered sugar

Instructions

Cream margarine and sugar
Add eggs and dry ingredients with buttermilk
Add bananas last
Bake in Jelly roll pan
20-25 minutes at 350⁰
Frosting
Cream margarine, cream cheese, and vanilla
Add powdered sugar
Beat with electric mixer until right consistency for spreading
