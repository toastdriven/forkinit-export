Forkinit: Vanishing Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup butter
1 cup brown sugar
½ cup sugar
2 egg
1 tsp vanilla
1 ½ cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
½ tsp salt
3 cup oatmeal
1 cup raisins
1 cup butterscotch chips

Instructions

Beat together butter, sugars until creamy
Add eggs and vanilla, beat well
Add combined flour, soda, cinnamon, mix well
Drop by rounded Tbsp onto ungreased cookie sheet
Bake 10-12 minute or until golden brown in 350० oven
Cool 1 minute on cookie sheet and put on wire rack
Bar cookies: bake 30-35 minutes in ungreased 9x13 melt baking pan about 4 dozen cookies
