Forkinit: Salted Peanut Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup flour
⅔ cup brown sugar
½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp baking soda
½ cup butter
1 tsp vanilla
2 egg yolks
3 to 3 ½ cup mini marshmallows

Topping:
1 cup white Karo syrup
2 tsp vanilla
¼ cup butter
1 12 oz package peanut butter chips
½ cup Rice Krispies
2 cups salted peanuts
(1 Cup Krispies and 3 cups peanuts)

Instructions

Mix all except marshmallows together like a pie crust press in 9x13 pan
bake 325 -350० for 15 minutes until light brown
do not overbake
After crust is baked, remove from oven
spread marshmallows on top and put back in oven for 1-3 minutes until fluffy
melt topping except Krispies and peanuts in large pan
Stir in Rice Krispies and peanuts
Mix well and spread over marshmallows and refrigerate until set
