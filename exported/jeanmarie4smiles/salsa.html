Forkinit: Salsa

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 green pepper
1 tomato
1 medium onion
2 cup salsa
2 cup Italian dressing
1 can corn
1 can black beans
1 can black-eyed peas

Instructions

Cut up fresh ingredients
Rinse and drain canned beans
Add remaining ingredients
Mix and serve
