Forkinit: Rhubarb Cobbler

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 cup rhubarb
3 Tbsp butter
¾ cup sugar
1 tsp baking powder
½ cup milk
1 cup flour
1/4tsp salt
Topping:
1 cup sugar
1 cup boiling water with red food coloring
1 ½ Tbsp corn starch

Instructions

Cut rhubarb into a 9x9 pan
Mix butter, sugar, baking powder, milk, flour and salt together
Place on top of rhubarb
Sprinkle sugar and corn starch from topping over dough
Pour water over all
Bake at 375⁰ for 45 minutes
Double for a 9x13 pan
