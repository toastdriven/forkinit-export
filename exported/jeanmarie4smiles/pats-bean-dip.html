Forkinit: Pat’s Bean Dip

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups cooked brown rice
1 ½ cups tomato salsa
1 tsp. chili powder
15 oz can black beans (not drained) or refried beans
1 cup frozen whole corn
2 oz cheddar cheese, or mozzarella cheese
½ cups sliced black olives

Instructions

Combine all ingredient except cheese
Mix well and put in greased casserole
May microwave to heat or bake until heated thru
Top with cheese and melt
Serve with tortilla chips
