Forkinit: Mac ‘n cheese surprise

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

8 oz cavatappi pasta
¾ cup frozen peas
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ cup buttermilk
1 tsp dry mustard
1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
½ tsp turmeric
¼ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
4 oz Colby Jack cheese, cut into ½ inch pieces (1 cup)
¼ cup chopped chives
Shredded Colby Jack cheese, optional

Instructions

In large pot of salted boiling water cook pasta according to package directions
Add peas duringthe last 3 minutes of cooking
Drian, run under cold water until cool, Drain again
In largeserving bowl, combine mayonnaise, buttermilk, mustard,Worcestershire, turmeric, salt, and pepper
Add pasta, pease, cubed cheese and chives
Toss until evenly coated
Optional, add cheese
