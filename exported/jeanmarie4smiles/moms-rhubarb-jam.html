Forkinit: Mom’s Rhubarb Jam

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


5 cups rhubarb- chopped in small pieces
4 cups sugar
1 (3oz) box strawberry jello

Instructions

Add sugar to rhubarb and let stand for 2 hours
Then bring to boil for 15 minutes on medium high
Add jell and stir well
Put into containers and cool
Then freeze
