Forkinit: Double Fudge Fancifill Cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Filling:
¼ cup sugar
1 Tbsp cornstarch
2 Tbsp butter
½ tsp vanilla
8 oz. cream cheese (softened)
1 egg
Base:
1 package Devil’s Food Cake Mix
1 cup water
1/3 cup oil
3 eggs

Instructions

Heat oven to 350⁰
Grease 9x13 pan
In small bowl, blend all filling ingredients
Beat at highest speed until smooth and creamy
Set aside
In large bowl, blend cake mix, water, oil and eggs until moistened
Beat 2 minutes at highest speed
Pour half of batter in prepared pan
Pour cream cheese mixture over batter, spreading to cove
Pour remaining batter over cream cheese mixture
Bake at 350⁰ for 45-55 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean
Frost completely cooled cake with “Creamy Frosting”
