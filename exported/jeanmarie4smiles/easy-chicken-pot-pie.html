Forkinit: Easy Chicken Pot Pie

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 2/3 cup frozen mixed vegetables, thawed
1 cup cut up chicken
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 cup Bisquick Mix
½ cup milk
1 egg

Instructions

In ungreased 9 inch pie plate, stir together vegetables chicken and soup
In bowl, stir remaining ingredients until blended
Pour into pie plate
Bake at 400◦ for 30 minutes or until golden brown
