Forkinit: Peanut-butter bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup butter
½ cup sugar
½ cup brown sugar
1 egg
⅓ cup peanut butter
½ tsp baking soda
¼ tsp salt
½ tsp vanilla
1 cup flour
1 cup quick oats
1 cup chocolate chips
Topping:
½ cup powdered sugar
¼ cup peanut butter
2-4 Tbsp milk (to spread)

Instructions

Cream butter, add sugars and egg and other ingredients
add flour and oatmeal
spread in greased 9x13 pan
Bake at 350०for 20 minutes
cool partially and spread on topping
