Forkinit: Colette’s White Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup powdered sugar
1 cup sugar
1 tsp cream of tarter
1 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
4 cups flour
2 eggs
1 cup oil
1 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Poll cookies into balls and press down with bottom of glass dipped in ¼ cup sugar mixed with cinnamon
Bake at 350० until edges are browned
