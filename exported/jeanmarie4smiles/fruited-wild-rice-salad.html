Forkinit: Fruited Wild Rice Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Dressing:
Â¼ cup olive or vegetable oil
1/3 cup orange juice
2 Tbsp honey
Salad:
1 cup uncooked wild rice
2 Golden delicious apples, chopped
Juice of one lemon
1 cup golden raisins
1 cup seedless red grapes, halved
2 Tbsp fresh minced mint
2 Tbsp fresh minced parsley
2 Tbsp fresh minced chives
Salt and pepper to taste
1cup pecan halves

Instructions

Combine dressing and set aside
Cook rice according to package directions, drain if needed and allow to cool
In large toss apples w/ lemon juice
Add raisins, grapes, mint, parsley, chives, and rice
Add dressing and toss
Season with salt and pepper
Cover and chill several hours or overnight
just before serving add pecans and toss lightly

Yields: Yield 8-10 servings
