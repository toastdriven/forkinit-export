Forkinit: Bohemian Nut slices

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 Tbsp dry yeast
¼ cup warm water
1 tsp sugar
2 cups sifted flour
½ tsp salt
¾ cup margarine (butter)
2 eggs separated
1 tsp vanilla
¼ cup chopped nuts
Powdered sugar

Instructions

Mix warm water, yeast and 1 tsp sugar.
Let stand 10 minutes. Sift together flour and salt.
Add margarine.
Blend in egg and yeast- mix
Beat egg whites until stiff
Add ½ cup sugar and vanilla.
Divided dough in half.
Roll each ½ into 9x13 inch rectangle
Spread with egg whites and sprinkle with nuts.
Roll up as a jelly roll. Put on greased cookie sheet.
Cut ½ in deep cuts lengthwise dough center of each roll.
Bake immediately 375⁰ for 22 minutes.
Sprinkle with powdered sugar when done
