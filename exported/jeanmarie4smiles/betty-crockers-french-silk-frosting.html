Forkinit: Betty Crocker’s French Silk Frosting

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ⅔ cup powder sugar
⅔ cup soft butter
2 squares 1 oz unsweetened chocolate
1 tsp vanilla
2 Tbsp milk

Instructions

Melt the chocolate
Beat together with powder sugar, butter, and vanilla
gradually add milk
Beat until smooth and fluffy
