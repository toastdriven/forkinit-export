Forkinit: Four Cheese Pizza

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 16 oz loaf frozen bread dough thawed
2 cups green and/or red sweet peppers
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
¾ cup shredded Fontina cheese
½ cup grated parmesan cheese
½ cup crumbled feta cheese
2 Tbsp parsley
1 tsp basil
3 med cherry tomatoes or 1 medium thinly sliced
1 Tbsp oil
2 cloves garlic minced

Instructions

Press dough evenly into greased 12 inch pizza pan
Prick generously with fork
Bake at 375 for 20 -25 minutes or until light brown
Assemble in this order: pepper, mozzarella, fontina, parmesan, feta, parsley, basil, and tomatoes
Brush with oil and garlic mixture
bake at 375 for an additional 15-20 minutes until cheese is melted
Let stand 5 minutes before cutting

Yields: 6 servings
