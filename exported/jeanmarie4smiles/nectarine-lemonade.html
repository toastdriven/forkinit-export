Forkinit: Nectarine Lemonade

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2-3 fresh nectarines
1 can (6oz) frozen lemonade concentrate
3 cans (6 oz) cold water
Ice cubes
Mint sprigs (optional)

Instructions

Slice nectarines to make 2 cups
Whirl 1 cup of the slices in blender with concentrate and water
Pour into pitcher and float remaining nectarine slices in lemonade
Serve over ice in tall glasses
Garnish with mint sprigs
