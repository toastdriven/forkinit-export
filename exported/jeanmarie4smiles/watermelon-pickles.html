Forkinit: Watermelon Pickles

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Watermelon rinds green and white parts
1 pint vinegar
4 cups sugar
1-2 tsp whole cloves
½ lemon cut fine

Instructions

Cut watermelon rinds in small pieces
Soak in salt water overnight then drain
Cook in clear water for ½ hour
Drain then cook with remaining ingredients for 1 hour
