Forkinit: Gems

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups oatmeal
1 ½ cup sour milk or buttermilk
Soak awhile then add:
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
1 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt

Instructions

Use tin liners in muffin pan
Bake at 350⁰ for 20 minutes or more
