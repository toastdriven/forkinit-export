Forkinit: Rachel's Bakery Frosting

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 ½ cups powdered sugar
2 egg whites beaten stiff
1 tsp vanilla/almond extract
½ cup shortening
¼ tsp salt

Instructions

Beat eggs stiff then add ½ of powdered sugar
in another bowl, mix shortening with powdered sugar and vanilla and salt
mix both together for frosting
