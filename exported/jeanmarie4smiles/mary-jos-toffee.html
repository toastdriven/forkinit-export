Forkinit: Mary Jo’s Toffee

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 pound butter
2 cups brown sugar
3 bags chocolate chips
2 cups chopped walnuts

Instructions

Boil butter and brown sugar until 300 degrees at med/high heat stirring constantly
Pour onto a well buttered jelly roll pan
Sprinkle half of chocolate chips over top spreading with back of a spoon until smooth
Sprinkle with half of walnuts
Then quickly flip over onto another well buttered jelly roll pan and top that side with 1½ cup chocolate chips and 1 cup nuts
Let cool and break
