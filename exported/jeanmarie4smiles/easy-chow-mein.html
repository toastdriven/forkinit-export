Forkinit: Easy Chow Mein

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb hamburger
1 can cream of chicken soup
1 can chicken noodle soup
1 cup rice
top with chow mein noodles

Instructions

Brown hamburger add rice
Stir for 2 minutes
Add rest of ingredients for 10-15 more minutes
Put in a baking pan and bake for 35 minutes
Check to see if rice is fully cooked, may need longer
