Forkinit: Apple Pie bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Pastry:
4 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
1cup shortening
4 egg yolks
2 Tbsp lemon juice
8-10Tbsp cold water
Filling:
7 cups finely chopped peeled apples
2 cups sugar
¼ cups flour
2 tsp cinnamon
A dash of ground nutmeg
Glaze:
1 cup powdered sugar
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp milk

Instructions

In a large bowl combine flour, salt, and baking powder
Cut in shortening until mixture resembles coarse crumbs
In a small bowl, whisk eggs yolks, lemon juice, and water
Gradually add to flour mixture
Tossing dough with a fork until forms a ball
Divide into ½ and chill for 30 minutes
Roll out one portion of dough between 2 large sheets of waxed paper into a 17x12 rectangle
Transfer to an ungreased 15x10inch backing pan
Press pastry onto the bottom and up the sides of pan
Trim pastry even with top edge
Filling:
In a large bowl, toss the apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, and nutmeg then spread over crust
Roll out remaining pastry to fit top of pan
Place over filling
Trim edges
Brush edges between pastry with water or milk
Pinch to seal
Cut slits in the top
Bake at 375 for 45-50 minutes or until golden brown
Cool on a wire rack
Combine glaze ingredients until smooth
Drizzle over bars before cutting

Yields: Makes 2 dozen bars
