Forkinit: Pat’s chili

By Mary Jo Kurka
Makes a large recipe

Ingredients

2 pounds hamburger
1 cup chopped onion
2 29oz cans diced tomatoes
229oz cans tomato sauce
2 15 oz cans kidney beans- Drained
1 cup salsa
2 Tbsp chili powder
1 tsp garlic powder
½ -3/4 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
1 tsp salt
¼ tsp ground cayenne red pepper

Instructions

Brown hamburger and onion and drain grease
Add remaining ingredients
Simmer for 30 minutes
May adjust by adding more or less of these spices according to taste
