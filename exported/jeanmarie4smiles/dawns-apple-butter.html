Forkinit: Dawn’s apple butter

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Quartered apples
5 cups sugar
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp ground cloves
1 shake ginger
1 box red hots

Instructions

Wash and quarter apples, put in a big pot with a couple inches of water
Bring to boil, cover and turn on low for ½ to3/4hours until mushy
Put thru a sieve
Keep doing until looks like unsweetened applesauce
Add to applesauce that almost fills a 5 quart crockpot the remaining ingredients
Put crockpot on high until it warms up then turn to low
Put cover on but not quite all the way
Cook for 24 hours
