Forkinit: Easy Sweet Pickles

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

6 cups sugar
6 cups vinegar
6 cups cold water
6 Tbsp salt
Garlic cloves
Onion slices
Dill heads
Pickle chunks

Instructions

Mix sugar, vinegar, water, and salt together and let set cold
Put one clove garlic, one slice onion and dill in bottom of each jar
Fill jars with pickle chunks and put more dill on top
Fill with above liquid
Heat lids and screw on but not tight
Hot water bath until the vinegar solution starts to bubble
Remove and screw lids down tight
