Forkinit: Oatmeal Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka
From Lutie

Ingredients

2 cup rolled oats
1 cup shortening (Crisco)
2 Tbsp. salt
2/3 cup Brown sugar
4 cup boiling water
2 pkgs dry yeast
1 cup warm yeast
12-13 cups flour

Instructions

Scald oats, shortening, salt and sugar with boiling water, cool.
Soak yeast in warm water. Stir 6 cups flour into oatmeal mixture and add yeast. Add rest of flour to knead. Let rise. Work into 4 or 5 loaves. Let rise again.
Bake 45 -60 minutes. Start at 400⁰ for 10 minutes- finish at 350⁰

May use for buns also

Yields: Makes 4-5 loaves
