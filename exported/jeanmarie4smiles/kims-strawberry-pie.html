Forkinit: Kim's strawberry pie

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Crust:
½ cup oil
2 Tbsp milk
2 Tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
1 ½ cup flour
Filling:
1 cup water
1 cup sugar
3 Tbsp cornstarch
3 Tbsp strawberry jello
4-6 cups fresh cut strawberries

Instructions

Crust:
Mix well, press in pie plate
Bake at 375 for 10-15 minutes
Filling:
Boil water, sugar, and cornstarch until clear
Add jello
Cool and fold into berries
