Forkinit: Tator tot Hot dish

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 lb hamburgr Raw
1 can cream of mushroom soup
Veggie of choice
Tator tots
Lowry’s seasoning
Sprinkle shredded cheese

Instructions

Press raw meat into baking dish
Spread soup
Sprinkle on veggies
Top with tator tots
Sprinkle Lowry’s seasoning
Bake at 350⁰ for 1 hour
Lat 10 minutes sprinkle on shredded cheese
