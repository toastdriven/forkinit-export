Forkinit: Zucchini Carrot Cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 eggs
1 ½ cup sugar
1 cup oil
Skimpy 2 cups flour
1 ½ tsp baking powder
1 ½ tsp baking soda
1 ½ tsp cinnamon
¾ tsp salt
1 ½ cup graded carrots
1 ½ cup graded zucchini
Optional
1 cup raisins
1 cup walnuts

Instructions

Beat eggs, add in sugar, beat til frothy.
Gradually beat in oil and add dry ingredients
Stir in remaining ingredients
Bake at 350⁰ for 35 to 40 minutes until springs back
