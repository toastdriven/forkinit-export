Forkinit: Cream Puff Dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup boiling water
1 stick butter
1 cup flour
4 eggs
2 ⅔ cup milk
1 8oz cream cheese softened
1 large instant (chocolate) pudding
8 oz cool whip
Hersheys syrup
chopped walnuts

Instructions

Boil water and butter
Remove from heat and flour and mix until forms a fall
stir in eggs one at a time
spread in a 9x13 pan
bake 35 minutes at 400०
cool
Mix milk, cream cheese, pudding
pour over crust and spread with cool whip
drizzle Hershey’s syrup or sprinkle with walnuts
