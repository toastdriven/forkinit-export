Forkinit: Betty’s Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka
Betty Sorenson

Ingredients

½ cup butter
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup white sugar
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla
¾ cup wheat germ
¾ cup flour
1 tsp baking powder
¼ cup oatmeal
½ cp chocolate chips

Instructions

Cream butter and sugars
Add egg and vanilla
Mix together dry ingredients and add to creamed mixture
Bake at 375० for 12-15 minutes
