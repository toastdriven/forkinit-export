Forkinit: Fruit Pizza

By Mary Jo Kurka
Pam Golbraith

Ingredients

½ cup soft (not melted) butter
¼ cup brown sugar
1 cup flour
⅓ cup sugar
8 oz cream cheese
1 tsp vanilla
Canned fruit and cut fruit
Reserve 1 cup liquid
Glaze for top:
1 cup juice
11/2 cup sugar
2 Tbsp cornstarch
1 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

mix butter, brown sugar, and flour and spread in a pizza pan
bake 10 minutes at 350०
cool
mix sugar, cream cheese, and vanilla and spread on crust
Drain fruit (ex: pineapple, mandarin orange)
slice fruit and arrange
mix glaze and cook until thick
drizzle over top of fruit and cool
