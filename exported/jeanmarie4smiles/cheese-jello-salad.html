Forkinit: Cheese Jello Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 package lime jello
1 package cream cheese
1 small can crushed pineapple
1 cup whipping cream or cool whip
Pecans or walnuts

Instructions

Make jello- allow to thicken and cool
Heat the pineapple and melt the cheese in the hot pineapple stirring to blend
Let cool
Whip the jello and add the whipping cream
Add the cooled pineapple and cheese, mix well
Add nuts and put in refrigerator until ready to use
