Forkinit: Fish Casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ to 2 lbs white fish in greased dish
1 cup fresh mushrooms
1 cup white cheese
2 diced green onions
2 stalks celery chopped
Chopped parsley

Instructions

Sprinkle with paprika
Lightly salt and pepper fish
Place fish in greased dish
Layer with remaining ingredients
Bake at 350◦ for 30 minutes
