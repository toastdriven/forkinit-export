Forkinit: Breakfast rolls

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Use Basic Sweet Roll dough

Instructions

Roll about dough that has risen once to 9 x 12.
Cut into inch strips
Give each strip 2-3 twists
Place loosely on greased baking sheet
Put a tsp of filling in each
When golden, frost lightly with powdered sugar frosting and sprinkle with ground nuts toasted or plain coco-nuts
