Forkinit: Baked Apple Butter

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

10-12 lb apples
Apple cider
9 cups sugar
Juice and grated rind of 2 lemons
1 Tbsp cinnamon
1 ½ tsp ground cloves
½ tsp allspice

Instructions

Wash and quarter apples
Place in large pot with just enough apple cider to keep apples from sticking and cook until soft and mushy
Cool slightly and put through sieve
You should have about 18 cups of apple puree
Using a large ovenproof casserole or roasting pan, combine the puree, sugar, lemon parts, cinnamon, cloves, and allspice
Mix thoroughly
Place casserole in cold oven and set the temperature to 300 degrees
Cook several hours until the apple butter is desired thickness
Stir every ½ to1 hour and then more frequently as butter thickens
You can test the butter for doneness by placing a small quantity on a plate
When no rim of liquid separates from edges of butter, it’s done
Pour the mixture into hot, sterilized jars and seal
Process in boiling water bath for 5minutes
Fills about 8 12oz jars
