Forkinit: Freezer Coleslaw

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 finely chopped pepper
1 finely grated carrot
1 head cabbage finely cabbage
1 tsp salt
2 cup sugar
1 cup vinegar
1 tsp celery seed
1 tsp mustard seed

Instructions

Mix together pepper, carrot, cabbage, and salt then let sit for 1 hour
In a saucepan combine sugar, vinegar, celery seed and mustard seed and boil for one minute then cool
Squeeze out cabbage and pour cooled syrup over cabbage then freeze
