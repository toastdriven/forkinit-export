Forkinit: Bisquick Chocolate chip cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup butter softened
1 cup packed brown sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1 egg
2 ¾ cups original bisquick
1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
½ cup chopped nuts, optial

Instructions

Heat oven to 375०
Mix butter, brown sugar, vanilla, and egg in large bowl
Stir in Bisquick, chocolate chips and nuts
Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls about 2 inches apart on ungreased cookie sheet, flatten slightly
Bake 10 minutes or until golden brown
Remove from cookie sheet to wire rack

Yields: 4 1/2 dozen
