Forkinit: Easy Brown and wild rice pilaf

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ½ cups chicken broth
1 Tbsp butter
1 cup uncooked brown rice
½ cup uncooked wild rice
¼ cup sliced onion
1/4cup chopped carrot
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp butter

Instructions

Saute the veggies in butter in a dutch c=oven of top of the stove
Then stir in the rice
Baked covered in oven at 350⁰about 35 minutes oruntil rice is tenderand liquid is absorbed
Fluff with a fork before serving
