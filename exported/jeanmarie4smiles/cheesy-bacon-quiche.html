Forkinit: Cheesy Bacon Quiche

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ¼ cups Bisquick
¼ cup butter, softened
2 Tbsp boiling water
1 cup shredded Swiss cheese
1 package (6 oz) sliced Canadian bacon, chopped
4 medium green onions, sliced
1 ½ cups half and half
3 eggs
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp red pepper

Instructions

Heat oven to 400⁰
Grease pie plate
Stir Bisquick and butter until blended
Add boiling water, stir vigorously until soft dough forms
Press dough on bottom and up side of pie plate, forming edge on the rim of pie plate
Sprinkle cheese, bacon, and onions over crust
Beat half and half and eggs
Stir in salt and red pepper
Pour into curst
Bake 35-40 minutes or until knife inserted in center comes out clean
Let stand 10 minutes before serving
