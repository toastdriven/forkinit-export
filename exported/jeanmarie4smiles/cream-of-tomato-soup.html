Forkinit: Cream of Tomato Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 qt tomatoes and juice
1 can chicken broth
1-5 Tbsp butter
1 Tbsp sugar
1-2 Tbsp onions- finely chopped
½ tsp baking soda
1 cup heavy cream
1 ½ cup milk

Instructions

In a saucepan, combine all except cream and milk
Cover and simmer (do not boil)
Add cream and milk to tomato mixture just before serving
