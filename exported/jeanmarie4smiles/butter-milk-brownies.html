Forkinit: Butter milk Brownies

By Mary Jo Kurka
Sandy Wyffels

Ingredients

1 stick Margarine
1 cup water
¼ cup cocoa
½ cup Mazola oil
Bring to a boil and cool
2 cups sugar
1 tsp soda
½ cup buttermilk
½ tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs slightly beaten
2 cup flour
Frosting
6 Tbsp Milk
6 Tbsp Butter
1 1/3 cup sugar
½ cup chocolate chips

Instructions

Mix margarine, water,coco, and oil
Bring to a boil and cool
Mix soda and buttermilk
Add remaining ingredients
Bake at 350⁰ for 20-25 minutes don't over bake
Frosting:
Boil Milk, butter,sugar 1 minute
Remove from heat add chocolate chips
Then beat
