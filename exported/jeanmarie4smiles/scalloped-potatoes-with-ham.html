Forkinit: Scalloped Potatoes with Ham

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb fully cooked ham slice (1/2 inch thick)
5 cups thinly sliced potatoes
1 can cream of mushroom soup or cream of celery soup
¼ cup milk
½ cup chopped onion
¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
dash of pepper
2 Tbsp butter
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, optional

Instructions

Cut ham in bite-sized cubes
place half of the potatoes in a greased 2 quart baking dish
Cover with ham pieces
Top with remaining potatoes
Combine soup with milk, onion, green bell pepper, and pepper, pour over potatoes
Dot with butter
Cover with foil and bake at 350 for 1 hour
Remove covering bake 30-45 minutes longer or until potatoes are done
If desired, sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the top of scalloped potatoes the last 15 minutes of baking time

Yields: Serves 6
