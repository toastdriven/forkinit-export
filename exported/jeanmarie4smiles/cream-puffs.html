Forkinit: Cream Puffs

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup hot water
½ cup butter
1 cup flour
4 eggs
Filling
½ cup sugar
3 Tbsp flour
1 cup milk
1 egg
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Boil together water and butter
When butter is melted, stir in flour
Remove from heat and stir until a smooth paste
When cool, add 4 unbeaten eggs one at a time, stirring 5 minutes
Drop on cookie sheet
Makes 12 large cream puffs
Bake 15 minutes at 425०
30 minutes at 350०

Filling
Mix sugar and flour in a pan
Add milk, whisk well
Cook on medium heat until thickened
Add small amount of cooked mixture to beaten egg
Then add egg mixture to milk mixture
Cook 3-5 minutes stirring constantly
Add vanilla
