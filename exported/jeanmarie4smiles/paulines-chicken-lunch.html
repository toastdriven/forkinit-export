Forkinit: Pauline’s Chicken Lunch

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 package 14 oz Catherine Clark sage and onion dressing
1 ½ cup hot water
5 Tbsp melted butter
2 packages string beans cooked
2 chicken breasts cooked and cubed
1 can mushroom soup
1 can water
1 bouillon cube
Silvered almonds

Instructions

Mix dressing, water, and butter until formed
In greased pan 9x13 place ¾ of dressing mixture
Then a layer of chicken
Mix mushroom soup, can of water, and bouillon cube
Spread over top of chicken
Top with remaining dressing mixture
And slivered almonds
Bake at 350◦ for 45 to 60 minutes
