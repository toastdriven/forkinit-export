Forkinit: Sherry’s Quiche

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup swiss cheese
1 cup bacon
1 cup mushrooms
Crust:
1 ½ cup milk
½ cup bisquick
¼ cup soft butter
¼ tsp salt
3 eggs
Dash of pepper


Instructions

Blend or mix crust ingredients together
Grease pie pan
Put cheese, bacon, and mushrooms in bottom of pan
Pour egg mixture over
Bake at 350⁰ for 45 minutes
