Forkinit: Triple Chocolate mess chocolate cake

By Mary Jo Kurka
CROCK POT recipe

Ingredients

1 package chocolate cake
1pint whip cream
1 package instant chocolate pudding
1 small bag chocolate chips
3/4cup oil
4 eggs
1 cup water

Instructions

Spray non-stick spray in crock pot
Mix all ingredients together and place in crock-pot
Cook on low 6-9 hours
