Forkinit: Dutch babies

By Mary Jo Kurka
Sally Wyatt

Ingredients

1/3 cup butter
5 eggs
1 cup milk
1 cup flour

Instructions

Put butter in a 9x13 inch baking dish
Set in a 425⁰ oven to melt
While butter is melting, put eggs in a blended and blend on high for 1 minute
Gradually add milk, then slowly add flour
Blend for 30 seconds
Removed the baking dish from the oven and pour the batter into the hot melted butter
Return to heated 425⁰oven and bake until puffy and nicely browned, approximately 20-25 minutes
Serve immediately with powdered sugar, syrup and fresh lemon wedged,
Other options for toppings: fresh strawberries, brown sugar and sour cream, banana or papaya slices sautéed in butter and served with lime wedged, or sautéed apple slices sweetened with cinnamon and sugar and served with sour cream or plain yogurt. Strawberry jam, powdered sugar, and lemon
