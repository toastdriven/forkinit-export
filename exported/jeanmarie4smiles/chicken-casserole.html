Forkinit: Chicken Casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 cups diced chicken (cooked)
2 cup frozen asparagus -2 packages
1 ½ cup diced American cheese
8 oz package egg noodles (wide-cooked)
8 oz can mushrooms
1 small jar pimento- drained and diced
1 cup diced celery
¼ cup onion
½ cup green pepper
½ cup ripe olives
½ cup cashews
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 can cream of mushroom soup
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ cup sour cream

Instructions

Cook chicken and pull apart or dice
Mix in remaining ingredients
Bake at 325◦ for 1 hour covered
Uncover for last 20 minutes and add chow mien noodles
