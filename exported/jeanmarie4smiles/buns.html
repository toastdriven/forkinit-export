Forkinit: Buns

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup milk
1/2 cup sugar
1 tsp salt
1/4 cup butter
2 Tbsp yeast
2 eggs
5 cups flour

Instructions

Scald ½ cup milk
Add ½ cup sugar, 1 tsp salt, ¼ cup butter
Cool
Measure ½ cup warm water and 2 Tbsp yeast- stir
Mix together and beat 2 eggs and 3 cups flour
Stir in 2 cups flour and knead
Bake 350⁰ for 10-15 minutes
