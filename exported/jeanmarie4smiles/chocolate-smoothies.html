Forkinit: Chocolate smoothies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

¾ cup milk
¼ cup chocolate drink powder (Nestlie’s)
4 cups vanilla ice cream

Instructions

Combine all ingredientd in a blender
blend on medium speed until creamy
stir if necessary
If too thin, freeze the mix in the blender or in cups until thicker
