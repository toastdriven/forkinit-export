Forkinit: Seafoam

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 egg whites
3 cups sugar
½ cup corn syrup
½ cup water
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Boil sugar, syrup, and water on medium heat until soft ball forma
Add half mixture to egg whites and beat until smooth
Boil other half until hard ball
Add and mix
Add vanilla
