Forkinit: Blue cheese Dressing

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1/3 cup crumbled blue cheese
1 Tbsp finely chopped onion
½ clove garlic
½ cup mayonnaise
½ cup sour cream
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp vinegar
Dash pepper

Instructions

Combine blue cheese, onion, and garlic
Mix at low speed for 3 minutes
Adding remaining ingredients and mix 2 minutes more until smooth
