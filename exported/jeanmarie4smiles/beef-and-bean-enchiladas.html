Forkinit: Beef and bean enchiladas

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


¾ lb ground beef
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1 can (15 oz) re-fried beans
1 can picante sauce
1 ½ tsp ground cumin
½ tsp salt
12 flour tortillas heated (7 to 8 inch)
1 ½ cup (6 oz) shredded cheddar or Monterey jack cheese
Garnishes: shredded lettuce, sliced radishes, sour cream, ripe olive slices
Brown meat with onion and garlic in 10 inch skillet: drain
Stir in beans, ½ cup of the picante sauce, cumin and salt

Instructions

Simmer 5 minutes stirring occasionally
Spoon scant 1/3 cup meat mixture down center of each tortilla, roll and place seam-side down in lightly greased 9x13 baking dish
Spoon remaining picante sauce evenly over enchiladas
Cover and bake at 350◦ for 15 minutes
Uncover, top evenly with cheese
Continue baking uncovered for 3 minutes or until cheese is melted
Garnish as desired and serve with additional picante sauce

To heat tortillas stack and wrap in foil, bake at 150◦ for 15 minutes or wrap loosely in plastic wrap and microwave on high for 1 to 1 ½ minutes

Yields: Makes 6 servings
