Forkinit: Reunion Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1cup light corn syrup
2 cup peanut butter
1cup brown sugar
5 cups rice krispies
1 small box instant vanilla pudding
4 cups powdered sugar
6 Tbsp milk
½ cup butter melted
2 cups chocolate chips
½ cup butter

Instructions

1st layer
Melt brown sugar with corn syrup and add peanut butter
Add rice Krispies, mix well
Put in a buttered jelly roll pan
Cool in fridge before mixing 2nd layer
2nd layer
Mix dry pudding, powdered sugar milk, and butter together
Spread on top of 1st layer
Return to fridge until set
3rd layer
Melt chocolate and butter together
Spread over 2nd layer and store in fridge
