Forkinit: Cranberry apple crisp

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

5 cups tart apples
1 ½ cup fresh or frozen cranberries
⅓ cup sugar
½ cup flour
½ cup brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
¼ cup butter

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375०
Lightly grease a 9x9 baking pan
pare and core apples, then layer apples and cranberries in pan sprinkling with sugar as you layer
Mix flour, brown sugar, and cinamon
Work in butter until light and crumbly
Sprinkle topping evenly over apples and cranberries
Bake 45 minutes or until apples are tender

Yields: Makes 9 servings
