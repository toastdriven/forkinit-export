Forkinit: Peach Crisp

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

6-7 cup sliced and peeled peaches
1 cup sugar
2-3 heaping Tbsp flour
½ tsp salt
2 ½ cup oatmeal
½ cup flour
1 cup brown sugar
½ tsp baking powder
½ tsp baking soda
⅔ cup melted butter

Instructions

Mix peaches, sugar, flour, and salt together
Put in buttered 9x13 baking dish
Crumble rest of ingredients together and put on top of peaches
Bake at 350० for 30- 40 minutes
