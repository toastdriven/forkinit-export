Forkinit: Sour Cream Sugar Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka
Roberta Millard

Ingredients

2 cups white sugar
1 cup oleo (oil/shortening)
3 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp vanilla
4 ½ cup flour or a little more

Instructions

Chill roll out and cut
350 ० for 10-12 minutes

Yields: 5 dozen
