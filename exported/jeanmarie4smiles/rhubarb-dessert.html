Forkinit: Rhubarb dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups rhubarb
crust:
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
3 Tbsp butter
¾ cup milk and 2 Tbsp
sauce:
1 cup sugar
1 ½ Tbsp flour
¼ tsp salt
1 cup hot water
2 Tbsp butter

Instructions

Mix crust and spread ½ in dish
Add rhubarb
Mix sauce: pour over rhubarb
Top with remaining crust
