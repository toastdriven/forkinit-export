Forkinit: Strawberry Tossed Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup vegetable oil
1/3 cup sugar
¼ cup cider or red wine vinegar
1 clove garlic minced
¼ tsp salt
¼ tsp paprika
1/8 tsp pepper
6 cups mixed greens
2 ½ cup sliced strawberries
½ cup glazed walnuts

Instructions

Mix oil, sugar, vinegar, garlic, salt, pepper, and paprika in a jar and shake
Pour over mixed greens, strawberries, and walnuts
