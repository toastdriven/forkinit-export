Forkinit: Apple butter Spiced

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

16 cups thick apple pulp
1 cup vinegar
8 cups Sugar
4 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Core and slice apples, DON’T peel
Add only enough water to cook apples until soft
Press and measure
Combine all ingredients
Cook until smooth
Boil about 1 ½ hours
Stir frequently
