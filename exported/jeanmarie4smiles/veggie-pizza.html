Forkinit: Veggie Pizza

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Cooled pizza crust
8 oz cream cheese
Hidden Valley Ranch powder
Carrots
Celerly
Peppers
Any other veggies
Grated cheese

Instructions

Cool pizza crust
Mix 8 oz cream cheese with Hidden Valley Ranch (powdered)
Spread over crust and top with veggies of your choice
Top with grated cheese
