Forkinit: Vegetable Cheese Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 cups chopped potatoes
½ cup celery-diced
½ cup carrots-diced
¼ cup onions-diced
1 tsp parsley flacks
1 chicken bouillon cube (1 tsp)
1 tsp salt
Dash of pepper
1 ½ cup milk
2 Tbsp flour
½ pound American cheese- cubed
1 cup water

Instructions

Combine first 8 ingredients with water, cover and cook until done (20 minutes)
In a small bowl and add milk to flour shake well
Pour into vegetables and cook until thickened
Add cheese, serve when melted
