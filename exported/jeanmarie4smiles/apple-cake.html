Forkinit: Apple cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1/3 cup boiling water
2 cups chopped apples
2 cups flour
1 ¼ cup sugar
1 ¼ tsp baking soda
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp cloves
½ cups oil
3 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup chopped nuts

Instructions

Pour 1/3 cup water on 2 cup chopped apples. Beat apples and remaining ingredients on low speed for 1 minute. Beat on med speed for 2 minutes. Bake at 350⁰ for 45-50 min. Cover with cream cheese frosting.
