Forkinit: Rhubarb Sauce

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1-2 cups water (less if using frozen rhubarb)
enough rhubarb to fill 8 qt pan
1 large can pineapple tidbits (don’t drain)
1 quart strawberries (fresh or frozen)
1 package small strawberry jello
5+ cups sugar

Instructions

Put water, rhubarb, pineapple and strawberries in a 8 quart pan on low/med, stirring frequently
After it’s cooked down a bit, you can add more rhubarb if desired, add more water if needed
Towards end of cooking time add sugar to taste
Add 1 package of strawberry jello
Keep stirring to avoid burning on bottom
Cook until desired consistency
cool completely and freeze in containers
