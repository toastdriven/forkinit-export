Forkinit: Subway Chocolate Chip cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ¼ cup all purpose flour
1 tsp Baking soda
1 cup butter
¼ cup white sugar
¾ cup brown sugar
1 package instant vanilla pudding
2 large eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 bag chocolate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375
Mix flour with baking soda in a bowl and set aside in large bowl
Combine butter, sugars, and dry pudding mix
Beat until smooth and creamy
Beat in eggs and vanilla
Gradually add flour mixture
Stir in chips
Roll into balls and flatten slightly or drop onto a non-greased cookie sheet
Bake at 375 for 10 minutes
