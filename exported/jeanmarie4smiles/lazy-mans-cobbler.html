Forkinit: Lazy Man’s cobbler

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
1 cup flour self-rising
1 cup milk
1 stick butter, melted
1 pint fresh blueberries

Instructions

In a bowl, mix sugar, flour, and milk together. Melt butter in a 8x8 pan. Pour sugar, flour and milk mixture over butter.
Do not stir
Sprinkles blueberries over mix
Do not stir
Bake at 350० for 50 minutes. A crust will form over the blueberries as the cobbler bakes.
