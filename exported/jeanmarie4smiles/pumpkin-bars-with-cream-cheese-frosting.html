By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


Bars:
2 cups flour
1 tsp cinnamon

2 cups sugar
3 eggs, beaten
1 cup oil
1 cup pumpkin
1 cup chopped walnuts
Cream Cheese Frosting:
2 (3oz) packages cream cheese, softened
3 cups powdered sugar
6 Tbsp butter softened
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Frosting:
Combine frosting ingredients in a small bowl and mix until well blended
Bars:
Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, salt, and sugar
Stir in eggs, oil, and pumpkin until thoroughly combined
Stir in chopped walnuts
Spread batter into an ungreased 15x10pan
Bake at 350 for 30 minutes
Cool and frost

Yields: Makes 28 bars
