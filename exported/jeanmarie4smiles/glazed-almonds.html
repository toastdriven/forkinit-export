Forkinit: Glazed almonds

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 Tbsp butter, divided
2 cups blanched whole almonds
¾ cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
½ tsp salt

Instructions

Line a baking sheet with foil
Grease the foil with 1 Tbsp butter, set aside
In large heavy skillet combine almonds, sugar, remaining butter
cook and stir over medium heat for 6-8 minute or until sugar is golden brown
Remove from heat, carefully stir in vanilla
Spread onto foil immediately sprinkle with salt
Cool before breaking into pieces
Store in an airtight container

Yields: 8 servings
