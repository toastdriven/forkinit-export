Forkinit: Mile High Pie

By Mary Jo Kurka
I couldn't quite figure out this recipe but I think this is what it is.

Ingredients

Crust:
1 ½ cup crushed pretzels
4 ½ Tbsp brown sugar
¾ cup butter
1 cup sugar
Filling:
2 egg white
1 small carton strawberries
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 cup whipping cream
Topping:
¼ cup pecans
¼ cup brown sugar
1 Tbsp flour

Instructions

Mix crust and bake at 350⁰ for 10 minutes
Mix topping and toast in oven for 10 minutes
Beat egg, strawberries, sugar, vanilla, and lemon juice until well mixed pour into crust
Bake 10 minutes
Beat 1 cup whipping cream and fold into topping mixture
Put topping on pie when cool
