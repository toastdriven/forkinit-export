Forkinit: Fairy Food (angel food candy)

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
1 cup dark corn syrup
1 Tbsp vinegar
1 tsp baking soda

Instructions

Combine sugar, syrup and vinegar in a large heavy saucepan
Cook stirring while sugar melts
Cook to 300 degrees
Take off stove to stir in soda
Pour into buttered 9x9 inch pan
Break into chunks when cool
May be coated with melted chocolate
Do Not spread in pan, just pour and let it bubble
Make in a large saucepan as mixture foams up as soda is added
Coating can be made with melted chocolate chips
