Forkinit: Escalloped turkey or Chicken

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 cups cooked cubed chicken (may use Turkey)
Dressing:
6 cups cubed dry bread
½ cup melted oleo
1 ¼ tsp powdered sage
¼ cup chicken stock or cream
¾ tsp salt, pinch of pepper
2 tsp onion or chives (optimal)
Gravy:
¼ cup chicken fat (optional)
½ cup flour
4 cup chicken stock

Instructions

Put chicken in a greased rectangular pan
Put the dressing over the chicken
Reserve some for top layer
Make gravy and pour over top of chicken and dressing
Gravy will be thin
Bake until set about 1 hour at 350◦
