Forkinit: Pam's banana-bran muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup flour
1 cup wheat bran
1 tsp baking soda
½ tsp salt
½ cup chopped walnuts or pecans
1 cup mashed banana
½ cup butter
½ cup brown sugar
1 egg

Instructions

Mix banana and butter add brown sugar and egg beat add dry ingredients and stir until blended
Bake 15-20 minutes at 375⁰
Cool in tins 5 minutes and remove

Yields: 1 dozen muffins
