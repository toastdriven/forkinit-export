Forkinit: Lasagna Primavera

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups carrots, shredded
2 cups yellow squash, shredded
2 cup Zucchini, shredded
2 cup plain yogurt cheese
1 tsp oregano
1 tsp basil
½ tsp thyme
½ tsp salt
½ tsp pepper
2 eggs
2 Cup Mozzarella cheese shredded
12 cookedLasagna noodles
1 32 oz jar Spaghetti Sauce

Instructions

Strain whey from 1 quart yogurt overnight to make yogurt cheese
Preheat oven to 375⁰
In Lare mixing bowl fold vegetables, 1 cup mozzarella cheese, herbs,salt, pepper, and eggs into yogurt cheese.
Evenly spread 2 Tbsp sauce over bottom of a 9x13 inch pan
Place a layoer of noodles in a pan
Top with half of vegetable filling, then ½ of the sauce
Repeat for the 2nd layer
Finish with remaining noodles nad cheese
Bake covered with foil for 1 hour
Remove foil for the last 10 minutes for cheese to brown
