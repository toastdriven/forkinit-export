Forkinit: Caramel Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka
Lois Johnson

Ingredients

Layer 1:
¾ cup soft butter
1 cup flour
¾ cup brown sugar
1 cup oatmeal
½ tsp soft
1 tsp baking soda
Save ¼ for later
Layer 2:
32 caramels
3 Tbsp cream
3 Tbsp butter
6 oz chocolate chips
½ cup chopped nuts

Instructions

Mix layer one ingredients and set asside 1/4 of mix
Press in a 9x13 pan and bake 10 minutes at 350०
Layer 2:
Melt caramels, cream, and butter
Pour over crust
Top with chocolate chips and nuts
Add remaining crust
Bake 10 minutes
