Forkinit: Peanutsy Surprise cupcakes

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1/2cup old-fashioned oats, uncooked
3/4cup water, boiling
/4 cup canola oil
1/4cuphoney
2 egg whites
1/2tsp vanilla
2 ripe bananas
1 cup whole wheat pastry flour
1/2tsp baking soda
½ tsp ground allspice
1/8 tsp salt
2 oz light cream cheese
3 Tbsp natural peanut butter
1 Tbsp Honey

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375⁰
Combine oats and boiling water in a small bowl, stirring well, aside and let cool
Cream together oil and honey, beating well on medium speed electric mixer
Add egg whites and vanilla, beating well
Add bananas and beat until smooth, set mixture aside
In a separate bowl, combine flour baking soda, cinnamon, and allspice, and salt
Add half of flour mixture to the creamed mixture and stir until well mixed
Add half of the oatmeal mixture, stirring until well mixed
Add the remaining flour mixture, mixing well
Add the remaining oatmeal mixture, set aside
Combine the cream cheese, peanut butter and honey in a small bowl
Spoon 1 Tbsp of the cupcake batter into each of the 12 paper-lined muffin cups
Spoon 2 tsps of the peanut butter mix into each muffin cup
Fill each cup 2/3 full with remaining batter
Bake 20 minutes
Remove cupcakes from pans and let cool on wire racks

Yields: Makes 1 dozen cupcakes
