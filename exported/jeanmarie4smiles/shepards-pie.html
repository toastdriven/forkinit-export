Forkinit: Shepard’s Pie

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb ground beef
1 medium onion, chopped
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 Tbsp ketchup
1/8 tsp ground black pepper
1 cup frozen peas and carrots
1 cup milk
2 Tbsp butter
1 1/3 cups instant mashed potato flakes

Instructions

Cook beef and onion in skillet over medium high heat until well browned
Stir in soup, ketchup, pepper, and vegetables
Put in a 9 inch pie plate
Heat milk and butter in saucepan- heat to a boil
Remove from heat and slowly stir in potato flakes
Spoon potatoes over beef mixtue
Bake at 400⁰ for 15 minutes or until potatoes are lightly browned
