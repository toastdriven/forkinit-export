Forkinit: Sally’s Ham balls

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ½ -3 lb hamball mix or
1 ¼ lb ground ham
1 lb ground pork
½ lb ground beef
1 egg
1 ½ cup crushed graham crackers
1 cup milk
Sauce
1 can tomato soup
⅓ cp white vinegar
1 ¼ cup brown sugar
1 tsp dry mustard

Instructions

Mix together, make into balls
Cover with sauce, basting during baking
Bake 1 hour at 350
