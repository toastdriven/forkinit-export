Forkinit: Chicken Korma

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 medium to large onions -chopped
3 Tbsp peeled and grated fresh ginger root
6 large cloves of garlic, minced
1 tsp salt
1 ½ tsp tumeric
1 tsp chopped chili peppers
¼ pint cream
1- 1 1.2 cup yogurt
4 large or 6 medium boneless chicken breasts
2 tsp garam masasa
¼ tsp vegetable oil
½ tsp baking soda
cilantro- chopped

Instructions

Fry onion in ¼ cup vegetable oil for 10-15 minutes
Add garlic, ginger, salt, turmeric, chili peppers and fry another 10-15 minutes
Add tomato sauce, simmer for 20 minutes
Cut up chicken to bite size pieces and add to sauce and cook 20 minutes
Add soda, once frothiness dies down, add cream and yogurt and simmer for 15 minutes
Add garam masala and cilantro simmer 10 minutes
Serve with flour tortillas or pita bread
