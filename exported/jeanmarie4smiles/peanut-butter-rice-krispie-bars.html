Forkinit: Peanut Butter Rice Krispie Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
1 cup corn syrup
1 cup peanut butter
6 cups rice krispies
½ bag butterscotch chips
½ bag chocolate chips

Instructions

On medium heat combine sugar and corn syrup
Cook until starts to boil
Remove from heat and add peanut butter
Stir until smooth and then add to rice krispies
Mix well then put into greased 9x13 pan
Melt together butterscotch and chocolate ships on low heat then spread on top of bars
