Forkinit: Baked Pheasant

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

¼ cup hot water
½ cup diced onion
½ tsp or less liquid smoke
½ Tbsp Worchestershire sauce
1 chicken bouillon cube
½ cup diced celery
dash pepper

Instructions

Bone, flour and brown pheasant pieces
Place pheasant in baking dish
Mix together above ingredients
Pour over pheasant, cover and bake at 325 until tender
