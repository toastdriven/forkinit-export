Forkinit: Brenda’s Dip with Chips

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 can refried beans with green chillies
1 lb hamberger
1 onion
1 small jar picante sauce

Instructions

shredded cheddar cheese
brown hamburger with onion
Mix with picante sauce in greased cake pan
Layer beans
then hamburger mixture
Top with shredded cheese
Bake ½ hour or until done
