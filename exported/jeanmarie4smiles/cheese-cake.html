Forkinit: Cheese cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 (8oz) packages cream cheese
½ cup sugar
½ tsp vanilla
2 eggs
1 ready to use graham cracker crumb crust (6oz or 9 inch)

Instructions

Mix cream cheese, sugar and vanilla at medium speed with electric mixer until well blended.
Add eggs; mix until blended
Pour into crust
Bake at 350⁰ for 40 minutes or until center is almost set
Cool
Refrigerate 3 hours or over night
Flavor variation:
Stir ½ cup mini semi-sweet chocolate chips into batter
Sprinkle with additional ¼ cup chips before baking
