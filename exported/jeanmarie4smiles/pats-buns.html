Forkinit: Pat’s buns

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup warm water
2 Tbsp. soft butter
1 egg
3 ¼ cup flour
1 Tbsp gluten
¼ cup sugar
1 tsp. salt
3 tsp yeast
Put in gread machine for 7 minutes
Add more flour if needed
Make into buns and let rise (double)
Bake at 350⁰ for 10 minutes on middle rack
