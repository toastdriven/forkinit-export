Forkinit: Six Bean casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


½ lb bulk sweet Italian sausage
¼ lb pepperoni, sliced thin
½ lb smoked kielbasa, sliced
½ cup spicy barbecue sauce
1 can (16oz) pork and beans, undrained
1 can (16 oz) red kidney beans, undrained
1 can (16 oz) hot chilies undrained
1 can (16 oz) white kidney beans (Canellini) drained
1 can (16 oz) lima beans, drained
1 can (16 oz) Butter beans, drained
1 can (10 ¾ oz ) tomato soup, undiluted
3 oz tomato paste
½ cup brown sugar or to taste
6 sliced bacon

Instructions

Form the Italian sausage into 1 inch balls
Brown in skillet and drain
Combine with remaining ingredients, except bacon in 5 quart casserole or in a small roasting pan
Partially cook bacon in microwave or on stovetop; arrange over casserole
Bake, uncovered, at 325◦ for 1 ½ hours
Serve hot or cold

Yields: Yield: 16 generous servings
