Forkinit: Ground Beef Sukiyaki

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 lbs lean ground beef
2 Tbsp sugar
1/3 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup steak sauce
1 tsp salt
1 can sliced mushrooms
2 medium onions, thinly sliced
1 green pepper, sliced in thin strips
6 scallions, cut in 1 inch pieces
1 cup thinly sliced celery
1 can water chestnuts, drained
1 can bamboo shoots, drained
1 Tbsp cornstarch
1 1/2 cups rice, cooked

Instructions

In large skillet, brown beef until crumbly
In small bowl, mix sugar, soy sauce, steak sauce, and salt
Set aside
Drain mushrooms, reserving liquid
When meat is cooked , mix in vegetables
Add sauce
Simmer 3 minutes or until vegetables are just tender crisp
Combine cornstarch and reserved mushroom liquid
Stir into sukiyaki
Cook just until thickened
Serve over rice
