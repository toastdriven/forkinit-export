Forkinit: Shelly’s cabbage Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 bag cabbage
Chopped lettuce (optional)
1/3 cup sliced almonds
Ramen noodles –broken in pieces
Dressing:
3 Tbsp vinegar
¼ cup oil
1 Tbsp red wine vinegar
Chicken flavor packet from Ramen Noodles
2 Tbsp sugar
½ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper

Instructions

Wisk together all dressing ingredients
Chill in fridge
Just before serving mix salad ingredients and top with dressing
