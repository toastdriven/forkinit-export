Forkinit: Rhubarb Custard Dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka
Mary Lou

Ingredients

Crust:
1 ¼ cup flour
2 ½ Tbsp sugar
¾ cup butter
Filling:
2 ½ cup sugar
½ cup flour
4 eggs
1 ¼ cup whipping cream
6 ¼ cup rhubarb
Topping:
1 ¼ cup whipping cream (or 2 cups cool whip)
10 oz cream cheese
¾ cup sugar
¾ tsp vanilla

Instructions

Mix crust well
Bake at 350० for 10 minutes in a 9x13 glass pan
Filling:
Combine sugar and flour
Whisk in eggs andcream
Stir in rhubarb
Pour over crust
Bake at 350० for 1 hour or until set
Check by inserting knife until knife comes out nearly clean
cool
Topping:
Whip cream and set aside
Beat remaining ingredients until smooth
Fold in whipped cream
Spread over top
Refrigerate
