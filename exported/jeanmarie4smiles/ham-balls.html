Forkinit: Ham balls

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ½ lbs ground ham
2 lb ground pork
1 lb ground beef
3 beaten eggs
2 cups milk
3 graham crackers crushed or ground
Sauce:
2 cans tomato soup
2 ⅓ cups brown sugar
¾ cup white vinegar
2 tsp dry mustard

Instructions

Mix meats together first then add remaining ingredients
Form into 25-30 balls
Mix sauce together and pour over ham balls
Bake 1 hour at 350
I like to bake them at least 1 ½ hours
The ham balls sort of pack together and the inside ones need more time
