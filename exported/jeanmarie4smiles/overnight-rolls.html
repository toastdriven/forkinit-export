Forkinit: Overnight rolls

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

24 frozen dinner rolls
1 small pkg regular butter scotch pudding (not instant)
1/3 cup brown sugar
¼ cup melted butter

Instructions

Grease bundt pan and line bottom with frozen rolls, sprinkle a little pudding, or brown sugar and butter over
Repeat until ingredients are gone
Let rise overnight and cover with tin foil (making a tent)
Bake in morning for 30 min at 350⁰
