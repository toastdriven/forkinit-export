Forkinit: Dill pickles

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Small cucumbers
Slices of onion
Pieces of dill
Garlic cloves
Salt
6 cups vinegar
6 water/with 1/16 tsp alum
2 Â¼ cups sugar

Instructions

Prepare 7 quart jars for hot water bath
Place a thin slice of onion, twig of dill, some minced garlic, and 2 tsp salt in each quart jar
Fill jars with cucumbers and add another slice of onion and dill
Bring vinegar, water, and sugar brine to a boil
Pour brine over cucumbers
Seal jars put in hot water bath for 5 minutes

Yields: Makes 7 quarts
