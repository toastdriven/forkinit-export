Forkinit: Zucchini Blueberry Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 eggs (slightly beaten w/ 1 cup oil)
3 tsp vanilla
2 ¼ cup sugar
2 cups shredded zucchini
Mix all together then add:
3 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking powder
¼ tsp soda
1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Gently stir in 2 cups blueberries
Grease and flour 3 small loaf pans
Bake at 350⁰ for 1 hour – test with knife
