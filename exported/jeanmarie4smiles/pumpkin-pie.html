Forkinit: Pumpkin Pie

By Mary Jo Kurka
Grandma Birch

Ingredients

¾ cup sugar
2 eggs
½ tsp ginger
¼ tsp cinnamon
1 cup milk
2 cups pumpkin

Instructions

Mix together and pour into a pastry crust
Bake at 375 for 45 minutes or until knife comes out clean
