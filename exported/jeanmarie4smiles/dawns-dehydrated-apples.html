Forkinit: Dawn’s Dehydrated Apples

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Apples
4 cups water (1 hot)
½ cup honey
1 ½ Tbsp fruit fresh
1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Peel and slice apples
Mix 1 cup hot water with honey then add remaining ingredients
Soak apples in mixture
Use about 2 recipes to fill dehydrator
Drain liquid well before putting in dehydrator
