Forkinit: Peanut Brittle

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup white sugar
½ cup water
½ cup white karo syrup
1 cup raw peanuts
1 Tbsp butter
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt

Instructions

Boil sugar, water, and karo about 3 minutes to 240 degrees
Add peanuts and continue to cool until 300 degrees
Remove from heat and add butter, baking soda, and salt
Mix well and quickly pour onto greased cookie sheet
Spread with a fork
