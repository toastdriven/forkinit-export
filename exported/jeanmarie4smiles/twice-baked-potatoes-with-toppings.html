Forkinit: Twice Baked Potatoes With Toppings

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 large baking potatoes
8 slices bacon
1 cup sour cream
½ tsp salt
½ tsp pepper
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese, divided
8 green onions, sliced, divided
½ cup milk
4 Tbsp butter

Instructions

Preheat oven to350⁰
Bake potatoes in preheated oven for 1 hour
Meanwhile place bacon in a large skillet, cook until evenly brown
Drain and crumble bacon and set aside
When potatoes are done, allow them to cool for 10 minutes
Slice potatoes in half lengthwise and scoop the flesh into a large bowl, save skins
To the potato flesh add sour cream, milk, butter, salt, pepper, and ½ cup cheese and ½ the green onions
Mix with hand mixer until well blended and creamy
Spoon the mixture into the potato skins
Top each with remaining cheese, green onions, and bacon
Bake for another 15 minutes
