Forkinit: Mom's cornmeal muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka
Ruth Burch

Ingredients

1 cup cornmeal
1 cup flour
½ cup sugar
1 tsp baking soda
¼ tsp salt
1 cup buttermilk
½ cup oil
1 egg

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients
Add liquid ingredients
Mix until just blended
Put in muffin pan
Bake 350⁰ for 20 minutes
