Forkinit: Refrigerator Pickles

By Mary Jo Kurka
Keeps 1 year

Ingredients

6 cups cucumbers sliced
1 cup onions
1 tsp celery seed
1 cup vinegar
2 cups sugar
1 tsp pickling salt

Instructions

Stir celery seed, vinegar, sugar, and salt together
Add onions and cucumbers
Refrigerate
