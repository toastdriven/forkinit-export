Forkinit: Broccoli Coleslaw

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup oil
½ cup vinegar
1 cup sugar
2 tsp soy sauce
½ tsp ginger (gingerroot works too)
Garlic salt- sprinkle
½ cup sliced almonds
1 cup sunflower seeds
2 packages Ramen noodles
1 package broccoli coleslaw

Instructions

Mix together oil, vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic salt-set aside
Sauté almonds and sunflower seeds- set aside
Right before serving add Ramen noodles and add to broccoli coleslaw
Mix together and add oil mix
