Forkinit: Pineapple Pork chops or chicken

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 pork chops or 4 chicken breasts
1 can beef broth (or chicken broth)
1 can pineapple tidbits
1/2 cup green or red pepper
1/4 cup ketchup
1 Tbsp vinegar
1 Tbsp brown sugar

Instructions

Cube meat and brown in oil
Add broth,ketchup, vinegar, and brown sugar, stir and cover and let simmer 20 minutes
Then add pineapple, peppers and cook additional 10 minutes
Mix 2 Tbsp water with 1 Tbsp cornstarch and stir into mixture until it thickens
Serve over rice
