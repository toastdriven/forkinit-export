Forkinit: Peanut Blossoms

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Peanut Blossoms
Mix:
3 ½ cup flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp soda
Cream:
1 cup butter
⅔ cup peanut butter
Add:
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup white sugar
Add and beat well:
2 eggs
2 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Blend in dry ingredients
Roll into balls and roll in sugar
Put on greased cookie sheet
Bake at 350० for 7 to 10 minutes
Remove from oven and put a chocolate kiss on top of each cookie
Push down and bake 1-2 more minutes WATCH carefully
