Forkinit: Birthday Cupcakes

By Mary Jo Kurka
Sherry Anderson

Ingredients

Filling:
Beat: 1 - 8oz cream cheese
1 egg
1/3 cup sugar
Dash salt
Add: 1 cup chocolate chips

Cupcakes:
1 ½ cup flour
¼ cup cocoa
½ tsp salt
1 cup water
1 cup sugar
1 tsp soda
½ cup oil
1 tsp vinegar

Instructions

Fill ½ full and spoon on topping
Bake 25-30 minutes at 350⁰
Makes 20-24
