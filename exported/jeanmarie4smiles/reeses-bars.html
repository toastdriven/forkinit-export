Forkinit: Reese’s bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups crushed graham crackers
2 cups powdered sugar
1 cup creamy peanut butter
1 ½ sticks butter, melted
1 12 oz bag chocolate chips
1 Tbsp peanut butter

Instructions

Mix together grahnm crackers, p. sugar, peanut butter, and butter and put in a 9x13 pan
Melt together chocolate chips and peanut butter and spread on top of crust
