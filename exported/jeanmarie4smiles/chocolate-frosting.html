Forkinit: Chocolate frosting

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup sugar
6 Tbsp butter
6 Tbsp milk
1 -1 ½ cup chocolate chips

Instructions

Boil sugar, butter, and milk for one minute
Add chocolate chips
stir until melted
Wait until frosting thickens and cools before frosting cake (also cool cake before frosting)
Tag Wonderful chocolate cake
