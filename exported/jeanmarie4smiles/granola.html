Forkinit: Granola

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

6 cups regular oatmeal
6 cups quick oatmeal
½ cup brown sugar
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup slivered or sliced almonds
1 tsp cinnamon
2/3 cup oil
2/3 cup honey
2 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Mix together dry ingredients in a large bowl
In a separate bowl, mix oil, honey, and vanilla
Add liquid to dry mixture
Put into 2 greased jelly roll pans
Bake at 350⁰ for 30-45 minutes
Stir after first 15 minutes then every 7 minutes
May add dried fruit after it is cooled (3 cups)
