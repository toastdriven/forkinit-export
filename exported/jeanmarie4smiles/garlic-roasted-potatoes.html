Forkinit: Garlic Roasted Potatoes

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

5-6 potatoes
Â¼ cup olive oil
6-8 cloves garlic
Salt to taste

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400
Wash but do not peel potatoes
Cut each into about 8 chunks
Spread out on a Cookie sheet
Drizzle with olive oil and garlic
Using a fork, stir and mix together
Bake 15-20 minutes or until tender and golden brown
Sprinkle with salt and serve
