Forkinit: Apple Dumplings

By Mary Jo Kurka
French’s

Ingredients

½ cup sugar
2 cup water
¼ tsp cinnamon
¼ tsp nutmeg
¼ cup butter
6 medium apples
Crust:
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
¾ cup shortening
½ cup milk

Instructions

Heat sugar, water, spices, and butter in saucepan to make a syrup and let cool slightly
Peel and core apples
Make crust like a pie crust
Roll crust to about ⅛ inch and place apples on sections (making 6 sections)
Pour some syrup into core
Fold up crust around apple and place in 9x13 baking dish
Pour remaining syrup around them
Bake 375० for 35 minutes or more
