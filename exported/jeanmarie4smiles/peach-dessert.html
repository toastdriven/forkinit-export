Forkinit: Peach Dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cup flour
1 cup butter
¼ cup sugar
6 cup sliced peaches
2 cup sugar
9 Tbsp flour
2 eggs beaten
½ cup cream
2 Tbsp sugar

Instructions

Mix flour, butter, and sugar together with hand mixer and place in 9x13 greased pan
Mix peaches with sugar and remaining flour
Put on crust
Mix eggs, cream, and sugar
Pour over peaches
Bake 1 hour at 350०
