Forkinit: Molasses Oatmeal Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka
Ruth Burch

Ingredients

2 cups oatmeal
4 cups boiling water
½ cup molasses
½ cup sugar
2 tsp. salt
4 tsp. shortening
1 ½ cup raisins
10-12 cups flour
2 Tbsp. yeast in ½ cup warm water

Instructions

Pour water over oatmeal and let stand 30 minutes. Add remaining ingredients and mix as you do for bread. Make into loaves and let rise.
Bake at 350⁰ for 35 minutes or so
