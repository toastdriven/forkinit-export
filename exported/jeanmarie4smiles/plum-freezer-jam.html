Forkinit: Plum Freezer Jam

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 cups plum pulp (about 3 pounds of fruit)
½ cup lemon juice
1 package MCP pectin
1 cup light corn syrup
4 ½ cup sugar

Instructions

Wash, pit, slice and finely grind fruit
Stir in Pectin and mix thoroughly
Set aside for 30 minutes stirring every 5 minutes
Add corn syrup and mix well
Add sugar gradually, stir well
Pour into containers and seal
