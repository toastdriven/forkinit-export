Forkinit: Susan’s Baked Beans

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


¼ cup brown sugar
1 medium onion
1 ½ Tbsp mustard
½ cup ketchup
2 lb. can pork and beans
2-4 slices bacon

Instructions

May slightly cook bacon or just cut in chunks and place in large saucepan with all other ingredients
Simmer for 10 minutes
