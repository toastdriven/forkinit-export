Forkinit: Chicken Noodle Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


1 whole chicken or chicken pieces
6-8 cups water
2 bay leaves
2-3 stalks celery chopped
1 onion chopped
3 peppercorns
4-6 chopped carrots (and other veggies)
1 tsp poultry seasoning
½ tsp garlic powder
1 tsp basil and oregano
¼ tsp thyme
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Cook whole chicken or chicken pieces in water with bay leaves, celery, onion, and peppercorns
Simmer 1-2 hours and removed chicken and cut into bite sized pieces
Skim off fat
Add carrots and any other vegetables you like
Simmer 30 minutes
Add egg noodles and chicken pieces as desired
Can also add chicken bouillon but then use less salt
