Forkinit: Corn Dogs

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup cornmeal
4 tsp baking powder
¼ cup sugar
1 egg
2 tsp salt
1 ½ cup flour
1 cup milk

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients
Add eggs and milk and beat only until mixed
Deep fat fry until brown
Have hotdogs dry to batter will stick
Drain on paper towels

Yields: Yield 18
