Forkinit: Chex Party Mix

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 cup corn chex
3 cup rice chex
3 cup wheat chex
1 cup mixed nuts
1 cup bite sized pretzels
1 cup garlic flavored bagel chips broken in pieces
6 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp worcestershire sauce
1 ½ tsp seasoned salt
¾ tsp garlic powder
½ tsp onion powder

Instructions

Heat oven to 250
Mix cereal, nuts, pretzels, and bagel chips
In large ungreased roaster pan, melt butter
Stir in seasonings
Gradually stir in cereal mix until evenly coated
Bake 1 hour stirring at least every 15 minutes
Spread on paper towels
Cool for 15 minutes
Store in airtight container
