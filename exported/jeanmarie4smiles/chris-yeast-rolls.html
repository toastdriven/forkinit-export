Forkinit: Chris’ Yeast Rolls

By Mary Jo Kurka
May double easily

Ingredients

4 – 4 ½ cup flour (may use 2 cups whole wheat flour)
1 Tbsp yeast
1 cup milk
1/3 cup sugar or honey
1/3 cup butter
1 tsp salt
2 eggs
Topping:
¼ cup brown sugar
¼ cup butter
¼ cup brown sugar
1 tsp honey
Chopped nuts

Instructions

Combine 2 cup flour and yeast
In saucepan heat milk, sugar, butter and salt until warm. Add to flour mixture. Add eggs.
Beat 3 minutes at high
Stir in as much flour as you can
Turn out onto a lightly floured surface.
Knead in enough of the remaining flour to make a moderately stiff dough (6-8min)
Shape and place in greased bowl. Cover. Let rise until double (1 hour)
Punch down and divided in half.
Roll into 12 inch circles
Cut into 12 and brush with butter.
Roll and place onto greased sheet.
Let rise 30 minutes
Bake at 375⁰ for 10 minutes
For sweet rolls 350⁰ for 20 minutes
Spread with butter and sprinkle with cinnamon and sugar.
Top with prepared topping
