Forkinit: Hashbrown Cheese Potatoes

By Mary Jo Kurka
Sally and Dawn

Ingredients

1 2lb bag of hashbrowns (little cubes)
1/3 cup melted butter
1 chopped onion
1 can cream soup (chicken)
1 ½ cup sour cream
2 cups grated cheese
2 cups cornflakes
¼ cup melted butter
1 tsp salt
1 tsp garlic powder
½ tsp pepper

Instructions

Mix hashbrowns, butter, onion, soup, sour cream, and cheese together
Place in baking dish
Mix cornflakes and ¼ cup butter together and place on top of potatoes
Bake at 350 uncovered for 60-75 minutes until bubbly
