Forkinit: Caramel Turtle Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup bisquick
1 cup Quick cook oats
¾ cup packed brown sugar
1/3 cup butter, softened
1 egg
1 bag caramels
2 Tbsp milk
1 cup chopped pecans
¾ semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Heat oven 375
Mix Bisquick, oats, brown sugar, butter, and egg
Bake 15 minutes until golden brown
Heat caramels and milk until melted
Spread on crust
Sprinkle pecans
Drizzle chocolate on top
Cool 30 minutes
Cut into bars
