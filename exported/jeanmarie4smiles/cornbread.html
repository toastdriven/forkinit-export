Forkinit: Cornbread

By Mary Jo Kurka
from Janet Alalard

Ingredients

1 ¼ cup flour
¾ cup cornmeal
¼ cup sugar
2 tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
1 cup milk
¼ cup oil
1 egg beaten


Instructions

Mix dry ingredients
Add wet ingredients
Blend just until mixed
Put in 8 or 9 inch sq. pan
Bake at 400⁰ for 20-25 min
Double ingredients for 9x13 pan
