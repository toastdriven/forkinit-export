Forkinit: Family Meat Loaf

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


1 egg
3 slices bread
1 cup milk
1 ½ lbs hamburger
¼ cup onion
1 tsp salt
½ tsp dry mustard
½ tsp celery salt
¼ tsp pepper
¼ tsp sage
1 Tbsp Worchestershire sauce

Instructions

For meatballs use ½ cup less of milk
Mix together ingredients
Bake at 350 for 1 hour
