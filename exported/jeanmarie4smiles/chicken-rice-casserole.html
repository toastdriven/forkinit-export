Forkinit: Chicken Rice Casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup rice
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of chicken soup
2 cup milk
Chicken cut in pieces
1 package Lipton onion soup mix or 1 small chopped onion

Instructions

Bring soups, milk, and onion to a boil
Flour chicken pieces and dip in hot batter and layer on top of rice (partially boiled) and pour remaining batter over top
Cover
Bake 1 ½ hours at350
Uncover and brown for ½ hour
