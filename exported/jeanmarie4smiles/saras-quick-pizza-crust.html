Forkinit: Sara’s quick Pizza Crust

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

can make 1 large pizza or 2 smaller pizzas
1 (0.25oz) package active dry yeast (or 2 ½ tsp)
1 tsp white sugar
1 cup warm water
2 ½ cup bread flour
2 Tbsp oil
½ tsp salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 450 degrees
In medium bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water
Let stand until creamy (about 10 minutes)
Stir in flour, salt, and oil
Beat until smooth
Let rest for 5 minutes
Turn dough out on a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a round
Spread out on greased (Crisco) pan
Bake about 15 minutes or until golden brown and bottom looks cooked.
