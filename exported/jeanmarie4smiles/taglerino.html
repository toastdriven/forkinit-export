Forkinit: Taglerino

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ Tbsp cooking oil
1 clove garlic
1medium onion chopped
1 lb ground hamburger or turkey
1 (8oz) can tomato sauce
½ pound grated cheese
½ -1 can pitted olives (put in whole)
Salt and pepper
8 oz egg noodle
1 can con somme (diluted 1 1 can water)
1 (16oz) can of tomatoes diced

Instructions

Brown onion, garlic, and meat in oil
Add tomato sauce and tomatoes
Cook noodles in consomme and water
Add half the cheese and olive to the meat sauce
Add noodles
Top with remaining cheese
Cover with foil
Bake 350⁰ 30 minutes to 1 hour until bubbly
Makes 9x13 pan
