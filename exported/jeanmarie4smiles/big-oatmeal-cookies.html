Forkinit: Big Oatmeal Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
¼ tsp ginger
½ tsp cinnamon
½ tsp salt
2 cups oatmeal
¾ cup honey
½ cup cooking oil
1 beaten egg
1 Tbsp hot water
1 cup raisins
¾ cup chopped nuts

Instructions

In large bowl mix the dry ingredients
Stir in oil, honey, egg, hot water, raisins, and nuts
Mix well
Drop dough on cookie sheet using teaspoon measure
Flatten with bottom of glass dipped in water
Bake at 350० about 8 minutes or until brown
Variation use ½ cup molasses and ½ honey in place of all honey

Yields: Makes about 40 large cookies
