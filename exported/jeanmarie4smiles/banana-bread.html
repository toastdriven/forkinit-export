Forkinit: Banana Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 large bananas (mashed)
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
3 Tbsp milk
2 cups flour
½ tsp salt
½ tsp soda
1 ½ tsp baking powder
½ cup melted butter
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Grease and flour 9x5 inch loaf pans
Beat together bananas, sugar and eggs until light.
Stir in milk
Milk together flour, salt, soda, baking powder, and add to mixture.
Mix thoroughly stir in melted butter and vanilla
Pour into loaf pan
Bake at 350⁰ about 1 hour or until done
