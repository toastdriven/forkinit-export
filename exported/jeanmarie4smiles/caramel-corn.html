Forkinit: Caramel Corn

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 qts popped corn-unsalted
2 sticks butter
2 cup brown sugar
½ cup white karo syrup
1 tsp salt
½ tsp baking soda

Instructions

Boil butter, sugar, syrup, and salt together
Add baking soda
Stir until foaming and pour over popcorn
Put in buttered pan and bake at 250 for 45 minutes
stir every 15 minutes
