Forkinit: Oatmeal Muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup oats
1 ½ cup buttermilk
¾ cup brown sugar
¾ cup corn oil
2 large eggs
1 ½ cup flour
1 ½ tsp baking soda
¾ tsp baking powder
¾ tsp salt
1 ¼ tsp cinnamon
1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

In small bowl soak oats in buttermilk in large bowl beat brown sugar, oil, and eggs.
In another bowl combine dry ingredients
Gently mix fry ingredients into oat mixture, mixing as little as possible
Fold in nuts
Pour batter into greased muffin tins, filling 2/3 full
Bake 12-15 minutes
