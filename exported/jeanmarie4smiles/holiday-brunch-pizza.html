Forkinit: Holiday Brunch Pizza

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup cheese
Crust:
1 bag frozen shredded hashbrowns thawed
2 eggs
Salt and pepper to taste
Egg Topping:
7 eggs
2 cups shredded velveeta cheese
½ cup milk
Optional Toppings:
Chopped ham, pepper, olives, onions, green chilies, crumbled cooked bacon or sausage

Instructions

Preheat oven t0 400⁰
Combine potatoes and 2 eggs in a bowl
Place parchment paper on cookie sheet
Spread potato mixture on paper forming a 15 inch circle
Pat potato crust with back of spoon to firm
Season with salt and pepper
Bake crust for 20 minutes or until golden brown
Meanwhile whisk eggs and milk in a bowl
Add cheese
Cook in microwave on high for 3 minutes, stir, then cook an additional 3 minutes and stir again
Spread slightly cooked eggs evenly over baked potato crust
Top with optional ingredients
Sprinkle with remaining shredded cheese and bake an additional 10 minutes
Cut with pizza cutter into wedges
Serve immediately

Yields: Makes 8 servings
