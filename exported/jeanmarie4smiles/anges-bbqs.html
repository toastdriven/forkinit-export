Forkinit: Ange’s BBQ’s

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 ½ lb hamburger
1 onion
3 celery stalks
1 can tomato soup
1 Tbsp ketchup
2 Tbsp worchestershire sauce (can add more)
2 Tbsp Hunz 57 or A1 steak sauce

Instructions

Fry hamburger, onions, and celery together
Drain grease and mix in remaining ingredients
Cook for 10 minutes
