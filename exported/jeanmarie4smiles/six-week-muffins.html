Forkinit: Six week muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka
from June Hall

Ingredients

10oz box Raisins Bran
2 ½ cups sugar
1 quart buttermilk
5 tsp baking soda
1 cup salad oil
4 eggs
5 cups flour
Pinch of salt

Instructions

In giant bowl combine raisin bran, sugar, flour, soda, and salt
Add beaten eggs, oil, and buttermilk
Mix well
Store covered in refrigerator and use as desired
Keeps 6 weeks
Do not stir when putting mix into tins
Grease tins or use paper cups
Bake 400⁰ for 15 minutes
