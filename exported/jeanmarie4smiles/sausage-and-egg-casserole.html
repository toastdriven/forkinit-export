Forkinit: Sausage and egg casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb sausage
6 slices of bread
1 cup shredded cheese
6 eggs
2 cups milk
½ tsp dry mustard
¼ tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Instructions

Mix together eggs, milk, mustard, salt, and pepper
Brown sausage and drain fat
Toast bread and remove crusts
Cut bread into quarters
In greased casserole lay half of toast pieces
½ of sausage and half of cheese
Pour half of egg mixture over this
Repeat layers, cover and refrigerate overnight
Bake covered at 325⁰ for 45-60 minutes
Let stand 5 minutes before serving
