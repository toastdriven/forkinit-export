Forkinit: Rhubarb Cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Beat with electric hand mixer
1 cup brown sugar
½ cup white sugar
½ cup butter
1 egg
Mix together and add to above mixture
1 cup buttermilk
2 cup flour
1 tsp soda
1 tsp cinnamon
Dash salt
2 cup rhubarb Fold into cake mixture.
Pour into greased 9x13 pan and sprinkle with topping.
Mix:
¼ cup sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
¼ - 1/3 cup chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions


Bake at 350⁰ for 35-40 minutes or until tests done with toothpick
