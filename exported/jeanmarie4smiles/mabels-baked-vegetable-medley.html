Forkinit: Mabel’s baked vegetable medley

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 package frozen mixed vegetables
1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
1 cup sliced celery
½ chopped onion
¾ cup mayo
1 stick butter
2 package Ritz crackers

Instructions

Oil 9x13 inch pan
Combine vegetables, cheese, celery, onion, and mayo
Top with butter and ritz crackers
Bake at 350 for 20 minutes
