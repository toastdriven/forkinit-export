Forkinit: Banana Breeze

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 8oz cream cheese
1 can sweetened condensed milk
Â¼ cup lemon juice
1 tsp vanilla
3 bananas

Instructions

Beat cream cheese until fluffy
Add milk and blend thouroughly
Add lemon juice and vanilla
Stir until thickened
Slice bananas into a graham cracker crust (put some cinnamon in the crust)
Pour filling over bananas
Chill 2-3 hours
Garnish with 2 bananas and graham crumbs
