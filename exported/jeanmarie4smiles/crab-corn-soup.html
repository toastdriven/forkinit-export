Forkinit: Crab-Corn Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup butter
1 medium onion chopped
1 garlic clove minced (or garlic powder)
¼ cup flour
2 bottle clam juice
1 can whole kernel corn
¼ tsp dried thyme
1 tsp salt
½ tsp pepper
¼ tsp red cayenne pepper
2 cup chicken broth
2 cups whip cream or half and half
1 pound crab meat (or imitation)

Instructions

Melt butter and onion, garlic and sauté
Until wilted (5 minutes)
Add flour- stir cook 3-4 minutes
Slowly stir in clam juice and broth bring to boil to thicken
Stir in corn, thyme, salt and pepper
Cook 25minutes
Stir in cream cook 10 minutes
Add crab meat

Yields: Makes 8-10 servings
