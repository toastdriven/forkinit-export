Forkinit: Brown Sugar Maple Ham Glaze

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 fully cooked ham (6-8 lbs)
½ cup pure maple syrup
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup apple juice
1 heaping Tbsp Dijon mustard
Dash of cinnamon and ginger or allspice

Instructions

Place ham, fat side up, on rack in a foil-lined roasting pan; score fat and stud with cloves if desired
Cook at 325 for about 18 minutes per pound, until meat thermometer or instant read thermometer registers 148
Combine glaze ingredients i saucepan
Bring to a boil and boil for about 2 minutes - don’t over boil
About 20 minutes before the ham is done, spoon half of glaze over top of ham,
10 minutes before ham is done add remaining glaze

Yields: Serves 8-10
