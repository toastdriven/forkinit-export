Forkinit: Shepherd Bread- flat bread

By Mary Jo Kurka
From Shelly
Great with soup!

Ingredients

2 cup warm water (110⁰)
1 pkg yeast
1 Tbsp sugar
2 tsp salt
Mix together in large bowl until yeast dissolves
Add:
4 cups flour


Instructions

Stir with wooden spoon, do not knead
Spray another big bowl wit hpam and put dough in and let rise until double in size (Dough will be sticky)
Grease cookie sheet, divide dough into three and spread with back of spoon until each loaf is about 1 ½ inch thick
Melt ¼ cup butter and brush on top of loaves
Let rise until doubled in size
Bake at 425⁰ for 10 minutes and then at 375⁰ for 20 minutes
