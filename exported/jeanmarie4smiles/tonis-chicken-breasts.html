Forkinit: Toni’s Chicken Breasts

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

8-10 boneless chicken breasts
1 box flavored stove top stuffing (you choose)
2 packages Hollendaise sauce mix

Instructions

prepare hollandaise per directions on package
Prepare stove Top per directions on package
Slice breasts to form large pockets
stuff brest with stop top
Bake at 350 for 40 minutes
Pour ½ sauce mixture over chicken breast
Bake another 10 minutes
Reserve remainder of sauce for dipping
