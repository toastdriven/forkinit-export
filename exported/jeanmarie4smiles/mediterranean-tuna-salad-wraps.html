Forkinit: Mediterranean Tuna Salad Wraps

By Mary Jo Kurka
Salad can be prepared up to a day ahead, just add lettuce before making wraps

Ingredients

2 cups chopped lettuce
3 cans tuna
1 medium tomato, chopped
¼ cup chopped red onion
¼ cup chopped cucumber
¼ cup chopped red bell peppers
¼ cup pitted Kalamata olive, cut in half
¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
2 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1 ½ tsp Dijon mustard
1 Tbsp lemon juice
½ tsp salt
1/8 tsp black pepper
6 large tortillas

Instructions

In a large mixing bowl, combine lettuce, tuna, tomatoes, onion, cucumbers, peppers, olives and feta cheese until mixed together
In a small bowl, whisk oil, mustard, lemon juice, salt and pepper until well combined
Toss with salad
Divide salad between 6 tortillas and roll into wraps
Place 2 decorative toothpicks into each wrap and cut diagonally in half to serve
