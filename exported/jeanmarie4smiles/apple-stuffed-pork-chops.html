Forkinit: Apple Stuffed Pork Chops

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 Tbsp butter
1/2 cup yellow onion, finey chopped
1/2 cup celery, finely chopped
1/2 cup granny smith apple, finely chopped
1/2 cup mushroom, finely chopped
1 1/2 cups herb stuffing mix
14 oz chicken broth
5 fresh sage leaves, finely chopped
6 Tbsp fresh parsley, finely chopped, DIVIDED
1 tsp salt, divided
1 tsp pepper, divided
6 pork chops
1/4 cup olive oil, divided

Instructions

Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat
Add onion and next 3 ingredients
Saute for 10 minute or until vegetables are tender and liquid evaporates
Remove from heat
Add Stuffing Mix and broth
Stir until liquid is absorbed
Let stand 20 minutes
Preheat oven to 375 degrees
Trim excess fat from each pork chop, and cut a slit in 1 side of each chop to form a pocket
Spoon stuffing mixture evenly into each pocket
Combine remaining 4 Tbsp parsley, 1/2 tsp salt, and 1/2 tsp pepper
Rub both sides of stuffed pork chops evenly with 2 Tbsp oil and spread parsley mixture evenly over chops
Cook chops in remaining 2 Tbsp hot oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat in batches
2 minutes on each side or until browned
Place on a lightly greased rack in a broiler pan
Ad1 cup water to bottom of broiler pan
Bake for 30 -40 minutes
Let stand 5 minutes before serving
