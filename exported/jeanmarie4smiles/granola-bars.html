Forkinit: Granola Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka
Sharon mills

Ingredients

3 1/2cup oatmeal
1 cup raisins
1 cup chopped
2/3 cup butter
1/3 cup honey or corn syrup or molasses
1 egg beaten1/2 tsp vanilla
½ tsp salt
½ cup brown sugar

Instructions

Toast oatmeal on ungreased pan for 15- 20 minutes at 350⁰
Combine other ingredients together and mix well
Add toasted oats
Press firmly in well greased 15x10 pan
Bake in 350⁰ oven for 20 minutes
