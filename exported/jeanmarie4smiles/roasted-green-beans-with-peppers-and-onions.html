Forkinit: Roasted Green Beans with Peppers and onions

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 pound whole frozen or fresh green beans
1 red pepper, cut into thin strips
1 yellow pepper, cut into thin chips
1 large onion, cut into thin strips
½ cup Balsamic Vinaigrette Dressing
½ cup shredded Mozzarella Cheese



Instructions

Preheat oven to 400
Toss vegetables with dressing in large roasting pan
Bake 30 minutes or until vegetables are tender, stirring after 15 minutes
Sprinkle with cheese and bake another 2-3 minutes or until cheese is melted

Yields: Serves 6
