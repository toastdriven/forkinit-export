Forkinit: Sherry’s Bran Muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup boiling water over 1 cup bran cereal
Drain if needed –cool
Add:
¾ cup honey
½ cup butter
2 beaten eggs
2 cup buttermilk
2 ½ cup flour
2 ½ tsp soda
½ tsp salt
Add to above mixture add 2 cup dry bran cereal

Instructions

Keeps 30 days in fridge
Bake 400⁰ 20 minutes
