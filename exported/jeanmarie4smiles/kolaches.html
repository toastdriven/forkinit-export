Forkinit: Kolaches

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Use sweet roll dough

Cherry: cherry pie filling
Apricot: cook dried apricots until very tender
Mash- leave some juice on them and sugar to desired sweetness
Prune filling: cook dried prunes until very tender ad a little cinnamon or nutmeg when mashing and sweeten
Cottage cheese:
1 carton cottage cheese
Press until dry
2 egg yolks
1 ¼ cup sugar
Salt to taste
Vanilla
Raisins
Blend – no cooking
poppy seed:
Grind ½ lb. poppy seed
2 cups milk
1 cup sugar
¼ tsp salt
Vanilla
Cook until thick consistency about 20-30 minutes

Instructions

Shape into balls
Let raise about 45 -60 minutes
Make depression in each and put desired filling in each
