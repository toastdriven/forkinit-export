Forkinit: Bean Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka
*see BEAN MIX

Ingredients

2 cups bean mix
11/2 tsp ginger
1 Â½ cups tomatoes
2 Tbsp molasses
1 medium onion chopped
2 carrots- sliced
1 Tbsp parsley flakes
2 ribs celery chopped
1 tsp chili powder
Ham bone or bouillon cubes

Instructions

Seasons to taste (salt, pepper, vegit, bay leaves)
Mix bean mix and ginger with enough water to cover and bring to a boil
Boil 5 minutes
Let stand one hour, drain and add fresh water and cook 2 to 3 hours
Then add remaining ingredients
*the ginger removes the gas from the beans
