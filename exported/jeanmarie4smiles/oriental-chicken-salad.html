Forkinit: Oriental Chicken Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup honey mustard dressing
4 boneless skinless chicken breasts
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp vegetable oil
½ head iceberg lettuce, shredded
½ head Napa cabbage, shredded
1 carrot, peeled and shredded
3 green onions, thinly sliced
½ cup crunchy chow mein noodles
Toasted sesame seeds
Honey Mustard Dressing:
2 Tbsp Dijon style mustard
3 Tbsp cider vinegar
3 Tbsp honey
½ cup canola or corn oil

Instructions

Make honey mustard Dressing and set aside
Season chicken with salt and pepper
Heat oil and chicken and fry about 5 minutes on each side until done
Cool 5 minutes and slice into thin strips and toss with 2 Tbsp dressing
Combine lettuce, cabbage, carrot, and remaining dressing
Top with chicken onions, noodles and sesame seeds
Dressing:
Place in blender
Slowly blend in oil
Makes one cup dressing

Yields: Serves 4
