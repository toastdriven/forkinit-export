Forkinit: Spinach Dip

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 package 8 oz cream cheese, softened
½ cup mayonnaise
¼ cup parmesan cheese
1 package (10 oz) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and squeezed dry
1 cup (4 oz) shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

In a small bowl, beat the cream cheese, mayonnaise and Parmesan cheese until blended
Stir in spinach and mozzarella cheese
Spoon into an ungreased microwave safe 9 inch pie plate
Microwave, uncovered, on high for 4-5 minutes or until bubbly, stirring twice
Serve with crackers or vegetables

Yields: Yield 8-10 servings
