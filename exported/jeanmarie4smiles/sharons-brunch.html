Forkinit: Sharon’s Brunch

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ½ cups seasoned croutons
2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
2 lb Italian sausage cooked
4 eggs
¼ tsp dry mustard
2 ½ cup milk
1 can mushroom soup
½ cup milk

Instructions

In a 9x13 inch pan layer croutons, cheese, and sausage
Beat together eggs, mustard, and milk
Pour over layers
Refrigerate overnight
Mix soup and ½ cup milk
Spread over egg
Bake at 300⁰ for 1 ½ hours

Yields: Serves 6-8
