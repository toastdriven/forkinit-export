Forkinit: Special Rhubarb Dessert

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 ½ cup rhubarb
1 3oz package Strawberry Jell-o
¾ cup sugar
1 cup water
1 plain white cake mix
1 stick oleo shortening (1/2 cup?)

Instructions

Put chopped rhubarb in a 9x13 pan chopped
Sprinkle strawberry Jell-o and sugar over rhubarb
Pour on water
Sprinkle cake mix over top
dribble shortening over cake mix
Bake at 350० for 30 minutes
