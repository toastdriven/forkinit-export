Forkinit: Egg Bake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

12 slices of bread
12 oz shredded cheddar cheese
1 package chopped broccoli-cooked
2-3 cup diced ham
6 beaten eggs
1 tsp salt
1 ¼ tsp dry mustard
3 ½ cups milk
2 Tbsp minced onion

Instructions

Remove crusts from bread, cut out shapes with donut cutter and set donut and holes aside
Line 9x13 pan with bread scraps
Layer cheese, broccoli and ham in order on bread scraps
Then arragen donuts and hole on top
MIxeggs, salt, mustard, milk, and onion
Pour over top of bread
Let stand overnight
Bake at 325⁰ for 1 ½ hours

Yields: Serves 12
