Forkinit: Fudge

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ sticks butter
3 cup sugar
5 ½ oz evaporated milk
12 oz chocolate chips
7 oz marshmallow creme
1 tsp vanilla
Nuts optional

Instructions

Melt butter, add sugar and milk, mix well
Microwave until boiling about 5 minutes
Stir in chocolate chips , marshmallow creme, vanilla, and nuts
