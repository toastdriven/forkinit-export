Forkinit: Strawberry Rhubarb crisp

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

¾ cup sugar
3 Tbsp flour
4 cups strawberries
4 cups ruhbarb
1 cup flour
¾ cup brown sugar
¾ cup butter
¾ cup rolled oats

Instructions

Mix fruit, 3 Tbsp flour, sugar in pan
Mix rest with a pastry blender
Crumble on top of fruit
Bake at 375० for 45 minutes
