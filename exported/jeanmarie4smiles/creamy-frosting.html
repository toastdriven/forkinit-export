Forkinit: Creamy Frosting

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
⅓ cup flour
1 cup milk
¾ cup softened margarine (can use butter but margarine makes fluffier frosting)
¼ tsp vanilla
2 squares of unsweetened chocolate

Instructions

Combine sugar and flour in saucepan
stir well to blend
Add milk to sugar and flour, blend well with whisk
add chocolate squares to above mixture
heat over medium heat until mixture boils for 1 minute and thickens, stirring constantly
Remove from heat
cool at room temperature for 20 minutes
Refrigerate 1 hour until thoroughly chilled
Meanwhile cream margarine in small mixer bowl until light and fluffy
add cooled chocolate mixture gradually
Beating constantly until light and fluffy
May also use this recipe for vanilla frosting or any other flavor by omitting square of chocolate and adding 1 tsp or more of desired flavoring
