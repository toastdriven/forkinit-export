Forkinit: Susan and Emily’s 24hr Fruit Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cups pitted wild or red cherries
2 cups drained pineapple chunks
2 oranges, sectioned and cut in chunks
2 cups miniature marshmallows
2 eggs
2 Tbsp sugar
2 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp butter
Dash of salt
1 cup whipping cream

Instructions

Toss together cherries, pineapple chunks, oranges, marshmallows
Blend in old fashioned dressing:
Dressing:
Beat together eggs, sugar, lemon juice, butter, and salt
Microwave on medium for 1 minute, stir
Microwave 30 seconds, stir
Repeat until cooked
Let cool
Fold in whipped cream
