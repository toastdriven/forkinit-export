Forkinit: Lemon Herb Rose Potatoes

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 lb rose potatoes
¼ tsp paprika
½ tsp dried thyme
½ tsp dried oregano
1 tsp sea salt
2 Tbsp lemon juice
¼ cup olive oil
½ tsp pepper finely ground

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 425
Wash potatoes, rinse and pat dry
Cut each potato into quarter
Combine olive oil, lemon juice, salt, oregano, thyme, rosemary, paprika, and pepper in a large bowl and mix well
Add the potatoes and toss
Arrange on an oiled baking sheet
Bake about 35 minutes
Turning every 15 minutes until tender and well browned
Taste for seasoning
Turn into a serving dish and serve immediately
