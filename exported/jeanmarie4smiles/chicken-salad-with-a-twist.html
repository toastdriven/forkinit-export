Forkinit: Chicken Salad with a twist

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

6 oz package corkscrew macaroni
3 cup cooked chicken
½ cup Italian dressing
½ cup mayo
3 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp prepared mustard
1 medium onion chopped
¾ cup olives sliced
1 cup diced cucumber
1 cup diced celery

Instructions

Salt and pepper to taste
Cook macaroni and directed on package
Mix cooked chicken and Italian dressing with hot macaroni and cool
Blend mayo, lemon juice, mustard
Stir in onions, olives, cucumbers, celery, and seasonings
Add to macaroni
Mix well
Chill 2 or more hours to blend flavor

Yields: Makes 4-8 servings
