Forkinit: Ruth’s Ginger Snaps

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup sugar
¾ cup shortening (or use half butter and half shortening)
4 Tbsp molasses
2 cup flour
2 tsp soda
½ tsp cinnamon
½ tsp salt
½ tsp cloves
½ tsp ginger
1 egg

Instructions

Beat sugar and shortening together
Add molasses and egg
Mix dry ingredients together and add to above mixture
