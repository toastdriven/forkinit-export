Forkinit: Dawn’s Raspberry Vinaigrette Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Lettuce (romaine or green leaf) and/or spinach
Sliced strawberries
Sliced sweet red peppers
Sliced sweet or green onions
Mushrooms
Mandarin oranges
Candied pecans

Instructions

Layer on a pretty platter
Pick and choose what ingredients you want
Goes with Raspberry vinaigrette dressing
