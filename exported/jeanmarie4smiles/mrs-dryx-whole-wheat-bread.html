Forkinit: Mrs. Dryx Whole wheat bread

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ¼ cup warm water
3 Tbsp. Honey
2 Tbsps. Oil (canola)
2 ½ cup whole wheat
1 cup white flour
1 Tbsp. dry milk
1 Tbsp. gluten
1 ½ tsp. salt
2 tsp yeast


5/8 cup warm water
2 Tbsp. Honey
1 Tbsps. Oil
1 ¼ cup whole wheat
½ cup white flour
1 Tbsp. dry milk
1 ½ tsp. gluten
1 ½ tsp. salt
2 tsp yeast

Instructions


Place in order in brad machine, put on dough cycle
Put in greased loaf pan
Bake at 350⁰ 40- 50 minutes

Mary Jo’s Version: add 1 cup sunflower seeds and 1 cup cracked wheat flour (use only 1 ½ cups whole wheat flour)
