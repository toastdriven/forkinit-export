Forkinit: Cleaning Emulsion

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 part linseed oil
One part of vinegar
One part turpentine
Quarter part methylated spirit
(this is alcohol you can buy at a drug store)

Instructions

Mix together ingredients
Shake vigorously each time before use
This will polish as well as clean
