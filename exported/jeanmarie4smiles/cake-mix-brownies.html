Forkinit: Cake mix Brownies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 yellow cake mix
½ cup oil
3 Tbsp water
2 eggs
2 cups miniature marshmallow
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

Mix cake mix, oil, water, eggs with fork
Add marshmallows and chocolate chips
Spread in greased cookies sheet Bake 8-15 minutes at 350⁰ until golden brown
