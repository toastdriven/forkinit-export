Forkinit: Company French Toast

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1tspcinnamon
1stick butter
1 cup brown sugar
18 slices of bread
7 eggs
11/4cup milk
Bash of salt

Instructions

Melt cinnamon, butter, and brown sugar together
Place in bottom of 9x13 in pan
Lay 2layers of bread slices on top of mixture
Mix together eggs, milk, and salt
Pour over bread
Cover and put in fridge overnight
