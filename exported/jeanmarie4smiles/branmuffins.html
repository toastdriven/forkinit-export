Forkinit: BranMuffins

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 ½ cup sugar
5 tsp soda
2 cup boiling water
4 cups milk
4 cup Crisco
2 cup Nabisco bran flakes
5 cups flour
1 tsp salt
4 beaten eggs
4 cups kellogs all bran

Instructions

Pour water over N. bran flakes
Cream sugar, Crisco, add egg and beat until fluffy
Stir buttermilk and other bran
Soda, salt, and flour
Add Nabisco and water mix
Bake at 400⁰ for 25 minutes
Can add raisins before baking
Keeps in fridge 6 weeks
