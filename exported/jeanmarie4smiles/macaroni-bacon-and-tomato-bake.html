Forkinit: Macaroni, bacon, and tomato Bake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cups elbow macaroni
½ package bacon, chopped
1 ½ cups pasta sauce
½ tsp ground black pepper
3 eggs, beaten
½ cup heavy whipping cream
½ cup milk
2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350⁰
Greasea 9x13 inch baking dish
Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil
Addpasta and cook for 8-10 minutesor until al dente; drain
In a large skillet over medium heat, cook bacon until crisp
Stir in pasta sauce, black pepper and cooked pasta
Spread mixture in the baking dish
In a medium bowl combine eggs, cream, milk and stir until smooth
Pour gently over macaronimixture in bakingdish
Sprinkle mozzarella ontop
Bake in a preheated oven 40 minute until golden brown
