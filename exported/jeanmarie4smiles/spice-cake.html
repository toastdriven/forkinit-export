Forkinit: Spice cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 cup white sugar
1 cup shortening
3 eggs (separated)
1 cup raisins boiled in 2 ½ cup water (save 1 cup raisin juice
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp allspice
1 tsp cloves
1 tsp soda
2 ½ cup flour
½ cup nuts


Instructions

Cream shortening and sugar. Add beaten eggs yolks. Sift flour and dry ingredients. Add alternately with raisin juice. Add raisins and fold in beaten egg whites. Bake 45 minutes at 350⁰

Top with Brown Sugar Frosting

4 tsp butter
6 tsp cream
¾ cup brown sugar
Boil 1 minute and add powdered sugar an vanilla
1 ¾ cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla
