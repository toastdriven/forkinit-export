Forkinit: Rocky Road Candy

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 Package Mini-Marshmallows
2 pounds hershey bars (melt very slowly)
1 pound walnuts (or peanuts)

Instructions

Melt chocolate then add nuts and marshmallows
Place chunks on wax paper and let set
Store in an airtight container
