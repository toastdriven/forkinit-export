Forkinit: How to dry fresh flowers

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Combine equal parts borax and white corn meal
Add 3 Tbsp salt for every quart

Instructions

Cover the bottom of a large cardboard or plastic container with the mixture
(Do not use a metal container)
Carefully place the flowers into the pan and gently cover with the powder
It should be deep enough to completely bury the flowers
Store uncovered container in a warm, dry spot where it wonâ€™t be disturbed during the drying period
Leave them for about 10 days, then test for dryness
To do this, insert your finger into the mix and feel to see if the petals are stiff
If not ,leave for a few more das
When you remove the flowers, shake them gently
This will remove any mixture still clinging to them
If traces are still left, try using a small artist paintbrush or makeup brush
For the best results, use the freshest flowers you can find
