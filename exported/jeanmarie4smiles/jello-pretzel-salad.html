Forkinit: Jello Pretzel Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup crushed pretzels
4 ½ Tbsp sugar
¾ cup butter-milted
1 cup sugar
2 8oz package cream cheese
1 8 oz package cool whip1 6 oz package rasp or strawberry jello
2 cup boiling water
1 16oz package frozen raspberries or strawberries

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350
Mix together pretzels sugar and butter
Press into bottom of a 9x13 inch pan
Bake 10 minutes
Set aside and cool completely
Beat sugar and cream cheese until smooth
Fold in whipped topping
Spread evenly over cooled crust
Refrigerate until set about 30 minutes
Stir together jello and boiling water
Mix in frozen fruit, stir until thawed
Pour over cream cheese mixture in pan
Refrigerate until completely chilled about 1 hour
