Forkinit: Fresh Pack or quick Dill Pickles

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Pickling cucumbers 2 ½ to 3 inches in length
6 cups water
2 cups vinegar
1/3 cup canning salt
Dill heads, washed
Onion slices ½ in chick
Garlic cloves
Carrot slices (optional for added color)

Instructions

Wash and scrub the cucumbers carefully
Place 1-2 garlic cloves, slice of onion, carrot, and head of dill into the bottom of a quart canning jar
Put the cucumber into clean, hot jars
Pour brine over
Place lids on jar and hot water boil and process for 10 minutes
