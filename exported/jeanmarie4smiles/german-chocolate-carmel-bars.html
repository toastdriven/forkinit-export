Forkinit: German Chocolate Carmel Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka
From Pat

Ingredients

1 package German chocolate cake mix
¾ cup melted butter
1/3 cup milk
2 cups chocolate chips
50 caramels
1/3 cup milk


Instructions

Combine cake mix, butter, and milk
Spread ½ chocolate mix into greased 9x13 pan
Bake at 350⁰ for 6 minutes
Sprinkle chocolate chips over top
Melt caramels and milk in a saucepan on low
Drizzle the caramel over the chips
Top with the rest of the chocolate mix
Bake at 15-18 minutes 350⁰
