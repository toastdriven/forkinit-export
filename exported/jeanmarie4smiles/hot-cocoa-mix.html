Forkinit: Hot Cocoa Mix

By Mary Jo Kurka
Use 2 Tbsp mix to 1 cup hot water

Ingredients

12 cups Carnation Dry Milk
6 oz none-dairy creamer
2 lb box Nestle Quick
1 tsp salt
1 bag miniature marshmallows

Instructions

Mix well
Put in dry seated container
