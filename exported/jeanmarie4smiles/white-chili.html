Forkinit: White chili

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 Â½ cup water
1 tsp lemon pepper
1 tsp cumin seed
4 chicken breasts
1 tsp live oil
1 cup chopped onion
1 clove minced garlic
2 9oz packages frozen corn
2 4 oz cans chopped green chilies
1 tsp ground cumin
2-3 tsp lime juice
2 15 oz cans great northern beans
Serve with crushed tortilla chips and shredded Monterey Jack cheese

Instructions

Mix water, lemon pepper, and cumin seed in large kettle and heat to a boil
Kettle and heat to boiling
Add chicken breast, cook on low, cover and simmer until chicken is cooked
Remove chicken and cut into bite size pieces
Return to pan
Heat olive oil in skillet and cook onion and garlic for 1-2 minutes then add chicken mix
Stir in green chilies, ground cumin, lime juice and heat to a boil
Add beans and heat through
To serve place crushed tortilla chips and some shredded cheese in bowl and ladle chili over
