Forkinit: Salisbury Steak

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb hamburger
1 egg
2 Tbsp chopped onion
1 Tbsp butter
1 tsp salt
1/2 tsp pepper
2 Tbsp parsley
1/2 cup crushed cornflakes
2 Tbsp shortening

Instructions

Shape into patties and dredge in cornflakes
