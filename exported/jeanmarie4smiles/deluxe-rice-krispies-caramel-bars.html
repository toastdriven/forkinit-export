Forkinit: Deluxe Rice Krispies Caramel Bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

12 cups rice krispies
1/2 cup butter
10 ½ oz bag marshmallows
2 cups mini marshmallows
Caramel mix
1 lb caramels
1 ½ cup butter
1 can evaporated milk

Instructions

Mix rice krispies, butter, and marshmallows and pat half into jelly roll pan
Melt caramel mix in double boiler
Sprinkle 2 cups mini marshmallows over top of rice Krispies mix
Then pour caramel mix over marshmallows
Pour rest of rice krispies mix over caramel mix
Cut in bars and refrigerate
