Forkinit: Special K bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup Karo syrup
1 cup sugar
2 cups peanut butter
6 cups special K cereal

Instructions

Stir Karo and sugar on stove until bubbling
Add peanut butter stir until smooth
add cereal
spread out in a 9x14 pan
can melt chocolate chips for topping
