Forkinit: Cherry/ Blueberry Crisp

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


1 ½ cup brown sugar
2 cup oatmeal
1 cup flour
1 cup butter
1 can cherry pie filling
1 can blueberry pie filling

Instructions

Combine sugar, flour, oatmeal, cut in butter and mix until crumbly
Press half of mixture in a greased 9x13 pan
Pour cherry filling on one side and blueberry on other side
Spread so they share the pan equally
Top with remaining crumbs
Bake at 350⁰ for 40-45 minutes
Serve warm with ice cream
May use any combo of pie fillings or 2 cans of the same filling
