Forkinit: Apple Crisp

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 cup sliced apples
2/3 -3/4 cup brown sugar
½ cup flour
¾ tsp cinnamon
¾ tsp nutmeg
1/3 cup butter

Instructions

Grease 9x9 pan
Put in apples
Mix remaining ingredients and sprinkle over apples
Bake at 375⁰ for 30 minutes
