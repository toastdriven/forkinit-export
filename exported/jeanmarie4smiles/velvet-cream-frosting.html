Forkinit: Velvet Cream Frosting

By Mary Jo Kurka
makes enough frosting to make one round 9inch two layer cake

Ingredients

1 package (15.4or 14.3 oz) creamy white, chocolate fudge, dark chocolate fudge, or lemon frosting mix
1 ½ cups whipping cream
1 tsp vanilla
OR
(1 ½ cup powdered sugar, ½ cup cocoa, 1 tsp vanilla)
2-3 Tbsp hot water
1Tbsp light corn syrup

Instructions


Combine whipping cream, vanilla, and 2 cups frosting mix (or powdered sugar, cocoa, and vanilla instead of frosting mix) in a small bowl
Cover and chill one hour
Reserve remaining frosting mix
Blend chilled frosting mix and cream
Beat on medium speed until stiff
Spread between cake layers
For Glaze, beat remaining dry frosting mix (or ¼ cup cocoa, 1 cup powdered sugar)
Water and corn syrup until smooth
If necessary, add 1-2tsps more water until glaze is of proper consistency
Frost top of cake with glaze allowing some to drizzle down sides
Sprinkle top of cake with chopped nuts
Chill before serving
Refrigerate any leftover cake
