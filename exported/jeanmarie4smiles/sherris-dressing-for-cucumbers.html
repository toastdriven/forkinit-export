Forkinit: Sherri’s Dressing for Cucumbers

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

½ cup mayo
½ cup sour cream
¼ cup chopped onion
1/8 cup sugar
Salt
1 tsp dill weed

Instructions

Slice cucumbers then soak in cold salty water for 1 hour
Add dressing
Chill overnight
