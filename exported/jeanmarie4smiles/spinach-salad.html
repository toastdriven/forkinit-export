Forkinit: Spinach Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ lbs fresh spinach
8-9sliced fresh mushrooms
½ lb chopped bacon
1 11oz can mandarin oranges, drained
Dressing:
¼ cup vinegar
½ cup olive oil
¼ cup sugar
½ tsp salt
½ tsp paprika
¼ dry mustard
1/8 tsp pepper
¼ tsp celery salt
½ med onion, chopped


Instructions

Chill all ingredients
Tear spinach into bite-sized pieces
Mix spinach, mushrooms, bacon, and oranges together in a large bowl
Just before serving, toss with dressing
Top with some of the bacon if desired
Mix dressing ingredients and shake
Pour over salad

Yields: Serves 8
