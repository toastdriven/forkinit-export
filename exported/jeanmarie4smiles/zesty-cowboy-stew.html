Forkinit: Zesty Cowboy Stew

By Mary Jo Kurka
Crock pot recipe!!!

Ingredients

1 lb ground beef, browned and drained
1 8oz can tomato sauce
1 cup water
Â¾ -1 cup barbecue sauce
1-2 medium tomatoes, diced
8 oz frozen green beans, thawed
1 can frozen corn, thawed
2 medium potatoes, chunked
2 medium carrots, sliced
1 Tbsp dried onion flakes
Garlic powder to taste
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Place ground beef in crock pot
Add tomato sauce, water, tomatoes, BBQ sauce, carrots, green beans, corn and potatoes
Add minced onion
Shake some garlic powder and adobo over the contents
Add salt and pepper to taste
Cook on low for 8-10 hours
