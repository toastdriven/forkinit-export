Forkinit: Hash Brown and Egg Bake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

5 slices bacon
1 ½ tsp olive oil
½ medium red bell pepper sliced
¼ medium red onion sliced
2 cups sliced mushrooms
1 can diced tomatoes drained
1 package (10oz) frozen chopped spinach –thawed
1 tsp fresh chopped Tarragon
1 tsp Salt
½ tsp black peppercorn
10 large eggs
2 cups milk
1 bag (1 lb 4 oz) refrigerated shredded hash browns
4 oz feta cheese crumbled
2 green onions sliced

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325⁰
In a large pan cook bacon over medium heat 8-10 minutes or until crisp drain on paper towels
In separate pan heat oil over medium heat
Add mushrooms and cook 6 minutes and remove from heat
Stir in tomatoes, spinach, tarragon, ¼ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper
In a large bowl whisk together eggs and milk
Generously spray a 9x13 inch pan with cooking spray
In a medium bowl toss hash browns with remaining ¾ tsp salt and remaining pepper
Press hash browns onto bottom and side of baking dish
Evenly spread pepper-onion mixture over hash browns

Yields: Serves 12
