Forkinit: Wild Rice Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka
Kurka’s Favorite

Ingredients

2/3 cup rinsed uncooked wild rice
1 medium onion- chopped
1 rib of celery –diced
3 thinly sliced carrots
1 ½ Tbsp butter
4 cups chicken broth
2 ½ cup water
1 cup corn
1 ½ Tbsp cornstarch diluted in cold water
1 cup half and half
Salt and pepper to taste
1 Tbsp Worcestershire sauce

Instructions

In a large pot sauté wild rice, onion, celery and carrots in butter for about 3 minutes
Stir in chicken broth and water
Heat to boiling, stirring regularly, then reduce heat
Add corn, simmer covered for 40 minutes
Stir cornstarch into soup
Cook several minutes
Removed from heat and stir in half and half
Season with salt and pepper and Worcestershire sauce

For a meaty flavor- cook a whole chicken and use the broth with the fat skimmed off
Dice some of the chicken and add it to the soup
