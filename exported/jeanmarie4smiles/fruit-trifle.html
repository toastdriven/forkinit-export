Forkinit: Fruit Trifle

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 (12oz) whipped topping, thawed
1 (8oz) container sour cream
1 angel food cake
1 package instant vanilla pudding mix
3 kiwis, peeled and sliced
1 pint fresh strawberries, sliced
1 (15oz) can crushed pineapple, drained
1 sprig fresh mint

Instructions

In a medium bowl, fold sour cream and unprepared pudding mix into the whipped topping
Cut cake into thirds, horizontally
Line a large trifle or other glass serving bowl with kiwi and strawberry slices, reserving two whole strawberries and two kiwi slices
Place one layer of cake on bottom of bowl
Top with ⅓ bananas and pineapple and ⅓ of whipped topping mix
Repeat layering until all ingredients are used
Make fan garnishes of wholestrawberris by slicing from just below the stem
Garnis assembled trifle with fanned strawberries, reserved kiwi slices and a sprig of mint
Refrigerate until serving
