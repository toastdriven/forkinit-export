Forkinit: Bean ‘n Bacon Soup

By Mary Jo Kurka
See BEAN MIX

Ingredients

2 cups dry bean mix
1 package (8oz) bacon, cut into ½ inch pieces
1 large onion- chopped
1 bay leaf
½ tsp salt
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp pepper
1 can (14 ½ oz) taco sauce
1 package (12 oz) smokie links or 1 pound ham

Instructions


Rinse beans, sorting out imperfects. Combine beans and water in large saucepot or Dutch oven.Cover and bring to a boil. Boil 2 minutes. Remove from heat, let stand 1 hour. Add bacon onion, bay leaf, salt, garlic powder, and pepper. Bring to a boil. Turn down heat, cover, and simmer covered 1 ½ -2 hours or until beans are tender, stirring occasionally. Add remaining ingredients. Simmer 15 minutes more.

Yields: Makes 10 cups (6-8 servings)
