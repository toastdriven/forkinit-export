Forkinit: Shirley’s Zucchini Hotdish

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Shred 8 cups zucchini
1 cups carrots
1 onion chopped
1 cup sour cream
1 can cream of chicken soup
7 oz dried croutons
1 ½ cup liquid from veggies
Sage and poultry seasoning
1 ½ lb hamburger

Instructions

Cook vegetables together until transparent
Drain and and save liquid
Add sour cream and chicken soup to the vegetables
Mix together croutons, spices, and liquid
Brown hamburger
Layer in a 9x13 greased pan
1st hamburger
2nd crouton mixture
3 rd vegetable mixture
Pour ½ cup melted butter over top
Bake at 350⁰ for 40-45 minutes or until bubbly.
