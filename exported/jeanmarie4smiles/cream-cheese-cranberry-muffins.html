Forkinit: Cream Cheese Cranberry Muffins

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup butter, softened
1 package (8 oz) cream cheese, softened
1 ½ cups sugar
1 ½ tsp vanilla extract
4 eggs
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
½ teaspoon salt
2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries
½ cup chopped pecans

Instructions

In large mixing bowl, cream butter, cream cheeses, sugar and vanilla.
Add eggs, one at a time beating well after each addition
Combine the flour baking powder and salt
Stir into creamed mixture just until moistened
Fold in cranberries and pecans
Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups ¾ full
Bake at 350⁰ for 20-25 minutes or until a tooth pick come out clean
Cool for 5 minutes before refrigerating
