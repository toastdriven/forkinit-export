Forkinit: Dawn’s ‘quick’ corn freezing method

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2o cups corn
½ cup sugar
1 Tbsp salt

Instructions

Cut uncooked corn off the cob
Add remaining ingredients
Stir together and let set together for 20 minutes
Then freeze as normal
