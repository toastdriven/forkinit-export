Forkinit: Sara’s Mint Cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

¾ cup butter
1 ½ cup brown sugar
2 Tbsp water
2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips
2 eggs
2 ½ cups flour
1 ¼ tsp baking soda
½ salt
3 (4.5 oz) packages Andes Mints

Instructions

Combine butter, water and sugar in a saucepan
cook on medium heat until melted
Removed from heat and stir in chocolate chips until melted
set aside to cool for 10 minutes
Pour chocolate mixture into a large bowl and beat in eggs one at a time
Combine dry ingredients then add to chocolate mixture
cover and refrigerate for 1 hour
Form dough into walnut size balls and put on greased cookie sheet
Bake at 350० for 8-10 minutes
will be puffy, wait a minute until deflates then put a mint on top
