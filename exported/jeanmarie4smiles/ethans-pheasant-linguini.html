Forkinit: Ethan’s Pheasant Linguini

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Debone Pheasant using breast and thighs cut into cubes
1 large onion
2 garlic cloves
¼ cup butter
1 Jar Ragu Parmesan Mozzarella
½ box of linguine noodles

Instructions

Fry onion and garlic in butter
Add pheasant and fry until cooked
Pepper to tastes
Add Sauce and linguine noodles
Bake 1 hour at 345
May use Cheesy Alfredo instead of Red sauce
Add sour cream to make creamier

Yields: Serves 6
