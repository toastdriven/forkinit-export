Forkinit: Naomi's bread

By Mary Jo Kurka
for a bread machine

Ingredients

1 cup water
¼ cup honey
3 Tbsp oil
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup unbleached white flour
1 cup oatmeal
2 Tbsp soyflour (rounded)
1 Tbsp gluten (optional)
1 ½ - 2 tsp salt
2 tsp yeast (rounded)
Add after first kneading:
2 Tbsp flax seeds (grind)
2 tbsp sunflower seeds

Instructions

Add together according to ingredient list
