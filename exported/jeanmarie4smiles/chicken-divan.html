Forkinit: Chicken Divan

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 package frozen broccoli (or fresh cooked)
2 cup cooked chicken
2 cans cream of chicken soup
1 cp real mayo
1 tsp lemon juice
¼ to ½ tsp curry powder
½ cup shredded cheese
1 can French Fried onion rings

Instructions

Heat soup, mayo, lemon juice, and curry powder together then set aside
Place cooked broccoli in bottom of greased 9x13 inch pan, top with chicken, then heated sauce
Sprinkle with cheese
Bake 50 minutes at 350◦
Last 10 minutes add onion rings
Serve over brown rice
