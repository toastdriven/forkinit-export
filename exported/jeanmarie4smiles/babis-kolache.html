Forkinit: Babi’s Kolache

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ cup sugar - or less
1 stick butter
1 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs unbeaten
½ cup milk
2 cups fresh fruit

Instructions

Mix sugar, butter, and flour until crumbly. Then take ½ cup out and set aside
To the rest ass baking powder, eggs, and milk
Mix well and put in a greased 9x13 pan
Put fruit of your choice and sprinkle with ½ cup mixture you had saved out
Bake at 350० for 40 minutes
