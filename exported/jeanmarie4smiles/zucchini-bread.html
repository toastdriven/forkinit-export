Forkinit: Zucchini Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

3 eggs beaten
1 cup oil
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup sugar
3 tsp maple flavoring
2 cup raw zucchini (grated)
2 ½ cup flour (possibility 1 cup white 1 ½ cup wheat)
½ cup wheat germ
2 tsp soda
½ tsp salt
1 cup walnuts (chopped)

Instructions

Mix in order given. Bake in 350⁰ oven for 1 hour
For 3 loaf pans, bake about 40 minutes
Optional: sprinkle 1/3 cup sesame seeds on top of batter before baking
