Forkinit: Cashew Chicken

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 cup raw cashews or almonds
2 tsp oil
1 small onion
2 cloves garlic
4 chicken breast cubed
1 ½ cup broccoli flower
1 ½ cup sno-peas
2 Tbsp soy sauce
2 tsp cornstarch
¼ tsp salt
¼ tsp pepper
1 cup chicken broth

Instructions

Lightly brown nuts in oil in non -stick skillet over medium heat for 3 minutes
Stir constantly
Remove nuts and put onion and garlic in same skillet for 2 minutes
Add chicken and saute until brown about 8 minutes
Add broccoli and peas, ¾ cup chicken broth, and soy sauce
Simmer covered for 5 minutes
Dissolve cornstarch in remaining chicken broth
Stir into skillet, simmer, stirring over medium heat until thickened
Boil 1 minutes
Return cashews to skillet with salt and pepper

Yields: Serves 4-6
