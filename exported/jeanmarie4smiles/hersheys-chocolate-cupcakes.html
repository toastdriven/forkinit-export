Forkinit: Hershey’s Chocolate cupcakes

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

60 milk chocolate kisses
¾ cup butter softened
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 cups flour
1 2/3 cups sugar
3 eggs
2/3 cup cocoa
1 ¼ tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
¼ tsp baking powder
1 1/3 cups water

Instructions

Remove wrappers from chocolate pieces, set aside
Heat oven to 350⁰
Line muffin cups with paper baking cups
Mix butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla in large bowl
Beat on high speed 3 minutes
Mix together flour, cocoa, baking soda, salt and baking powder
Add alternately with water to butter mixture
Beating just until blended
Fill prepared muffin cups about ½ cup full with batter
Place chocolate piece in center of each
Bake 20 minutes or until top springs back when touched lightly in center
Move from cups to wire rack
Cool completely
Frost as desired
Place another chocolate piece on top of each

Yields: 30 cupcakes
