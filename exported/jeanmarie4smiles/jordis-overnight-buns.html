Forkinit: Jordi's overnight buns

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients


Instructions

5pm
Mix: 1 package dry yeast in 1/3 cup water and 1 Tbsp sugar – cover and let activate
Mix ½ cup oil and 3 cups lukewarm water
1 cup sugar, 1 Tbsp salt, add yeast, 2 beaten eggs, 10-11 cup unbleached flour- mix well and cover (for 2 hours)
Stir again at 7pm and at 9 pm (stir after 2 hours stir again and let rise one more hour)
At 10pm grease pan
Drop by large Tbsp and cover overnight.
Bake in morning 350⁰ 25 minutes

Yields: 6 dozen
