Forkinit: Rolls -sweet dough

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Scald ½ cup milk
Stir in ½ cups sugar
1 ½ tsp salt
¼ cup shortening
Cool to lukewarm
Measure into bowl ½ cup warm water,
Sprinkle or crumble in 2 package dry yeast
Stir until dissolved
Stir in with lukewarm milk mixture
Add 2 beaten eggs (room temperature)
3 cups flour

Instructions

Beat until smooth
Stir in additional 2 cups flour (about)
Turn dough on lightly floured board
Knead until smooth (about 10 minutes)
Place in greased bowl and brush on shortening, cover
Let rise in warm place until doubled (about 1 hour)
Form into rolls
Let rise until doubled
Brush with melted butter
Bake 375⁰ about 10 minutes
