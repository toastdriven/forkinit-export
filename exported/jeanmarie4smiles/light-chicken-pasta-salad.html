Forkinit: Light Chicken Pasta Salad

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

8 oz whole wheat pasta (bow tie or rotini)
2 cups chicken
2 cups halved red grapes
1/3 cup finely chopped sweet or red onion
1 cup chopped celery
½ cup toasted slivered almonds
2 Tbsp fresh parsley
3 Tbsp chives
Dressing:
1/3 cup orange juice
1 juiced lemon (juice only)
2 Tbsp oil
2 tsp honey
1 tsp salt
1 Tbsp rice vinegar
½ tsp pepper
1 Tbsp stone ground mustard

Instructions

Cook pasta according to package directions
Chop parsley and chives
Cook chicken and dice
Add remaining ingredients
Top with dressing
Keep refrigerated
