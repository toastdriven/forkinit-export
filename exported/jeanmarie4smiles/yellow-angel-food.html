Forkinit: Yellow Angel Food

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Cake
6 egg yolks (separated) 6 egg whites
½ cup cold water
1 ½ cup sugar
½ tsp vanilla
½ tsp orange or lemon extracts
1/3 tsp almond extract
1 ½ cup flour
¼ tsp salt
¾ tsp cream of tarter

Instructions

Beat egg yolks until lemon colored. Add cold water, continue beating until thick. Add sugar gradually. Add flavorings. Fold in flour a little at a time. Beat egg whites until foamy and the cream of tartar and beat until it holds peaks fold into egg yolk mixture. Put in angel food pan and bake at 325⁰ for 1 hour
