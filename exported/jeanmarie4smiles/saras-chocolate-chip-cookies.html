Forkinit: Sara’s chocolate chip cookies

By Mary Jo Kurka
Sara Kurka

Ingredients

⅔ cup crisco
⅔ cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 cup brown sugar
2 eggs 2 tsp vanilla
3 cups flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 cup nuts (optional)
12 oz bag chocolate chips

Instructions

Mix together everything except butter
Add hot melted butter after everything is mixed
Bake at 375० for 8-10 minutes
