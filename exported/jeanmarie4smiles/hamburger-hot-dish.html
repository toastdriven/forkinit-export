Forkinit: Hamburger Hot dish

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 lb hamburger browned
½ cup chopped onions browned
1 lb carrots sliced
2 medium potatoes peeled and diced
2 cans mushroom soup or cream of chicken
½ tsp salt
¼ or 1/8 tsp pepper

Instructions

Mix up and bake at 350◦ for 1 hour in covered casserole dish
