Forkinit: Indian Fry Bread

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

4 cup flour
1 ¼ cup water
2 ¼ tsp baking powder
Dash salt
Vegetable oil or shortening (canola oil)

Instructions

Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and 1 cup water (add more as needed)
Mix well
Knead 1 minute
Cover bowl and let rise 15-20 minutes
Then pat or roll out to ½ inch thickness.
Cut in wedges and cut 1 inch slices in middle of each wedge.
Heat oil 1 ½ inch deep in large skillet
Fry wedges until golden brown
Serve with butter, honey, jelly or cool in sugar

Yields: 25-30 pieces
