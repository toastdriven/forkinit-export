Forkinit: Enchilada sauce

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

2 Tbsp oil
1 medium onion, chopped
3 ½ cups tomato sauce
2 cloves garlic, minced
1-2 Tbsp chili powder
½ tsp oregano
1 tsp salt

Instructions

Saute oil and onion in a saucepan
When onion is just yellow add remaining ingredients
Simmer and cover about 30 minutes
Stirring frequently
