Forkinit: Hearty Casserole

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

8 cups uncooked potatoes, cubed
1 lb smoked sausage, sliced ¼ inch
1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can vegetable beef soup
1 tsp garlic salt
1 package (8 oz) cheddar cheese, shredded
Dijon mustard (optional)

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine all ingredients except cheese and Dijon mustard; mix well
Pour mixture into a greased 9x13 inch dish
Bake at 350⁰ for 11/2 hours
Sprinkle cheese over casserole the last 15 minutes of cooking time
Use Dijon mustard as a dipping sauce for sausage pieces
