Forkinit: Garden Style Turkey Meatloaf

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

1 ½ lb lean ground turkey
1 10 oz package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained or
½ cup finely chopped green or red bell pepper
1 cup oatmeal
½ cup finely chopped onion
½ cup shredded carrots
1 egg slightly beaten
⅓ cup milk
1 ½ tsp Italian seasoning
½ tsp salt and ¼ tsp pepper

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in large bowl, mix well
Press meat ingredients evenly across bottom of 8x4 loaf pan
Bake at 350 for 55-60 minutes
Drain, let stand 5 minutes before slicing
