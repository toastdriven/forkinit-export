Forkinit: Robert Redford Cake

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Chocolate cake baked in 9x13
jar caramel topping
1 can condensed milk
cool whip
3 candy bars

Instructions

Bake a chocolate cake like directions say
when done, poke holes over entire cake with fork
Pour 1 jar caramel ice cream topping over warm cake
Pour 1 can sweet condensed milk over warm cake
when cool, top 1 cool whip and 3 skior candy bars crushed
