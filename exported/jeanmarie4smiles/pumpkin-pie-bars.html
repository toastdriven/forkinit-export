Forkinit: Pumpkin pie bars

By Mary Jo Kurka

Ingredients

Pumpkin Pie Squares
Crust:
1 cup flour
½ cup oatmeal
½ cup brown sugar
½ cu butter
Topping:
½ cup brown sugar
½ cup chopped nuts
2 Tbsp butter
Fillin:
2 cans pumpkin (15 oz ea)
2 cans evaporated milk (1 oz ea)
4 eggs
1 ½ cups sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp gine
½ tsp cloves
1 tsp salt

Instructions

Combine crust ingredients until crumbly,
Press into greased 9x13 pan
Bake at 350 ० for 20 minutes or until golden brown
Meanwhile beat filling ingredients in mixing bowl until smooth
Pour over crust
Bake for 45 minutes
Combine topping ingredients and sprinkle over top
Bake 15-20 minutes longer or until knife inserted near center comes out clean
cool
store in fridge
