Forkinit: Pizza Muffins

By Heather
These are mini-pizzas cooked in a muffin pan. It makes a great personal very deep dish pizza. I've only done this once, but it was a lot of fun. The leftovers are super portable (can anyone say lunchboxable ?) This would be great of a kids "make your own pizza" party so everyone could have their own toppings...

Ingredients

Pizza Dough (I used my Whole Wheat Pizza Dough http://forkinit.com/heather/recipes/whole-wheat-pizza-dough-2/ )
Tomato Sauce
Cheese
Your favorite pizza toppings

Instructions

Make your favorite pizza dough.
Instead of shaping it into standard pizzas, grease a muffin pan. Make sure you grease the top (flat part) of the pan as well as the muffin cup part.
Preheat oven to 450 (or if your muffin pan can do hotter, go as hot as you can).
Divide the pizza dough into small pieces, one for each "muffin" you want to make. My standard honey whole wheat dough makes more than 12 of these muffins.
Shape dough into a Thin flat circle. (the dough will rise in the muffin pans)
Put dough circles into the muffin pan. Push the centers in.
Fill with sauce, cheese, etc. Whatever you want. If you are using a topping that needs to be fully cooked (i.e. chicken/ ground beef, etc) make sure it's cooked before adding it. There isn't a lot of cook time.
Bake until the crust is a nice golden brown and the cheese is melted. It took about 10 minutes for me.

Yields: 15-18 mini pizzas
