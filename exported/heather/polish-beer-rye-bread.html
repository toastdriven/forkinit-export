Forkinit: Polish Beer Rye Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup flat, room temp beer ( 8oz)
2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
2 tbsp honey
1 tbsp butter, melted
3/4 tsp salt
2 cup flour
3/4 cup light or medium rye flour
1/4 cup whole wheat flour
3 tsp yellow cornmeal
1 tsp minced raw shallot
2 tsp caraway seeds
2 tbl gluten
3 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put all ingredients in bread machine (in the order your machine manufacture recommends) and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
