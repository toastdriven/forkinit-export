Forkinit: Cornmeal Skillet Rolls

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup boiling water
1/2 cup cornmeal
1/4 cup mild-flavored molasses
3 tbsp butter
1/2 tsp salt
1 egg
1/4 cup warm water (105 to 115 deg )
1 package active dry yeast (2 1/2 tsp)
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions

Place boiling water in small bowl. Slowly add cornmeal, stirring constantly, until well mixed.
Add molasses, 2 tbsp butter and salt. Stir until butter is melted. Add egg; mix well. Set aside.
Combine warm water and yeast in another small bowl. Stir until yeast is dissolved.
Place flour in large bowl. Add cornmeal and yeast mixtures; stir until soft dough forms. Turn dough onto well-floured surface. Knead with well-floured hands until ball forms (1 to 2 min). (Dough will be sticky.) Cut dough into 16 pieces with sharp knife.
Shape each piece of dough into ball with well-floured hands. Place balls into greased 10 in cast iron skillet or 9 in round baking pan. Cover; let rise in warm place until double in size (30 to 45 min)
Heat oven to 375 deg
Bake for 30 to 35 min or until golden brown and rolls sound hollow when tapped. Remove from pan; cool on wire rack. Brush with 1 tbsp melted butter.

Yields: 16 rolls
