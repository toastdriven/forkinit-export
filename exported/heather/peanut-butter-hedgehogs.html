Forkinit: Peanut Butter Hedgehogs

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
2/3 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1/3 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup mini chocolate chips

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 deg.
Combine flour, baking powder and salt in medium bowl. Set aside.
Place butter in large mixer bowl. Beat at medium speed, scraping bowl often, until creamy (1 to 2 min). Add peanut butter, brown sugar and sugar; continue beating until well mixed.
Add 1 egg and vanilla; continue beating until well mixed.
Reduce speed to low, add flour mixture. Beat until well mixed (1 to 2 min).
Beat in remaining egg in small bowl. Place chocolate chips in another bowl.
Shape dough into 1 in balls. Picking up ball between thumb and index finger, gently shape the nose of hedgehog. Dip back of hedgehog into egg mixture and then into chips. Place 2 inches apart onto greased cookie sheet. Place 2 chips on each cookie to form eyes.
Bake 11 to 14 min or until cookies are just set.

Yields: 3 doz
