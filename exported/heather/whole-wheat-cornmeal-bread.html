Forkinit: Whole Wheat Cornmeal Bread

By Heather
This is a whole wheat variant of the Indian Grahm bread

Ingredients

1 ¼ c water
2 tbsp butter, in pieces
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp sugar
1 ½ c bread flour
1 c whole wheat
½ c yellow cornmeal
1 tbsp gluten
2 ½ tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in the bread machine and run on wheat cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
