Forkinit: Italian Chicken

By Heather
This is a family favorite. If you marinade it ahead of time, it's a really quick meal.

Ingredients

1 package chicken breasts
olive oil, enough to cover the chicken
1 squirt lemon juice
Basil, to taste
garlic powder, to taste
Onion powder, to taste,
Italian seasoning, to taste
rosemary, to taste
thyme, to taste
oregano, to taste

Instructions

Cut chicken up into small cubes
Put some olive oil, lemon juice and herbs into a plastic bag.
Add the chicken to the bag. Add more olive oil, if needed, to coat the chicken.
Let marinade for at least 1 hour up to over night.
Heat large skillet over medium high heat.
Add chicken and marinated mix. Let sit for 2 to 3 min. Stir. Cook stirring for 5 mim.
Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook until chicken is done.

Yields: 4
