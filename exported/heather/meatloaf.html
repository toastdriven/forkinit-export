Forkinit: Meatloaf

By Heather

Ingredients

1.5 lb ground beef
3 cups oats
1 cap Browning Sauce (optional)
1 handful Italian Seasoning
Parsley, to taste
Olive Oil
1 package Brown gravy mix (optional)
1 package Onion Soup mix (or minced onions)
Garlic powder, to taste
Onion powder, to taste
1 egg
1 can tomato soup

Instructions

Pre heat oven to 350 def %
Combine all ingredients. (A stand mixer works great for this)
Put into greased loaf pans.
Bake at 350 for 1 hour
Put soup on top and bake for 15 to 20 min.

Yields: 5
