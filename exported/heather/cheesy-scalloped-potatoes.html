Forkinit: Cheesy Scalloped Potatoes

By Heather
I haven't tired this yet, but anything that combines cheese and potatoes can't be that bad, right ?

Ingredients

1/2 cup chopped onion
1 clove garlic, minced
2 tbsp all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper
1 1/2 cups milk
4 medium potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese (2 oz)
fresh parsley (optional)
nonstick spray

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 deg.
For sauce, spray an unheated small saucepan with nonstick coating. Preheat over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic. Cook and stir until the onion is tender.
In a small bowl combine flour, salt, and pepper. Gradually stir in milk until smooth. Add to saucepan. Cook and stir over medium heat until thickened and bubbly.
Spray a 1 1/2 quart casserole with nonstick spray. Place half the potatoes in the casserole. Top with half the sauce. Repeat.
Bake, covered for 40 min. Uncover and bake about 30 minutes more or until potatoes are tender. Remove from oven and sprinkle with cheese.
Let stand for 10 min before serving. If desired, garnish with parsley.

Yields: 8
Source: "New Dieter's cookbook" by Better Homes and Gardens
