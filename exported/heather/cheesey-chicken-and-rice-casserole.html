Forkinit: Cheesey Chicken and Rice Casserole

By Heather

Ingredients

1 can Cream of Chicken (Or Potato or any cream soup) 10 3/4 oz
1 1/3 cup water
3.4 cup uncooked long grain rice (works with white and brown rice)
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp group black pepper (optional)
2 cup forzed vegetables
4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (about 1 lb)
1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese or mozzarella cheese
garlic powder, to taste (optional)
Italian seasoning, to taste (optional)

Instructions

Heat oven to 375 deg F
Stir the soup, water, rice, seasonings, and veggies in a shallow 2 qt baking dish
Top with chicken.
Bake Covered for 50 min (or 1 h 20 min if frozen chicken) or until done.
Top with Cheese and let stand for 10 min

Yields: 4
