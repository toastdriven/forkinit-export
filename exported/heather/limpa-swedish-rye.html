Forkinit: Limpa (Swedish Rye)

By Heather

Ingredients

3/4 cup water
1/3 cup milk
1 tbsp molasses
3 tbsp butte,r in pieces
3/4 tsp salt
1 3/4 cup flour
1 1/4 cup medium rye flour
1 1/2 tbsp brown sugar
1/2 tsp crushed fennel seeds
1/2 tsp crushed aniseed
1 1/2 tsp grated orange zest
1 tbsp gluten
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
