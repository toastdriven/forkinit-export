Forkinit: Butter Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

7/8 cup water
1 egg
5 tbsp butter, in pieces
3/4 tsp slat
3 cups flour
1 tbsp gluten
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the dough cycle. Take out and shape into 2 loves and run on bake. OR just run it on the while cycle

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
