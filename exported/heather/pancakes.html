Forkinit: Pancakes

By Heather

Ingredients

3 eggs
2 cups flour
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 tbl cinnamon
1 tbl brown sugar
2 tsp baking powder
2 tbl sugar
2 cups milk
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbl veggie oil

Instructions

1. Beat the eggs until fluffy
2. Mix in all other ingredients. Beat until smooth
3. Cook in a skillet over medium heat

Yields: 24
