Forkinit: 100% Whole Wheat Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

3/4 cup water
3/4 cup milk
2 tbsp canola oil
1/4 cup light molasses
1 tsp salt
4 cups whole wheat flour
3 tbsp gluten
1 tbsp yeast
1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in the order specified by your bread machine and run on the wheat cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 124
