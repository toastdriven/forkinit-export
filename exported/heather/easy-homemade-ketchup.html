Forkinit: Easy Homemade Ketchup

By Heather
I am not sure if I want to make my own ketchup. But, it's kinda cool to know how to do it, if I am every stuck....

Ingredients

1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
1 medium onion, chopped
1 (28-ounce) can whole tomatoes, pureed in a blender and strained to remove any seeds
2 tablespoons tomato paste
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground allspice
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Add onion and saut√© for 5 minutes until softened. Add remaining ingredients and stir to combine. Bring mixture to a simmer and cook uncovered for 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Transfer to a blender and blend until smooth. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to two weeks.

Yields: 2 cups
