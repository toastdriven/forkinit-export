Forkinit: Cheesy Baked Tortilla Chips

By Heather
This is my favorite evening snack. Crispy and cheesy ! What could be better

Ingredients

2 tortillas
grated cheddar cheese (or any grated cheese you want)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Grease a cookie sheet.
Cut the tortilla up (I use a pizza wheel and make triangles, but any shape works)
Lay the tortillas in a single layer on a cookie sheet. Bake for 5-8 minutes (keep a close eye on them.) They should be crisp, but not burnt.
Sprinkle the cheese on them.
Put back in the oven for 2 minutes or until the cheese is melted.

Yields: 2
