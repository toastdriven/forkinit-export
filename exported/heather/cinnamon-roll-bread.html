Forkinit: Cinnamon Roll Bread

By Heather
A very yummy breakfast treat. The dough is made the day before so in the morning all you do is warm up the oven and bake bread.

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups flour
1 1/2 tsp yeast
2 tbsp butter +
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup sugar +
1/3 cup brown sugar +
1 cup milk
1 tbsp cinnamon +
1 egg

Instructions

Combine 1/2 the flour, yeast, salt, sugars and cinnamon.
Add melted butter and eggs. Mix
Add milk. Mix
Add rest of flour slowly until the right consistency for bread.
Kneed by hand for a min or two.
Butter bowl and let bread rise in buttered covered bowl for at least 2 hours.
Roll bread out, coat with melted butter, sprinkle with cinnamon, brown sugar, and sugar.
Roll up into a long tube.
Let rise in pan overnight.

Preheat oven to 350 deg F
Cook for 30 to 40 min.
