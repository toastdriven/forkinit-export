Forkinit: Zucchini Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

2 eggs
1/2 cup oil
1 cup sugar
1 cup grated zucchini
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cup flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/8 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 tbsp cinnamon

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325 deg F
Beat eggs.
Add oil, sugar, zucchini, and vanilla and stir well.
Sift together dry ingredients and add. Mix well.
Put into a greased and floured 9x7x3 loaf pan.
Bake for about 1 hour or until done.
Cool 10 min, turn out of pan.

Yields: 1 loaf
