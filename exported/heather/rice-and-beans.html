Forkinit: Rice and Beans

By Heather

Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
1 onion, peeled and diced
1 tbsp minced garlic
4 cups cooked red or black kidney beans
1/4 cup white wine vinegar
3 cups cooled long-grain rice, warm
salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

Instructions

Heat oil in pan large enough to hold all ingredients. Add the onion and cook over medium heat for 3 minutes.
Add garlic and cook for 1 minute. Add beans and vinegar and cook for 5 minutes.
Add rice and mix well. Season to taste with salt and freshly ground black pepper.

Source: "The Cook's Bible" by Christopher Kimball
