Forkinit: Chocolate Mung

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/4 cups sugar, divided
1 cup all purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/3 cup melted butter
1/2 cup packed Light brown sugar
7 tbsp cocoa, divided
3 tsp baking powder
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/4 cup hot water

Instructions

Heat Oven to 350 deg F
In medium mixing bowl, combine 3/4 cup sugar, flour, 3 tbsp cocoa, baking powder, and salt. Blend in milk, melted butter, and vanilla. Beat until smooth.
Pour batter into square pan, 8x8x2 or 9x9x2.
In a small bowl combine remaining 1/2 cup sugar, brown sugar and remaining 4 tbsp cocoa. Sprinkle mixture evenly over batter.
Pour hot water over top. Do not stir !
bake 40 min or until center is almost set.
Let stand 15 min; spoon into dessert dishes, spooning sauce from bottom of pan over top.

Yields: 10
