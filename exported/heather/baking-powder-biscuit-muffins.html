Forkinit: Baking Powder Biscuit Muffins

By Heather

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1/4 cup sugar
4 tsp baking powder
3/4 cup cold butter
1 cup milk

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 deg
Combine flour, sugar and baking powder in large bowl; cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in milk just until moistened.
Spoon batter into paper-lined or greased muffin pans.
Bake for 13 to 15 min or until lightly browned.

Yields: 24 muffins
