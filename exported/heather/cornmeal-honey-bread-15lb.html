Forkinit: Cornmeal Honey Bread 1.5lb

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/8 cup water
1 1/2 tbsp butter, in pieces
3 tbsp honey
1/2 tsp salt
1/3 cup dry buttermilk powder
2 2/3 cup bread flour
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
1 tbsp gluten
1 tsp gluten
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in the machine and run on the White cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 148
