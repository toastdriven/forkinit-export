Forkinit: Pain De Maison Sur Poolish

By Heather
This is a bread machine bread that uses a starter

Ingredients

Poolish (starter):
1 cup water
1 1/4 cup flour
1/4 tsp yeast

Bread:
1/3 cup water
3/4 tsp salt
2 cup flour
1 tbsp sugar
1 tbsp gluten

Instructions

Put poolish ingredients into machine and run on the dough cycle for 10 min.
After 10 min, stop machine. Turn machine off. And let dough sit in machine for 6 hours.
Combine water and yeast, stirring to dissolve. Pour into the bread pan, and add other bread ingredients.
Rum machine on French cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
