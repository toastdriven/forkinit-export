Forkinit: Banana Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1/2 cup margarine or butter
3/4 cup brown sugar
1 egg
3 medium bananas (1 1/4 cup)
1/4 cup plain yogurt
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup white flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 deg F and grease a loaf pan.
Soften butter/margarine.
Beat butter/margarine with brown sugar and egg until smooth.
Mash bananas and add mashed bananas and yogurt. Beat again. It will look curdled.
Add whole wheat flour and stir it in.
Sift together rest of ingredients (white flour, b soda and b powder). Mix them in with a spoon.
Turn into the greased loaf pan.
Bake for 1 hour or until done.
Cool 10 min and then turn out of pan.

Yields: 1 loaf
