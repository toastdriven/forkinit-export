Forkinit: Never Fail Dumplings

By Heather
This is my grandmother's dumpling recipe

Ingredients

2 cups flour
1/4 tsp salt
4 tsp baking powder, level
1 egg
1/3 cup milk
1 tsp parsley, optional

Instructions

Sift flour, salt, baking powder
Beat egg and add milk.
On low beat egg mix into flour mix. Let stand 10 min. (Can add 1 tsp of parsley)
Drop with a tablespoon on top boiling stew or soup.
Cook covered 20 min (do not lift or open the lid)
Cook on medium until done

Source: My grandmother
