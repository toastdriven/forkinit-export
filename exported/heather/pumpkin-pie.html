Forkinit: Pumpkin Pie

By Heather
A light fluffy take on the traditional pumpkin pie

Ingredients

29 oz Canned pumpkin (the big can)
1 cup sugar
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground cloves
1 splash milk
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp ground ginger
4 large eggs
2 9 in pie shells (unbaked)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425 deg F
Wipe egg whites. Then add in only 2 of the egg yokes.
Wipe in the sugar, slowly.
Combine all the other ingredients in with the egg/sugar combo.
Mix together.
Pour into the pie shells
Wrap aluminum foil around edges of pie shells to keep them from burning.
Bake at 425 F for 15 min
Reduce heat to 350 F, bake for 40 to 50 more min. The pie is done when a toothpick comes out clean.

Yields: 2 9-in pies
