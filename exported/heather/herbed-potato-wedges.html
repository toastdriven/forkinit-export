Forkinit: Herbed Potato Wedges

By Heather

Ingredients

2 tsp olive oil
2 tsp balsamic vinegar
1 tbsp grated Parmesan cheese
1 tbsp dry bread crumbs
1/2 tsp dried Italian seasoning
2 med potatoes
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/4 tsp onion powder

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 deg F
2. In a small bowl, combine the oil and vinegar.
3. In another small bowl combine cheeses, bread and seasonings.
4. Scrub potatoes. Cut into wedges. Leave the skin on.
5. Put the wedges on a foil lined baking sheet.
6. Brush wedges with oil mix.
7. Coat with dry cheese/herb mix.
8. Bake for 20-25 min or until tender with crisp edges.

Yields: 8
Source: New Dieters Cookbook pg. 60
