Forkinit: Rice Cooker Oatmeal

By Heather
This is a quick easy way to make oatmeal on a busy morning.

Ingredients

1 1/3 cups oats
1 1/2 cups milk
1 1/4 cup water
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp cinnamon

Instructions

Add ingredients to the pot and stir.
Cover and press the Steam/Cook button. (set time for 20 min)
Halfway through the steaming cycle, carefully open the lid and stir.
Cover and continue cooking until the rice cooker switches to Keep-Warm mode.

Yields: 4
Prep time: 22 min
