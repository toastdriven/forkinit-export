Forkinit: Maple Oatmeal Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/3 cup buttermilk
1/2 cup maple syrup
2 tbsp butter, in pieces
2/4 tsp salt
3 cup flour
3/4 cup rolled oats
1 tsbp gluten
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put all ingredients in bread machine (in the order your machine manufacture recommends) and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
