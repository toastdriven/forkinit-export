Forkinit: Pana Toscana

By Heather
This is a bread machine recipe that uses a starter.

Ingredients

Starter:
1 1/3 cup water
1 cup flour
1/3 cup whole wheat flour
1 tbsp gluten
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Bread:
1 2/3 cup flour
pinch sugar
pinch salt

Instructions

Put starter ingredients into machine and run on the dough cycle for 10 min.
After 10 min, stop the machine. Unplug it. Let it stand for 1 hour.
Add the bread ingrediants and run on French cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
