Forkinit: Old- Frashioned Potato Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1 medium potato (8 to 9 oz), cut into chunks
3/4 cup water, plus more
2 tbsp butter, in pieces
3/4 tsp salt
1 tbsp sugar
3 cup flour
1 1/2 tsp gluten
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine potato and water in a small saucepan. Simmer covered until the potato is tender. (about 10 min)
Slip off the peels. Mash potato with its water.
Pour potato-water mix into a 2 cup measuring up and add water to make 1 1/3 cup total.
Let potato/water mix cool to room temp
Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
