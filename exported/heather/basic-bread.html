Forkinit: Basic Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

5 cups flour
2 tbsp sugar
1 tsp salt
2 1/4 tsp yeast
1/4 cup nonfat dry milk
2 cups hot water (120 to 130 deg)
3 tbsp shortening, room temp

Instructions

Mixer:

In bowl, mix 2 cups flour, yeast, sugar, dry milk and salt. Add the hot water. Beat together.
Add shortening.
When the dough is smooth, add additional flour slowly. Switch to dough hook or by hand as needed.
When the bread is the correct consistency, kneed by hand or with dough hook for 10 min.

Food Processor:

Add flour, salt, sugar, dry milk and yeast to the FP bowl. Pulse to mix.
Add in the shortening and process for 10 seconds.
With the FP running, slowly add in the water until the dough is the right consistency.
Run the FP for 45 seconds to kneed the dough.

For All:

Put the dough in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and let rise until it doubles, about 1 hour.
Grease 2 8"X4" bread pans.
Turn the dough onto a lightly flowered surface. Divide the dough into 2 pieces. Shape the dough and put them in the bread pans.
Cover the pans and let them rise until the dough has doubled, about 45 min.
Preheat the oven to 400 deg.
Bake at 400 deg for 10 min. Then lower the heat to 350 deg and let the bread cook for and additional 25 to 30 min.
The loaves are done when they are golden brown and sound hollow.

Yields: 2 l
