Forkinit: Golden Pumpkin Bread

By Heather
A yummy fall/winter bread. It makes the whole house smell wonderful

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 cup canned pumpkin (or mashed cooked pumpkin)
1/2 cup Butter, softened (1 stick)
2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground cloves

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 deg F. (Be careful to not have it to high. It can burn).
Combine all ingredients in a large mixer bowl. Beat at medium speed, scraping the bowl often, until well mixed (2 to 3 min).
Spoon into a greased 9X5 cake pan (or three greased 5X3 in mini loaf pans). Bake for 45 to 55 min (or 30 to 35 min if using mini pans). Until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.

Yields: 1 loaf
