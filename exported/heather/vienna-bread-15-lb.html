Forkinit: Vienna Bread 1.5 lb

By Heather
This is a variant of French Bread. This is a very, very high rising loaf ! (The 2 lb loaf exploded out of my machine. )

Ingredients

1/2 cup water
1/2 cup milk
3 tbsp butter, in pieces
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp sugar
3 cup bread flour
1 tbsp gluten
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all the ingredients in the machine and run on the French cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 64
