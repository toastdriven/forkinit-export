Forkinit: Vanilla Pudding

By Heather

Ingredients

1/3 cup sugar
1/8 tsp salt
2 egg yolks
1 tsp vanilla extract
3 tbsp cornstarch
2 cup milk
1 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

Wish the sugar, cornstarch, and salt together in a small pot. Add about 1/4 cup of milk to make a thin paste.
Wish in the remaining milk and egg yolks
Cook over low heat, stirring constantly for about 15 min or until mixture starts to thicken. Do not let it boil.
Once it develops a pudding like consistency, remove from heat and add vanilla and butter. Stir
Place mixture in a bowel, cover it with plastic wrap.
Gently push the plastic wrap onto the surface of the pudding so that it won't form a skin.
Seal bowl with another layer of plastic wrap and chill for at least 1 hour.
