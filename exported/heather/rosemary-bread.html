Forkinit: Rosemary Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

3/4 cup water, 80 to 90 deg F
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
1/2 tsp sea salt
3 cup flour
2 tsp dried rosemary
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
