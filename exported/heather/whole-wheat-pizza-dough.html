Forkinit: Whole Wheat Pizza Dough

By Heather
This is my favorite everyday pizza dough

Ingredients

1 tsp rapid rise yeast
3 cups all purpose flour
2 cups whole wheat flour
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp salt
1 1/4 cups water
2 tbsp honey
garlic powder, to taste
onion powder, to taste
Basil, to taste

Instructions

I put everything in my bread machine and run it on the dough cycle, but I used to do it with my stand mixer. If using stand mixer, combine half the flour and all the other ingredients, mix, slowly adding the rest of the flour until a sticky dough ball forms. Let rise for an hour or so.
Divide into 2 balls, and let rise at least a half hour.
Roll/shape.
Top
Bake in oven. I do it at 425 deg F for 11 min

Yields: 2 12 in pizzas
