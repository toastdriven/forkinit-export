Forkinit: Sweet Potato Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1/3 cup solid shortening (crisco)
3/4 cup sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup lite molasses
1 cooked mashed sweet potatoes
2 cup flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp nutmeg
1/2 tsp all spice
1/4 tsp cloves

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 deg F
Combine shortening, sugar and eggs. Beat until light and fluffy.
Stir in molasses and sweet potatoes.
Sift together dry ingredients, add to egg mixture and stir.
Pour into well greased 9x5x3 loaf pan
Bake for 1 hour. it often needs a little bit more time. It is done when a toothpick comes out clean.
Cool for 10 min, turn out of pan

Yields: 1 loaf
