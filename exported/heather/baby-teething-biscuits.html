Forkinit: Baby Teething Biscuits

By Heather
N loves these when she was little. They are softer (but more flexable) than the standard hard teething biscuits.

Ingredients

1 cup flour (i usually needed a pinch more than 1 cup)
1 cup baby cereal
1 cup juice

Instructions

Mix ingredients well.
Roll out and cut into shapes. It is super sticky dough so I usually added more flour to make it workable.
Bake for 20-30 min @ 350 deg. F.. Freezes Well

Prep time: 5
