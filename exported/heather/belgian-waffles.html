Forkinit: Belgian Waffles

By Heather

Ingredients

1/2 cup war mwater
2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
2 1/4 cups milk
2 cups all purpose flour
1/4 cup butter, melted
3 eggs, beaten
1 tbsp brow sugar
1 tbsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

In a large bowl, dissolve water in yeast.
Add remaining ingredients. Mix well.
Let stand for 45 min.
Heat waffle iron.
Pour batter into iron. Cook for 2-3 min or until steaming stops and waffles are golden brown
