Forkinit: Quick and Easy Powdered Sugar Iceing

By Heather
This is a really quick easy iceing. I use it on my Lebkucken.

Ingredients

2 cup powdered sugar (sifted)
1/2 tsp vanilla
2 tbsp milk

Instructions

Mix together. Add more milk or sugar to get the right consistency.
