Forkinit: Country French Loaf with Rosemary

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/4 cup water, 80 to 90 deg F
2 1/4 tsp extra virgin olive oil
1/2 tsp sea salt
3/8 tsp honey
1 1/2 tsp dired rosemary
1 3/4 cup flour
2 tbsp flour
6 tbsp barley flour
6 tbsp whole wheat flour
1/4 cup rye flour
1 3/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the French cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
