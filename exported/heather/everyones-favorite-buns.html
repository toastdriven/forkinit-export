Forkinit: Everyone's Favorite Buns

By Heather
A fluffy, buttery bun recipe.

Ingredients

1/2 cup milk
warm water
1 egg
1 tbsp yeast
6 tbsp butter
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 tsp salt
5 cups flour,

Instructions

Place milk, 4 tbsp butter in a 2 cup glass measuring cup. Microwave on high for 1 1/2 min. Add enough warm water to measure 2 cups.
Combine sugar, egg, and salt in a large mixer bowl. Mix well. Add milk mixture, mix well. Stir in yeast. Let stand 2 min.
Add 3 cups flour. Beat at medium speed, scraping the bowl often, until smooth and elastic (2 to 3 min). Add 2 cups flour. Mix dough with hook or by hand until smooth. Add enough flour to make dough easy to handle.
Place dough in a greased bowl. Melt 2 tbsp butter. Brush butter on top of dough. Cover. Let rise until doubles in size (about 45 min).
Punch down dough. divide in half. Shape and place on pan. Brush with melted butter. Cover. Let rise until doubles in size (1 hour).
Heat oven to 375 deg F. Bake 10-16 min until golden brown.
Remove from pan. Brush with butter.
