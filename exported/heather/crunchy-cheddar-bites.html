Forkinit: Crunchy Cheddar Bites

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup butter, very soft
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
1 cup toasted rice cereal

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 deg
Combine flour, butter and cheese in large bowl; mix until dough forms. Stir in cereal until combined.
Shape dough into 1 in balls. Place 2 inches apart on ungreased baking sheet. Bake for 10 to 15 min or until golden brown. Serve warm or at room temp.

Yields: 3 doz
