Forkinit: Olive Oil Bread

By Heather
This is a bread machine bread that uses a starter.

Ingredients

Biga starter:
3/4 cup water
1 1/2 cup flour
1/2 tsp yeast

Bread:
1/4 cup water
3 tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup flour
1 tsp gluten
1 3/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put in the starter ingredients and run on dough cycle for 10 min. After the 10 min, turn off and unplug machine.
Let it sit in the machine for 12 to 18 hours.
With a rubber spatula, break dough into 6 to 8 pieces. Leave starter in machine. Add the bread ingredients.
Run on the French cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
