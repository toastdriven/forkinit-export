Forkinit: Garlic Mashed Potatoes

By Heather

Ingredients

5 medium potatoes, peeled, cut into eigths
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 cup warm milk
6 tbsp butter, softened
1 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

Place potatoes in 4-qt saucepan or dutch oven. Add enough water to cover; add salt. Bring to a boil over high heat (7 to 9 min). Reduce heat to medium. Cook until potatoes are fork tender (15 to 18 min). Drain.
Place potatoes in a large mixer bowl. Add milk, butter and garlic. Beat at medium speed until smooth (2 to 3 min).

Yields: 6 (3/4 cup) servicings
