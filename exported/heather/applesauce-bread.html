Forkinit: Applesauce Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1/4 cup apple juice
1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1 large egg
2 tbsp butter, in pieces
3/4 tsp salt
3 tbsp light brown sugar, optional
3 cup flour
1 tbsp gluten
1 tbsp cinnamon
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the white or sweet bread cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
