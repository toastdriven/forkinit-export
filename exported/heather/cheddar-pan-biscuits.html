Forkinit: Cheddar Pan Biscuits

By Heather

Ingredients

1/3 cup butter
2 1/4 cups all purpose flour
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
4 oz cheddar, shredded (1 cup)
1 tbsp baking powder
1 tsp dried basil leaves
1 cup milk

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 deg F
Melt butter in 8 in square baking pan in oven ( 3-5 min)
Combine all remaining ingredients expect milk in medium bowl. Stir in milk until moistened.
Turn dough onto lightly floured surface. Knead until smooth (1 min). Pat or roll dough into 12X4 in rectangle. Cut into 12 1in strips.
Dip each strip into melted butter in pan. Fold in half and place in pan.
Bake for 23-28 min or until lightly browned

Yields: 12
Source: "Baking Basics" by Land o Lakes, pg 30
