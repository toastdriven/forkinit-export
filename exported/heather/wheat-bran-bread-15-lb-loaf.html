Forkinit: Wheat Bran Bread (1.5 lb loaf)

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup water, 80 deg, to 90 deg
1/2 tsp salt
1 tbsp butter, in pieces
1 1/2 tbsp molasses
3 cup whole wheat flour
1/3 cup old fashioned oats
1/3 cup wheat bran
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put all in bread machine and run on wheat cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
