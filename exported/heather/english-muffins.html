Forkinit: English Muffins

By Heather

Ingredients

2 1/4 cups bread flour
1/2 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/4 tsp yeast
1 tbsp shortening or butter, at room temp
3/4 cup milk or butter milk, room temp (may need more)

Instructions

Stir together flour, sugar, salt and yest. Stir in shortening and milk until it forms a ball. It should be soft.
Kneed dough. Put in bowl, cover, and let rise for 60-90 min
Wipe counter with damp cloth. Divide dough into 6 pieces. Shape. Put on parchment paper lined cookie sheet to rise. Let rise for 60-90 min.
Heat skillet or griddle to medium (350deg F). Also preheat oven to 350 deg.
Brush pan/griddle with veggie oil. Put on griddle (at least 1 inch apart). Cooke for 5-8 min until the bottom can't cook any more. Carefully flip and cook for another 5-8 min.
Bake in oven for 5-8min.
Cool for 30 min before serving.

Source: "The Bread Baker's Apprentice" by Peter Reinhart
