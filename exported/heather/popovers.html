Forkinit: Popovers

By Heather

Ingredients

1 tbsp melted butter or canola oil, plus more for greasing muffing tin
2 eggs
1 cup milk
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup all-purpose flour

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425 deg F. Grease standard muffin tin and place it in the oven while you make the batter.
Beat together the egg, milk, butter or oil, sugar and salt.
Beat in the flour a little bit at a time. The mixture should be smooth.
Fill the muffin tins at least halfway.
Place in oven and bake for 15 min. Then reduce the heat to 350 deg and continue baking for 15 min more, or until the popover are puffed and brown. Do not check the popover until they have baked for a total of 30 min.

Yields: 12
Source: "How to Cook Everything" by Bittman
