Forkinit: Ranch Crackekrs

By Heather
I can't stop eating these once I start !

Ingredients

1 cup oil
1 tsp dill
1 tsp garlic powder
1 package dry ranch salad dressing mix
24 oz of oyster crackers

Instructions

Preheat oven to 275 deg F
Mix all together
Put on cookie sheet. Bake for 15 to 20 min.
