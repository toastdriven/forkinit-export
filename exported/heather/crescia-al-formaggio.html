Forkinit: Crescia Al Formaggio

By Heather

Ingredients

1/2 cup water
1 tbsp water
3 large eggs
3 tbsp olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
3 1/4 cup flour
1 tsp sugar
3/4 cup grated Asiago or other hard cheese
1 1/2 tbsp dry milk
2 tsp gluten
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put all ingredients in bread machine (in the order your machine manufacture recommends) and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
