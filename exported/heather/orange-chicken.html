Forkinit: Orange Chicken

By Heather
This is my less work version of Crispy-Skinned Chicken A L'Orange

Ingredients

6 chicken breast tenders, boneless/skinless
Â½ c frozen orange juice concentrate (approx)
4 tbsp honey (approx)
spray veggie oil

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 deg.
In a microwave safe bowl, combine the honey and OJ concentrate
Microwave on 3 for 1 to 2 minutes. It should be warmed up. Mix it around.
Using the spray veggie oil, spray down one 8x8 glass pan. Put chicken in it. Add approx. 1/2 of the honey/ OJ mix.
Bake for 20 min uncovered.
Take out the chicken and flip it over. Add the rest of the sauce to the top of the chicken.
Bake for another 15-20 minutes, or until the chicken is done.

Yields: 4
