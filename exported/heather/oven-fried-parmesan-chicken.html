Forkinit: Oven Fried Parmesan Chicken

By Heather

Ingredients

6 boneless chicken breasts
1 1/4 cup oil
1 cup milk

Coating
1 1/2 cup bread crumbs
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 cup Parmesan cheese
1/4 tsp oregano
1/8 tsp pepper
garlic powder, to taste
3/4 tsp basil

Instructions

Combine coating ingredients in a plastic bag.
Heat oven to 400 deg F.
Pour 3/4 cup oil in a 13 X9 pan.
Dip chicken in milk than shake in coating mixture. Place in pan. Repeat for all chicken pieces.
Drizzle remaining 1/2 cup oil oven chicken.
Bake for 40 to 45 min.

Yields: 6
