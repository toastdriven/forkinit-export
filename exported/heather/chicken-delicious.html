Forkinit: Chicken Delicious

By Heather

Ingredients

4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon paprika
1 (10.75 ounce) can condensed X soup
1/3 cup chicken stock
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Dump everything into the crock pot, and let it cook for 4 hours on high or 6-8 hours on low.

Yields: 4
