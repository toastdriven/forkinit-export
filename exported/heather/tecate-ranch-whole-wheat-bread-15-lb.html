Forkinit: Tecate Ranch Whole Wheat Bread 1.5 lb

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/3 cup water
3 tbsp canola oil
3 tbsp honey
2 tbsp molasses
1 tsp salt
3 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
1/3 cup wheat bran
2 1/2 tbsp gluten
1 tbsp yeast
1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in the machine and run on the wheat cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 126
