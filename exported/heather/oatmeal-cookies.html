Forkinit: Oatmeal Cookies

By Heather

Ingredients

8 tbsp butter (one stick), softened
1/2 cup sugar
1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
2 eggs
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 cups rolled oats
pinch salt
1/2 cup milk
1 1/2 cup chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 deg F
Cream together butter and sugars
Add eggs one at a time, and beat until well blended.
Combine flour, oats, cinnamon, salt, and baking powder in a bowl.
Alternating with the milk, add the dry ingredients to the batter by hand, a little at a time, stirring throughly.
Stir in vanilla
Bake until light brown, 12-15 min

Yields: 3 doz
