Forkinit: Quick and Easy Waffles

By Heather

Ingredients

1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
2 eggs
4 tbsp butter, melted and cooled
2 cups all purpose flour
2 tbsp sugar
1 1/2 cups milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tbsp cinnamon, optional

Instructions

Preheat waffle iron
Combine the dry ingredients.
In a separate bowl, mix together milk and eggs. Stir in butter and vanilla. Stir the wet into the dry. (the big 4 cup measuring cup should work for the wet ing.)
Ladle into waffle iron.

Source: "How to Cooke Everything" by Mark Bittman, pg 746
