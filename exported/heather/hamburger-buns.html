Forkinit: Hamburger Buns

By Heather

Ingredients

5 cups of flour
1 1/2 tbsp yeast
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp butter, softened and at room temp
2 cups hot water (120 deg to 130 deg)

Instructions

Mixer:

In bowl, mix 2 cups flour, yeast and salt. Add the butter than the hot water. Beat together.
When the dough is smooth, add additional flour slowly. Switch to dough hook or by hand as needed.
When the bread is the correct consistency, kneed by hand or with dough hook for 6 to 8 min.

Food Processor:
Add flour, salt and yeast to the FP bowl. Pulse to mix.
Add in the butter and process for 10 seconds.
With the FP running, slowly add in the water until the dough is the right consistency.
Run the FP for 45 seconds to kneed the dough.

For All:
Put the dough in a bowl, cover with plastic wrap and let rise until it doubles, about 30 to 40 min.
Turn dough onto a flowered surface, and divide into 12 pieces. Shape into balls. Cover and let relax for 4 to 50 min.
Shape the balls into rolls and put on greased baking sheet. Cover. Let rise until they are puffy, about 30 min.
Preheat the oven to 400 deg.
Bake for 20 min or until they are browned on bottom.

Yields: 12
