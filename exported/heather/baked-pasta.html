Forkinit: Baked Pasta

By Heather

Ingredients

1 lb uncooked pasta
3 cups mozzarella cheese
1 small package ricotta cheese
2 cups water
Tomato sauce
onion powder
garlic powder
basil
Italian seasoning

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F
2. Put water and sauce in pan. Adjust water/sauce as needed to maintain a thin sauce (it will thicken as it cooks. )
3. Add herbs/seasonings and 2 cups mozzarella
4. Add pasta
5. Add in the ricotta cheese in small pieces.
6. Carefully mix so that the pasta has water/sauce over it.
7. Cover with aluminum foil.
8. Bake for 45 min. Until pasta is almost soft.
9. Take out of oven. Take cover off. Add remaining mozzarella cheese.
10. Bake for 15 additional minutes.

Yields: 6
