Forkinit: Cornmeal Pizza Dough

By Heather
A nice switch from the standard pizza dough.

Ingredients

1 1/2 cup water
1/4 cup olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
3 2/3 cups all purpose flour
1/3 cup cornmeal
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Add ingredients to bread machine and run on dough cycle. Or Combine by hand. Let rise for 1 hour.
Seperate into two pizzas. Let rise for 1 hour
Preheat oven to 425 deg F
Roll/shape into pizzas.
Top
Bake for 10-15 min until done

Yields: 2 12 in pizzas
