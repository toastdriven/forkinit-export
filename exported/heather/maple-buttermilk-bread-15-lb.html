Forkinit: Maple Buttermilk Bread 1.5 lb

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup water
1 tbsp water
3 tbsp maple syrup
1 1/2 tbsp butter, melted
1 tsp salt
1/3 cup dry buttermilk powder
3 cups bread flour
1 tbsp gluten
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in bread machine and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 58
