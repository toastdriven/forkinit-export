Forkinit: Virginia Light Rolls

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup milk
1 tbsp milk
2 large eggs
3 tbsp honey
6 tbsp butter, in pieces
1 tsp salt
4 1/4 cups flour
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put in bread machine and run on dough cycle. Or combine with mixer, and let rise for 1 hour.
Shape into rolls. Let double in size, about 45 min
Preheat oven to 375 deg F
Bake for 18-25 min, until golden brown

Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
