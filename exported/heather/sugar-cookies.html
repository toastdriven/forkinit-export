Forkinit: Sugar Cookies

By Heather

Ingredients

¼ cup of shortening
½ cup of butter (soften)
1 cup plus 3 tablespoons of sugar
2 eggs whites plus one whole egg
1 teaspoon if vanilla extract
2 ½ cups of all purpose flour
1 teaspoon of baking powder
1 teaspoon of salt

Instructions

Gently whip the eggs whites. You do not want them stiff, just slightly foamy. Add the whole egg and sugar. Beat. Add shortening, butter, and flavoring. Beat. Add baking soda and salt. Beat. Gentle blend in flour.

Bake at 375 for 6 to 8 minutes. To make this roll out cookie cutter cookies divide dough in half and refrigerate for at least one hour.
