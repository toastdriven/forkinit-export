Forkinit: Honeybee Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

3/4 cup milk
1/4 cup honey
3 tbsp butter, softened
1 egg
1/2 tsp salt
2 2/3 cups bread flour
1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 tsp bread machine yeast
3 tbsp roasted sunflower seeds

Instructions

Put all ingredients in machine in the order and run on the whole wheat cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
