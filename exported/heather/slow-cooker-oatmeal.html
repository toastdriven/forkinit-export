Forkinit: Slow Cooker Oatmeal

By Heather
Set up the night before, and then let it cook all night in the slow cookers

Ingredients

1 cup oats
3 cups water (adjust based on your slow cooker)
1 cup milk
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tbsp cinnamon

Instructions

Before going to bed, add in oats, water, brown sugar and cinnamon.
Set cooker on low.
In the morning, add milk and stir.

Yields: 4
