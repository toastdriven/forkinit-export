Forkinit: Pumpkin Rolls

By Heather
A quick yummy fall roll

Ingredients

1 3/4 cup all purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
3/4 cup canned pumpkin (about 1/2 small a small can)
3 tbsp cooking oil
2 tbsp brown sugar
2 tsp sugar (for topping)
1 tbsp cinnamon, plus more for topping

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 deg F
In a medium mixing bowl combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, nutmeg, salt and cinnamon.
In a small mixing bowl, combine pumpkin, oil, and brown sugar.
Add pumpkin mix to dry mix, stirring with a fork until combined.
Turn dough onto lightly floured surface.
Shape into rolls. Smaller rolls are better.
Put rolls onto a large baking sheet linned with foil.
Combine sugar and cinnamon. Sprinkle over rolls.
Bake for 9 to 11 min or until golden brown.
Serve warm !

Yields: 16 rolls
