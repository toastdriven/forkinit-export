Forkinit: DIY Croutons

By Heather
This is a great way to use up stale bread

Ingredients

1 small loaf of day-old French bread, or a baguette
1 tablespoon butter
2 tablespoons olive oil
Handful of herbs, chopped
Chunky salt (i wouldn't add the salt)

Instructions

Heat the oven to 325°F.
Cut the bread into chunks about 1-inch across. Spread over a large, heavy baking sheet.
Heat the butter and olive oil in a small pan over medium heat until warm, then add the herbs and cook for about a minute or until they are soft and fragrant. Pour over the bread chunks, toss evenly and sprinkle with chunky salt.
Bake at 325°F for 15-20 minutes, depending on the size of the bread chunks. Stir frequently with a spatula and remove when they are golden on all sides. Store in an airtight container.

Yields: 4 cups
