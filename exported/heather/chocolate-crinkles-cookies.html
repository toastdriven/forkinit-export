Forkinit: Chocolate Crinkles Cookies

By Heather

Ingredients

2 cups sugar
1/2 cup oil
4 oz melted unsweetened chocolate, cooled or 3/4 cup cocoa powder and an additional 1/4 cup oil
2 tsp vanilla
4 eggs
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 cup powdered sugar

Instructions

Mix together sugar, oil, chocolate and vanilla.
Mix in eggs one at a time.
Sift/mix flour, salt and baking powder. Add to wet mix. Cover and refrigerate at least 3 hours or overnight.
Shape dough by rounded teaspoonfuls into balls. Roll in powdered sugar.
Place 2 in apart on greased cookie sheet. Grease the sheet well as the sugar melts.
Bake 10-12 min. (MIL forgot to give me cooking temp. I would guess 350).

Yields: 6 dozzen cookies
