Forkinit: Cheesy Potatoes

By Heather
A warm, cheesy side dish, perfect for cold winter days.

Ingredients

8 to 10 medium baking potatoes
1 1/4 cup grated cheddar cheese
2 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
1 palm of "ground" parsley flakes
Onion powder, to taste
garlic powder, to taste
Italian seasoning, to taste

Instructions

Peal and cube potatoes
Microwave potatoes on high uncovered for 20 min
Preheat oven to 350 deg F
Grease a 9X13 cake pan
Mix all ingredients together and pour into cake pan.
Bake covered for 25 to 30 min

Yields: enough for 4 people
