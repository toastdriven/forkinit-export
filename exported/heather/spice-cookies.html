Forkinit: Spice Cookies

By Heather

Ingredients

1 c sugar
1 c crisco
1 c light molasses
2 eggs
4 c all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger

Instructions

Cream sugar and crisco.
Add molasses and egg. Mix well.
Sift/mix together flour, baking soda, salt and spices. Add dry ingredients and mix well.
Refrigerate for a couple of hours. (Dough will keep in fridge for up to 2 weeks according to my MIL.)
Preheat oven to 350 deg F
Make into balls the size of a walnut.
Roll in sugar and put on greased cookie sheet about 3 in apart.
Bake for 12 to 15 min.

Yields: 4 doz cookies
