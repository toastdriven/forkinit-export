Forkinit: Stuffing

By Heather

Ingredients

1 loaf stale bread
2 stick butter
1 egg
2 chicken bullion cubes
1 1/2 cups water
1 package turkey gravey mix
Italian seasoning
Parsley
Garlic Powder
Onion Powder

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 deg F
2. Put the bullion cubes in the water. Mix together. Use only half the butter.
3. Mix together
4. Put in a well buttered bowl
5. Put the remaining butter in as small pieces at random points.
6. Bake for 1 1/2 to 2 hours

Yields: 6
