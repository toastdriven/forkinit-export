Forkinit: Flakly Pie Crusts

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/8 cups all purpose flour
1 tsp sugar
3 tbsp ice water
1/2 tsp salt
8 tbsp unsalted butter

Instructions

Combine flour, salt, and sugar in food processor. Pulse 1 or 2 times. Add butter and process until butter and flour are blended and looks like cornmeal.
Place mix in a bowl and sprinkle 3 tbsp of water. Use wooden spoon or spatula to gather into a ball.
Wrap ball in plastic, flatten into a small dis and freeze for 10 min (or refrig to 30 min)
Roll out

Yields: 1 8-10 in pie crust
Source: "How to Cook Everything" by Bittman
