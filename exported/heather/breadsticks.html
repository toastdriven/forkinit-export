Forkinit: Breadsticks

By Heather

Ingredients

3 1/2 cups flour +
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp instant yeast
1 tbsp honey
1 1/3 cup water

Instructions

Combine 1/2 the flour, salt, oil, yeast, honey, and water in stand mixer. Blend with machine's paddle.
With machine on slow speed, add flour a little at a time until the mix has become a sticky ball that pulls away from the sides (switch to the dough hook if necessary).
Kneed by hand for 1 min.
Shape dough into a ball, place in a lightly oiled large bowl, and cover with plastic wrap or a damp towel.
Let rise at least 1 1/2 hours, until it nearly doubled. Deflate ball, shape it into a flat rectangle. Let rest for 15 min covered.
Cut into think strips. Pull/roll them into sticks. Let rest covered while the oven heat to 450 deg F.
Bake at 450 deg F for about 15 min or until lightly browned.

Yields: 30 breadsticks
Source: "How to Cook Everything" by Bittman
