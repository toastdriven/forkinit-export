Forkinit: Banana Honey Muffins

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups bran cereal
3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/3 cup honey
1 cup ripe bananas, mashed ( 2 medium )
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 tbsp large grain raw sugar

Instructions

Pre heat oven to 375 deg.
Stir together bran cereal and milk in large bowl. Let stand for 5 minutes.
Stir butter, honey, bananas and egg into bran cereal mixture.
Stir in flour, baking powder and salt just until moistened.
Spoon batter into paper-lined giant nonstick muffin cups. Sprinkle each muffin with raw sugar.
Bake 28 to 32 min or until lightly browned.

Yields: 6 muffins
