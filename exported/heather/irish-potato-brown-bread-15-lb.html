Forkinit: Irish Potato Brown Bread 1.5 lb

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/4 cup water
2 tbsp honey
3 tbsp butter, in pieces
1 tsp salt
2 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup bread flour
1 tbsp gluten
2 tsp gluten
1/4 cup instant potato flakes
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all the ingredients in the bread machine and run on the wheat cycle

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 117
