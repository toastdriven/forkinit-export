Forkinit: Agave Whole Wheat Bread 1.5 lb loaf

By Heather
Basic sandwich bread. It's a high rising loaf. My 2 lb machine barely contains the 1.5 lb loaf.

Ingredients

1 cup water
1 tbsp water
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp veggie oil
1 1/2 tbsp Agave
2 cups bread flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 tbsp wheat glueton
2 1/4 tsp yeast

Instructions

Add ingreadiants in the order recomended by your bread machine.

Run the machine on the whole wheat cycle

Yields: 1
