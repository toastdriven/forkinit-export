Forkinit: Three-Grain Bread

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/8 cup milk
2 tbsp olive oil
2 tbsp molasses
1/2 tsp salt
1 3/4 cup flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1/4 cup dark Rye Flour
2 tbsp yellow cornmeal
1 tbsp gluten
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put all ingredients in bread machine (in the order your machine manufacture recommends) and run on the wheat cycle.

Yields: 1.5
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
