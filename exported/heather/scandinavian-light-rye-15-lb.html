Forkinit: Scandinavian Light Rye 1.5 lb

By Heather

Ingredients

1 1/8 cup water
1 1/2 tbsp canola oil
1 tsp salt
1 7/8 cups bread flour
1 1/8 cups medium rye flour
2 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp gluten
1 tsp gluten
1 tbsp yeast

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in the bread machine and run on the white cycle.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook. p. 134
