Forkinit: Crispy Potato Galette

By Heather

Ingredients

24 oz russet potatoes, peeled, very thinly sliced
4 tbsp unsalted butter, melted
1 tsp kosher salt
1/2 tsp ground white pepper

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425 deg F
In a large bowl, gently toss the potatoes with 2 tbsp melted butter to coat each slice. Add salt and pepper and gently toss again.
Brush remaining melted butter in pan. Arrange potatoes slices overlapping in pan.
Place in oven and bake until golden brown and crisp, about 25 to 30 min. In necessary, place pan under preheated broiler for added crispness, if pan is broiler safe.

Yields: 6 servings
