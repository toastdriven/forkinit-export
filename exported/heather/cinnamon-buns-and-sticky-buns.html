Forkinit: Cinnamon Buns and Sticky Buns

By Heather

Ingredients

6 1/2 tbsp granulated sugar
1/2 tsp salt
5 1/2 tbsp shortening or unsalted butter at room temp
1 large egg, slightly beaten
1 tsp lemon extract
3 1/2 cups bread or all-purpose flour
2 tsp instant yeast
1 1/8 cups whole milk or butter milk (may need a pinch more)
1/2 cup cinnamon sugar

Instructions

Cream together sugar, salt and shortening.
Whip in the egg and lemon extract until smooth. Add the flour, yeast and milk. Mix until dough forms a ball.
Kneed dough.
Let rise for 2 hours
Roll out dough. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar on top. Roll up. Cut dough to make rolls.
Let rise for 75 to 90 min.
Preheat oven to 350 deg.
Bake for 20-30 min. Top with sugar icing of your choice.

Source: "The Bread Baker's Apprentice" by Peter Reinhart
