Forkinit: Healthy Whole Wheat Challah

By Heather
This bread can be made into a standard bread machine loaf or it can be taken out and shaped to create the more traditional challah loaf.

Ingredients

2/3 cup water
3 large eggs
3 tbsp veggie oil
2 tbsp honey
3/4 tsp salt
1 1/2 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/2 cup flour
1 1/2 tbsp gluten
1 tbsp instant potato flakes
2 1/2 tsp yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread machine (in the order recommended by the machine manufacturer) and run on the wheat cycle.

If you want the more traditional braided look to the bread. After the last kneed cycle, take the bread out. Remove the paddle. Roll the dough into 2 long "worms". Twist the "worms" together and put back in the machine.

Yields: 1.5 lb
Source: The Bread Lover's Breadmachine Cookbook.
