Forkinit: Butterballs

By Heather

Ingredients

1 cup butter, softened
1/4 cup sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp salt
2 cup flour
1 cup chopped pecans
powdered sugar for the frosting

Instructions

Mix everything together (except the powdered sugar). Chill for 1 hour.
Preheat the oven to 350 deg F
Make into small balls and put on a greased and floured cookie sheet.
Bake for 10 min or until brown on the bottom.
Roll in powdered sugar while warm and again when cold.
