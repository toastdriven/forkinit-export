Forkinit: Grilled Peanut Butter Chicken

By Craig Cottingham

Ingredients

2 1/2 lb boneless skinless chicken, breasts or thighs
1 C creamy peanut butter
1/4 C low sodium soy sauce
1/4 C lemon juice
2 tbsp white wine vinegar
2 tbsp brown sugar
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/2 to 1 tsp red pepper flakes
2 tsp fresh ginger, grated
1/4 C sliced green onions (optional)

Instructions

Place chicken in a plastic ziptop bag or a glass pan.
In a bowl, whisk together the peanut butter, soy sauce, lemon juice, vinegar, brown sugar, garlic powder, red pepper flakes and ginger. Pour over chicken.
Chill and allow the chicken to marinate for 6-8 hours or up to 24 hours.
Lightly oil grill.
Grill marinated chicken, discarding marinade, for 6-8 minutes per side or until done.
Serve sprinkled with green onions, if desired.

Yields: 6
Prep time: 30 min
