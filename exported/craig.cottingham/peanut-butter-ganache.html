Forkinit: Peanut Butter Ganache

By Craig Cottingham

Ingredients

12 oz cream cheese
1 3/4 C powdered sugar
2 C peanut butter
2 tbsp heavy cream, if needed

Instructions

In bowl of an electric mixer, whip the cream cheese until light and creamy.
Gradually beat in the powdered sugar, then the peanut butter.
If mixture looks lumpy, add 2 tbsp of heavy cream.
Continue beating until thoroughly incorporated and fluffy.
