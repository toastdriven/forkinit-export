Forkinit: Bacon-Herb Cupcakes

By Craig Cottingham
Try these as a perfect companion to a cocktail. Test Kitchen Specialist Marian Cooper Cairns likes to use a combination of parsley, thyme, and oregano, but sage, rosemary, basil, and chives will work just as well. Using a small cookie dough scoop for the batter gives the baked cupcakes a pretty round top.

Ingredients

1 1/2 C sour cream
1/2 C cooked, finely crumbled bacon
1/2 C melted butter
1/4 C finely chopped assorted fresh herbs
2 green onions, chopped
1/2 tsp pepper
2 C self-rising flour
6 oz cream cheese, softened

For garnish:
assorted fresh herbs
cooked and crumbled bacon

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375°F.
Stir together first 6 ingredients.
Stir in flour until blended. (Mixture will be thick.)
Spoon atter into lightly greased miniature muffin pans, filling completely full.
Bake at 375°F for 26 to 28 minutes or until golden brown.
Remove cupcakes from pans to a wire rack and cool completely (about 30 minutes).
Spoon or pipe tops of cupcakes with cream cheese. Garnish if desired.

Yields: about 32
Prep time: 1 hr 30 min
