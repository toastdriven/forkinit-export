Forkinit: Easy Béarnaise Sauce

By Craig Cottingham
"Béarnaise is traditionally as thick as mayonaise, as in the picture. That’s how I’d make it for a burger. If you want to make it looser, add a little water."

Ingredients

1 tbsp lemon juice
1 tbsp minced shallot
aggressive pinch of salt
pepper (optional)
2 egg yolks
6 oz salted butter
2 tbsp minced tarragon (or more to taste)

Instructions

Combine the lemon juice, shallot, salt, and pepper in a 2 C Pyrex measuring cup or other container with a narrow base. Let it sit for a few minutes.
Add the egg yolks and give it a buzz with the hand blender (if your blade doesn’t reach the yolk, use the whisk attachment).
Melt the butter in a 1 C measuring cup with a spout or some other container that will allow you to pour the butter in a stream. Make sure it’s piping hot.
With the hand blender running, pour the butter into the egg yolks, pumping the blender up and down (or side to side if using whisk) as you do so. Add half the tarragon and continue to blend. Stop and fold in the remaining tarragon.

Yields: about 1 C
