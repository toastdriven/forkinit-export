Forkinit: Chili Lime Mojo Sauce

By Craig Cottingham
Goes great with fish tacos.

Ingredients

2 tbsp ground cumin
1/4 C fresh jalape√±o pepper, seeded and minced
1/2 C garlic cloves, peeled and minced
1 tsp sea salt
1 tsp ground black pepper
3/4 C freshly squeezed lime juice
1 C fresh cilantro, chopped
3 tbsp apple cider vinegar
1 1/2 C olive oil

Instructions

In a blender, place all ingredients except olive oil.
Blend on low speed while drizzling the olive oil into the blender to emulsify.
Refrigerate.
