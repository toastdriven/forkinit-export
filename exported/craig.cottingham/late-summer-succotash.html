Forkinit: Late Summer Succotash

By Craig Cottingham
To prepare the soy beans, simply boil them in their pods until their tender, then chill them in ice water or in plenty of continuously running cold water. They pop right out of the pod. You can simply sprinkle a bowl of cooked pods with coarse salt and serve as is. You could oil and salt them and heat them over coals for a grilled soy bean treat.

Ingredients

4 oz bacon, diced
1/2 large onion, diced
1 C or so of cooked soy beans
1 C or so of fresh corn
2 oz butter
2 oz cream
salt and freshly ground pepper to taste

Instructions

In a large saute pan, cook the bacon over medium heat until it’s golden brown and its fat has rendered, about 10 minutes.
Add the onion and cook till it’s translucent, then add the soy beans and corn, stirring to heat them, a minute or so.
Add the butter and the cream, and raise the heat to medium high. Bring the cream and butter to a simmer, season the succotash with salt and plenty of freshly ground pepper, and serve.
