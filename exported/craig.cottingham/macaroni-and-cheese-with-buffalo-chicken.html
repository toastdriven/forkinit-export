Forkinit: Macaroni and Cheese with Buffalo Chicken

By Craig Cottingham

Ingredients

8 oz elbow macaroni
1 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 C thinly sliced celery
1/4 C finely chopped onion
1 tbsp all-purpose flour
1 1/4 C whole milk
2 1/2 tbsp Tabasco sauce
8 oz sharp cheddar cheese, cut into 3/4" cubes
4 oz pepper Jack cheese, cut into 3/4" cubes
1/2 rotisserie chicken, skin removed, meat shredded

For the topping:
1/2 tbsp unsalted butter
1/2 C panko bread crumbs
1/4 C crumbled blue cheese
1 1/2 tbsp chopped flat leaf parsley

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Spray 8" square baking dish with cooking spray.
Cook macaroni in boiling water until al dente, about 7 minutes. Drain well, then return to the cooking pot.
Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add celery and onion and cook 5 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally. Sprinkle flour over vegetables and cook 2 minutes, stirring frequently. Whisk in milk and heat to boiling over medium-high heat, stirring frequently. Stir in Tabasco, then cheddar and pepper Jack cheeses, adding about 1/2 C at a time and stirring until melted and smooth. Stir in chicken, mixing well to combine. Pour mixture over macaroni and toss to combine. Spread evenly in prepared baking dish.
In microwave-safe bowl, heat remaining butter on high 30 seconds or until melted. Stir in panko, blue cheese and parsley. Sprinkle evenly over macaroni mixture. Bake 30 to 40 minutes or until edges bubble and top is golden brown.

Yields: 6
