Forkinit: Peach Glaze

By Craig Cottingham
Good for topping a peach pie.

Ingredients

1 C sliced fresh peaches
1/2 C water
1 C sugar
3 tbsp cornstarch
1 tbsp butter

Instructions

Crush or pure√© peaches.
Add water, sugar and cornstarch.
Bring to a boil; cook over low heat until clear (2-3 minutes), stirring occasionally.
Add butter.
Cool slightly.

Yields: 1 1/2 C
Prep time: 5 min
