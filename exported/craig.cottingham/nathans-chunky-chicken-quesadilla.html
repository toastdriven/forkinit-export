Forkinit: Nathan's Chunky Chicken Quesadilla

By Craig Cottingham

Ingredients

4 large whole-wheat tortillas
2 C shredded cheddar cheese
1 Granny Smith apple, diced
1 red onion, diced
2 1/4 C cooked and sliced chicken or veggie chicken
3/4 C Peanut Sauce, recipe follows
sour cream, for garnish

Peanut Sauce:
1 C peanut butter
3/4 C hot water
1/8 C molasses
1 tsp soy sauce
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp sugar
1/8 tsp salt
1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions

Preheat grill or grill pan.
Place tortillas on a cutting board. On half of each tortilla place the cheese, apple, onion, and chicken. Fold the top half of the tortilla over and place on the grill.
Grill for 2 to 3 minutes on each side until the cheese has melted and the filling is warm.
Cut each quesadilla into 4 triangles.
Garnish with Peanut Sauce and sour cream.

Yields: 4
Prep time: 21 min
