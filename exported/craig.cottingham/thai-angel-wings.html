Forkinit: Thai Angel Wings

By Craig Cottingham
A perennial favourite at Vancouver's famous Salathai Restaurant on Cambie St. The look great, but they are easier to order than to make. They are not at all hot and spicy, just tasty. Serve with a sweet Thai and hot Thai chilli sauces.

Ingredients

1 lb chicken wings
1 C ground pork or chicken
1 C ground or minced shrimp
1/2 C mushrooms
1/2 C grated carrots
1 C bean thread noodles
2 tsp light soy sauce
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp ground coriander

Sauce:
1/2 C sugar
1/2 C water
1 tsp minced garlic
1/2 tsp salt
1 tsp chopped Thai red chilli

Instructions

Debone chicken wings leaving the wing tip intact, while removing the chicken meat and leaving the skin.
Mix all ingredients together.
Stuff each wing with mixture. You can always fold over the end flap of skin and secure it with a toothpick.
Deep fry until golden and cooked. Let cool.
Slice across each wing into pieces and serve with hot and sweet chilli sauces.

Sauce:
Combine all ingredients. Simmer until reduced and thickened.
