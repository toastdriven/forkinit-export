Forkinit: Indonesian Corn Fritters

By Craig Cottingham

Ingredients

3 ears corn, scraped and coarsely chopped
1/2 lb medium shrimp, shelled, deveined, and coarsely chopped
1 tsp chopped garlic
1/2 C finely chopped shallots or green onions
1 tsp ground coriander
1/4 tsp ground cumin
2 tbsp chopped cilantro
2 tbsp flour
1 tsp salt
2 eggs, beaten

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine corn, shrimp, garlic, green onions, coriander, cumin, cilantro, flour, salt and eggs.
Heat a thin layer of oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
Pour 1/4 cup of corn mixture into pan.
Add as many as will fit into the pan with 1/2-inch of space between the fritters.
Fry until golden brown and crisp; turn.
Cook about 1 minute on each side.
Remove and drain on paper towels.
Keep warm while frying remaining fritters.
Serve hot or at room temperature with sambal ulek (chili sauce) for dipping.

Yields: 6
