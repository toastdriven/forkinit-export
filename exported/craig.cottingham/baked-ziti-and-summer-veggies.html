Forkinit: Baked Ziti and Summer Veggies

By Craig Cottingham
Add pops of color to baked ziti with summer veggies like squash, zucchini, and tomato.

Ingredients

4 oz uncooked ziti
1 tbsp olive oil
2 C chopped yellow squash
1 C chopped zucchini
1/2 C chopped onion
2 C chopped tomato
2 garlic cloves, minced
1 C shredded part-skim mozzarella cheese, divided (4 oz)
2 tbsp chopped fresh basil
2 tsp chopped fresh oregano
3/4 tsp salt, divided
1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
1/4 C part-skim ricotta cheese (2 oz)
1 large egg, lightly beaten
cooking spray

Instructions

Cook pasta according to package directions, omitting salt and fat; drain.
Preheat oven to 400°F.
Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add oil to pan. Add squash, zucchini, and onion; sauté 5 minutes. Add tomato and garlic; sauté 3 minutes. Remove from heat; stir in pasta, 1/2 C mozzarella, herbs, 1/2 tsp salt, and pepper.
Combine ricotta, remaining salt, and egg. Stir into pasta mixture. Spoon into an 8-inch square glass or ceramic baking dish coated with cooking spray; sprinkle with remaining mozzarella. Bake at 400°F for 15 minutes or until bubbly and browned.

Yields: 4 servings, about 1 1/2 C
