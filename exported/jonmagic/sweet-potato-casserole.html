Forkinit: Sweet Potato Casserole

By Jon Magic

Ingredients

2 lg. cans of sweet potatoes, mashed
3/4 c. sugar
2 eggs, beaten
2 tsp. vanilla
1/2 c. milk
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 stick butter, melted
Mix all casserole ingredients well. Put in casserole dish.

Topping:
1 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. butter, melted
1/3 c. flour
1 c. chopped pecans

Instructions

Mix all topping ingredients and pour over top of sweet potato mix. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes.

Yields: 6
Prep time: 1 hour
