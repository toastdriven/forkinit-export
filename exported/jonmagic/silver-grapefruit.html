Forkinit: Silver Grapefruit

By Jon Magic
A grapefruit twist on a margarita (kind of) this drink is refreshing and yummy.

Ingredients

1 grapefruit
1oz of Patron Silver
1/2oz Grand Marneir
1/2oz fresh sweet and sour
Cocktail shaker
Chilled martini glass

Instructions

Make fresh grapefruit juice by squeezing all of the juice out of the grapefruit (I squeeze into a large bowl and then use a funnel to make sure not a single drop is lost when transferring it to the glass). 1 grapefruit should make about 1.5oz of juice.
In the shaker first add ice, then Patron Silver tequila, next Grand Marneir, and finally the grapefruit juice and sweet & sour.
Shake it for 1 minute (thats a long time, but its worth it).
Strain into chilled martini glass, sip, smile.

Yields: 1
Prep time: 10 minutes
