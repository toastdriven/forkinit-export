Forkinit: Island Booty

By Jon Magic
My wife came back from a pharmacy conference with little liquor samples from the vendors, and she made up this mojito recipe and its absolutely killer (and I don't like mojitos!).

Ingredients

pinch of raw sugar (large granules)
5 fresh mint leaves
Ice
1.5 ounces of Captain Morgan's Lime Bite Rum
1.5 ounces of Simply Orange Juice
1.5 ounces of Simply Limeaid

Tools:
muddler
straw

Instructions

Put the pinch of sugar in a tom collins style glass and then drop the mint leaves on top of the sugar.
Use the muddler to gently muddle the mint with the sugar, but be careful not to disintegrate the leaves because we don't want a million pieces in our glass.
Now fill the glass with ice.
Fill the glass 1/3rd of the way up with the Lime Bite Rum, then a 1/3rd with orange juice, then the last 1/3rd with limeaid.
Pop a straw in the glass and use it to mix the drink, then serve!

Yields: 1
Prep time: 2 minutes
