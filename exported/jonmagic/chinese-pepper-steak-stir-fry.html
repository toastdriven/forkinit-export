Forkinit: Chinese Pepper Steak Stir-Fry

By Jon Magic

Ingredients

1 lb of beef steak or venison steak
1/2 red bell pepper
1/2 green bell pepper
1 onion
3 green onions (scallions)
3 cloves of garlic minced
1 tsp fresh minced ginger
3 tbsp of soy sauce
1 tbsp of rice wine, dry sherry (I sub'd marsala cuz its all I had)
1 tsp sugar
1 tbsp corn starch
Vegetable oil for cooking

Instructions

If you use venison, marinate it in soy sauce before hand (other than the soy listed in the ingredients above). Chop all your veggies into whatever size you are comfortable eating, we like 1 to 2 cm squares.
Mix the soy, wine (or sherry), sugar, and corn starch together. Meanwhile heat 2 tbsp of oil in a wok to medium high heat until it just starts to smoke.
Toss in the meat (carefully) and stir-fry for 1 to 2 minutes and then remove the steak to a separate bowl.
Add enough oil to easily stir-fry the veggies, start by adding the peppers and onion. Stir-fry for 4 minutes and then add the garlic and ginger and stir-fry for 30 to 60 seconds.
Add the meat back in and then immediately add the sauce.

Yields: 4
Prep time: 30
