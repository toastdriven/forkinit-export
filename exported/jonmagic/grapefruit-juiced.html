Forkinit: Grapefruit Juiced

By Jon Magic
Lets make fresh squeezed grapefruit juice taste like fresh squeezed grapefruit juice, except its got gin in it now ;)

Ingredients

2oz of gin (we prefer New Amsterdam)
1 ruby red grapefruit
splash of sweet and sour
splash of TripleSec or Grand Marnier
glass full of ice

Instructions

Squeeze juice out of grapefruit and remove seeds.

Fill the glass with ice and then pour in the rest of the ingredients.

Stir, then sip. Should taste like it hasn't even been juiced up. Its magic.

Yields: 1
Prep time: 10 minutes
