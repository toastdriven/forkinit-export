Forkinit: Peach Cobbler

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
Back when McDonald's had peach cobbler it was my FAVORITE thing ever.... never tasted anything like it until now!!! AND this is a slow cooker recipe.

The original recipe calls for a 6qt cooker with the warning that is will not work in a 4 qt so I reduced the recipe down to a 4 qt size. Both recipes are here.

Ingredients

For 6 quart crock pot:
4 cups fresh or frozen sliced peeled (i didn't peel them) peaches
1/4 cup sugar
1 cup Original Bisquick mix (I substituted - 1 cup flour, 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, 1/2 tsp salt, 4 tsp butter or crisco)
1/2 cup sugar
1 cup milk

For 4 quart crock pot:
2 2/3 cups fresh or frozen sliced peeled ( i didn't peel them) peaches
3 Tbsp sugar
2/3 cup Original Bisquick mix (I substituted - 2/3 cup flour, 1 tsp baking powder, 1/3 tsp salt, 1 Tbsp butter or crisco)
1/3 cup sugar
1 cup milk

Instructions

Spray crock pot with non-stick cooling spray
In large bowel, gently toss peaches and 1st amount of sugar
Turn into crock pot
In medium bowl, gently beat Bisquick mix, sugar and milk with whisk until blended
Pour over peaches
Cover, cook for 3 hours on low heat setting or until cobbler is set in center
Serve cobbler with Ice Cream!!!!

Yields: 6-8
Prep time: 20minutes
