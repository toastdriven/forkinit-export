Forkinit: Baking Powder Biscuits

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker

Ingredients

6 cups flour
2 cups milk
1 1/2 sticks butter
3/4 cup sugar
1/4 cup baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

1/6th recipe
1 cup flour
1/3 cup milk
2 Tbsp butter
2 Tbsp sugar
2tsp baking powder
dash of salt

Instructions

Mix together with pastry fork
Cut out into round shapes
bake at 400 degrees for 10-12 minutes
