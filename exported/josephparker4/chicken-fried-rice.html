Forkinit: Chicken Fried Rice

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
One of my favorite recipes

Ingredients

4 cups cold cooked rice
8 oz cooked chicken -chopped
2 eggs
1/2 cup green peas
1 medium onion, diced
1 can water chestnuts-drained
1 green onion, diced
Seasonings
Soy Sauce
salt
pepper
oil for stir-frying as needed

Instructions

Beat the eggs.
Chop chicken and vegetables
Heat wok or LARGE frying pan and add oil.
Pour half the egg and cook turning once. Repeat with other half. Cut into thin strips and save for later.
Stir-fry the onion on med-high heat for a few minutes, set aside and do the same for the peas and water chestnuts.
Add oil, turn heat down and add brown rice.
add salt and pepper and whatever other seasoning you like.
Add the chicken, peas, onions, chestnuts, and egg
allow to heat thoroughly

Serve with soy sauce

you can also, use the green onion and egg as a garnish

Yields: 4
Prep time: 20
