Forkinit: Pie Crust no fail

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
Pretty much the best crust in the world

Ingredients

3 cups flour
1 cup crisco
1 tsp salt
1 egg
1 Tablespoon vinegar (or apple vinegar)
5-6 Tablespoons water

Instructions

Mix together flour and salt
cut in crisco until fine crumbs
Beat egg and add vinegar
Mix into flour
Add water as needed until dough stays together but is not sticky

Yields: Makes bottom and top crust
