Forkinit: Heavenly Cheesecake

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker

Ingredients

4 packages softened cream cheese
1 cup sugar
1 tsp vanilla
2 eggs
3 egg whites

Instructions

mix cream cheese and sugar with vanilla
add eggs one at a time
Blend until just mixed
Bake at 325 degrees for 45-50 minutes
