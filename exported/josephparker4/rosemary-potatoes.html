Forkinit: Rosemary Potatoes

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
Delicious alternative to baked potatoes

Ingredients

2 tablespoons olive oil (or 1 Tablespoon oil and 1 tablespoon butter)
Potatoes cut in 1 inch chunks (and sweet potatoes in fries)
Chopped onion (one medium one)
Rosemary
Salt


Instructions

2 tablespoons olive oil in pan (enough to cover the bottom)
Fill pan with potatoes cut in 1 inch chunks (and sweet potatoes in fries)
Add slices of onion (one medium one)
cover and cook for 5 minutes

Stir and add rosemary

Cover and cook until finished about 10 more minutes depending on amount of veggies
top with small sprinkling of salt

You can also use baby potatoes (quartered)
and Pearl onions

Prep time: 20
