Forkinit: Wonderful chocolate cake

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
from Ann Groeneweg

Ingredients

2 cup sugar
2 cup flour
½ cup cocoa
2 tsp soda
1 tsp salt
2 eggs
1 cup buttermilk or milk soured with vinegar
1 cup oil
1 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Mix all together until well blended.
Then add 1 cup boiling water or hot coffee
Stir until well blended
Pour into a greased 9x13 cake pan
Bake at 350⁰ for 35-40 minutes
