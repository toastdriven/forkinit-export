Forkinit: Brown Rice Muffins

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker

Ingredients

2 Tablespoons butter- softened
1 1/2 tsp honey
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 cup buttermilk (you may also decrease the buttermilk to 1/2 cup and add a heaping 1/2 cup of apple sauce)
1 1/2 cup brown rice flour

Options:
banana
black raspberries
nutmeg
cinnamon
apple

Instructions

Mix butter and honey together
add salt,baking powder, and baking soda mix well
add buttermilk
add rice flour slowly
mix well
add fruit if desired
Scoop into muffin pans filling 3/4 full
Bake 18-20 minutes at 375 degrees or until light brown

Prep time: 30
