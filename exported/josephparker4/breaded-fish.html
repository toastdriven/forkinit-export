Forkinit: Breaded fish

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
Brown rice

Ingredients

Fish
eggs
lemon juice
Brown rice flour (you can also use flour and cornmeal)
salt
pepper
celery seeds
ground oregano

Instructions

Beat one egg and 1 tsp of lemon juice
Breading:
Brown rice flour, salt, pepper, celery seeds, oregano
Dip fish in egg and then in the breading
Place into a skillet with hot oil
Cover and cook until meat is white about 5 minutes

Prep time: 10
