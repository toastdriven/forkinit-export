Forkinit: Tomato Pie

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker

Ingredients

6 sliced tomatoes (or 2 1/2 large)
3/4 cup mayonnaise
1 cup parmesan cheese
1 cup mozzarella cheese
basal leaves
pie crust

Instructions

Start with a pie crust (you may use store bought or homemade- I use 1/2 recipe of pie crust no fail)
slice tomatoes to cover bottom of pie crust
Mix together mayonnaise, and cheese
spread half of mix over tomatoes
place basal leaves on top
repeat with rest of ingredients
Bake at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes or until crust is golden brown
Let sit to solidify for at least 30 minutes
Serve warm
