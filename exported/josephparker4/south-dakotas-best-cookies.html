Forkinit: South Dakota's Best Cookies

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
The first cookies I ever ate!

Ingredients

2 eggs
1 cup white sugar
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup butter
1 tsp vanilla
3 cups oatmeal
2 cups flour
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 cup chocolate chips
1 cup coconut

Instructions

mix together ingredients
bake at 350 for 10-12 minutes
