Forkinit: Special K Bars

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker

Ingredients

1 cup Karo syrup
1 cup sugar
1 cup peanut butter
6 cups Rice Krispies
1 bag butterscotch chips
1 bag semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Heat syrup and sugar over medium heat
Stir often and bring to a boil
Remove from heat and add peanut butter
Stir well
Quickly pour mixture over Rice Krispies and mix well
Transfer mixture to a greased 9x13 pan
Gently press down evenly with a spatula
Heat chips on stove or in microwave (30 second increments and stir)
Pour melted chips on the Rice Krispie mixture
Put an Fridge to set melted chips
Thaw for 30 minutes before cutting
