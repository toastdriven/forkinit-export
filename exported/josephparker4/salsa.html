Forkinit: Salsa

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker
Grace Freeburg

Ingredients

16 cups chopped tomatoes
12 cups chopped onions
3-4 cups zucchini (50% peeled) and chopped
2 green pepper chopped
1 hot pepper (jalapeno)
2 1/2 cups white vinegar
1/2 cup sugar
2 tsp cummin
2-3 Tbsp salt
1tsp garlic powder
1tsp chili powder
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp alum
1 cup catch-up
15oz can tomato sauce
1 tsp tobacco sauce

Instructions

Heat tomatoes, onions,zucchini, and peppers to boiling
Simmer for ten minutes
Add remaining spices
Simmer for an hour
Pint jars hot water bath for 30 minutes

Yields: 7 pints
