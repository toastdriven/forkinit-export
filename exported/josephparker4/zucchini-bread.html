Forkinit: Zucchini Bread

By Jean Marie and Joseph Parker

Ingredients

3 eggs
3/4 cup brown sugar
3/4 cup white sugar
1/2 cup oil
1/2 cup milk
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp backing powder
2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp cloves
1 tsp vanilla
1 cup zucchini (graded)
1 cup carrots (graded or all zucchini)
3 cups flour (or 1 3/4 whole wheat and 1 cup white)
can put 1/4 cup chopped nuts

Instructions

Mix and place in 2 loaf pans
Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes

or can double it and place in 3 loaf pans and bake 55-60 minutes
