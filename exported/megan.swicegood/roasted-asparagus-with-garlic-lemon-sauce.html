Forkinit: Roasted Asparagus with Garlic Lemon Sauce

By Megan Swicegood
89 calories; 4 g fat; 10 g carbohydrates; 6 g protien

Ingredients

2 bunches of asparagus (about 2 pounds), trimmed
2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
1/8 teaspoon salt
2 tablespoons low-fat mayonnaise
2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
2 tablespoons water
1 tablespoon lemon juice
2 anchovy fillets, minced
1 small clove garlic, minced
2 chopped hard-boiled eggs

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425
2. Toss asparagus with oil and salt in a large bowl. Spread on a baking sheet and roast, stirring once halfway through until tender, 15-20 minutes.
3. Combine mayonnaise, parmesan, water, lemon juice, anchovies and garlic in a small bowl. Drizzle the asparagus with the sauce and top with hard-boiled egg

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 15 minutes active, 25 minutes total
