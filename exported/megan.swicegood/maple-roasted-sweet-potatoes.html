Forkinit: Maple-Roasted Sweet Potatoes

By Megan Swicegood
96 calories; 2 g fat; 19 g carbohydrate; 1 g protein

Ingredients

2 1/2 pounds sweet potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces (about 8 cups)
1/3 cup pure maple syrup
2 tablespoons butter, melted
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1/2 teaspoon salt
Freshly ground pepper to taste

Instructions

1. Preheat over to 400
2. Arrange sweet potatoes in an even layer in a 9-by-13-inch glass baking dish. Combine maple syrup, butter, lemon juice, salt and pepper in a small bowl. Pour the mixture over the sweet potatoes; toss to coat. Cover with foil.
3 Bake the sweet potatoes for 15 minutes. Remove the foil. Stir and continue baking, stirring every 15 minutes, until tender and starting to brown, 45 to 50 minutes.

Yields: 12 servings, about 1/2 cup each
Prep time: 10 minutes active, 1 hour 10 minutes
