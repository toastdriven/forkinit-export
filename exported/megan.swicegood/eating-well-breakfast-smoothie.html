Forkinit: Eating Well Breakfast Smoothie

By Megan Swicegood
Nice fruit smoothie - good for breakfast. 355 calories; 0 g fat, 14 g protein, 74 g carbohydrate

Ingredients

1 cup of nonfat vanilla yogurt
1/4 cup fruit juice
1.5 cups frozen fruit

Instructions

Puree nonfat vanilla yogurt with fruit juice until smooth. Keep motor running and add frozen fruit until smooth.

Yields: 1
Prep time: 5 minutes
