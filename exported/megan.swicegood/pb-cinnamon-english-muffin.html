Forkinit: PB Cinnamon English Muffin

By Megan Swicegood
quick & easy breakfast: 247 calories; 9 g fat; 10 g protein

Ingredients

1 whole-wheat English muffin
1 teaspoon sugar
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1 tablespoon peanut butter

Instructions

Toast muffin.
Mix cinnamon and sugar together
Top toasted muffin w/ Peanut Butter and sprinkle w/ cinnamon and sugar.

Yields: 1
Prep time: 5 minutes
