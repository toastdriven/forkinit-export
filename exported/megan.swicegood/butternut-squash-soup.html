Forkinit: Butternut Squash Soup

By Megan Swicegood
Sweet and spicy butternut squash soup

Ingredients

3 butternut squash, cooked, peeled, and cut up
1 gallon of water
2 yellow onion coarsely chopped
1 tablespoon cumin
.5 tablespoon white pepper
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
3 large carrots coarsely chopped
1/3 cup honey
3 gloves of garlic or 3 teaspoons garlic powder

Instructions

cut squash in half and bake at 350 for 20 minutes.
saute' onions in small amount of oil until soft, add garlic, carrots, cumin, white pepper, and italian seasoning continue to cook until carrots begin to soften.
Add squash, honey, and water simmer for 1 hour or until carrots are easily cut with a fork.
Puree into thick soup in a food processor or blender (or use an immersion blender)

Yields: 8
Prep time: 20 minutes active 1 hour inactive
