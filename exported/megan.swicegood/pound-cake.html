Forkinit: Pound Cake

By Megan Swicegood
Yummy dessert - rich, butter, thick, gooey pound cake - from The New Moosewood Cookbook

Ingredients

1 lb. (4 sticks) butter
3 cups sugar
6 eggs
4 cups flour
1 Tbs. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 cup milk
2 tsp. vanilla extract

Instructions

1) preheat oven to 350 F. Butter and flour the botton and sides of a 10-inch tube or bunt pan.
2) in a large bowl, cream together butter and sugar with an electric mixer at high speed until light and fluffy.
3) Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each. Set aside.
4) Sift together the dry ingredients in a separate bowl. Mix together the milk and vanilla. Add dry and wet alternately to butter mixture, beginning and ending with the dry. Mix by hand - just enough to blend thoroughly without excess beating. If adding fruit - add with the last of the dry ingredients.
5) Spread the batter into the prepared pan. Bake 50-60 minutes, or until a sharp knife inserted all the way down comes out clean. Allow to cool for 10 minutes in he pan, then turn out onto a plate. Cool completely before slicing.

Yields: 12-16
Prep time: 15 minutes
