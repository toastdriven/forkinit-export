Forkinit: Roasted Tomato Vinaigrette

By Megan Swicegood
Rich earthy salad dressing; 19 calories; 1 g carbohydrate, 0 g protien

Ingredients

12 ounces of plum tomatoes, halved lengthwise and cored
1 tablespoon chopped garlic
1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
1 teaspoon Italian seasoning mix
1 teaspoon kosher salt
freshly ground pepper to taste
2 tabelspoons sherry vinegar or red-wine vinegar

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 300 F. Coat an 8-inch-square baking dish with cooking spray.
2. Toss tomatoes, garlic, oil, Italian seasoning, salt and Pepper in a medium bowl. Spread the tomatoes in the prepared baking dish. Bake until the tomatoes are broken down and the juices are thick and syrupy. 1 hour 20 minutes to 1 hour 35 minutes.
3. Transfer the tomato to a blender. Add vinegar and puree. Cool completely before using.

Yields: 1 cup
Prep time: 10 minutes active 1 hour 40 minutes total
