Forkinit: Buttermilk-Herb Mashed Potatoes

By Megan Swicegood
85 calories; 2 g fat; 14 g carbohydrate; 2 g protein

Ingredients

2 large Yukon Gold potatoes, peeled and cut into chunks.
2 peeled garlic cloves.
2 teaspoons butter
1/4 cup nonfat buttermilk
1 tablespoon fresh hers (dill or chives)

Instructions

Place potatoes in medium saucepan and cover with water. Add the garlic cloves. Bring to a boil;cook until the potatoes are tender. Drain, add the butter and buttermilk. Mash with potato masher to the desired consistency. Stir in 1 tablespoon fresh herbs. Season with salt and freshly ground pepper

Yields: 4 servings
