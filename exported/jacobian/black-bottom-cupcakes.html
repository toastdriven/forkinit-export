Forkinit: Black Bottom Cupcakes

By Jacob Kaplan-Moss

Ingredients

The White:
8 ounces cream cheese
1/3 cup sugar
1 egg
2 ounces chopped bittersweet chocolate

The Black:
1 1/2 cups flour
1 cup brown sugar
5 tablespoons cocoa
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup water
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 tablespoon white vinegar
1 teaspoon vanilla

Instructions

Assemble The White via the creaming method.
Assemble The Black via the muffin method.
Pour The Black into lined muffin tins.
Drop spoonfuls of The White on top.
Bake at 350Â° for 25 minutes.

Yields: 12 cupcakes
Prep time: 15 minutes
