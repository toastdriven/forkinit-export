Forkinit: Herbalife instant apple cinnamon oatmeal shake

By Dustin Fluke
Basic and easy breakfast shake that tastes like apple cinnamon oatmeal

Ingredients

2 scoops vanilla Formula 1 powder
1 cup milk / soy / maybe water
1 packet of instant apple cinnamon oatmeal
1 cup of ice

Instructions

Put it in a blender and blend for 30 seconds

Note: it's gritty, so presoaking the instant powder may proove useful.

Optional: play the "will it blend" theme song

Yields: 1 12 oz serving
Prep time: 5 minutes
