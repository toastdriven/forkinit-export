Forkinit: Cream of Molasses Cookies

By Elissa Green

Ingredients

1 cup molasses
1 cup brown sugar
1 cup shortening
1 cup boiling water
1 TBS cinnamon
1/2 TBS ginger
1 tsp salt
1 scant TBS soda dissolved in hot water
3 1/2 cups flour

Instructions

First put together molasses, sugar and shortening with spices. Then add hot water with soda. Then add flour. Then put in the refrigerator overnight. In the morning bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes.

Source: Great Grandma Young
