Forkinit: Pumpkin Spice Coffee Syrup

By Melanie Streu
For homemade pumpkin lattes that are just as good as the ones from your favorite coffee shop.

Ingredients

1 tsp pumpkin pie mix
1 c. brown sugar
1 c. water

Instructions

(For pumpkin mix: 1 tsp cinnamon + 1/2 tsp nutmeg + 1/2 tsp ginger + 1/4 tsp cloves)
Stir together spice mix and brown sugar in medium sauce pan. Add 1 cup water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat and let simmer approximately 5 minutes, until reduced to half. Store in glass container in refrigerator.

Yields: about 1 cup
