Forkinit: Gumbo

By Christian Metts

Ingredients

1/4 cup + 1 Tbsp. bacon drippings or oil
1/4 cup + 1 Tbsp. flour
2 medium onions chopped
1 1/2 cups chopped celery
1 clove garlic, minced
1 can (28oz) tomatoes, undrained
1 can (16oz) tomato sauce
2 cans Ro-tel tomatoes with green chilies
5-6 cups water
1 Tbsp. salt
1 tsp. pepper
Either: 2 lbs. shrimp, 1 1/2 lb. crabmeat and 1 pint oysters or 2 lbs chicken (cooked) and 2 lbs sausage
1 pkg. cut okra
3 Tbsp. worstershire sauce

Instructions

Combine oil and flour in Dutch oven. Cook over meduim heat stirring 10-15 minutes. Color is of copper penny.
Stir in onion, celery, and garlic. Cook 5 minutes.
Add tomatoes and sauce and simmer 1 hour.
Add shrimp, crab and okra (or chicken, sausage and okra) and simmer 20 minutes.
Add oysters and worstershire sauce. Simmer 10 minutes.

Serve over rice.

Yields: Yields 4.5 quarts.
