Forkinit: Espresso Martinis

By Nick Lang

Ingredients

2 oz Vanilla Vodka
1 oz Coffee Liqueur
1 oz fresh shot of espresso

Instructions

Be sure to add the espresso to the shaker with ingredients within 30seconds of pulling the shot.

Mix ingredients into a shaker with ice, shake, pour.

Yields: 1
