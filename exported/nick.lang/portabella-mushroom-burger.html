Forkinit: Portabella Mushroom Burger

By Nick Lang
Portabella mushroom caps marinated and grilled to perfection

Ingredients

2 Portabella Mushroom caps
1/4 cup Balsamic vinegar
2 Tablespoons olive oil
1 teaspoon dry basil
1 teaspoon dry oregano
1 tablespoon minced garlic
1 cup grated provolone cheese

Instructions

Turn on grill and pre-heat to medium heat
Place mushroom caps on a plate, smooth side up
Whisk together vinegar, olive oil, basil, oregano and garlic
Brush the marinade on the mushrooms, nice and thick
Place mushroom on grill
Grill for 3-4 min
Flip, and spread more marinade
grill for 3-4 more min (or until tender)
Make sure the the smooth side is down, sprinkle the grated cheese over the mushroom cap and wait till melted.
Place on bun and enjoy!!

Yields: 2
Prep time: 5 min
