Forkinit: Nick's "Get-drunk-really-fast" Margaritas

By Nick Lang
The amazingly tasty, amazingly strong margaritas.

Ingredients

7 parts Jose Cuervo Gold Tequila
5 parts Triple Sec
3 parts lime juice

Instructions

For making batches of margaritas, I typically will take a 1.75L bottle of tequila, a 1L bottle of Triple Sec and a 750mL bottle of lime juice.

The lime juice is the key ingredient. If you're using fresh squeezed lime juice, you might want to sweeten it up a little with some sugar or simple syrup.

I prefer to use premixed lime juice that comes sweetened already.

Yields: a whole bunch!
Prep time: 3 min
