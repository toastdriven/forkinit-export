Forkinit: Brat Hottub

By Nick Lang
Beer brats in a hot tub!

Ingredients

One aluminum baking pan
6 pack of beer (typically a light beer, cheap beer is good for this like, Bud or Coors)
X Brats - as many as it takes!
1 Green Pepper
1 Onion

Instructions

Place the aluminum baking pan on the grill, and fill halfway with beer.
Heat up the grill
Chop up the onion and the green pepper and add to the hot tub.
Place brats in the hot tub till the tub is full.
Let the brats soak in the hot tub for a while while the grill heats up.
After about 15-20 min, take the brats out of the hot tub (leave the hot tub on the grill)
Grill the brats.
After the brats have been fully cooked it's time to serve.

Any brats left over after the first serving can go back into the hot tub to stay warm and soak up extra beery goodness!

Yields: As many brats as you can afford
Prep time: 15 min
