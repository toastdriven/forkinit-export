Forkinit: Quick Pasta Sauce

By barbara.shaurette
This sauce is adapted from the one used in my favorite vegan lasagne recipe - the original called for roasted red peppers, but a happy accident revealed that sun-dried tomatoes make for a slightly sweeter sauce that's just as rich.

Ingredients

2 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 medium onion, chopped
6 cloves garlic, minced
2 (10 ounce) jars sun-dried tomatoes
2 ripe beefsteak tomatoes, chopped
2 oz. bread (approximately 2 slices), torn into pieces
2 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper

Instructions

In a medium nonstick skillet, heat 1 tablespoon of the remaining oil over medium heat. Add the onion and cook, until softened, about 3 minutes. Add the garlic and cook, stirring, 1 minute. Set aside to cool slightly.

In a food processor, combine the onion mixture, tomatoes, bread, vinegar, remaining 1 tablespoon oil, salt, red pepper flakes and black pepper. Process until smooth and pureed.

I've been making this sauce a few days ahead of time, storing it in the refrigerator, then heating it up in a small saucepan when I'm ready to use it over pasta. I haven't tried freezing it yet, though, so I don't know how long it keeps that way.

Yields: 4 cups
Prep time: 10 mins
