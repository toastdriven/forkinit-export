Forkinit: Golden Pecan Pie

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

Crust:
1 refrigerated pie crust, softened as directed

Filling:
1/3 cup packed brown sugar
1 1/2 teaspoons all-purpose flour
1 1/4 cups light corn syrup
1 1/4 teaspoons vanilla
3 flax eggs (3 Tbsp ground flax meal + 6 Tbsp water)
1 1/2 cups chopped pecan halves
2 tablespoons Earth Balance margarine, melted

Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.

2. In large bowl, mix brown sugar, flour, corn syrup, vanilla and flax eggs until well blended. Stir in pecans and margarine. Pour into crust-lined pan.

3. Bake 40 to 50 minutes or until filling is puffed and pie is golden brown. Cool completely, about 2 hours. Store in refrigerator.

Yields: 8
