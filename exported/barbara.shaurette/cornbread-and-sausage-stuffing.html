Forkinit: Cornbread and 'Sausage' Stuffing

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 pound Field Roast apple-sage sausage
1 cup chopped celery
1 Tbsp finely chopped garlic
2 Tbsp finely chopped fresh sage
1 Tbsp finely chopped fresh thyme
1 cup low-sodium vegetable broth
Salt and pepper to taste
4 cups (1/2-inch cubes) cornbread (about 3/4 pound)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375Â°F.

Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add sausage and cook, breaking it up as you go, until browned and cooked through. Using a slotted spoon, transfer sausage to a large bowl and set aside.

Add celery and garlic to the skillet and cook until translucent. Add sage and thyme and cook about 1 minute more. Stir in broth, salt and pepper and bring to a boil. Add cornbread to bowl with sausage. Pour broth mixture over the top and mix until well combined. Transfer to a (9-inch) casserole dish and bake until hot throughout, 25 to 30 minutes. If desired, place under the broiler for a few minutes more to brown the top.
