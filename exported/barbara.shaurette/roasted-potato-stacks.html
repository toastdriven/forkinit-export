Forkinit: Roasted Potato Stacks

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

2 large potatoes, any variety
1/4 cup olive oil
1 Tbsp chopped rosemary
1-1/2 Tbsp lemon juice
sea salt
freshly ground black pepper

1 regular-sized muffin pan

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 220˚C (425˚F).

Lightly oil the 12 wells in a regular-sized muffin pan.

Slice all the potatoes thinly with a Mandoline or a sharp knife (peeling is optional). Wash the potatoes, drain well and pat dry with a paper towel.

In a large bowl, toss the potatoes with olive oil, rosemary, lemon juice and season with salt and pepper.

Stack the potatoe slices in the muffin pan wells and bake for about 25-30 minutes, or until the potatoes turn crispy on the outside and the flesh is soft.

Transfer the muffin pan to a rack to cool for 2 to 3 minutes before carefully removing the potatoes. Serve warm.

Yields: 1 dozen
Prep time: 15 mins
