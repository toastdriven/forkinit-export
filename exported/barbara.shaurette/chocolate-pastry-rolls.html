Forkinit: Chocolate Pastry Rolls

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 box of Pepperidge Farm puff pastry, thawed overnight
1 bar of vegan chocolate, grated
soy milk (or other non-dairy milk) for glazing

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350F. Grease a large baking sheet (or lay out a Silpat liner).

Onto a lightly floured surface unfold the pastry into two large squares.

Brush each square with soy milk and sprinkle grated chocolate on top - one bar of chocolate grated should be enough to split between the two pastry sheets.

Roll up the pastry, jelly roll style. As you do so, brush the pastry with soy milk to help seal it together.

Turn the rolled pastry seam side down and glaze the whole thing with soy milk.

Slice each roll into pieces about 1" wide and place onto the baking sheet leaving a large space between each.

Cook for 15 â€“ 20 minutes or until golden brown. Leave to cool on a wire rack.

Yields: 2 dozen
Prep time: 15 mins
