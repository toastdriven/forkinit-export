Forkinit: Stuffed Poblanos II

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 can (28 ounces) whole tomatoes in puree
1 can chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
3 garlic cloves (2 whole, 1 minced)
Coarse salt and ground pepper

1 can (19 ounces) adzuki beans, rinsed and drained
1/2 cup yellow cornmeal
1 cup shredded Daiya vegan cheese shreds, cheddar style
1 teaspoon ground cumin
12 oz. package Soyrizo

4 large poblano chiles, halved lengthwise (stems left intact), ribs and seeds removed

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.

2. In a blender, combine tomatoes, chipotles in sauce, and 2 whole garlic cloves; puree. Season with salt. Pour sauce into a 9-by-13-inch baking dish; set aside.

3. In a medium bowl, combine beans, cornmeal, 1/2 cup cheese, minced garlic, soyrizo, and cumin; season with salt and pepper.

4. Stuff poblano halves with bean mixture; place on top of sauce in baking dish. (If you have extra bean filling, you can add it to the dish.) Sprinkle poblanos with remaining 1/2 cup cheese; cover dish with aluminum foil.

5. Bake until poblanos are tender, about 45 minutes. Uncover, and continue to cook until sauce is thickened slightly and cheese is browned, 10 to 15 minutes more, rotating after 6 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes.

Yields: 4
