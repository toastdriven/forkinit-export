Forkinit: Nepali Sesame Cabbage

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds
1/4 cup toasted sesame oil
1/4 cup water
3 Tablespoons lemon juice
1/2 medium-sized cabbage, shredded
1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1 teaspoon salt
2 Tablespoons sesame oil
1/4 teaspoon turmeric

Instructions

Blend the sesame seeds, water, sesame oil and lemon juice until they form a smooth paste.

In a large bowl, combine the sesame paste, cabbage, salt and cayenne.

In a wok, heat the oil over high heat until it begins to smoke. Immediately add the turmeric. Toss in the cabbage mixture and heat until warmed through, about 5 mins.

Yields: 4-6
Prep time: 15 mins
