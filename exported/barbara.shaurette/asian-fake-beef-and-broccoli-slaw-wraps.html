Forkinit: Asian (Fake) Beef and Broccoli Slaw Wraps

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

2 12-oz packages Lightlife Smart Ground Veggie Protein Crumbles, Original
Sesame oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
3 cups cabbage coleslaw mix
1/4 cup General Tso's sauce
4 burrito-size (approx. 10-inch diameter) flour tortillas, warmed

Instructions

Heat large nonstick skillet over medium heat until hot. Add crumbles and sesame oil and cook 8 to 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper. Stir in slaw mix and 1/4 hoisin sauce; heat through.

Spread 1/4 of mixture (approx. 1 cup) evenly over each tortilla, leaving 1-1/2-inch border on all sides. Fold right and left edges of tortilla over filling; fold bottom edge up over filling, then roll up tightly, jelly roll fashion. Place seam-side down on plates; cut in half, if desired. Serve with additional sauce, if desired.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 10 mins
