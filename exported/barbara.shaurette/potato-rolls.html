Forkinit: Potato Rolls

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

3/4 cup soy milk
1 cup cooked mashed potato
2 Tbsp margarine, melted
2 Tbsp unsweetened applesauce
2-1/4 tsp rapid rise yeast
3 to 3-1/2 cups bread flour
1 tsp salt

Instructions

Heat soymilk in the microwave for about 1 minute.

In a bowl, mash the potato, margarine and applesauce until smooth.

In a separate mixing bowl, combine the yeast, 2 cups of flour and salt.

Add the soy milk and potato mixture to the flour mixture and mix until
combined. Add the rest of the flour 1/4 cup at a time.

Knead dough for 5 minutes.

Lightly oil the inside of a large bowl and then place your kneaded
dough in it. Turn over to coat the top of the dough. Place a towel
over the bowl. Let the dough rise for about an hour or until doubled
in size.

Next, lightly oil a 13x9-inch pan. Flour your counter and pour the
dough out onto the floured surface. Knead a few times and then
separate into 12 equal rolls.

Place in the prepared dish. Place the towel back over the rolls and
let them rise for another 30 minutes to an hour.

Place in a preheated 400 degree oven for 15-20 minutes. Bake until golden brown.

Yields: 1 dozen
Prep time: 2 hrs
