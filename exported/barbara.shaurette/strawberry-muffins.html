Forkinit: Strawberry Muffins

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1-1/2 C flour
1/2 C oatmeal
1/2 C sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1 Tbsp flaxmeal
3 Tbsp water
1/4 C veggie oil
1 C almond milk
1/2 C strawberries, chopped into pea-sized pieces

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 375F.

In a large mixing bowl, whisk together the flour, oatmeal, sugar, and baking powder.

In a small mixing bowl, whisk the flaxmeal and water, then whisk in the almond milk and oil until all of the wet ingredients are really well combined.

Pour the wet ingredients into the bowl with the dry, and mix just until combined. Gently fold in the sliced strawberries.

Pour the batter a into lined muffin tin and bake 20-22 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

Yields: 12 muffins
Prep time: 10 mins
