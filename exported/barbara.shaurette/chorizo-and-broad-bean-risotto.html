Forkinit: Chorizo and broad bean risotto

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

2 tbsp olive oil
1/2 C vegan bacon bits
2 garlic cloves, crushed
2 Field Roast Mexican Chipotle sausages (http://www.fieldroast.com/products/retail/field-roast-sausages/), sliced into half-moons
2 C risotto rice
4 C hot vegetable stock
2 C lima beans

Instructions

Heat the oil in a medium-sized pan, then add the bacon bits, garlic and
chorizo. Gently cook for 6-8 mins, stirring occasionally so everything
cooks evenly.

Stir in the rice, coating all over with the cooking juices in the pan. (Feel free to add more oil at this point, since the vegan sausage won't add any to the pan.)

Add just enough stock to cover the rice, bring to a simmer and gently
cook until all the liquid is absorbed. Continue adding the stock a
ladleful at a time, and gently cook.

Just before the final ladleful of stock, add the broad beans and stir through, cooking for 3-4 mins until tender.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 15-20 mins
