Forkinit: Savory Vegan Pie

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 package frozen mixed veggies (approx. 10 oz)
1/3 cup vegan margarine
1/3 cup flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
1-3/4 cup vegetable broth
2/3 cup soy milk
2 cups sliced vegan sausage
Frozen whole wheat vegan pie crusts (one for the bottom and one for the top)

Instructions

Set out frozen vegan pie crust for about 20-30 minutes to thaw while you are preparing the rest of the ingredients.

Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.

Melt margarine in saucepan over medium heat.

Stir in flour, salt and pepper, broth and soy milk. Heat to boiling, stirring constantly for 1 minute. Stir in sausage and veggies; remove from heat.

Pour the mix from the saucepan into the bottom crust. Cover with the top crust and crimp the edges. Also make a few slits on top of the crust so the steam can escape.

Bake 35 minutes, until golden brown. Let cool at least 10 minutes before serving.
