Forkinit: Vegan Bran Muffins

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 large mashed banana
1/4 cup unsweetened applesauce
1/4 cup molasses
1/4 cup raspberry preserves/jam
1 cup soy/rice milk
1/8 cup brown sugar
1 cup raisins
2 cups raw bran

1-1/4 cup flour
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees and line a 12-muffin tin with paper liners.

In a large bowl mix banana, applesauce, molasses, raspberry jam, milk, brown sugar, raisins and bran.

In a small bowl whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.

Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix well. Pour into muffin cups. Bake for 20 minutes.

Yields: 12
Prep time: 10 mins
