Forkinit: Whole Wheat Bread with Olive Oil

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

7 cups whole wheat flour
1-1/2 Tbsp granulated yeast (or 2 packets)
1 Tbsp kosher salt
1/4 C vital wheat gluten
3-1/2 C lukewarm water
1/2 C olive oil

Instructions

Whisk together the flour, yeast, salt and vital wheat gluten in a mixer bowl.

Add the liquid ingredients and mix without kneading, using the dough hook attachment.

Cover (not airtight) and allow the dough to rest for approximately two hours, until it rises and collapses.

Refrigerate in a lidded (notairtight) container.

On baking day: dust the surface of the refrigerated dough and cut off half of the original piece. Dust with more flour and shape into a ball by stretching the surface of the dough around to the bottom on all four sides, rotating the ball a quarter turn as you go.

Elongate the ball into a narrow oval. Allow the loaf to rest, loosely covered with plastic wrap, on a silicon mat for 90 minutes.

Thirty minutes before baking, preheat the oven to 450. Place an empty broiler tray on any other rack that won't interfere with the bread.

Just before baking, put the dough into a large loaf pan. Use a pastry brush to paint the top with water. Slash the loaf with 1/4-inch deep cuts, using a serrated bread knife.

Slide the loaf pan into the hot oven. Put about 1-2 cups of water into the broiler tray and quickly close the oven door.

Bake for about 30 minutes. Allow the bread to cool on a rack before slicing and eating.

Yields: 2 loaves
Prep time: 5-10 minutes prep; 30 minutes baking
