Forkinit: Cabbage-Corn Slaw

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

2 teaspoons honey
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
kosher salt and black pepper
1/4 small green cabbage (1/2 pound), cored and shredded
1 tomato, thinly sliced
1 cup corn kernels (from 1 to 2 ears)

Instructions

In a large bowl, whisk together the honey, oil, lemon juice, 1/2 teaspoon salt, and 1/4 teaspoon pepper.

Add the cabbage, bell pepper, and corn and toss to combine.

(Optional) Chill for about 15 minutes before serving.

Yields: 6
Prep time: 10 min
