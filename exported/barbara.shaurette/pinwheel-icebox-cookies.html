Forkinit: Pinwheel Icebox Cookies

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 cup vegan margarine (8 ounces)
1 cup granulated sugar
3 tsp Ener-G egg replacer + 4 Tbsp water
1 teaspoon vanilla
3 cups flour
Raspberry jam

Instructions

In large mixing bowl cream butter and sugar together; add egg replacer and vanilla and beat until light and fluffy. Gradually add flour. Divide dough into 2 equal portions. Form each portion into ball. Place each ball between two sheets of waxed paper lightly dusted with powdered sugar or flour and roll each into a rectangle. Spread a thin layer of raspberry jam over each rectangle.

Starting at long edge, roll each rectangle into a log. Refrigerate dough for at least 4 hours, until very firm. Cut in 1/4-inch slices and place on a greased baking sheet about an inch apart. Bake at 350Â° for 8 to 10 minutes.

Prep time: 20 mins
