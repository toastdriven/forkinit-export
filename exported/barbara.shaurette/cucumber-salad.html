Forkinit: Cucumber Salad

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

3 large cucumbers, peeled, halved lengthwise, seeded, and cut into 1/4-inch slices
1 Tbsp salt
1/2 cup white sugar
1/2 cup rice wine vinegar
1/4 cup water
1 Tbsp garlic chili pepper sauce
garnish: 3 Tbsp chopped mint, 3 Tbsp chopped peanuts

Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, combine the sugar, vinegar, water, chili
pepper sauce and salt. Stir, cook over a medium heat until sugar is
dissolved

2. Add cucumbers and onion to appropriately sized dish that will
allow all the cucumber mixture to be covered with marinade.

3. Pour vinegar mixture over the vegetables and let marinate for 30 minutes.

4. Just before serving, sprinkle with mint and peanuts.
