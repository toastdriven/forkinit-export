Forkinit: Chocolate Almond Butter Cookies

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1/2 C whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp cinnamon
3/4 C almond butter
1/2 C brown sugar
3 Tbs flax meal plus 3 Tbs water
1 bar of your favorite chocolate, broken into 20 1/2 inch pieces.

Instructions

In a small bowl mix the flax meal and water. Cream together the almond butter and brown sugar. Whisk together the other dry ingredients: whole wheat flour, baking soda, and cinnamon. Add flax mixture to almond/sugar mixture, then add in dry ingedients. Mix until incorporated. Using your hands grab 1" size balls and stuff a hunk of dark choclate into the center of each, roll into balls and place on an ungreased baking sheet.

Bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes

Yields: 20 cookies
Prep time: 10 mins
