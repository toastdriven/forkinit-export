Forkinit: Black-Eyed Pea Hummus with Olive Oil

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

3 cups cooked black-eyed peas
2 tsp. garlic puree or minced garlic
6 T fresh-squeezed lemon juice
1/2 tsp. salt
2 T olive oil
1/2 tsp. ground cumin
6 T tahini
water to thin hummus as needed
whole wheat pita bread for serving

Instructions

Rinse the black-eyed peas with cold water and let drain for a minute or two, then put them into the food processor with the garlic, lemon juice, salt and olive oil and process until pureed, about 1 minute.

Add the ground cumin and tahini and process until combined.

If the hummus seems too thick, add a few tablespoons of water and process.

Optional for serving: drizzle with olive oil, sprinkle with Sumac (or a smaller amount of paprika.) Serve with whole wheat pita bread.

Hummus will keep in the fridge for at least a week.

Yields: 3 cups
Prep time: 5 mins
