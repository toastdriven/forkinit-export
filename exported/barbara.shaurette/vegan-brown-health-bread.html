Forkinit: Vegan 'Brown Health Bread'

By barbara.shaurette
This came from the recipes section of the web site for an Irish dairy - Cuinneog. I 'veganized' it by replacing the buttermilk, egg, and butter, and it turned out delicious. It's great just lightly toasted with a little bit of margarine, but I think it'll also be a great soup accompaniment.

Ingredients

12 ozs (1-1/2 cups) whole wheat flour
4 ozs (1/2 cup) all-purpose flour
2 Tbsp oat bran
2 Tbsp oats
1 Tbsp wheat germ
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 cup soy milk
1 egg replacement (1 Tbsp ground flaxseed meal + 3 Tbsp water)
1/4 C Earth Balance or other vegan margarine

Instructions

Sift white flour into a bowl.

Fold in the whole wheat flour, oat bran, oats.

Sift baking soda and salt into the bowl.

Rub in the butter.

Make a well in the center of the flour mixture; add the flax egg and soy milk.

Mix to a fairly loose dough, knead lightly and form into a cake.

Bake in a covered dish at 425F for 35 minutes.

Uncover and cook for a further 10 minutes or until fully baked.

Yields: 1 loaf
Prep time: 10 mins
