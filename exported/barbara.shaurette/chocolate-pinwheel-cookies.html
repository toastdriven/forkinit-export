Forkinit: Chocolate Pinwheel Cookies

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

Cookies
1/2 cup vegan margarine
1 cup granulated sugar
1 tsp almond extract
3 tbsp flaxseed meal mixed with 6 tbsp water - microwave for 18 seconds
2 tsp baking powder
2-1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 tsp salt
2 tsp almond milk

Filling
1/8 cup cocoa powder
1/8 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

Cream together margarine and sugar until smooth. Add almond extract, prepared flaxseed meal and baking powder.

In separate bowl, whisk together flour and salt. Gradually mix into the wet ingredients until well incorporated and slightly crumbly. Gradually add the 2 tsp almond milk, mixing until the dough clumps together into one or two balls. Chill briefly in freezer; while dough is chilling, mix together cocoa powder and sugar to make filling.

Once dough is chilled, cover a workable area with flour. Place dough in the center and gently roll out into about an 8"x12" rectangle. To make sure it will roll up nicely, gently slide a spatula underneath to loosen.

Once you have your rectangle rolled out, sprinkle generously with sugar mixture (you may have some of the filling mixture left over).

Now, firmly grasp the dough and roll up lengthwise, gently squeezing to keep taut as you go along. Pinch the edges slightly to keep the roll closed.

Transfer to a baking sheet lined with wax paper and chill in the freezer for about 15 minutes.

Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.

Using a sharp knife, cut 1/2" sections from the roll. Place slices on an ungreased cookie sheet; bake for 8 minutes.

Transfer to a wire rack and let cool thoroughly before eating.

Once they are cool, feel free to drizzle a little melted chocolate on top for added yumminess.
