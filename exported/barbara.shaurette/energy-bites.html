Forkinit: Energy Bites

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1/2 C nut butter (almond, peanut, whatever you have on hand)
1/3 C honey (or brown rice syrup)
1 C old fashioned rolled oats
1 C shredded unsweetened coconut
2 tsp vanilla
pinch of sea salt
1/2 C other addins (roughly chopped nuts or seeds, dried fruit, chocolate chips, etc.)

Instructions

In a large bowl, mix the nut butter, honey, vanilla and salt. If all of your ingredients are at room temperature you should be able to stir to combine. Once mixed, add the remaining ingredients.

Once itâ€™s all combined place in the fridge to chill for about 30 minutes. Scoop the dough into 1" balls and place in an airtight container in the fridge.

Yields: 2 dozen
Prep time: 10 mins
