Forkinit: Vegan Ranch Dressing

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1/2 cup soy milk
1/2 cup soy vegan mayonnaise
1 teaspoon onion powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
2-3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon dried or fresh parsley (finely chopped)
salt & pepper to taste

Instructions

Mix all ingredients in a food processor. That's it. To make it thicker, for use as a vegetable dip, omit the vinegar and add a little more vegan mayonnaise.

Prep time: 5 mins
