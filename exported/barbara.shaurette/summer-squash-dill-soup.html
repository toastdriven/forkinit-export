Forkinit: Summer Squash Dill Soup

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

8 ribs celery, cut into 1" chunks
3 cloves garlic, crushed
3 Tbsp olive oil
1/2 tsp celery seeds
1 tsp salt, plus 1-1/2 tsp more
cayenne (or paprika) to taste
1 c white wine
2 Yukon gold potatoes, peeled and cut into 1" chunks
5 c water
8-10 golden summer squash, cut into 1" chunks
1/4 to 1/3 c fresh dill, chopped

Instructions

In a large stock pot, combine olive oil, celery, garlic, celery seeds, tsp salt, and cayenne (or paprika). Cook covered over low heat until vegetables are soft.

Raise heat to medium and cook until vegetables start to color. Add white wine and stir. Cook until the liquid is reduced by half.

Add potatoes, water and additional salt and simmer until potatoes are very tender (about 20 minutes).

Add squash and cook until just tender (about 10 minutes).

Using a stick blender, puree until smooth. Stir in fresh dill and adjust salt and cayenne (or paprika) to taste.

Yields: 6-8 servings
Prep time: 15 mins
