Forkinit: Crispy Grilled Kale and Smokey Tofu Risotto

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

About 3 tbsp. olive oil, divided
1 teaspoon minced garlic
3/4 teaspoon kosher salt, divided
1/2 teaspoon pepper, divided
1 small bunch (6 oz.) Tuscan kale, stems and coarse ribs removed
1 ounce thinly sliced smoked tofu
2 cups Arborio rice
2 tablespoons finely chopped onion
6-1/2 cups reduced-sodium chicken-flavored veg broth
1/2 cup Follow Your Heart vegan mozzarella
1/3 cup chopped toasted walnuts
2 tablespoons unsalted Earth Balance, cut into chunks
1/4 cup chopped oil-packed dried tomatoes

Instructions

1. Turn on the broiler. In a large bowl, mix 1 tbsp. oil, the garlic, and 1/4 tsp. each salt and pepper. Add kale and toss to coat well. Arrange on a baking pan in a single layer and broil until slightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes. Set aside to cool.

2. Brown tofu in a few tp of sesame oil, in a medium saucepan over medium heat, about 4 minutes. Transfer tofu to small bowl, leaving about 2 tbsp of oil in the pan.

3. Add rice to pan, reduce heat to medium, and cook, stirring, until rice starts to toast slightly, 1 to 2 minutes. Add onion, 1/2 tsp. salt, and 1/4 tsp. pepper; cook until onion is softened, 2 to 3 minutes. Add enough broth to just cover rice; cook, stirring constantly, until liquid is absorbed. Continue adding broth to just cover rice and stirring until it's barely tender, 15 to 18 minutes. If needed, add more broth so risotto is loose-textured; it will thicken a bit as it stands.

4. Remove risotto from heat and loosely fold in tofu, mozzarella, walnuts, margarine, and tomatoes. Fold in most of kale and spoon into a serving dish. Top with remaining kale and serve immediately.

Yields: 6-8
Prep time: 50 mins
