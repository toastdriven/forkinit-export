Forkinit: Green Curry Savory Pie

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

2 medium zucchini
2 long purple Asian eggplants
1 can unsweetened coconut milk
2 to 3 tablespoons green curry paste
1-1/2 cups vegetable broth or water
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon brown sugar
2 tablespoons corn starch
6 to 8 wild lime leaves, torn or cut in half (optional)
A dash of dried basil
1 premade pie crust

Instructions

Slice and drain the zucchini and eggplant ahead of time. Cut the zucchini in half lengthwise and then crosswise into 1" chunks; set aside. Cut the eggplant into 1" chunks.

Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.

In a medium saucepan or heavy skillet, bring 1/2 of the coconut milk to a gentle boil over medium-high heat. Cook for 2 minutes, until it begins to thicken and becomes fragrant. Add the curry paste and cook 2 minutes, pressing and stirring to dissolve it into the coconut milk.

Add the remaining coconut milk, the vegetable broth, zucchini and eggplant, soy sauce, brown sugar, corn starch and about half the wild lime leaves, if using, and bring to a gentle boil. Reduce heat to maintain a lively simmer and cook, stirring now and then, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove from the heat and stir in the remaining lime leaves and the basil.

Pour the mix from the saucepan into the bottom crust. Cover with the top crust and crimp the edges. Also make a few slits on top of the crust so the steam can escape.

Bake 35 minutes, until golden brown. Let cool at least 10 minutes before serving.

Prep time: 20 mins
