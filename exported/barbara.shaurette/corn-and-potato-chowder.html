Forkinit: Corn and Potato Chowder

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 tablespoon Earth Balance
1 cup chopped onion
3/4 cup chopped green pepper
Kosher salt and black pepper
1 tablespoon minced garlic
2 tablespoons flour
4 cups vegetable broth
4 cups peeled, finely diced all-purpose potato
1 1/2 cups shredded carrot
2 cups fresh corn kernels
1 cup almond or soy milk
1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
Pinch of cayenne pepper
2 tablespoons chopped fresh Italian parsley

Instructions

Sling the butter into a soup kettle and melt over medium-high heat. Toss in the onions and peppers, seasoning them with a pinch of salt and pepper. Cook till soft and then throw in the garlic, cooking it all for 1 minute more.

Sprinkle on the flour and mix into the veggies. Dump in the broth and potatoes. Cover the pot and bring to a boil; then lower the heat and simmer for 10 to 12 minutes, or till the potatoes are tender. Add the shredded carrots and corn. Cover and simmer everything 5 to 6 minutes longer to blend the flavors.

Stir in the milk. Season with the thyme, cayenne, some more salt, and lots of freshly ground black pepper to taste. Sprinkle with parsley. Give it one last stir, and ladle it up piping hot.

Yields: 8
Prep time: 20 mins
