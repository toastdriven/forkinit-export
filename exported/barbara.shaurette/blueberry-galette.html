Forkinit: Blueberry Galette

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

Pastry:

1-1/2 cups unbleached all purpose flour
1 tablespoon sugar plus 1 teaspoon
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 stick (1/2 cup) cold margarine (Earth Balance)
1 flax egg (1 Tbsp ground flax seed + 3 Tbsp water)
3 to 4 tablespoons ice water

Filling:

3 cups blueberries
1/2 cup sugar
2 tablespoons cornstarch
1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
1 teaspoon grated lemon zest
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 tablespoon margarine (Earth Balance)

Instructions

1. Make the pastry: In a large bowl, mix together the flour, 1 tablespoon of the sugar and the salt. Add the margarine and blend with a fork until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Add the flax egg and 3 tablespoons of water. Pulse the machine or stir with a fork until the mixture just begins to come together and form a ball. If it seems dry, add a little more water. Do not over mix.

2. Scrape the dough onto a piece of plastic wrap and form it into a disk. Wrap tightly and refrigerate at least one hour or up to 3 days.

3. Make the filling: In a large bowl, toss together the blueberries, sugar, cornstarch, lemon juice and zest, cinnamon and salt.

4. Preheat the oven to 425° F. margarine a large baking sheet.

5. On a lightly floured surface, roll out the dough to a 14-inch circle. Center the dough on the baking sheet. Pile the blueberry mixture in the center of the dough leaving a 1-1/2-inch border all around. Dot the filling with the margarine. Pleating it as you go, fold the border of the dough over the filling.

6. Bake 25 to 30 minutes or until the pastry is golden and the filling is bubbly. Cool the pan on a wire rack 10 minutes. Slide the galette onto a serving platter. Serve warm or at room temperature dusted with confectioner’s sugar.

Yields: Serves 6 to 8
