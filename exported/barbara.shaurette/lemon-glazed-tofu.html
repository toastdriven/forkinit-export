Forkinit: Lemon-Glazed Tofu

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 (14 ounce) block extra firm tofu, drained and pressed
1 tablespoon soy sauce
1 tablespoon lemon juice
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 teaspoon sugar (agave nectar is fine too)
1 tablespoon dijon mustard
1 teaspoon Italian herb blend (basil, thyme, rosemary, oregano)
salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

Cut tofu into strips or cubes. Whisk together all other ingredients. Put mixture in a shallow pan or zip-lock bag, and add tofu to coat well.

Allow tofu to marinate for 1 hour+ (the longer the better), turning to coat well with marinade.

Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Put tofu and marinade in a baking dish, and bake for 25-30 minutes, turning halfway through. Pour extra marinade over the tofu, as needed. Add salt and pepper, to taste.

Yields: 4-6
Prep time: 30 mins
