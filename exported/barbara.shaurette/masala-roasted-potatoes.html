Forkinit: Masala Roasted Potatoes

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

12-15 washed and halved Baby Red Potatoes
Oil, 3 tbsp

Mustard seeds, 1 tsp
Cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp
Curry leaves, 4-5

Green Chilli, 2 chopped (or to taste)
Ginger, 1" minced
Garlic, 3 fat cloves minced

Salt, to taste
Turmeric, 1/4 tsp

Cilantro and mint leaves, 1/2 cup packed
Chaat Masala, 1 tsp
Cayenne pepper powder, 1/2 tsp or to taste
Lemon juice, 2 tsp

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400ÂºF.

2. Grease a large non stick pan and add the potatoes and set aside.

3. In an medium pan add in the oil on medium heat. Add in the mustard, cumin seeds, followed with curry leaves. Allow them to splutter. Add in the chilli, ginger and garlic and cook for a minute. Add the salt and turmeric. Take off the heat and pour it over the potatoes and stir to coat evenly.

4. Bake the potatoes until tender and browned, about 25-30 minutes. Keep stirring every 10 minutes. Remove from the oven and stir in the cilantro, mint, chaat masala, cayenne pepper and lemon juice. Serve hot with some ketchup and lemon wedges. ENJOY!

Yields: 4
Prep time: 30 mins
