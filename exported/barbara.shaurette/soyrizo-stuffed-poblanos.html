Forkinit: Soyrizo-Stuffed Poblanos

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 tablespoon oil
12 oz. package Soyrizo
2 tsp garlic, minced
2 cups brown rice (cooked)
1 14-1/2 oz. can whole kernel corn
1 cup low-sodium vegetable stock
1/2 cup white wine
1/2 cup water
4 large, fresh poblano chiles
1 cup Daiya Cheddar Style Shreds

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Place poblano chiles on baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes. Remove from oven and let cool for about 10 minutes. Once cool, lay the poblanos flat, and cut a T-shaped slit into the top of each one. Pry the slit apart, and remove all of the ribs and seeds.

While the poblanos are baking, prepare the filling: In a medium saucepan, heat oil and soyrizo, cook for 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another 3 minutes. Add rice and corn and cook until all the grains of the rice are coated with oil. Add all liquids and stir over high heat for 3 minutes, cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer for about 15 minutes.

Stuff chiles with 1/4 of the rice mixture. Place all the chiles on baking sheet and place into oven for 5 minutes. Remove from oven, top each pepper with Daiya. Return to oven and broil for 3 minutes to melt and brown the Daiya.

Yields: 4
Prep time: 45 mins
