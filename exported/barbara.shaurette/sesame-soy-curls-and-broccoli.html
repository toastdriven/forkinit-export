Forkinit: Sesame Soy Curls and Broccoli

By barbara.shaurette
I've also made this with a head of cabbage - you could probably substitute just about any vegetable that will stir-fry within a few minutes.

Ingredients

1/2 cup toasted sesame seeds
2 T low-sodium tamari
2 T tomato paste
1-1/2 T toasted sesame oil
1-1/2 T hoisin sauce
1 T sugar or natural sweetener
1 T rice wine vinegar
2 T safflower or canola oil
1 pound Soy Curls
2 cups broccoli florets, blanched
2 scallions, minced

Instructions

Rehydrate soy curls with boiling water.

Using a stick blender, grind the sesame seeds, tamari, tomato paste, sesame oil, hoisin sauce, sugar and rice wine vinegar in a bowl.

Heat oil in a large skillet, add soy curls, and stir-fry until browned, about 5 minutes.

Add broccoli and scallions and stir-fry another minute.

Add sauce, turning to coat soy curls and broccoli.

Yields: 4-6
Prep time: 15 mins
