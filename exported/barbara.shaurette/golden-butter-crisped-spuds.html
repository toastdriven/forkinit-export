Forkinit: Golden Butter-Crisped Spuds

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

4 lbs. yukon gold potatoes, quartered
7 tbsp. cold butter, cubed
Salt and pepper

Instructions

In a pot, cover potatoes with cold water and bring to boil. Cook until fork-tender, about 15 minutes; drain. Add potatoes, butter, 1 1/2 tsp. salt and 3/4 tsp. pepper to large bowl; using a jerking motion, flip until coated and slightly softened. Spread on baking sheet and roast at 425Â° until a golden crust forms, 20 minutes.
