Forkinit: Cherry Corn Muffins

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1-1/4 cups all-purpose flour
3/4 cup yellow corn meal
2/3 cup dried tart cherries
1/2 cup granulated sugar
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
1 cup soy milk
1/4 cup vegetable oil
1-1/2 tsp Ener-G Egg Replacer + 2 Tbsp water (or egg substitute of your choice, equivalent of 1 egg)
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

Combine flour, corn meal, cherries, sugar, baking powder and salt in a medium mixing bowl; mix well.

Stir in soy milk, vegetable oil, egg replacer and vanilla just until dry ingredients are moistened.

Fill paper-lined muffin cups 3/4 full with batter.

Bake in a preheated 400-degree oven 20 to 25 minutes, or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean.

Let cool in pan 5 minutes.

Remove from pan and serve warm or at room temperature.

Yields: 12
Prep time: 15
