Forkinit: Caramelized Onions and Sweet Red Onion Pasta

By barbara.shaurette
The sweet red onion pasta came from a vendor at our local farmer's market - http://www.pappardellesonline.com/servlet/Detail?no=70 - but I would think this would go just as well with a plain linguine.

Ingredients

1 lb. Pappardelle’s Sweet Red Onion Pasta
2 tablespoons Earth Balance
2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
4 large onions, halved, thinly sliced
3 red bell peppers, roasted, peeled and chopped*
1 cup water or veggie broth
1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
1/2 tablespoon fennel seeds
1/2 cup vegan parmesan

Instructions

1. Melt 1 tablespoon Earth Balance with oil in heavy large skillet over medium-high heat. Add onions and sauté until tender and caramelized, about 20 minutes. Add roasted peppers. Saute 5 minutes.

2. Add water/broth, vinegar, and fennel seeds. Reduce heat to medium and simmer until sauce reduces slightly, about 5 minutes. Whisk in remaining 1 tablespoon Earth Balance. Season sauce with salt and pepper. Remove from heat and cover to keep warm.

3. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain. Return to pot. Add sauce and toss to coat. Transfer to serving bowl. Sprinkle with vegan parm and serve.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 30 mins
