Forkinit: Mashed Potato Pizza

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1 tbsp. chopped fresh rosemary
2 tbsp. EVOO
2 cups leftover mashed potatoes
12-inch prebaked thin pizza crust

Instructions

Microwave rosemary and EVOO until fragrant, about 30 seconds; strain and reserve oil. Stir rosemary into potatoes. Spread over crust and drizzle with rosemary oil.

Optional: sprinkle with frozen peas and fake ground beef (e.g., Lightlife Smart Ground).

Bake at 450Â° until golden, about 20 minutes.

Yields: 6 servings
Prep time: 30 mins
