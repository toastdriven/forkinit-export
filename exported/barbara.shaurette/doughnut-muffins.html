Forkinit: Doughnut Muffins

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

3/4 C sugar, plus 1 C extra for dusting
2 C plain flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 C soy yogurt
2 flax eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
3/4 C butter, melted
12 tsp seedless raspberry jam

Instructions

Heat oven to 375.

Lightly grease a 12-hole muffin tin. Put 3/4 C sugar, flour and baking soda in a bowl and mix to combine. In a separate bowl, whisk together the yogurt, flax eggs and vanilla.

Tip the wet ingredients and melted butter into the dry ingredients and quickly fold with a metal spoon to combine.

Divide two-thirds of the mixture between the muffin holes. Carefully add 1 tsp jam into the center of each, then cover with the remaining mixture.

Bake for 16-18 mins until risen, golden and springy to touch.

Leave the muffins to cool for 5 mins before lifting out of the tin and rolling in the extra sugar.

Yields: 12
Prep time: 20-30 mins
