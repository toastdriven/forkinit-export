Forkinit: Smoky Mexican Meatball Stew

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

1-1/2 tbsp olive oil
1 packet taco seasoning (or 1 tsp each ground cumin and coriander)
1/2 tbsp chipotle paste
2 bags soy meatballs
2 c can chopped tomatoes
1 c can kidney beans, drained and rinsed
small handful cilantro, to serve

Instructions

In a large bowl, toss the meatballs with the oil, seasoning, and chipotle paste. Heat a little extra oil in a large frying pan. Fry the meatballs for about 5 mins until brown.

Add the tomatoes, as well as a can full of water. Simmer for 5 mins, then add the kidney beans and cook for a further 10 mins, until the sauce has thickened.

Scatter with coriander and serve over rice.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 15 mins
