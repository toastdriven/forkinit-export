Forkinit: Sauteed Green Beans

By barbara.shaurette

Ingredients

5 slices LightLife Smart Bacon
1 Tbsp Earth Balance margarine
1/2 cup slivered almonds
1 lb. fresh green beans
1/4 cup Earth Balance margarine

Instructions

1. Place fake bacon and margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook until browned on one side, then turn over to cook on the other side. Add the almonds to the skillet at this time, and cook, stirring until light golden brown.

2. Remove the fake bacon from the skillet. Crumble or chop bacon, and return to the skillet. Melt the margarine in with the drippings. Add the green beans; cook and stir until tender,
about 8 minutes.
