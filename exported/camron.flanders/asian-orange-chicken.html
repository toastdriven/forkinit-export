Forkinit: Asian Orange Chicken

By Camron Flanders
Fun to make and rather enjoyable to eat. Asian spicy orange chicken and sauce adds to a special dinner with rice, your favorite steamed vegetables and egg rolls. This recipe is for two servings. Enjoy!

Ingredients

3 Chicken breasts

Marinade:
1/2 c. fresh orange juice
2 minced garlic cloves
2 Tbsp. fine orange zest
1 Tbsp. minced ginger
1/2 c. rice vinegar
2 Tbsp. soy sauce
salt and pepper

Coating:
2 c. flour
salt and pepper
Optional: egg wash

Orange Sauce:
Marinade
2 red chili peppers or 1 tsp. cayenne pepper
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. orange juice
3 tsp. cornstarch
2 Tbsp. chopped green onions
Cooking oil

Instructions


1:
Mix marinade ingredients, pour into a large resealable bag and add one inch cubed chicken meat. Refrigerate 2 or more hours, shake it up occasionally.

2:
Save marinade and put chicken cubes in resealable bag of flour mixture.
(Optional: Place chicken in egg wash then again into flour mix in resealable bag). Shake up to completely cover chicken.

3:
Place flour coated chicken in 375 degree cooking oil for 5 minutes or until golden. Set aside on paper towels to absorb oils.

4:
Thicken spicy orange sauce
Pour marinade into a large skillet, add other ingredients and cook until it nicely thickens. Add chicken and cook for one minute.

5:
Great with rice and extra orange sauce
Serve while hot with extra sauce on the side.

Yields: 2
