Forkinit: Portable Apple &amp; Cheese Pleasers

By monihami
High protein breakfast cookies, a favorite with children; low in sugar

Ingredients

3/4 c all-purpose flour 2/3 c butter or margarine, softened
1/3 c brown sugar 1 egg
1 tsp vanilla 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp baking powder 1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 c oatmeal, old fashioned, uncooked 1 c(4 oz) shredded Cheddar cheese
3/4 c raisins or currants 1 c chopped apple

Instructions

1. Combine flour, butter, sugar, eff, vanilla, cinnamon, baking powder and salt in a large bowl; mix well.
1. Add oats, cheese and raisins or currants; mix well Stir in apple.
1. Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet; bake in preheated moderate oven (375 degreses F) 15 minutes or until golden brown.
1. Store in tightly covered container in refrigerator or in loosely covered container at room temperature.

Yields: about 2 dozen
Source: Quaker Oats Wholegrain Cookbook
