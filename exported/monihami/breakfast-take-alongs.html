Forkinit: Breakfast Take Alongs

By monihami
Crisp cookies made with bacon and cheese;high in protein and good nutrition

Ingredients

2/3 c butter or margarine 2/3 c sugar
1 egg 1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp soda 1/2 tsp salt
1 c (4 oz) shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 c wheat germ or finely chopped nuts
6 crisply cooked bacon slices, crumbled
3/4 c flour or 1/2 flour and 1/4 cup Shaklee instant protein
1 1/2 c oatmeal, old fashioned, uncooked

Instructions

1. Beat together butter, sugar, egg and vanilla until well blended. Add combined flour, soda and salt; mix well.
1. Stir in oats, cheese, wheat germ and bacon.
1. Drop by rounded tablespoonfuls onto greased cookie sheet; bake in preheated moderate oven (350 degrees F) 12-14 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
1. Cool one minute on cookie sheet; remove to wire cooling rack.
1. Store in loosely covered container in refrigerator or at room temperature.
1. Makes about 3 dozen cookies.

Yields: about 3 dozen
Source: Quaker Oats Wholegrain Cookbook
