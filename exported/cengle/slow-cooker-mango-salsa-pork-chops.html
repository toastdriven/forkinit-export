Forkinit: Slow Cooker Mango Salsa Pork Chops

By Cheryl Engle
slow roasted to tender perfection with a melt in your mouth taste; easy to make; a definite company dish

Ingredients

boneless,center cut, one inch thick pork chops
small can pineapple juice
1/4 cup dark brown sugar
mango salsa (purchased at Sam's Club)

Instructions

Mix together the pineapple juice and dark brown sugar. Pour a little on the bottom of the slow cooker. Put chops in and cover with salsa and the rest of the pineapple juice/dark brown sugar mixture. Cook on low for three hours, and then on high for three hours.

Yields: 2-4 servings
Prep time: 15 minutes
