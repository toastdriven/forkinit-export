Forkinit: Seasoned Steamed New Carrots

By Moriah Lindsley
A zippy twist on steamed carrots.

Ingredients

1 bunch new carrots
1/2 T butter (vegan)
Parsley, to taste
Granulated Garlic Powder, to taste
Salsa Salad Seasoning, to taste

Instructions

1. Steam new carrots for 5 to 8 minutes.
2. Toss in butter, then sprinkle with seasonings.
