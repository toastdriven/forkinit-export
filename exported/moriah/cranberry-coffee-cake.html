Forkinit: Cranberry Coffee Cake

By Moriah Lindsley
A great brunch item!

Ingredients

3 C fresh or frozen cranberries
1 C chopped walnuts
2 C sugar, divided
3/4 C butter or margarine, softened (vegan)
3 eggs, lightly beaten (Egg Replacer)
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 1/2 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt

TOPPING:
1 T sugar
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

In a greased 9-in. square baking pan, combine cranberries, walnuts and 1 C sugar. In a mixing bowl, cream (vegan) butter and the remaining sugar. Add eggs (Egg Replacer) and vanilla; mix well. Combine flour, baking powder and salt; add to creamed mixture and mix well. Drop batter by large tablespoonfuls over cranberry mixture; carefully spread to cover. For topping, combine sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle over batter. Bake at 350Â°F for 50-60 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted near the center comes out clean.
Yield: 8-10 servings
