Forkinit: Reuben Crock Pot Sandwich Snacks

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 qt sauerkraut, drained and rinsed
3 (2 1/2 oz) pkg corned beef, diced OR 7 1/2 oz seitan, thinly diced
1 lb Swiss cheese, shredded OR vegan Monterey Jack
1 C mayonnaise OR plain soy yogurt
1/2 C Thousand Island dressing

Instructions

Mix ingredients together in a crock pot on low heat until heated through (approximately 2 hours). Serve on rye bread.
