Forkinit: Chocolate Lover's Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley
To go with Moist and Tender Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

3 C sifted powdered sugar, divided
2/3 C NESTLE TOLL HOUSE Baking Cocoa
1/2 C (1 stick) [vegan] butter or margarine, softened
5 to 6 T [rice] milk, divided
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Beat 1 C powdered sugar, cocoa, [vegan] butter, 2 T [rice] milk and vanilla extract in small mixer bowl until creamy. Gradually beat in remaining sugar and [rice] milk until smooth.
Makes 2 Cups

Source: Nestle Toll House Cocoa
