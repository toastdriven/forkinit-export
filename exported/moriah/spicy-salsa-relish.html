Forkinit: Spicy Salsa Relish

By Moriah Lindsley
A versatile recipe, using pre-made or fresh ingredients.

Ingredients

1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes or 2 lg diced tomatoes
1/4 C Dehydrated Sliced Onions or 1 thinly sliced medium onion
1 T Salsa Salad Seasoning
1/4 tsp dried Cilantro or 1 tsp minced fresh cilantro
1 T olive oil
1 tsp balsamic vinegar
1/4 tsp pure maple syrup
1 to 3 tsp California Crushed Red Pepper Flakes

Instructions

Blend all ingredients and serve with tortilla chips.
