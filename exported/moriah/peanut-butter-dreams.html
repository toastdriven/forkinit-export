Forkinit: Peanut Butter Dreams

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C vegan butter or margarine
2 C packed brown sugar
1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
Egg replacer equal to 2 eggs
1 C peanut butter
3 C flour
2 tsp baking soda
1/8 tsp salt
2 C chocolate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Cream together thoroughly the butter, brown sugar and Vanilla. Add egg replacer, mixing well. Then add the peanut butter, flour, baking soda and salt. Mix well. Fold in the chocolate chips. Drop by the teaspoon onto ungreased cookie sheets. Bake at 375Â°F for 10-12 minutes.
