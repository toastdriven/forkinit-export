Forkinit: Minestrone Soup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Nonstick cooking spray
1 C carrots, julienned
1 C celery, cut on a bias
1 C zucchini or yellow summer squash, sliced
2/3 C onion, chopped
1/2 of a 15 oz can cannellini, great northern or red kidney beans, rinsed and drained
4 C reduced-sodium vegetable broth
1 C water
2/3 C elbow macaroni, ditalini, bow-ties, small shells or tri-colored rotini
1 (14 oz) can low-sodium whole tomatoes, cut up
Herb Pistou (a mixture of basil, rosemary and garlic, moistened with a bit of oil)

Instructions

1. Spray a 4-quart Dutch oven with nonstick cooking spray. Add carrots, celery, zucchini and onion and cook for 10 minutes. Stir in beans, broth and water. Bring to boiling; reduce heat. Simmer for 15 minutes.
2. Stir in pasta. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes more or until pasta is just tender. Stir in tomatoes and Pistou. Heat through.
Makes 5 side-dish servings

Herb Pistou: In a small bowl stir together 2 T snipped fresh basil or 2 tsp dried basil, crushed; 2 tsp snipped fresh rosemary or 1/4 tsp dried rosemary, crushed; 2 cloves garlic, minced; 1/4 tsp pepper; and 1/8 tsp salt. Stir in 1 tsp cooking oil to make a paste.

Source: Adapted from Better Homes and Gardens' Healthy Family Cookbook
