Forkinit: Hazelnut Herb-Crusted Field Roast

By Moriah Lindsley
A breaded and fried patty made from wheat roast, herbs, rice and nuts.

Ingredients

-------------------------------------
Filtered water
Wheat protein (vital wheat gluten flour)
Toasted hazelnuts

Cranberries

Yeast extract
Garlic
Lemon juice
Organic unbleached white wheat flour
Lentils
Garlic powder
Yellow pecan flour
Onion powder
Parsley
Carrageenan
Spices

Breading
-------
Artisan bread crumbs
Organic wheat flour
Toasted hazelnut meal and nuts
Onion powder
Chives
Spices

Instructions

After making patties with the first part, dip them in the breading and fry until golden brown.
