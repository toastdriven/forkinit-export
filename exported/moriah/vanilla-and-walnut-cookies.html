Forkinit: Vanilla and Walnut Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

150g (5.3 oz) dairy free margarine
150g (5.3 oz) brown sugar
1 tablespoon maple syrup
1 tablespoon vanilla paste (or extract)
250g (8.8 oz) all purpose flour (half whole wheat flour would work as well)
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 180 degrees.
Cream the margarine and sugar together.
Stir in the maple syrup and vanilla paste.
Add the walnuts, sifted flour and baking powder and mix well (you may need to use your hands).
Bake on a greased cookie tray for 10 â€“ 15 minutes. Leave the cookies to cool for 10 minutes before removing from the tray.

Yields: About 24 cookies
