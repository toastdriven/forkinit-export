Forkinit: Caramelized Onion Pizza

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 ready to bake pizza crust
2 T olive oil
4 C sliced yellow onions (about 3 medium onions)
1 T minced garlic
2 T balsamic vinegar
1/2 C grilled sweet bell peppers, chopped
6 oz vegan mozzarella
1/4 C chopped grilled seitan (optional)

Instructions

To caramelize the onions, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook over medium-low heat for about 20-30 minutes, stirring frequently, until the onions are very soft and lightly browned. Add the balsamic vinegar and cook a few more minutes, then remove from heat.

Preheat the grill to low-medium heat (or preheat oven to 400Â°F).

Spread the caramelized onions evenly over the pizza crust. Top the onions with the chopped peppers, vegan cheese, and grilled seitan (if using). Bake for 10-15 minutes, making sure the crust does not burn. Remove from the grill or oven and let set 5 minutes before slicing.

Yields: 1 pizza
Prep time: 40 minutes
