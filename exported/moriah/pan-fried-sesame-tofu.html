Forkinit: Pan-Fried Sesame Tofu

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

14 oz 365 Organic Everyday Value Tofu
1/4 C cornstarch or flour for dusting
Canola or corn oil for frying
1/2 C sesame seeds, lightly toasted
1 bunch scallions, trimmed and cut into 1" pieces

<u>Sauce</u>
1/3 C honey (or agave nectar)
3 T tamari
3 T finely minced ginger
2 T sesame oil (not toasted)
2 T rice wine vinegar
2 cloves finely minced garlic
1/2 tsp red chili flakes

Instructions

Wrap tofu with paper towels and place on a cutting board. Put another cutting board or heavy plate on top to press out liquid for about 20 minutes. Stir together sauce ingredients in a saucepan. Simmer sauce while you cook tofu. Dry drained tofu with paper towels and cube. Dust very lightly with cornstarch or flour. Heat 1" oil in a deep frying pan. Fry tofu in 350Ã‚Â°F oil until golden brown. Place fried tofu on a large platter or bowl and toss with 2/3 C warmed sauce, sprinkle liberally with sesame seeds and scallions, toss gently. Serve with remaining sauce for dipping or to drizzle over vegetables. Serves 4.

<i>Serve with steamed bok choy or broccoli, sauteed watercress or eggplant, and brown rice.</i>
