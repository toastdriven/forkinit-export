Forkinit: Berry Explosion Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/4 C frozen blueberries
1/4 C frozen strawberries or raspberries
1/2 banana
1 serving Vega One Berry
1 C cold water (optional unsweetened berry juice)
2-3 ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

Blend until smooth.

Yields: 1 smoothie
Source: Vega
