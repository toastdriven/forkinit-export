Forkinit: Vegetarian Minestrone Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
Adapted from the Good Housekeeping "Minestrone Soup with Ground Beef" recipe.

Ingredients

1 lb beans of choice
1 small onion, chopped
1 large celery stalk, sliced
1/4 small head green cabbage, thinly sliced
1 14-1/2 to 16 oz can tomatoes
1 7-oz can whole-kernel corn
1 can mushrooms
1 medium-sized zucchini (about 10 oz), sliced
3/4 C medium-sized shell macaroni
1 can vegetable broth or 1 cube vegetable bouillon dissolved in 2 C water
1 tsp dried basil leaves

Instructions

1. Cook beans ahead of time in a separate pot; drain.
2. In soup pot, cook onion, celery and cabbage.
3. Add beans, tomatoes, corn, and mushrooms, all with their liquid, zucchini, macaroni, and vegetable broth or dissolved bouillon. Over high heat, heat to boiling, stirring to break up tomatoes. Reduce heat to low; cover and simmer soup 8 to 10 minutes until macaroni and vegetables are tender.
4. Garnish with basil leaves and serve.
