Forkinit: Vegan Cheese

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 c sunflower seeds
2 c cashews (soaked overnight)
1/4 c nutritional yeast
2 1/2 c warm water
1 tsp ACV
1 1/2 tsp fresh lemon juice
Few twists pick salt
Several shakes crushed red pepper flakes (less if you don't like heat)
1/2 tsp each garlic, mustard powder, turmeric
1 pkg Pamona's Pectin

Instructions

Oil a glass bowl with olive oil, set aside. Mix calcium pack with 1/2 C water, set aside.
In a food processor, blend everything but the pectin. Add remaining 2 C water to a pot and bring to a boil. Slowly add the pectin, mix for 2-3 minutes. Add seeds/spice mixture to combine, then add calcium water.
Once everything looks fairly smooth, carefully pour into oiled glass bowl and smooth out. Refrigerate 2-3 hours, uncovered, to set.
Enjoy shredded, sliced or with crackers!
