Forkinit: Butternut Squash Sweet Potato Soup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 C vegetable stock
1 medium sweet potato, diced
1 butternut squash (~2 lbs), diced
1 leek, chopped
1 T Thai red curry paste
1 tsp fresh garlic, minced
1" fresh ginger, minced
1-2 C coconut milk beverage, or 1 can coconut milk
Sesame oil, to garnish
Salt, to taste

Instructions

Add first seven ingredients to a large pot and bring to a boil. Simmer, covered, until squash and sweet potato are soft, about 20-25 minutes. Puree in a blender or with an immersion stick. Put soup back into the pot and add coconut milk. Heat through. Garnish soup with a drizzle of sesame oil and a crack of fresh salt to taste.

Source: Based off soup in the deli at The Community Mercantile
