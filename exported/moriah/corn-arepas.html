Forkinit: Corn Arepas

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 1/4 C gluten-free vegetable broth or water
1 T corn oil or olive oil
1/4 tsp salt
2 C masa harina
1 tsp cumin
1 C fresh cooked or canned white corn
1 T olive oil

Instructions

1. In a saucepan over high heat, bring 2 1/4 C broth, oil, and salt to a boil. In a mixing bowl, combine the masa harina and cumin. Pour the liquid over the masa and stir until the consistency of a batter. Add the corn. Stir until combined.
2. Cover and set aside for ten minutes.
3. Divide dough into 8 pieces. Form each into a ball and then press into 1/2" patties.
4. Grease a griddle pan or cast iron pan with remaining tablespoon oil and heat pan to medium high heat until hot.
5. Working in batches and taking care not to overcrowd the pan, cook the cakes until blackened, about 4 to 6 minutes per side.
Serve warm.
