Forkinit: Spicy Carrot Bars

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/4 C brown sugar
2/3 C vegan butter
Egg replacer equal to 1 egg
2 tsp vanilla
1 1/3 C all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1 1/3 C quick oats
1 C carrots, shredded
1/2 C nuts, chopped
Powdered sugar, optional

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Beat sugar and vegan butter until light and fluffy; blend in egg replacer and vanilla. Combine next five ingredients and add to butter mixture, mixing well. Stir in oats, carrots and nuts. Spread evenly in greased 13x9" pan. Bake 25-30 minutes. Cool; sprinkle with powdered sugar if desired. Cut into bars.

Yields: 8 large bars
