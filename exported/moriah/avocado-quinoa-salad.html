Forkinit: Avocado Quinoa Salad

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 ripe avocados
2 C vegetable broth
1 large tomato, diced
2 stalks celery, diced
1 C quinoa
2-3 T lime juice
1/2 cucumber, diced
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

In medium saucepan, cook quinoa according to package directions, using vegetable broth in place of water. Let cool. Cut avocados in half, remove pit and peel. Cut into chunks. Toss with lime juice. Add avocado and all remaining ingredients to quinoa and toss gently to mix. Chill until ready to serve.
