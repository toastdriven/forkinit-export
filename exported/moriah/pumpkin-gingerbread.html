Forkinit: Pumpkin Gingerbread

By Moriah Lindsley
A spiced dessert bread, great for the holidays!

Ingredients

2 eggs (or Egg Replacer)
1/2 C egg substitute (applesauce, mashed banana, etc.)
1 can (15 oz) solid-pack pumpkin
1 1/2 C honey (Agave nectar or maple syrup)
1/2 C butter or stick margarine, melted (vegan)
1/2 C fat-free plain yogurt (soy)
1 C wheat germ
3 C all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking soda
2 tsp ground ginger
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp ground cloves

ICING:
3/4 C confectioners' sugar
2 tsp fat-free milk (non-dairy)
1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

In a large bowl, beat eggs (Egg Replacer) and egg substitute. Stir in the pumpkin, honey (Agave or syrup), (vegan) butter, and (soy) yogurt until smooth. Stir in wheat germ. Combine the dry ingredients; gradually add to pumpkin mixture and mix well. Coat a 10" fluted tube pan with nonstick cooking spray and dust with flour; add batter.
Bake at 350Â°F for 55-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack to cool completely. Combine icing ingredients until smooth; drizzle over cake.
Yield: 16 servings
