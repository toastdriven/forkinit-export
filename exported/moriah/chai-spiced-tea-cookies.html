Forkinit: Chai-Spiced Tea Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C (1 stick) vegan butter, room temperature
1/4 C granulated sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground cloves
3/4 tsp ground cardamom
1/4 tsp salt
1 C all-purpose flour
3/4 C powdered sugar

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Beat butter with granulated sugar in a medium bowl. Stir in vanilla, spices, salt and flour. Scoop teaspoons of dough and roll with hands into small balls. Place on a baking sheet, spacing them about 2" apart. Bake until lightly golden, 15 to 20 minutes. Cool on the sheet for 5 minutes. Place powdered sugar in a pie plate. Roll warm cookies in sugar and cool cookies on a rack.
