Forkinit: Orange Creamsicles

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 can full fat coconut milk
1 cup orange juice concentrate
3 tbsp agave nectar
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Blend all the ingredients together until mixture is completely smooth.
Pour into popsicle molds and place in freezer until frozen all the way through.
Pop out of molds and enjoy!

Notes: The amount of orange juice concentrate and agave nectar can be adjusted to taste. I like a strong orange flavor, but you may want to start with 1/2 to 3/4 cup concentrate and adjust accordingly.

Source: Sarah McMinn - "The Sweet Life", http://www.thesweetlifeonline.com/2014/05/22/homemade-orange-creamsicle/
