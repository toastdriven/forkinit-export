Forkinit: Blueberry Lemon Tart Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C blueberries
2 T lemon juice (the juice of one lemon)
1 serving Vega One French Vanilla or Natural flavor
1/2 C water
1/2 C ice (omit if using frozen blueberries)
1/2 C spinach (optional)

Instructions

Pre-prep: Fill blender pitcher with the fresh ingredients and put in the fridge. In the morning, add ice, water and a scoop of Vega One to make a smoothie in less than a minute.

Yields: 1 smoothie
