Forkinit: Baked Apple Streusel

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 medium apples, peeled, cored and thinly sliced (use firm apples)
1/2 C unpacked brown sugar
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
2 T fresh lemon juice
1/4 C all-purpose flour
1/4 C uncooked old fashioned oats
3 T reduced-calorie margarine

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
2. Stir together apples, 1/4 C of brown sugar, cinnamon and lemon juice; pour into a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
3. Cut flour, oatmeal, remaining brown sugar and margarine together with a pastry blender or fork in a medium bowl; sprinkle over apple mixture.
4. Bake until apples are tender and top is browned, about 45 minutes. Yields about 1/2 C per serving.
