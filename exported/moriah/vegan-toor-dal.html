Forkinit: Vegan Toor Dal

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C red lentils
1 clove garlic
1/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp coriander
1/4 tsp turmeric
1/4 tsp cayenne (optional, does not make it very hot though)
1/4 tsp black mustard seed
salt to taste
3 C water
1/2 T (or less) canola oil--(you can leave this out...I always do and just add the spices right to the lentils)

Instructions

1. Rinse lentils and bring to a boil in 3 C water. Reduce heat and cover. Cook for 30-40 minutes until lentils are very soft, the consistency of thick soup.

2. Add cumin, coriander, turmeric, salt and cayenne. Allow to simmer uncovered while preparing garlic and mustard seeds. In a small fryingpan, heat oil to med hi. Mince garlic and sautÃƒÂ© in oil with mustard seeds until just beginning to brown. Add to dal and serve immediately.
