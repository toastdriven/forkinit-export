Forkinit: Chocolate Creme Cakes

By Moriah Lindsley
Moist layers of chocolate cake sandwich a sweet and creamy filling.

Ingredients

1 pkg (18-1/4 ounces) chocolate cake mix
1 pkg (3.9 ounces) instant chocolate pudding mix
3/4 C vegetable oil
3/4 C water
4 eggs (or Egg Replacer)

FILLING:
3 T all-purpose flour
1 C milk (non-dairy)
1/2 C butter, softened (vegan)
1/2 C shortening (vegan)
1 C sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

1. In a large mixing bowl, combine cake and pudding mixes, oil, water and eggs (Egg Replacer); mix well. Pour into a greased and floured 13x9x2 inch baking pan. Bake at 350Â°F for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool for 10 minutes; invert onto a wire rack to cool completely.

2. In a small saucepan, combine flour and (non-dairy) milk until smooth. Bring to a boil; cook and stir for 2 minutes or until thickened. Cool. In a mixing bowl, cream the (vegan) butter, (vegan) shortening, sugar and vanilla; beat in milk mixture until sugar is dissolved, about 5 minutes.

3. Split cake into two horizontal layers. Spread filling over the bottom layer; cover with top layer. Cut into serving-size pieces. Freeze in an airtight container for up to 1 month. Remove from freezer 1 hour before serving. Yield: 12-18 servings.
