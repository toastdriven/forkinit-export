Forkinit: Banana Cake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C whole-wheat pastry flour
2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 C wheat germ
4 medium ripe bananas, mashed
1/2 C sugar
3/4 C vanilla-flavored soy milk
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/3 C blueberries or raisins
Vegetable cooking spray, for pans

Instructions

1. Pre-heat oven to 350Â°F.
2. In a medium sized bowl, mix whole wheat pastry flour, baking soda, salt and wheat germ.
3. In a large bowl, mash bananas and mix in sugar.
4. Mix in soymilk and vanilla.
5. Add the flour mixture, stir lightly to mix.
6. Fold in blueberries.
7. Lightly coat 2 8" cake pans, and spread half the batter into each.
8. Bake for about 20-25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
