Forkinit: Raw Cashew Ice Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 Cup of water (or 1 cup coconut water)
3/4 cup of cashews (raw preferably)
3/4 cup of pitted mejol dates
Pinch of sea salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
2 tablespoons of cocoa powder (raw preferably)

Instructions

Put in a blender and blend until thick and smooth.
Pour into your ice cream maker and make according to instructions OR place in container and freeze for 1 hour.
