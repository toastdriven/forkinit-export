Forkinit: Cinnamon Maca Bars

By Moriah Lindsley
Containing crunchy raw nuts, sweet coconut and no cooking, these delicious maca bars are easy to make and are the perfect snack to provide an instant energy boost whenever you want it.

Ingredients

1/2 cup soaked almonds
1/2 cup soaked cashews
1/2 cup maca
Dash of sea salt
2 dates, pitted
2 T raw honey
1 cup shredded coconut
1/4 cup coconut oil
2 tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 pinch cayenne

Instructions

Soak almonds and cashews overnight. Melt coconut oil over very low heat, or in an Excalibur dehydrator. Combine all ingredients except cashews and almonds in a blender or food processor until a dough ball forms. Remove from the blender and mix the nuts in by hand. Flatten into a small pan and freeze for 10 minutes. Remove from the freezer and cut into squares. Enjoy!
