Forkinit: Chocolate Orange Cake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C unbleached flour
1 1/2 C whole wheat flour (I used white whole wheat)
1 1/2 C sugar
2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1/2 C cocoa
1 tsp cinnamon
3/4 C soy yogurt
1 tsp vanilla
2 T balsamic vinegar
1 1/2 C water
1/2 C fresh orange juice
2 T grated orange peel

Chocolate Icing:
1/2 C powdered sugar
1 T cocoa
2 tsp orange juice
1/4 tsp vanilla extract (or 1/8 tsp if double strength)
Plus extra orange juice as needed

Orange Icing:
1/2 C powdered sugar
1 tsp orange juice
1/4 tsp vanilla extract (or 1/8 tsp if double strength)
Plus extra orange juice as needed

Instructions

1. Combine the flours, sugar, baking soda, salt, cocoa, and cinnamon in a large mixing bowl. Add the yogurt, vanilla, balsamic vinegar, water, and orange juice. Beat by hand or with a mixer on low speed just until well-combined, about 1-2 minutes. Stir in the grated orange peel, and pour into the prepared pan.
2. Bake for about 30-40 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean. Cool in the pan for 10 minutes and then invert it onto a serving platter and cool completely.
3. When the cake is cool, make the icings. For each icing, mix the ingredients in separate small bowls. One half tsp at a time, stir in enough extra orange juice to make a drizzling consistency. Drizzle the chocolate icing over the cake, wait a few minutes for it to set, and then drizzle the orange icing.
