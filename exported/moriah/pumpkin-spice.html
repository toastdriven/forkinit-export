Forkinit: Pumpkin Spice

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 tsp ground nutmeg
1 tsp ground ginger
1/2 tsp ground cloves

Instructions

In a small bowl, mix together cinnamon, nutmeg, ginger and cloves. Store in an airtight container.
