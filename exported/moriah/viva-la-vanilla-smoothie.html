Forkinit: Viva la Vanilla Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 banana
1 C cold, unsweetened non-dairy milk (coconut, rice, almond or hemp)
1 serving Vega One French Vanilla
1 C fresh spinach (optional)
2-3 ice cubes (optional)

Instructions

Blend until smooth.

Yields: 1 smoothie
Source: Vega
