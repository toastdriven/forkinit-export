Forkinit: French Breakfast Puffs

By Moriah Lindsley
These fantastic, rich breakfast muffins are perfect for Sunday brunches!

Ingredients

1/2 C + 2 T shortening
1/2 C sugar
1 egg (or Egg Replacer)
1 1/2 C flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/2 C milk
1/2 C butter, melted
1/2 C sugar
1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

1. Mix together the shortening, 1/2 C sugar and the egg.
2. Sift the flour, baking powder, salt and nutmeg into the shortening mixture, alternating with the milk.
3.When fully blended, fill greased muffin pans 2/3 full. Bake at 350Â°F for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
4. Roll immediately in the melted butter, then in a mixture of 1/2 C sugar and cinnamon.
