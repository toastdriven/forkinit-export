Forkinit: Strawberry Shortcake

By Moriah Lindsley
Fluffy shortcakes are topped with fresh strawberries and nondairy whipped cream.

Ingredients

4 C strawberries, sliced
2 T granulated sugar, plus additional for sprinkling
1 C all-purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp cream of tartar
1/4 tsp baking soda
4 T margarine
1/2 C soymilk mixed with 3/4 T lemon juice (let stand for 5 minutes)
1 1/2 C nondairy whipping cream (try Richâ€™s at Richs.com), whipped according to package directions, or nondairy cream-cheese sauce (see below)

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 450ÂºF.
2. Grease a baking sheet and set it aside.
3. In a medium bowl, gently stir together the strawberries and 2 T of sugar. Set aside.
4. In a large bowl, stir together the flour, baking powder, cream of tartar, and baking soda. Cut in the margarine until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients, then pour in the soymilk mixture all at once. Using a fork, stir until just moistened.
5. Divide the dough into quarters and drop each quarter in a mound onto the prepared baking sheet. Sprinkle them with sugar and bake at 450Â°F for 10 to 12 minutes or until golden.
6. Split the shortcakes into 2 layers. Place the bottom layers into individual serving bowls. Spoon some of the strawberries and nondairy whipped cream or 'cream cheese' sauce (see below) over the bottom layers. Add the top layers. Spoon more strawberries and the remaining nondairy whipped cream or 'cream cheese' sauce over tops. Serve immediately.

For the 'Cream Cheese' Sauce: In a small bowl, stir together 1/4 C nondairy sour cream and 4 T nondairy cream cheese (try Tofutti brand at Tofutti.com) with 2 tsp sugar until smooth.
Makes 4 servings
