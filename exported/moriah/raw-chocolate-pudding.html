Forkinit: Raw Chocolate Pudding

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 large avocados, pitted and peeled
2 1/2 T coconut butter
1 T coconut oil
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
4 T xylitol or coconut sugar
1/2 large banana
1/4 to 1/2 C raw cacao powder
1 tsp maca (optional if making for children)
1/4 tsp sea salt
3 strawberries for topping
1/2 C walnuts

Instructions

Blend all ingredients (except, strawberries and walnuts) in a food processor or Vitamix.
Top with berries and walnuts and serve! So easy and so delicious!
