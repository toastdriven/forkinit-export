Forkinit: Healing Miso Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
Great for a diet clean-up.

Ingredients

2 tsp olive oil
1 C sliced onion
1 C sliced carrot
2 C chopped cabbage or bok choy
3 cloves garlic, sliced
1 T fresh ginger, grated
4 C water or stock
3 1/2 T fresh red, barley or Hatcho miso
2 T fresh minced green onion

Instructions

Heat a soup pot, then add olive oil, onion and carrot. Sauté a few minutes. Add cabbage, garlic and ginger and sauté a few minutes more. Add water or stock and simmer for 15 minutes. Dissolve the miso in a small bowl with some of the hot broth. Turn off the heat and add the dissolved miso back to the broth with vegetables. Stir well. Serve with green onions on top.

Source: Hilary Kass, Nutrition Educator and Cook
