Forkinit: Good Morning Hemp Latte

By Moriah Lindsley
This recipe is fantastic when you are craving something creamy. Create a “latte” with hemp seeds and superfoods to *supercharge* your day! This recipe is delicious and easy to make. Try it iced as well!

Ingredients

3 Tbsp hemp seeds
2 cups warm water
1 tsp maca powder
1 tsp vanilla powder
1-2 Tbsp yacon syrup
1 T raw cacao powder, optional

Instructions

Blend hemp and water until frothy to make hemp milk. Blend in the rest of the ingredients and adjust flavors to your liking. Enjoy!

Optional: To make a mocha latte, simply add in cacao powder.

Yields: 1 latte
