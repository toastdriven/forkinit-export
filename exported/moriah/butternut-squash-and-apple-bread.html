Forkinit: Butternut Squash and Apple Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C all-purpose flour
1 C light brown sugar
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp allspice
1/4 tsp salt
2 tsp baking powder
1/3 C chopped walnuts or pecans
1/3 C sun-dried cranberries
1 (1 lb) pkg frozen butternut squash, thawed
2 prepared Egg-Replacer eggs, lightly beaten
1/2 C applesauce
1/3 C apple juice or rice milk

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
Lightly grease a 9x5x3" loaf pan.
Combine all flour, sugar, spices, baking powder, nuts and cranberries in a large mixing bowl.
Combine Egg Replacer, applesauce and apple juice or milk in another bowl.
Make a well in the center of the dry ingredients and pour in wet ingredients. Fold in until just combined.
Pour into prepared pan and bake in the center of the oven 1 1/4 hours until baked through or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. If baked as muffins, cooking time will be approximately 30 minutes.

Yields: 8 slices
