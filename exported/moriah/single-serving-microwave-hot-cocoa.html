Forkinit: Single Serving Microwave Hot Cocoa

By Moriah Lindsley
Small version of Nestle hot chocolate

Ingredients

2 T granulated sugar
1 T NESTLE TOLL HOUSE Baking Cocoa
1 C milk (non-dairy)
1/4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Stir together sugar and cocoa in large, microwave-safe mug. Stir in a few tablespoons (non-dairy) milk to make a smooth paste; stir in remaining (non-dairy) milk.
Microwave on HIGH (100%) power for 1 minute; stir. Microwave for a few seconds longer or until hot. Stir in vanilla extract.
Makes 1 serving.
