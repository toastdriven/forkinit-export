Forkinit: Spinach Fettucine with Savory Herbed Tempeh

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

16 oz tempeh, cut into 1/2" squares
1 small white onion, chopped
12 crimini mushrooms, cut in half
1 to 2 T canola oil
1/2 tsp thyme
2 T tamari
1 to 1 1/2 cup water or vegetable broth
2 medium carrots, sliced into thin strips
1 red bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
1 lb (dry) organic spinach fettuccine pasta
Toasted walnuts for garnish
