Forkinit: Creamy Avocado Dressing

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 large avocado
2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice
1/2 cup Greek-style vegan yogurt
1 teaspoon hot sauce
1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
2 garlic cloves
3/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions

Blend ingredients together in a mixer and enjoy!
