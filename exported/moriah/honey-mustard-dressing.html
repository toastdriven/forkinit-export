Forkinit: Honey Mustard Dressing

By Moriah Lindsley
Use agave nectar in place of honey for a cruelty-free substitute.

Ingredients

5 T medium body honey (sourwood is nice), or agave nectar
3 T smooth Dijon mustard
2 T rice wine vinegar

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a bowl and whisk until smooth. Serve as a dressing or a dip.
