Forkinit: Pumpkin Pie Chia Pudding

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup almond milk
2 tablespoons chia seeds
1-2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
1-2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
1/2 - 1 cup pumpkin puree
optional: 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
optional: 1/4 cup toasted walnuts

Instructions

Soak chia seeds in almond milk.
Use a fork to give it a quick stir or shake it vigorously!
Store in the refrigerator overnight. Chia seeds will expand.
Mix the rest of the ingredients together and add to the chia mixture.
Enjoy!

Yields: 1 serving
