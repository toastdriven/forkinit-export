Forkinit: Strawberry Daiquiri Sherbet

By Moriah Lindsley
Refreshing sherbet with a kick.

Ingredients

7 C strawberries, without syrup (fresh or frozen)
10 T lime juice
6 T lemon juice
3/4 C white rum*
1/2 granulated sugar

To halve:
3 1/2 C strawberries
5 T lime juice
3 T lemon juice
6 T white rum*
1/4 C granulated sugar

Instructions

1. Puree the berries until smooth - you should have about 4 C of puree (2 if halving).
2. Mix the unstrained puree with the other ingredients.
3. Freeze in an ice cream maker.

*Note: Be very careful not to add any extra alcohol or the sherbet will not set!
