By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 tsp matcha green tea powder
2 T hot water (175Â°F)
6 oz steamed non-dairy milk
Sweetener, to taste

Instructions

Place matcha tea in a warmed bowl or cup. Add hot water and blend. Add sweetener, if desired, and steamed milk. Sprinkle a pinch of matcha on top.

Yields: 1 serving
