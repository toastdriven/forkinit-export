Forkinit: Baked Onion Rings

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 large onion
1/4 cup Ener-G Egg Replacer Eggs, or other wet batter
1/2 cup crushed Fiber One

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Cut edges off onion, and remove outer layer. Cut onion into 1/2 inch wide slices, and separate into rings. Next, fill a small bowl (just large enough for onion rings to fit in) with Ener-G Egg Replacer eggs. Using a blender or food processor, grind Fiber One cereal to a breadcrumb-like consistency. Pour crumbs onto a small dish. One by one, coat each ring in wet mixture and then crumbs (give each ring a shake after dipping in wet mixture). Place on an oven-safe baking dish sprayed with nonstick spray. Cook for 20-25 minutes, flipping rings over about halfway through.

Yields: One serving = 15 rings
