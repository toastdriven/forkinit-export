Forkinit: Mexican Salad

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Dressing:
1/2 C mayonnaise (regular or vegan)
1/2 C sour cream (regular or vegan)
1 T lime juice
2 tsp mashed chipotle chilies in adobo
1 tsp grated lime peel

Salad:
Lettuce, chopped
Cilantro, chopped
Corn
Tomato, chopped
Avocado, chopped
Orange bell pepper, chopped
Black beans, drained if canned
Scallions, chopped
Pepper jack cheese, shreds or cubes
Tortilla chips, crushed

Instructions

Combine dressing ingredients and mix well. Let sit to bring out flavors.
Combine salad ingredients. Add dressing to desired amount. Toss well and enjoy!
