Forkinit: Homemade Brownie Mix

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Mix:
1 Cup sugar
1/2 Cup all-purpose flour
1/3 Cup cocoa
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder

Add at baking time:
Egg Replacer equal to 2 eggs
1/2 C vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla
Chocolate chips (optional)

Instructions

Store "mix" in plastic bags or mason jars until ready to use. Mix "baking time" ingredients into dry ingredients until all moist. Bake @ 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes in an 8x8 or 9x9 pan. Makes great gifts!

Source: Adapted by Moriah Lindsley from "Pampered Cooking with Rachael"
