Forkinit: Teriyaki Tofu Marinade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 lbs local tofu
1/4 C soy sauce
2 T honey, sugar or agave
4 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
1 T freshly grated ginger

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Lightly grease a 9x13" baking dish. Slice tofu 3/8" thick and line up in baking dish. Bake 15 minutes, then flip pieces and bake another 15 minutes. This prebaking will turn the tofu slightly golden and give it a firmer texture. Remove tofu from oven.
Mix remaining marinade ingredients and pour evenly over the prebaked slices. Return to oven and heat through, 10-15 minutes.
Delicious served hot with rice, also great for cold sandwiches.
