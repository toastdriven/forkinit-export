Forkinit: Yeast Rolls

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C lukewarm water
1 pkg dry yeast
1 tsp salt
1 egg (or Egg Replacer)
6 T sugar
3 T melted butter
6 C flour

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in large mixing bowl. Let dough rise 1 hour or until double in size. Punch down. Form into rolls and let them rise for 1 hour in a lightly greased pan.
