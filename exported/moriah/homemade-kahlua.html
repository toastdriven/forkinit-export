Forkinit: Homemade Kahlua

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

6 cups brewed coffee (Use your favorite kind! I used Goshen coffee since I know that Bride and Groom 3.0 and many of my coffee connoisseur friends love it.)
1 lb light brown sugar
3 1/2 tablespoons vanilla extract
1/2 liter of vodka or rum (If you have access to a pure grain alcohol like Everclear, you may want to use that. I simply used SVEDKA Vodka and it worked just fine.)

Instructions

While the coffee is still hot, mix in the brown sugar.
Let cool.
Mix in vanilla and vodka.
Serve over ice, in mixed drinks like a frozen mudslide, or use to make a cupcake.
