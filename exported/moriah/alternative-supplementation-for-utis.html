Forkinit: Alternative Supplementation for UTIs

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Garlic, fresh or tablets
Fresh organic blueberries
Organic cranberries/100% organic cranberry juice
Raw almond milk
Homemade barley water*
CranActin supplements
Alfalfa tablets
Fresh wheatgrass juice

*Barley Water
1/2 cup of whole pot barley
5 cups of water
1/4 of a cinnamon stick
Grated ginger to your taste
Freshly squeezed juice of 1-2 lemons

Instructions

Take 2 cloves or 2 garlic tablets 3x per day for 10 days.
Eat as many fresh blueberries as you want. They contain some of the same UTI-fighting enzymes as cranberries.
Drink 3-4 oz of pure cranberry juice 2x daily for 10 days.
Drink 1/2-1 C raw almond milk combined with 1/2-1 C barley water* daily for urinary problems.
Take 1-2 CranActin chewables to support a healthy urinary tract as needed.
Take 5-10 alfalfa tablets daily, and drink up to 4 oz freshly-squeezed wheatgrass juice daily to cleanse and detox the system.

*Barley Water
Place the whole barley, water, the cinnamon stick and some grated ginger into a pan and simmer for 20 minutes. If you have organic unwaxed lemons you can simmer the peel for the final 5 minutes which intensifies the taste.

After cooling, strain the mixture and finally add fresh lemon juice for extra flavor. Store in a cool place and drink between 1 and 3 cups daily, diluted to your taste if necessary.

Source: Barley Water recipe from http://www.herbs-hands-healing.co.uk/detox-and-cleansing/barley-water, list put together by Moriah Lindsley
