Forkinit: Veggie Burgers

By Moriah Lindsley
They're veggie burgers, duh!

Ingredients

2-4 vegan burgers
Vegan cheese, sliced
2-4 buns
Ketchup
Mustard
Lettuce

Instructions

1. Grill vegan burgers.
2. Place burgers and toppings on buns. Serve.
