Forkinit: Chewy Vegan Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley
These are brownie-like and chewy.

Ingredients

3/4 C canola oil
2 C sugar
2 tsp vanilla
1 T plus 1 tsp flax seeds
1/2 C soy milk
2 C flour
3/4 C cocoa powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 C semisweet vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Ã‚Â°F.
2. Grind flax seeds in a blender until they become a fine powder, then add the soy milk and blend for about 30 seconds more. Set aside.
3. In a large bowl, sift together cocoa, flour, baking soda and salt.
4. Cream the sugar and oil in a separate large bowl.
5. Add the flax/soy milk mixture and mix well.
6. Stir in the vanilla.
7. Slowly mix in the dry ingredients.
8. Mix in the chocolate chips.
9. Roll dough into 1" balls and flatten into disks about 1 1/2" in diameter.
10. Place on an ungreased cookie sheet about 1" apart and bake for 10 minutes.
11. Let rest on the cookie sheet for about 5 minutes, then remove with a spatula to a cooling rack and cool completely.

-For chewy chocolate chip cherry cookies, replace 1 tsp of the vanilla with almond extract and use 3/4 C chocolate chips and 3/4 C chopped cherries.
-For chocolate nut cookies, replace 1 tsp of the vanilla with a nut extract (almond, walnut, etc.) and replace the chocolate chips with nuts.
