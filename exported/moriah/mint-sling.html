Forkinit: Mint Sling

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 (3") sprigs fresh mint, leaves removed
1 1/2 oz brandy
1 oz lime juice
1/2 oz Green Chartreuse liqueur
1/2 oz simple syrup
2 dashes Angostura bitters
3 oz seltzer water
1 small sprig fresh mint for garnish

Instructions

Place mint leaves in bottom of tall Collins or highball glass; add brandy. Gently bruise mint with a muddle or bar spoon to release the oils into the brandy.
Add lime juice, Chartreuse, simple syrup and bitters; stir well. Add ice, top with seltzer. Stir mint so it floats throughout the drink. Rub small mint sprig between fingers to release aromas; garnish top of glass.

Yields: 1 serving
