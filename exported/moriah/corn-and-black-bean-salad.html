Forkinit: Corn and Black Bean Salad

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C fresh or frozen corn, thawed
2 14 oz cans (or 2 C home cooked) black beans, drained and rinsed
1 pint cherry tomatoes, cut in half
Fresh cilantro, to taste
1/4-1/2 C Italian or homemade vinaigrette dressing
1 14 oz can black olives, sliced
Scallions
Avocado

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together in a large mixing bowl and let marinate overnight. Serve cold and enjoy!
