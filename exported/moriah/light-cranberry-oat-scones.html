Forkinit: Light Cranberry Oat Scones

By Moriah Lindsley
Berry fresh, berry frestive, berry easy!

Ingredients

1 1/2 C flour
1 C quick cooking oats
1/4 C sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/2 tsp orange peel
1/4 C butter or margarine (vegan)
1/2 C skim milk (non-dairy)
1/4 C non-fat plain yogurt (soy)
1/4 C real egg substitute (mashed banana, Egg Replacer)
3/4 C Ocean Spray Craisins Sweetened Dried Cranberries

TOPPING:
2 T brown sugar, optional

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Combine first six ingredients in a medium mixing bowl. Work (vegan) butter or margarine into dry ingredients until batter is the size of small peas, using a pastry blender or fork.
2. Combine (non-dairy) milk, (soy) yogurt, and egg substitute in a small mixing bowl. Add to dry ingredients, mixing thoroughly. Stir in sweetened dried cranberries.
3. Spread batter into a 9-inch circle on an ungreased cookie sheet. Sprinkle top of scones with brown sugar. Cut into 8 wedges. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until golden brown. Remove scones from oven and re-cut edges. Makes 8 scones.
