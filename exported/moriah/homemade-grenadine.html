Forkinit: Homemade Grenadine

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 C (32 oz bottle) 100% organic pomegranate juice
2 C sugar

Instructions

Bring 4 cups of pomegranate juice (the whole 32oz bottle) to a boil.

Lower heat and simmer until it reduces in half. You can let it reduce more that half if you want it to be a bit thicker. This process took about 15 minutes.

Add 2 cups of sugar and let it dissolve. Chill.
