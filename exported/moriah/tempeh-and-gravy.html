Forkinit: Tempeh and Gravy

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 T safflower, corn or canola oil
1/2 lb mushrooms, quartered
1 onion, thinly sliced
1/2 tsp sage
1/2 tsp thyme
1/4 tsp celery seed
1/4 tsp marjoram
1 lb tempeh, cut into 1" squares
1 T dark barley miso, dissolved in 1/4 C water
3 C water or vegetable stock
4 T whole wheat flour

Ingredient Options: use barley or rice flour or 2 T kuzu or arrowroot instead of whole wheat flour.

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a saucepan. SautÃ© mushrooms and onions for 5 minutes. Add seasonings, tempeh, diluted miso and stock. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover and simmer for 45 minutes. Dissolve flour or kuzu in 1/4 C water. Add to tempeh "stew", stirring constantly until thick. Adjust seasonings and serve.

Nutrition Info
Per Serving (264g-wt.): 230 calories (120 from fat), 13g total fat, 2g saturated fat, 17g protein, 15g total carbohydrate (1g dietary fiber, 2g sugar), 0mg cholesterol, 60mg sodium
