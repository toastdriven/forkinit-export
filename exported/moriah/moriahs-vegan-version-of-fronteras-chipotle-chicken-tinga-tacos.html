Forkinit: Moriah's Vegan Version of Frontera's Chipotle Chicken Tinga Tacos

By Moriah Lindsley
Tinga is a flavorful saucy mixture originally from Puebla, Mexico.

Ingredients

1 lb extra firm tofu
Sea salt & fresh ground black pepper
2 T extra virgin olive oil or canola oil
1 medium white onion, thinly sliced
1 16-oz jar Frontera Chipotle Salsa
1 15-oz can diced tomatoes
1/4 C chopped fresh cilantro
12 corn or flour torillas
Romaine or iceberg lettuce, thinly sliced
Ripe avocado, diced
Vegan cheddar cheese, shredded (Follow Your Heart brand is great)
Vegan sour cream (Tofutti brand is great)

Instructions

1. Season tofu with salt and pepper, if desired.
2. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium. Add tofu and cook until heated through, about 4-5 minutes on each side.
3. Remove from skillet, cool, the slice into 1/4" wide strips.
4. Add onion to skillet and cook, stirring frequently, until lightly browned, about 8 minutes.
5. Stir in Frontera Salsa and tomatoes. Simmer 5-10 minutes until mixture thickens slightly. Stir in tofu strips and cilantro; remove from heat.
6. Serve mixture folded into warm tortillas topped with lettuce, avocado, cheese and sour cream, as desired.

Variation: Chipotle Chicken Tinga Tostaditas
Arrange Frontera Tortilla Chips on a platter. Pile about 1 T warm "chicken" tinga onto each chip. Sprinkle sparingly with cilantro and finely grated vegan cheese.

Source: Frontera Grill Restaurant and Moriah Lindsley
