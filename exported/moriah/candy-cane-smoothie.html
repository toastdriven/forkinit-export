Forkinit: Candy Cane Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup almond milk
6 large fresh mint leaves
1 frozen banana
1 serving French Vanilla Vega One Nutritional Shake

Instructions

Blend all ingredients together in blender and enjoy!

Yields: 1 serving
