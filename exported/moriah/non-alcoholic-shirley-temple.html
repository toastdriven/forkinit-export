Forkinit: Non-Alcoholic Shirley Temple

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Enough ice to pack a high ball (tall) glass
Orange juice (enough to fill the glass 1/3 full when it already contains ice)
Ginger ale
Cherries and an orange wedge to garnish

Instructions

Pack a high ball glass with ice.
Fill with orange juice until 1/3 full.
Pour the ginger ale into the glass until full.
Garnish with cherries and orange wedge.

Yields: 1 serving
