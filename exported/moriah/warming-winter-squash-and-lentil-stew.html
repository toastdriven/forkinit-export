Forkinit: Warming Winter Squash and Lentil Stew

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 medium shallots, thinly sliced
1 T fresh ginger, peeled and finely chopped
1 T vegetable oil
1 tsp ground coriander
1/2 tsp ground cardamom
1 small butternut squash, peeled, seeded and cut into 1 1/2" chunks
1 lb green lentils, picked over
6 C vegetable broth
5 C packed baby spinach
1 T cider vinegar

Instructions

In a pressure-cooker pot on medium, cook shallots and ginger in oil 5 minutes or until shallots are golden, stirring. Add coriander and cardamom; cook 1 minute, stirring. Add squash, lentils, broth and 1/4 tsp salt.
Cover, lock and bring up to pressure on high. Reduce heat to medium-low. Cook 12 minutes. Release pressure by using quick-release function.
Stir in spinach, vinegar and 1/2 tsp each salt and pepper.

Note: You can also cook this in a pot on the stove instead.
