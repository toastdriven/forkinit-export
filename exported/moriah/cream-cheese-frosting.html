Forkinit: Cream Cheese Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley
One of the best cream cheese frostings I've ever tasted!

Ingredients

2 1/2 C powdered sugar
3 oz cream cheese
1/3 C butter
1 T pineapple juice
1/2 tsp vanilla

Instructions

Blend all ingredients in a bowl with a hand blender until smooth and creamy.
