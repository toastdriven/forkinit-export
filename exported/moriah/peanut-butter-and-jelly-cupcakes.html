Forkinit: Peanut Butter and Jelly Cupcakes

By Moriah Lindsley
Celebrate a "Kid's Only" birthday party or give your child a mid-afternoon treat with easy-to-serve and fun-to-eat peanut butter and jelly cupcakes!

Ingredients

Basic Buttermilk Cake

Peanut Butter Frosting:
1/2 C creamy peanut butter
2 T vegan butter, softened
3 T coconut milk or vegan heavy cream
2 C confectioner's sugar (sifted if lumpy)
1/4 C grape jelly

Instructions

1. Make the batter as directed in the basic cake recipe. Line two muffin tins (1/2 C size) with paper liners. Fill cups 3/4 full (you may have a little batter left). Bake cupcakes 15 to 18 minutes, until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean. Cool in pan on a rack for 2 minutes. Then turn the cupcakes out on a rack to cool completely before frosting (at this point, cupcakes can be frozen for 2 to 3 weeks).
2. Beat peanut butter and butter until smooth, either in a large bowl or using an electric mixer. Add the cream and sugar and beat until creamy. Then, frost the cupcakes, and, with your fingertip, scoop out a small amount of the frosting from the center of the cake.
3. Place the jelly in a glass dish and heat in a microwave approximately 1 minute until softened. Place 1/2 tsp of jelly in the center of each cupcake.
