Forkinit: Almond Crunchies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C vegan butter or margarine
1 C packed brown sugar
Egg replacer equal to 1 egg
1/2 C finely chopped almonds
1 3/4 C unsifted flour
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp Cinnamon

Instructions

Cream together the butter, brown sugar, egg replacer and finely chopped almonds. Mix in the flour, baking soda and Cinnamon. Refrigerate at least 1 hour. Form the dough into 2 rolls about 2 inches in diameter. Cut into 1/4" thick slices. Place the cookies 1" apart on an ungreased cookie. sheet. Bake at 375Â°F for 10-12 minutes.
