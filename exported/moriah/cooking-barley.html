Forkinit: Cooking Barley

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C barley
2 1/2-3 C liquid

Instructions

Put liquid into a pot. Bring to a boil. Add barley; stir.
Cover; reduce heat. Cook 35-40 minutes.

OR:
Presoak overnight in 2 parts liquid to 1 part grain and cook 15 minutes.
