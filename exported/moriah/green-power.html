Forkinit: Green Power

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 carrots
2 stalks celery
Handful of spinach
Handful of parsley

Instructions

Trim the carrots and cut them into 2-3" pieces. Cut the celery into 2-3" pieces. Process the vegetables in a juicer or in a Vitamix and strain.
