Forkinit: Fresh Summer Strawberries with Balsamic Vinegar

By Moriah Lindsley
A great recipe with a low glycemic index (47) and glycemic load (10).

Ingredients

5 C fresh strawberries
3 T balsamic vinegar
1 T fresh lemon zest, grated
3 T fresh mint leaves, finely chopped
2 T powdered sugar, to taste

Instructions

Slice strawberries into thirds and place in a large mixing bowl. Add balsamic vinegar; mix well and let marinate for 30 minutes. Add lemon zest, mint and sugar (optional). Mix well and serve.
Servings: 4

Per Serving: 90 cal, 1g fat, 0mg cholesterol, 10mg sodium, 20g carbs, 2g dietary fiber, 2g protein
