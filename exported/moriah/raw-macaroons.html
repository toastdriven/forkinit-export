Forkinit: Raw Macaroons

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Shredded coconut
Almond flour
Coconut oil
Maple syrup
Pure Mexican vanilla
Sea salt

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and scoop out into small bite-size rounds.
