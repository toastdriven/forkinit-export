Forkinit: German Chocolate Cake

By Moriah Lindsley
This cake is unbelievably moist and delicious. Serve it with a steaming cup of espresso or coffee for the perfect dessert.

Ingredients

For the Cake:
2 1/4 C flour
1 3/4 C sugar
3/4 C cocoa
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1 1/4 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2 C soy milk
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 C vegan mayonnaise (try Nayonaise)
1/2 C applesauce

4. Slowly add to the dry mixture and stir to combine. Pour into the prepared pans and bake for 35 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool before icing.

For the icing:
1. Using a hand mixer on medium speed, beat the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl until well blended.

To Assemble:
1. Slice the rounded top portion off each cake, creating a level surface.
2. Place 1 cake on a cake plate and frost the top. Place another cake on top and frost, then place the last cake on top.
3. Frost the sides and top of the entire cake.

Makes 8 to 10 servings
