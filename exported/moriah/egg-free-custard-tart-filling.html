Forkinit: Egg-Free Custard Tart Filling

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C scalded non-dairy milk
1/3 packed C Ener-G Egg Replacer
1/2 C sugar
1/4 tsp salt, optional
1 tsp vanilla
2 tsp fruit pectin

Instructions

Scald milk. Stir all dry ingredients together. Slowly add ingredients to hot milk while mixing. Mix 1 minute. Pour into pie shell or custard cups. Refrigerate until firm.
