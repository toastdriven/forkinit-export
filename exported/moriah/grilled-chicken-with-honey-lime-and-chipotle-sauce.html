Forkinit: Grilled 'Chicken' With 'Honey' Lime and Chipotle Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
This spicy and sweet dish is perfect for a summer cookout!

Ingredients

1/4 C fresh lime juice
2 T Just-Like-Honey (available at www.Suzannes-Specialties.com) or agave nectar
4 cloves garlic, peeled and chopped
1 serrano chile or jalapeno pepper, minced
1 chipotle chile, in adobo
Pepper, to taste
1/4 tsp cornstarch
1 T cilantro, minced
1 lb mock chicken or seitan

Instructions

1. Preheat the grill.
2. In a food processor or blender, puree lime juice, "honey," garlic, serrano chile or jalapeno pepper, chipotle chili, pepper, and cornstarch.
3. Transfer the mixture to a medium-sized sauce pan and boil until slightly thickened, about 1 minute. Stir in the cilantro.
4. Brush the "chicken" lightly with the glaze.
5. Grill for 3 to 4 minutes on each side, turning once. Just before removing the "chicken" from the grill, baste with the remaining glaze.
6. Remove from the grill and serve.
Makes 4 servings
