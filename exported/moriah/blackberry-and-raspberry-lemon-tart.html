Forkinit: Blackberry and Raspberry Lemon Tart

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 9" tart shell, pre-baked
7-8 oz lemon curd (or lemon juice blended with vegan butter)
3 pts blackberries and/or raspberries
1/2 C apricot preserves

Instructions

Fill cooled shell with 1/2" layer lemon curd. Starting from the outer edge, arrange berries closely in cocentric circles, alternating rings of blackberries and raspberries. Heat apricot preserves with 1 T water, then carefully brush on top of tart to set berries and glaze tart.
Serves 6-8
