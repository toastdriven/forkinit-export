Forkinit: Matcha Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

8 oz vegan butter, softened
8 oz vegan cream cheese, softened
4 C powdered sugar, sifted
2 tsp vanilla extract
1-2 tsp non-dairy milk (add more if frosting is too thick)
2 T matcha

Instructions

In a large bowl, beat together the butter and cream cheese with an electric mixer. With the mixer on low speed, add the powdered sugar a cup at a time until smooth and creamy. Beat in the vanilla extract. Adjust thickness of the frosting by adding milk, 1/2 tsp at a time, to desired consistency. Beat in matcha. Spread on a cake or cupcakes.
