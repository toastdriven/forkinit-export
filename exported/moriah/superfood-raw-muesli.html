Forkinit: Superfood Raw Muesli

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 banana, sliced
1 kiwi, chopped
1 apple, chopped
1/4 cup raspberries
1/4 cup dried coconut
1 tablespoon ground flax seeds
1 tablespoon hemp seeds
1/3 cup almonds, chopped
Fresh almond milk

Instructions

Place everything in your bowl, cover with almond milk and stir.
