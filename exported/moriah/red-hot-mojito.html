Forkinit: Red-Hot Mojito

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Handful of fresh spearmint leaves
2 small or 1 large lime
1/4 C sugar
3-4 oz white Rum
Splash of 365 Everyday Value Pomegranate Juice
365 Everyday Value Sparkling water or club soda

Instructions

Gently crush mint leaves and lightly squeeze lime in a pitcher or 2 tall glasses. Add sugar and blend into lime/mint mixture. Fill glass with ice. Add rum, a splash of pomegranate juice and top off with club soda. Stir well. Garnish with a lime wedge and a few sprigs of mint. For a refreshing "all ages" cocktail, simply remove the rum.
