Forkinit: Mom’s Tuna Salad

By Moriah Lindsley
One of my favorites from childhood!

Ingredients

2 cans tuna
2 hard boiled eggs
Green olives, chopped
2 stalks celery, minced
Shredded cheddar cheese
Mayonnaise
Yellow or Dijon mustard
Salt and pepper

Instructions

Combine ingredients to taste. Enjoy on lettuce leaves or as a cold sandwich.
