Forkinit: Marinated Baked Tofu, Red Pototoes and Steamed Veggies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Marinade:
1/3 C tamari sauce
1/4 C white wine vinegar
1/4 C olive oil
1 clove garlic, crushed
1/2 tsp basil
1/2 tsp rosemary

1 pkg extra firm tofu
Red potatoes, sliced and lightly tossed in olive oil and seasonings
Kale or other vegetable
Tahini sauce

Instructions

1. Slice tofu and press in paper towels to drain moisture.
2. Soak tofu in the marinade for a few minutes.
3. Bake tofu with all the marinade on a cookie sheet, along with the potatoes, at 375Ã‚Â°F for about 45 minutes.
4. Steam vegetable(s) of choice. Cover with tahini sauce. Serve with potatoes and tofu.
