Forkinit: Individual Strawberry Shortcakes

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Strawberry Mixture:
4 C fresh strawberries, washed, hulled and sliced
2 T granulated sugar

Shortcake Biscuits:
1 3/4 C all-purpose flour
1 T granulated sugar
1 T baking powder
1/2 tsp salt (optional)
1/4 C shortening (vegan)
2/3 C milk (non-dairy)

Topping:
1 C plain yogurt (vegan)
3 T brown sugar
1/2 tsp vanilla

Instructions

1. For Strawberry Mixture, combine strawberries and granulated sugar. Cover and refrigerate.
2. Heat oven to 450Ã‚Â°F.
3. For Shortcake Biscuits, combine flour, granulated sugar, baking powder and salt in bowl. Cut in shortening with pastry blender until mixture forms coarse crumbs.
4. Add milk. Stir until dry ingredients are just moistened. Place on floured surface. Knead gently with fingertips 8 to 10 times. Pat or roll into 9" circle about 1/2" thick. (Hint: cover dough with waxed paper. Press and flatten with 9" round cake pan until dough is desired thickness.)
5. Cut with 2 1/2" round biscuit cutter. Press dough scraps into ball and flatten again. Cut out a total of 10 biscuits. Place on ungreased baking sheet. Bake 12 minutes or until tops are golden brown.
6. For Topping, combine yogurt, brown sugar and vanilla. Stir gently until smooth.

To assemble, split warm or cooled biscuits in half crosswise. Spoon about 1/4 C fruit over bottoms of biscuits. Add tops. Spoon yogurt sauce over tops. Add another spoonful of fruit or top with strawberry fans, if desired.
