Forkinit: Zucchini Muffins

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C flour
1/2 C whole wheat flour
1 1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
2 tsp cinnamon
1 beaten egg (or Egg Replacer)
1 C grated zucchini
2/3 C honey (or agave nectar)
1/3 C vegetable oil
1 tsp vanilla
3/4 C chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions

Combine ingredients and spoon into greased muffin pans. Bake at 400Â°F for 18 minutes.
Makes 14 large muffins
