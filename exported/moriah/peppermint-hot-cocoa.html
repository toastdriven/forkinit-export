Forkinit: Peppermint Hot Cocoa

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 Traditional Medicinals Peppermint tea bags
1 C boiling water
1/4 C hot half and half, or creamer of choice
2 T Dutch-processed unsweetened cocoa
1-2 T honey or sweetener of choice
Whipped cream and peppermint stick, for garnish

Instructions

Boil water and pour one cup onto tea bags.
Cover cup with saucer and allow to steep for 10-15 minutes to extract every bit of plant power.
Pour hot tea into blender and add the remaining ingredients. Blend until froth begins to form, then pour into the teacup.
Enjoy topped with whipped cream and a peppermint stick.

Yields: 1 serving
