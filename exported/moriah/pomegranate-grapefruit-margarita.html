Forkinit: Pomegranate Grapefruit Margarita

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Syrup:
2/3 c sugar
2/3 c water
2 tbsp grapefruit zest

Drink Mix:
1 1/2 c tequila
1 c orange liqueur (such as Cointreau)
1 c fresh lime juice
2 C pomegranate juice

Glass Rim:
2 tbsp fresh lime juice
2 tbsp sugar
2 tsp grapefruit zest

Other:
Ice cubes
Grapefruit twist, for garnish

Instructions

For the syrup, add grapefruit zest to sugar and water. In a large jar with a tight-fitting lid, vigorously shake syrup ingredients until the sugar dissolves, about 1 minute. Strain the syrup into a pitcher.
Add all drink ingredients to the pitcher; stir.
To rim the glass, mix sugar and grapefruit zest. Put lime juice on one plate and sugar/zest mixture on another plate. Dip the rims of 8 glasses into the lime juice, then into the sugar mix.
Fill the glasses with ice, then add the margarita. Garnish with a grapefruit twist.

Yields: 8-10 margaritas
