Forkinit: Banana Porridge

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C raw cashews
1/2 C raw almonds
1/2 C raw pecan halves
Pinch sea salt
1 ripe banana
2 C coconut milk
2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Place nuts in a large bowl and sprinkle with salt. Add enough filtered water to cover nuts by at least 1". Cover and soak overnight.
Drain nuts and rinse 2-3 times, until water runs clear.
Add nuts to a food processor or high-speed blender. Add banana, coconut milk, and cinnamon and process until smooth.
Pour porridge into a saucepan and heat over medium-high heat for 8 minutes, or until thick.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 20 minutes
Source: "Against All Grain" by Danielle Walker
