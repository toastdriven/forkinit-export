Forkinit: Matcha Energy Bars

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C pitted fresh dates
1 C raw cashews or almonds
1/4 C raw pecans
1/2 C unsweetened shredded coconut
2-3 tsp matcha green tea powder

Instructions

Place dates in a food processor for about 1 minute. Add in nuts and coconut and blend for another minute. Add matcha and continue blending until smooth, scraping the sides to incorporate. When blended, press into a pan and cut into squares.

Yields: 8 servings
