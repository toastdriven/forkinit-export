Forkinit: Strawberry Chia Jam

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup fresh strawberries, quartered
1/4 C water
Sweetener, as desired, optional
2 Tbsp chia seeds

Instructions

In a small saucepan, combine strawberries, water and sweetener, if using, over low heat. Cook until strawberries are soft. Mash with a fork. Add in chia seeds. Cook for an additional 5-10 minutes or until a jam texture has formed.
Place in a jar, cool and refrigerate to set.

Source: Morgan Shupe, head chef at Vega
