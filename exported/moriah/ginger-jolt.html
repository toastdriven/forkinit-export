Forkinit: Ginger Jolt

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 apples
1 pear
1" knob gingerroot

Instructions

Cut the apple into narrow wedges. Slice the gingerroot if necessary. Beginning and ending with the apple, process half the apple and pear wedges in a juicer. Process the ginger. Process the remaining apple and pear wedges, beginning and ending with apple. Or process in a Vitamix and strain.
