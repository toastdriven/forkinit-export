Forkinit: Nut Butter Icing

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 T margarine
1/2 C dry sweetener
1/3 C peanut butter (or other nut butter)
2 T soy milk
1/3 C nuts, chopped (optional)

Instructions

1. In a medium bowl or food processor, mix together the margarine, sweetener, and nut butter.
2. Add the milk and mix well.
3. Stir in nuts before using.
