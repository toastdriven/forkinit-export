Forkinit: Pizza Bread Bowl

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 bread boule
1 cup marinara or pizza sauce
8 ounces vegan cheddar, slices or shreds
1 can black olives, sliced
1/2 onion, sliced
1/2 cup basil
1 cup mushrooms
1 green bell pepper, sliced
1 cup shredded vegan mozzarella

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Slice the top of the bread boule off and remove the insides. Spread 1/2 C of the marinara on the bottom of the boule, then layer with half the cheddar, half the black olives, onions, basil, mushrooms, bell peppers, the other 1/2 C of marinara, the remaining cheddar, half of the mozzarella, and place the cap of the bread boule on top.
Wrap the bread bowl in foil, then press with a heavy object for 30 minutes. Remove the foil, and sprinkle the remaining mozzarella on top. Bake for 30 minutes, until cheese is golden brown. Cool for 10 minutes, slice, then serve!

Yields: 1 boule
