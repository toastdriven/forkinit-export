Forkinit: Fruit Skewer With 'Yogurt' Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
Beautiful fruit kabobs served with a creamy lemon-strawberry dip make excellent hors dÃ¢â‚¬â„¢oeuvres for a cocktail party and are also wonderful at breakfast or as a snack.

Ingredients

1/2 cup strawberry soy yogurt
1 tsp Just Like Honey Rice Nectar (available at www.suzannes-specialties.com) or agave syrup
1/4 tsp freshly grated nutmeg
2 tsp lemon juice
Watermelon, balled
Cantaloupe, balled
Honeydew melon, balled
Pineapple, cut into 1" chunks
Strawberries, sliced in half
Seedless white grapes
4 wooden skewers

Instructions

1. Combine the yogurt, Just Like Honey or agave, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Set aside.
2. Thread the fruit on the skewers.
3. Serve with the yogurt sauce, freshly squeezed orange juice, and soy breakfast sausages.
Makes 4 servings
