Forkinit: Five-Herb Vinaigrette

By Moriah Lindsley
A tasty vinaigrette with yummy herbs!

Ingredients

1/4 C Dijon Mustard
1/4 C minced shallots
1 C white rice vinegar
2 T Chinese black vinegar, or balsamic vinegar
2 T mirin
1 T sugar
2 T dried mint
2 T dried cilantro
2 T dried basil, preferably Thai
1 tsp dried thyme
2 T dried tarragon
2 C grapeseed or canola oil

Instructions

In a medium non-reactive bowl, combine the mustard, shallots, vinegars, mirin, sugar, mint, cilantro, basil, thyme and tarragon. Whisk in the grapeseed oil. Use or store.
