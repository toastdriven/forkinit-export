Forkinit: Ginger Ale for Pain and Inflammation

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

675ml (3 C) purified water, divided
200g (1 C) ginger, peeled and finely chopped
225g (1 C) sugar
115ml (1/2 C) club soda (per glass)
A few drops lime juice
Lime slices, for garnish

Instructions

Boil 2 C water in a saucepan. Add the ginger, reduce the heat to medium-low, and leave the ginger simmer for 5 minutes. Then, remove the saucepan from the heat and leave for 20 minutes. Using a fine mesh strainer, strain the liquid and remove the ginger pieces.

In another saucepan, make the simple syrup by dissolving 1 C sugar into 1 C boiling water.

Now mix 1/2 C ginger water with 1/3 C simple syrup and 1/2 C club soda. Sweeten with honey to taste; you can also add some lime juice to taste.
