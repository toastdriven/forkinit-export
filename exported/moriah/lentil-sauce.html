Forkinit: Lentil Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
Serve this easy crockpot sauce over rice or pasta.

Ingredients

28 oz tomatoes, crushed
1/4 C lentils, rinsed
2 medium onions, chopped
5 cloves garlic, minced
1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp toasted cumin seeds
1 fresh green chili pepper, minced
1/2 C picante sauce

Instructions

Combine the tomatoes, lentils, onions, garlic, sugar, cumin, chili, and sauce in crockpot. Cover and cook on low until the lentils are tender, 6 to 8 hours.
