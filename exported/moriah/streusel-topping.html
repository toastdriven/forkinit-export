Forkinit: Streusel Topping

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 C firmly packed light brown sugar
4 T (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, slightly softened and cut into 1/2-inch pieces

Instructions

In a medium bowl, stir together the flour, cinnamon, and brown sugar. Add the butter pieces and combine the mixture with a fork until the topping resembles coarse crumbs.
