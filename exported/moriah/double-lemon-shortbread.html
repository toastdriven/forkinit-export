Forkinit: Double Lemon Shortbread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Shortbread:

1/2 C vegan butter, softened
1/4 C sugar
1 tsp lemon extract
1 tsp freshly grated lemon peel
1 1/4 C all-purpose flour

Glaze:

1/2 C powdered sugar
1 T non-dairy milk
1 tsp freshly grated lemon peel
1/4 tsp lemon extract

Instructions

Heat oven to 350Â°F.
Combine butter, sugar, lemon extract and peel in large bowl. Beat at medium speed until creamy. Add flour; beat at low speed until blended.
Press evenly into ungreased 9" round baking pan.
Bake 20 to 25 minutes until edges are light golden brown. Cool 10 minutes; cut into 16 wedges. Cool; remove from pan.
Combine glaze ingredients. Drizzle over shortbread.

Tip: Substitute for lemon peel - Use 1 tsp lemon extract for 1 tsp freshly grated lemon peel.

Yields: 16 shortbread wedges
