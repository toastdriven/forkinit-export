Forkinit: Peanut Butter Pie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

16 oz soft tofu
1 C creamy peanut butter
3/4 C sugar
2 T soy milk
2 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 C vegan chocolate chips
1 unbleached, 9-inch chocolate crust, chilled

Instructions

1. Combine all the filling ingredients in a food processor or blender and blend until smooth. Fold in the chocolate chips. Spoon into the pie shell.
2. Refrigerate at least 2 hours and serve with vegan whipped cream and non-dairy ice cream.
Makes 6 to 8 servings
