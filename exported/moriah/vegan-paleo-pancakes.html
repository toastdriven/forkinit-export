Forkinit: Vegan Paleo Pancakes

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup coconut milk (full fat)
1 ripe banana, mashed
1 tsp butter or vanilla extract
1 cup almond flour
1/4 cup coconut flour
1 tbsp baking powder
2 tbsp liquid sweetener of choice (stevia, honey, agave, maple syrup)
1/3 cup cacao nibs
dash of Himalayan salt

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together in a large bowl.
Heat a skillet over medium heat with coconut oil.
Place dollops of mixture onto pan and let cook until you begin to see bubbles.
Flip pancakes over, cook another 1-2 minutes.
Top with jam or syrup.

Yields: 2 servings
