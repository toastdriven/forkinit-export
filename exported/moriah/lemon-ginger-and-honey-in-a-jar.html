Forkinit: Lemon, Ginger and Honey in a Jar

By Moriah Lindsley
You can make this immunity boosting, body soothing concoction ahead of time and store in the fridge. Keeps for months when refrigerated. A great all-natural cold and flu remedy.

Ingredients

2 organic lemons (thoroughly cleaned)
2 pieces fresh organic ginger (about the size of your pointer and middle finger)
Raw honey
12 oz mason jar

Instructions

Slice lemons and ginger.
Place the lemon and ginger slices in the mason jar, alternating layers of each.
Slowly, pour honey over the lemon and ginger. Allow the honey to sink down and around the lemon and ginger slices. Fill jar to the top with honey and seal tightly.
Store in the refrigerator. Over time, the mixture will start to turn into a loose jelly.
When you are in need of some soothing tea, scoop 2-3 tablespoons into a mug full of hot water. Allow to seep for 3-4 minutes and sip away.
