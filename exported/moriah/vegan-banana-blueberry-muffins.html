Forkinit: Vegan Banana Blueberry Muffins

By Moriah Lindsley
You can substitute apples for the blueberries to make a tasty fall variation.

Ingredients

2 very ripe bananas, mashed
1/2 C white sugar
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 C all-purpose flour
1/2 C whole wheat pastry flour
1 1/2 tsp Egg Replacer (dry)
2 T water
1/2 C blueberries (or apples)
(Cinnamon)

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F. Grease muffin cups or line with paper muffin liners.
2. In a large bowl combine mashed bananas, sugar, baking powder, salt and flours (and cinnamon, if using); mix until smooth. In a small bowl or cup combine egg replacer and water; stir into banana mixture. Fold in blueberries (or apples).
3. Spoon batter evenly, about 1/4 C each, into muffin cups.
4. Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until golden brown.
