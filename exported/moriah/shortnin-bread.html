Forkinit: Short'nin' Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 C sifted all-purpose flour
1 C brown sugar, packed
2 C vegan butter

Instructions

Combine flour ad sugar in mixing bowl; cut in butter with pastry blender. Pat dough 1/2" thick on floured surface; cut with 1 1/2" cookie cutter; place on ungreased cookie sheets. Bake at 350Â°F about 20 minutes.

Yields: About 5 dozen
