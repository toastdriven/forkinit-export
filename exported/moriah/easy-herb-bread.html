Forkinit: Easy Herb Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 pkg yeast
2 3/4 C milk (non-dairy)
6 C flour
3 T sugar
2 tsp salt
2 T soft shortening (vegan)
1 T Fox Point Seasoning
1 1/2 tsp Dried Minced Onion
1 1/2 tsp Dill Weed
1 T agave nectar

Instructions

1. Heat milk to 110Ã‚Â°F; dissolve yeast in milk.
2. Add half the flour, along with the sugar, salt, shortening, agave, Minced Onion, Dill Weed and Fox Point Seasoning. Blend at low speed until mixed, then beat 2 minutes at medium speed. Add remaining flour and beat on low speed until dough is smooth (approximately 1 minute). Cover and let rise until doubled, about 45 minutes.
3. Stir the batter hard for 30 seconds. Spread into two 8 or 9" greased loaf pans (batter will be sticky). Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes.
4. Bake at 325Ã‚Â°F for about 40-45 minutes, until golden brown.
