Forkinit: Vegan Kheer - Indian Rice Pudding

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

8 C almond milk
1/2 C rice
1/2 C sugar
1/4-1/2 tsp cardamom powder
Sprinkle of nutmeg
Chopped almonds and pistachios to taste, a few T is good
Pinch of saffron

Instructions

Wash rice. Boil the milk in a saucepan and add the rice about 5 minutes after it starts boiling. Keep boiling off the liquid fairly rapidly while stirring until it's around half the original volume, then add the sugar and spices. Cook for another 8 minutes or so. Remove from heat and garnish with nuts and maybe some extra saffron.

Rice milk or coconut milk might work for this too.
