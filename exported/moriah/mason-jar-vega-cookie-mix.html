Forkinit: Mason Jar Vega Cookie Mix

By Moriah Lindsley
A great gift idea!

Ingredients

Dry Mix:
1 cup all-purpose gluten-free flour
1/2 cup gluten-free oats
1 serving Vanilla Vega Essentials
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 cup brown sugar, packed
1 T ground chia seed
1 tsp ground espresso or instant coffee (or cinnamon!)
1 1/2 cup chocolate chips

Wet Mix:
3/4 C vegan butter
3 T water
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Mason Jar Directions:
Sift flour with baking soda, baking powder and Vega Essentials.
In a one-quart Mason jar, layer sifted flour mix, oats, chia seeds, espresso and brown sugar.
Top with chocolate chips.
Seal Mason jar and decorate with festive ribbon and a personalized label.

Cookie Directions:
Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
Cream vegan butter, water, and vanilla together.
Add Mason jar ingredients to butter mixture.
Mix until combined.
Shape dough into balls and drop on ungreased baking sheet.
Cook until golden brown, approximately 9-11 minutes.
Cool for 2 minutes then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.
