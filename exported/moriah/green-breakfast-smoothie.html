Forkinit: Green Breakfast Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 lemon, quartered (washed and unpeeled)
1 whole avocado, peeled and pitted
2 stalks celery
2 cups spinach
1/2 bunch parsley
1/2 bunch cilantro
1 organic cucumber (washed and unpeeled)
1 teaspoon organic extra-virgin olive oil
Pinch of sea salt
*Ginger (optional)
Water as needed
Berries (optional) – adding berries will make your smoothie sweeter

*Editor’s note: We’ve added an additional ingredient to make this smoothie even healthier than it already is.

Instructions

Place all ingredients in a blender.
Blend together until smooth, adding water, as needed for desired consistency.

Yields: 1 smoothie
Prep time: 5 minutes
