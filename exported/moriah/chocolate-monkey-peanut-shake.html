Forkinit: Chocolate Monkey Peanut Shake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C soy milk, vanilla-flavored
1 banana, sliced and frozen
1/2 C ice cubes
2 T chocolate syrup
1 T peanut butter, creamy variety

Instructions

1. Blend all ingredients in blender on high for 30 seconds or until smooth.
2. Serve immediately.
3. If desired, before serving, drizzle chocolate syrup in a swirl down the inside of clear glasses. Pour shake into glasses and top with an additional swirl of chocolate.
