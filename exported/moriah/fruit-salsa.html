Forkinit: Fruit Salsa

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C finely chopped strawberries
1 orange, peeled, seeded, and finely chopped
3 kiwis, peeled and finely chopped
1/2 C finely chopped fresh pineapple
2 green onions, finely chopped
1/4 C finely chopped yellow or green bell pepper
1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 T fresh lime juice

Instructions

1. Combine the ingredients well in a large bowl. Cover and chill for several hours or overnight.
Makes 4 servings
