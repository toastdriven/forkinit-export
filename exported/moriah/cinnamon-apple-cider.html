Forkinit: Cinnamon Apple Cider

By Moriah Lindsley
Excellent for Halloween!

Ingredients

12 C apple cider
6 C cranberry juice
1/2 C brown sugar
2 T vegan butter
4 (4") Indonesian Cinnamon Sticks
2 Ceylon Softstick Cinnamon Sticks
10 Whole Cloves (we used Ceylon)
2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract

Instructions

1. Combine the apple cider, cranberry juice, brown sugar, butter, Cinnamon Sticks and Cloves in a large pot and bring to a boil.
2. Reduce the heat to low so the cider is gently simmering and add the Vanilla Extract.
3. Simmer for 45 minutes to blend the flavors and serve.
