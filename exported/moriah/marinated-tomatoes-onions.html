Forkinit: Marinated Tomatoes & Onions

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 onion, thinly sliced
3 large tomatoes, sliced
1 tsp garlic powder
2 tsp salt
4 tsp sugar
1 tsp pepper
2 T yellow mustard
1 C salad oil
1/4 C + 2 T vinegar

Instructions

Alternate tomatoes & onions in shallow bowl. Combine rest of ingredients & pour over tomatoes. Marinate 12-24 hours.

Variation: Fresh basil and slices of vegan mozzarella can be substituted for the onions.
