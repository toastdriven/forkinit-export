Forkinit: Vegan Coconut Ice Cream

By Moriah Lindsley
Scrumptious!

Ingredients

1 can coconut milk (not the reduced fat kind)
1 (12 oz) package silken tofu
1/3 C organic sugar, to taste
1 T vanilla
1/3-1/2 C toasted coconut, pineapple chunks, nuts OR chocolate chips, or more than one (optional)

Instructions

1. In a blender combine coconut milk, tofu, sugar and vanilla; mix well.
2. Test for sweetness and add more sugar if desired.
3. Transfer the mixture to an ice cream maker and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions.
