Forkinit: Spicy Broccoli

By Moriah Lindsley
A fabulous side dish!

Ingredients

This dish is gorgeous green and firecracker spicy. It's delicious served hot or cold. Enjoy it tossed into your salad bowl with crisp lettuce, cherry tomatoes and sweet red peppers.

1 T vegetable oil
4 C broccoli florets
1/4 C vegetable broth or water
1/2 tsp salt
2 T Thai Kitchen Red Chili Dipping Sauce

Instructions

1. In a hot wok or skillet, heat vegetable oil for 1 minute over medium high heat and then add broccoli.
2. Toss until bright green; add vegetable broth and cover tightly. Reduce heat to medium and cook 2 to 3 minutes, until tender crisp.
3. Uncover and add salt and the red chili dipping sauce, toss well and transfer to a serving dish.
Makes 3 to 4 servings.
