Forkinit: Mock Cream Cheese Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

8 oz lite firm tofu, squeezed of excess liquid
2 T Splenda sugar substitute
1/2 tsp vanilla extract or walnut extract

Instructions

1. Whiz all ingredients in a food processor.
2. Spread on cooled cake.
3. Enough to cover top of an 8X8 cake.
