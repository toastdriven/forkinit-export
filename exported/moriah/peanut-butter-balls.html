Forkinit: Peanut Butter Balls

By Moriah Lindsley
A Christmas family favorite growing up!

Ingredients

3 C (28 oz jar) peanut butter, plain or crunchy
1 1/2 lbs powdered sugar
2 sticks butter
1/2 square parafin (optional)
1-2 tsp shortening
14 oz pkg semisweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Mix first three ingredients thoroughly and shape into balls.
2. Melt parafin, if using, for 15 minutes on Hi in microwave.
3. Melt chocolate chips and shortening together for 3 minutes in microwave; mix with parafin.
4. Dip each ball into chocolate using toothpicks. If chocolate is too thick, add a little more shortening to thin it.
5. Place balls on waxed paper. Cool in refrigerator.
