Forkinit: Green Ice Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 c raw almonds
1 c water, divided
1 ripe avocado
1 c fresh spinach
2 c ice
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 tsp almond extract
Sweetener of choice, to taste

Instructions

In Vitamix blender, process almonds with 1/2 C water until smooth. Add remaining 1/2 C water with the rest of the ingredients and blend until smooth. Taste for sweetness and adjust as needed. Enjoy!

Yields: 1 serving
Source: A woman I met at Costco who has her own Vitamix.
