Forkinit: Pink Rhubarb Soda

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 T rhubarb syrup from Rhubarb Galette (per glass)
Sparkling soda water

Instructions

Fill tall glass with ice. Pour rhubarb syrup over ice and top off with sparkling soda water. Stir and enjoy!
