Forkinit: Fruity Oat Bars

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 pkg (6 oz) diced dried mixed fruit (about 1 1/3 C)
3/4 C water
1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
1 1/4 C rolled oats, uncooked
1/3 C firmly packed brown sugar
1/4 C all-purpose flour
1/4 C margarine, melted (vegan)

Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350Ã‚Â°F. In small saucepan, combine fruit, water and cinnamon. Cook over low heat 10 minutes, stirring constantly, or until almost all liquid is absorbed. Remove from heat; cover. Set aside.
2. Combine oats, brown sugar and flour. Add margarine; mix well until crumbly. Reserve 1/3 C oat mixture; press remaining mixture on bottom of 8 or 9" baking pan. Bake 10 to 15 minutes or until golden brown.
3. Spread fruit mixture evenly over base; sprinkle with reserved 1/3 C oat mixture, patting lightly. Bake 20 minutes or until topping is golden brown. Cool; cut into bars. Store loosely covered.
