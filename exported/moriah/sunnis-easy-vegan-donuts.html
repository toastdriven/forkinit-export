Forkinit: Sunni's Easy Vegan Donuts

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C whole wheat flour
1/2 C sugar
2 tsp baking soda
Dash of Himalayan salt
1/2 tsp each nutmeg, cinnamon, cloves
1/2 C non-dairy milk (almond, rice, coconut, soy)
1/2 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp apple cider vinegar (ACV)
3 T melted coconut oil

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
In a small bowl, mix milk of choice and ACV; set aside for 10 minutes.
In a large bowl, mix all dry ingredients together well. Mix the rest of the wet ingredients together, then add wet to dry.
Fill mixture 3/4 full in each circle of a doughnut pan. Bake 10-12 minutes.
