Forkinit: Sweet and Sour Seitan

By Moriah Lindsley
A vegan sweet and sour dish.

Ingredients

2 T canola or corn oil
2 red and/or green bell peppers, cut into 1" pieces
1 large carrot, peeled, halved longways & sliced diagonally into 1/4" slices
1 1/2 C fresh or canned pineapple, cut in 3/4" pieces
3/4 C 365 Organic Everday Value Soy Ginger Sauce
3 T rice wine vinegar
2 green onions, thinly sliced at an angle
8 to 12 oz seitan, cut into strips (or pre-cut, pre-marinated strips)
2 C cooked brown or white rice

Instructions

In a large saute pan or wok, heat oil and saute bell peppers and carrots about 3 to 5 minutes. Add pineapple and saute 2 minutes. Reduce heat to medium-low. Add soy ginger sauce, rice wine vinegar, and seitan strips. Heat through and serve with rice. Serves 4.
