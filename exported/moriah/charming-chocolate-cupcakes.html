Forkinit: Charming Chocolate Cupcakes

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C molasses
1 C soy milk
2 C chocolate or carob chips
6 T oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
4 T cornstarch
2 C flour
1 tsp baking soda
6 paper baking cups*

*Moriah's Note: When I made this recipe, it made way more than 6 cupcakes! I used a regular-sized 12-space muffin tin and even that wasn't enough. This recipe would make 18-24 medium cupcakes.

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Ã‚Â°F.
2. In a small saucepan, whisk together the molasses, 1/2 C of the milk, and the chips.
3. Cook on medium heat until chips have melted, stirring constantly with a spoon.
4. Add the oil, vanilla, cornstarch, and stir well until mixed.
5. Remove from heat and set aside.
6. In a medium bowl, sift the flour and baking soda together.
7. Add the remaining milk and the chocolate mixture and stir.
8. Spoon into cupcake paper liners and bake for 15-20 minutes.
9. Test with a knife to see if done.
10. Once cooled, you can frost with icing.

Variation:
1 C cocoa and 1-3/4 C sugar to replace the chips
1 tsp baking powder to replace the cornstarch
A pinch of salt added
