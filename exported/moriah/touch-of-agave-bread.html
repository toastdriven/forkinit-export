Forkinit: Touch of Agave Bread

By Moriah Lindsley
Use agave nectar instead of honey for a delicious vegan loaf!

1 C hot oat bran cereal, uncooked
1 package quick-rise yeast
1/2 tsp salt
1 1/4 C water
2 T agave nectar
