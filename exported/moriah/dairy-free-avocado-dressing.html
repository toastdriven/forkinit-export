Forkinit: Dairy-Free Avocado Dressing

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 avocados
1 garlic clove
Juice of 1 lemon
1/4 cup of olive oil
Rice wine vinegar

Instructions

Blend all ingredients together in a blender and enjoy!
