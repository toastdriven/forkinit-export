Forkinit: Black Bean Wrap

By Moriah Lindsley
A tasty, nutritious wrap with a Mexican flair.

Ingredients

3/4 C black olives, sliced
1 avocado, diced
1 medium tomato, diced
1 C corn
1 1/2 C black beans, cooked OR 1 can black beans, drained
2 large tortilla shells
Cilantro, as desired
Taco seasoning, as desired

Instructions

Divide the first five ingredients between the two tortilla shells. Add cilantro and taco seasoning to taste. Serve and enjoy!
