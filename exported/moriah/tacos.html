Forkinit: Tacos

By Moriah Lindsley
Tacos are an easy and quick meal!

Ingredients

6-8 small taco shells
1 can of refried beans
1 cup vegan cheese, shredded
Salsa
Taco seasoning
Fresh Cilantro

Instructions

1. Heat refried beans until warm.
2. Spread equal portions of refried beans on shells and top with remaining ingredients. Serve.
