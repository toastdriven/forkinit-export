Forkinit: Spiders!

By Moriah Lindsley
Excellent spooky treats for kids.

Ingredients

Round crackers
Peanut butter
Pretzel sticks
Chocolate chips
(All vegan of course)

Instructions

Put some peanut butter on a cracker
Break 4 pretzel sticks in half
Place on top of the peanut butter as 8 spider legs
Spread a bit of peanut butter on top of another cracker & place it on top
Then use a bit of peanut butter to "glue" the chocolate chips on top as eye balls
Kids love them and if you use natural peanut butter & whole wheat crackers like I do, they are pretty healthy treats!
