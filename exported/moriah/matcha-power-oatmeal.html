Forkinit: Matcha Power Oatmeal

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C old fashioned rolled oats
1 tsp matcha tea powder
1/2 C milk of choice
Sweetener, to taste
Sliced almonds, coconut flakes, chia seeds (optional)

Instructions

Blend first two ingredients well. Stir in milk and sweetener. Cook on stovetop or microwave until desired consistency. Sprinkle on toppings of choice.

Yields: 2 servings
