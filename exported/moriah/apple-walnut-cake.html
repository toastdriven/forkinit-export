Forkinit: Apple Walnut Cake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C vegetable oil
2 C granulated sugar
Egg substitute equivalent to 2 eggs or 1/2 C soft silken tofu
3 T vanilla
3 C flour
1 1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
1 C chopped walnuts
3 C diced apples

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. In a large bowl, combine the oil, sugar, egg substitute or tofu, and vanilla. In a separate bowl, sift the flour, baking soda, and salt.
2. Add this to the oil-and-sugar mixture and stir by hand, approximately 200 strokes, until thoroughly mixed. Fold in the walnuts and apples. The batter will be stiff.
3. Pour into a greased and floured 10" tube or bundt pan. Bake at 350Â°F for 1 hour and 15 minutes.
