Forkinit: Cucumber and Lime Spritzer

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 12-oz cans club soda
1/4 C fresh lime juice (from 3-4 limes)
1 Kirby cucumber, thinly sliced

Instructions

In a pitcher, combine the club soda, lime juice, and cucumber. Serve over ice.
For an alcoholic cocktail: Stir in 1/2 C gin before serving.

Yields: 4 servings
