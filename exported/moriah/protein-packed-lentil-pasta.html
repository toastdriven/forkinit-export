Forkinit: Protein-Packed Lentil Pasta

By Moriah Lindsley
Lentils add protein to this basic dish.

Ingredients

2-3 C dry eggless pasta of choice
1 jar pasta sauce of choice
1 C dry lentils

Instructions

1. Simmer lentils until soft. Drain.
2. Meanwhile, cook pasta and heat sauce.
3. Combine ingredients and serve.
