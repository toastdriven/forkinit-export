Forkinit: Mint Pesto

By Moriah Lindsley
Pesto can be made with basil, cilantro, mint or arugula. Mint brings a particularly fresh, somewhat spicy flavor to this classic Italian sauce.

Ingredients

2 C packed fresh mint leaves
3 garlic cloves, peeled
1/3 C grated Parmesan cheese (or Mock Parmesan)
1/4 C toasted pine nuts
1/2 C olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Put the mint leaves, garlic, cheese, and pine nuts in a food processor with 2 T of the olive oil and pulse several times. Gradually add the remaining oil with the blades turning and pulse until the pesto is smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper.
Toss mint pesto with pasta or grilled vegetables for a summer salad. Spread thin slices of toasted baguette with pesto and top with sliced tomatoes.

Makes about 1 cup
