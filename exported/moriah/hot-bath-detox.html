Forkinit: Hot Bath Detox

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C Epson salt
1 C baking soda
1 T ground ginger
Hot water to fill bath

Instructions

Fill tub with hot water and mix in first three ingredients. Soak for 40 minutes. Rinse.

Take two more baths with the same ingredients twice more in the same week for 20 minutes each time to complete the detox.
