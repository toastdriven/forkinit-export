Forkinit: Pizza Soup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C hot water
1 tsp Italian seasoning
1 vegetable bouillon cube
1 clove garlic
1 whole roma tomato
1 rib celery
1 carrot

Instructions

Put all ingredients into Vitamix blender and process on high 6-7 minutes, or until steam escapes vents. Adjust seasoning as needed, and add any pizza "toppings" desired, such as black olives, cheese cubes or other veggies. Blend again and enjoy!
