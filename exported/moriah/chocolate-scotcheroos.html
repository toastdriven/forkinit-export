Forkinit: Chocolate Scotcheroos

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C light corn syrup
1 C sugar
1 C peanut butter
6 C Kellogg's Rice Krispies OR 6 C Cocoa Krispies
1 pkg (6 oz, 1 C) semisweet chocolate morsels
1 C butterscotch chips

Instructions

1. Place corn syrup and sugar into 3-quart saucepan. Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until sugar dissolves and mixture begins to boil. Remove from heat. Stir in peanut butter. Mix well. Add Kellogg's Rice Krispies cereal. Stir until well coated. Press mixture into 13x9x2" pan coated with cooking spray. Set aside.
2. Melt chocolate and butterscotch chips together in 1-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly. Spread evenly over cereal mixture. Let stand until firm. Cut into 2x1" bars when cool.
