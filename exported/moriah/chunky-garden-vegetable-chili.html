Forkinit: Chunky Garden Vegetable Chili

By Moriah Lindsley
This chili gets its great texture from dense cracked wheat.

Ingredients

1 T olive or canola oil
1 medium onion, chopped
2 carrots, chopped
2 stalks large celery, chopped
1 medium green pepper, seeded and chopped
1 medium red pepper, seeded and chopped
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 jalepeno pepper, seeded and minced
1 T dried oregano
2 tsp cumin
1 T chili powder
1 C tomato puree
1 (28-oz) can chopped tomatoes
2 quarts cold water or vegetable stock
1 C cracked wheat
1 (16-oz) can red or white beans, rinsed and drained
1 C cooked black beans
Juice of 2 limes
1/2 C tamari or soy sauce
1 bunch fresh cilantro, minced

Instructions

1. Heat the olive or canola oil in a large sauce pot over medium heat, and saute the onion, carrots, celery, peppers, garlic, jalepeno, oregano, cumin, and chili powder for 2 minutes to combine well.

2. Add the tomato puree, tomatoes, cold water, cracked wheat, red or white beans, and black beans; simmer uncovered for 50 minutes, until blended. You may add additional vegetable stock, water, or tomato juice to adjust the consistency, if needed.

3. Add the lime juice, tamari, and cilantro. Serve with grated cheese, sour cream and minced scallions.
