Forkinit: Black Bean Charros

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/4 C dried black beans, cooked until tender
1 1/2 tsp salt
3 T canola oil
5 T finely chopped onion
4 garlic cloves, peeled and finely chopped
6 canned plum tomatoes, finely chopped, plus 1/4 C of their liquid
1/2 to 1 jalepeno chile, very finely chopped
2 T chopped fresh cilantro

Instructions

1. Heat oil in large frying pan over medium-high heat.
2. Add onions and garlic, stirring and sauteing until golden. Add the chopped tomatoes and their liquid and the jalepeno, and bring to a simmer.
3. Cover and cook gently for 10 minutes.
4. Stir the tomato mixture and the chopped cilantro into the beans, and bring to a simmer over low heat. Simmer gently for 5 minutes and serve hot.

Source: Madhur Jaffrey's World Vegetarian cookbook
