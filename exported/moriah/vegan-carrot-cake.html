Forkinit: Vegan Carrot Cake

By Moriah Lindsley
Complete with a luscious cream cheese frosting!

Ingredients

3/4 cup sugar
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 cups flour
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
3 cups finely-shredded carrots
3/4 cup vegetable oil
Cake:
3/4 C applesauce
2 tsp vanilla
4 egg substitute (Ener-G)
1/2 C chopped nuts (optional)

Cream Cheese Frosting:
3 oz Better Than Cream Cheese cream cheese substitute
1/4 C margarine
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 C powdered sugar

Instructions

1. Grease and flour a 10" X 9" or square cake pan.
2. Stir together dry ingredients.
3. Mix in the carrots, egg subs, oil, applesauce, and vanilla.
4. Beat about 2-3 minutes, until well mixed.
5. Stir in the nuts, if used.
6. Bake at 325Â°F for about 40-50 minutes , or until a toothpick comes out clean.
7. Cool on a wire rack.
8. Once the BTCC is at room temperature, mix all the frosting ingredients together. Frost cake.
