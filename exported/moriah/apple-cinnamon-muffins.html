Forkinit: Apple Cinnamon Muffins

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 1/4 C oat bran cereal
1/4 C firmly packed brown sugar
1 T baking powder
1 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
3/4 C apple juice or cider
1/2 C milk (non-dairy)
2 Egg Replacer
2 T vegetable oil
1 medium apple, peeled and chopped
1/4 C chopped walnuts
1/4 C raisins
