Forkinit: Strawberry Lemonade Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 cups lemonade
1 1/2 cups frozen strawberries
1 large frozen banana
2 Tbsp raw cashews
1 cup coconut water (or regular) ice cubes
Squeeze of fresh lemon juice
Lemon slices and/or mint leaves for garnish
Fresh strawberries blended in, optional

Instructions

Blend and serve!

Yields: 2 servings
