Forkinit: Korean BBQ Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 stick nondairy margarine
1/4 C apple cider vinegar
1 C tomato sauce
1/2 T salt
1 T hot mustard
2 tsp lemon juice
1 tsp hickory-flavored liquid smoke
3 T sugar

Instructions

1. Melt the margarine in a large saucepan.
2. Add the remaining ingredients and stir until well blended.
3. Bring to a boil, lower heat, and simmer 5 minutes.
Makes approximately 3/4 cup
