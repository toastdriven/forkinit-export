Forkinit: Avocado Corn Soup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 ripe avocados
1 can corn kernels
1 14-oz can vegetable broth
1-2 T lime juice
Cilantro, mint or basil leaves for garnish

Instructions

Cut avocados in half, remove pit and peel. Put avocados into a blender. Add broth and lime juice. Blend until smooth. Pour into medium saucepan, stir in corn. Heat over medium heat until thoroughly steamed. Garnish. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
