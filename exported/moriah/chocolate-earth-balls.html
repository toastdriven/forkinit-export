Forkinit: Chocolate Earth Balls

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C organic peanut butter
1/3 C honey (or agave)
2 tsp carob powder or unsweetened organic cocoa powder
1/2 C organic raisins
3/4 C unsweetened shredded organic coconut, divided
1/2 C organic chocolate chips
1/4 C organic sesame seeds
1/4 C finely chopped organic nuts (walnuts, pecans, etc.)

Instructions

1. Before measuring the peanut butter, stir it up well.
2. Mix the peanut butter, honey and carob or cocoa powder until well combined. Stir in the raisins and only 1/8 C of the coconut. Stir in the chocolate chips. Refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours.
3. Place the remaining coconut, sesame seeds and nuts into 3 separate bowls. Using a spoon, scoop small heaps of the peanut mixture from the bowl; roll into 1 1/4" balls. Rolling is easier if you form a rough ball, roll in the coconut, and then continue rolling into a more perfect shape. Roll each finished ball in more coconut, sesame seeds and chopped nuts. Arrange the balls on a plate, cover loosely with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

Nutrition Information
Per serving (42g-wt.): 210 calories (140 calories from fat), 15g total fat, 5g saturated fat, 3g dietary fiber, 5g protein, 18g carbohydrate, 0mg cholesterol, 85mg sodium
