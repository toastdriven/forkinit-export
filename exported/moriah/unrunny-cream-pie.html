Forkinit: Unrunny Cream Pie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Pie Filling:
16 oz package silken tofu
2 C sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 tsp lemon juice
Salt to taste
1 C rice flour
3/4 C water (room temperature)

Pie Crust:
1 1/4 C graham cracker crumbs
1/4 sugar
5 T margarine melted

Instructions

Pie Crust:
Mix graham cracker crumbs, sugar, and margarine. Transfer into a 9 inch pie pan and press into the sides and bottom with a spoon. Refrigerate.

Pie Filling:
In a food processor, blend tofu until totally smooth (2-3 minutes). While tofu is blending put water in a sauce pan, and slowly whisk in rice flour. Then turn on the heat and cook on medium heat stirring constantly until mixture thickens. Keep stirring until it turns into a thick, smooth, sticky paste. (This should occur pretty quickly and should not be lumpy at all. If it is lumpy, it wasn't mixed all the way prior to putting the heat on and you should start over.)

Add rice flour mixture into the food processor with the tofu and continue to blend. Add remaining ingredients (sugar, vanilla, lemon juice, & salt) and mix well.

Spoon filling into crust and refrigerate for an hour.
