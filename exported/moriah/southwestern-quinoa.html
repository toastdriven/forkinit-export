Forkinit: Southwestern Quinoa

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 scallions, sliced
1 jalapeno, minced
Olive oil
2 tsp tomato paste
1/2 tsp each ground cumin, ancho chile powder, kosher salt
1 1/4 C water
3/4 C quinoa, rinsed
3/4 C frozen fire-roasted corn
1 can pinto beans, drained and rinsed

Instructions

Saut√© scallions and jalapeno in olive oil until softened. Stir in tomato paste, cumin, chile powder and salt. Add water, quinoa, and corn. Simmer until the liquid is absorbed, 15-20 minutes. Stir in pinto beans; season with salt.
