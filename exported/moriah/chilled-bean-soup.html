Forkinit: Chilled Bean Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
This soup tastes best when you let it mellow overnight in the refrigerator.

Ingredients

4 C chopped seeded tomatoes
2 C picante V8 juice
1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
1 C chopped cucumber
1 C chopped sweet red or yellow pepper
1/2 C chopped red onion
2 T balsamic vinegar
1 tsp sugar
1/4 to 1/2 tsp hot pepper sauce
1/4 tsp ground cumin
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp pepper
7 T reduced-fat sour cream (vegan)
Sliced cucumber, optional

Instructions

In a blender or food processor, combine tomatoes and V8 juice; cover and process just until blended. Transfer to a large bowl. Stir in the beans, chopped cucumber, sweet pepper, onion, vinegar, sugar and seasonings. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight. Serve with sour cream. Garnish with sliced cucumber if desired.
