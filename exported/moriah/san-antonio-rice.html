Forkinit: San Antonio Rice

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup uncooked long-grain rice
1 12-ounce jar fresh salsa, mild or hot
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
1 cup fresh or canned corn kernels
1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
1/2 teaspoon salt
Fresh ground black pepper to taste
1 1/4 cups water
Minced fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Instructions

Add all ingredients, except cilantro, to the inner pot of a rice cooker, stir, close the lid and press the White Rice button. When rice cooker switches to Keep-Warm mode, open the cooker, quickly stir and re-cover. Allow to stand for 8-10 minutes before serving. Spoon onto a serving dish, garnish with fresh cilantro and serve.
