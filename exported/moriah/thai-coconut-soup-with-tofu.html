Forkinit: Thai Coconut Soup with Tofu

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 C vegetable broth
1 half-inch piece of ginger, peeled and sliced
2 stalks lemongrass, peeled, trimmed, and minced
1/2 C fresh lime juice (about 3 limes)
2 medium jalapenos, seeded and diced
2 T tamari
1 C carrots, peeled and sliced
1 1/2 C mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 C light coconut milk
2 T cilantro, minced
12 oz extra firm tofu, cut into 2" wide pieces

Instructions

In a large stock pot, combine the broth, ginger, lemongrass, lime juice, jalapenos and tamari. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 10 minutes. Add the mushrooms, coconut milk, cilantro and tofu. Return to a simmer and cook for another 10-15 until heated through.

Serving suggestion: Just add a spring roll appetizer to this main-dish soup for a quick meal. If you spot oyster mushrooms in the produce aisle, use these for an even more authentically Thai dish.
