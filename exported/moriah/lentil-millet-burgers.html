Forkinit: Lentil Millet Burgers

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C dry millet, cooked in 3 C water until fluffy
3 C cooked lentils, drained
2 carrots, finely grated
1 tsp fenugreek
1.5 dried chilies
1.5 tsp cumin
2 tsp tomato paste
1/4 C ground flax seed

Instructions

1. Put the lentils in a bowl. Grind the three spices in a coffee grinder and add into the lentils with the tomato paste and carrots. Add sea salt to taste. Mash it all up.

2. Add the cooked millet and flax seeds to the mixture, and run it through a food processor until smooth.

3. Form into patties and press slivered almonds into one or both sides.
