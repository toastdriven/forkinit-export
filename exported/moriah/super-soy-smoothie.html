Forkinit: Super Soy Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/4 block of silken-style soft tofu
1 C vanilla flavored soymilk (1/4-1/2 C less soymilk for a thicker smoothie)
1/2 C vegan vanilla yogurt
1 C of your favorite fruit sliced into chunks

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a blender and blend until mixed well.

Yields: 2 smoothies
