Forkinit: Traditional Mimosa

By Moriah Lindsley
Fresh squeezed juice and good quality champagne will make all the difference in the final product.

Ingredients

3 oz champagne
2 oz orange juice
Orange slice

Instructions

Fill a champagne glass 2/3 full with champagne. Top with fresh-squeezed orange juice and garnish with an orange slice. For an added kick, try our Whole Foods Market 365 Italian Tangerine Soda with a splash of champagne.
