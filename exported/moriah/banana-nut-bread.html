Forkinit: Banana Nut Bread

By Moriah Lindsley
The recipe I grew up on as a kid!

Ingredients

3 large bananas, mashed
1 1/2 C sugar
1/2 C vegan shortening
Egg Replacer equal to 2 eggs
6 T sour milk (non-dairy)
1 tsp cinnamon
3 C flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 C nuts, chopped or ground
1/2 C chocolate chips

Instructions

Mix bananas, sugar & shortening together, & add Egg Replacer eggs & milk. Sift dry ingredients together & add to the above. Add nuts & chocolate chips last. Bake in 2 greased loaf pans at 350Â°F for 45 minutes, or bake in washed & greased vegetable or soup cans filled 2/3 full for about 30 minutes.

Yields: 2 loaves
