Forkinit: Nanaimo Bars

By Moriah Lindsley
A favorite holiday cookie recipe from my childhood!

Ingredients

Crust:
1/2 C butter or shortening (vegan)
1/4 C sugar
5 T cocoa
1 tsp vanilla
1 egg (or Egg Replacer)
1/4 tsp salt
2 C graham cracker crumbs (28 single)
1 C coconut (optional)
1/2 C chopped pecans

Cream Filling:
1/2 C butter or shortening, softened (vegan)
3 oz cream cheese (vegan)
2 T powdered instant vanilla pudding mix
1 C powdered sugar
2 T milk (non-dairy)

Topping:
5-6 (1 oz) squares semi-sweet chocolate, melted

Instructions

For Crust:
Combine first five ingredients in bowl. Microwave until butter is melted and it starts sticking together. Combine remaining ingredients, blend well and add to cooked mixture. Pat into 9" square pan.

For Filling:
Blend ingredients with mixer until smooth. Spread over crust. Let stand in refrigerator 10-15 minutes. Cover with melted semi-sweet chocolate. Let partially set in refrigerator about 10 minutes (so chocolate doesn't crack), then cut into squares and let set well.
