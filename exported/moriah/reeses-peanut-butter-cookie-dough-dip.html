Forkinit: Reese’s Peanut Butter Cookie Dough Dip

By Moriah Lindsley
I'm making this vegan!!!

Ingredients

1/2 cup unsalted butter (or vegan buttersticks)
1/2 cup light brown sugar
1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
8 oz cream cheese, softened (vegan)
3/4 cup powdered sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/2 cup semi sweet mini chocolate chips (non-dairy)
8oz pkg Reese's peanut butter cup Minis (or about 1 1/2 cup chopped Reeses cups, or Justin's Dark Chocolate PB Cups to make vegan)

Instructions

In a small saucepan over medium heat, add brown sugar and butter. Whisk until completely combined and butter is melted. Bring to a boil, remove from heat. Whisk in the peanut butter and vanilla extract. Set aside to cool.
In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese with powdered sugar until creamy, about 3-4 minutes. On low, add in brown sugar mixture (that has cooled to near room temp). Mix until combined. Fold in mini chocolate chips and mini Reese's/Justin's cups. Serve immediately or store in refrigerator until ready to serve. Enjoy with pretzels, animal cracker and graham sticks.

Yields: 10-12 servings
