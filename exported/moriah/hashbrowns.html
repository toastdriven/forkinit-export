Forkinit: Hashbrowns

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 lb russet potatoes, diced
Olive oil
Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

1. Mix potatoes with olive oil, salt and pepper.
2. Cover and cook over low heat for 20 minutes.
