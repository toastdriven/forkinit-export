Forkinit: Quick Peanut Dip

By Moriah Lindsley
Try this Southeast Asian flavored dip for your next party. Or add more warm water to make a quick sauce.

Ingredients

3 T Maranatha Peanut Butter
3 T Mirin (sweetened rice vinegar)
1 T chopped cilantro
2 medium garlic cloves finely chopped
2 tsp light soy sauce
1 tsp Loriva 5-pepper oil
1 tsp finely chopped fresh ginger
2-6 T warm water

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in bowl or food processor.
2. Add warm water to desired consistency.
3. Serve cold with carrots, celery and cucumbers.
