Forkinit: Tropical Limeade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 pineapple
2 cucumbers
1 bunch parsley
1" piece ginger root
3 lemons, peeled
2 limes, peeled

Instructions

Wash ingredients thoroughly.
In a juicer, juice all ingredients.
Drink right away, or store it in tightly closed jars for 1-2 days. You can store it for 2-4 days if you use a vacuum sealer.

Yields: 4 C
Prep time: 30 minutes
