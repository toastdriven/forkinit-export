Forkinit: Roasting and Peeling Chestnuts

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Raw chestnuts, unpeeled

Instructions

Cut through the skin of the chestnut with a small knife, cutting an X on the flat or score across the "belly."
Cook using one of the following methods:
Microwave 6-8 nuts for about 30 seconds,
Cover and roast at 375Â°F for 20 minutes, more or less,
Immerse in boiling water 1-2 minutes, or,
Using a heavy skillet or wok, roast over the stove top on medium heat for 10-15 minutes.
Peel while still hot!
Roasting in the oven sweetens and intensifies the flavor of the nuts. Cooking time is a personal preference - chestnuts can be eaten raw.
Chestnuts are perishable - keep refrigerated!
