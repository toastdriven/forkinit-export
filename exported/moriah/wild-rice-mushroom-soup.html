Forkinit: Wild Rice Mushroom Soup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 T vegetable oil
1 C yellow onion, diced
1/2 C celery, diced
1/2 C carrot, diced
1/2 C red bell pepper, diced
3 cloves garlic, minced
1 lb button mushrooms, sliced
2 tsp dried thyme
1/2 C white wine
1 C wild rice (or wild rice blend)
6 C vegetable broth
1 C spinach, roughly chopped
1 C non-dairy milk or cream
Salt
Ground black pepper

Instructions

In a large Dutch oven or stockpot, heat the oil over medium-high heat. Saut√© the onion, celery, carrot and bell pepper for 5-10 minutes before adding the garlic, mushrooms, thyme, white wine and a pinch of salt. Cook for a few minutes until the mushrooms start to soften. Add the rice and broth and stir well. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, cover and simmer 30 minutes or until rice is tender. Add the spinach and cook for a few more minutes. Stir in the milk and season to taste with salt and ground black pepper. Serve warm.
