Forkinit: Par Avion

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 (3") sprigs fresh mint, leaves removed
1 1/2 oz white rum
1/2 oz lime juice
1/2 oz peach liqueur
1/4 oz simple syrup
3 oz sparkling wine, Prosecco, Cava or Champagne

Instructions

Reserve 1 mint leaf for garnish; place remaining leaves in cocktail shaker. Add all remaining ingredients, except sparkling wine. Fill cocktail shaker with ice; shake well.
Strain into coupe or wine glass; top with sparkling wine. Rub reserved mint leaf between fingers to release aromas; float mint leaf on the surface.

Yields: 1 serving
