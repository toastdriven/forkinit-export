Forkinit: Zesty Salsa

By Moriah Lindsley
Easy salsa recipe

Ingredients

4 firm, large tomatoes, halved
1/2 medium onion, grated
2 cloves garlic, minced or pressed
1/2 C cilantro, chopped
1 Jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped finely
Juice of 1 lime
Pinch or 2 of sugar
Salt to taste
Corn chips

Instructions

1. Grate tomatoes on largest holes of a hand grater. Use a flat hand grater if you have it. Do not use metal. If the tomato sauce looks too watery, strain a bit out.
2. Add the rest of the ingredients, except for the corn chips; refrigerate until serving time.
3. Taste just before serving, adding salt and lime if needed. Serve with corn chips. Serves 5.
