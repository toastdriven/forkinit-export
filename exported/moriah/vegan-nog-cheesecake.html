Forkinit: Vegan Nog Cheesecake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 9" deep-dish graham cracker crust
16 oz non-dairy cream cheese, softened
1/2 C sugar
2 T flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp nutmeg
2 T dark rum or 2 tsp vanilla
3/4 C Silk Nog
Egg replacer equal to 2 eggs

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325Â°F.
Beat cream cheese, sugar, flour, salt and nutmeg.
Beat in rum or vanilla and Silk Nog.
Beat in egg replacer eggs.
Pour into crust and bake 25-30 minutes.
Chill.

Yields: 8 servings
