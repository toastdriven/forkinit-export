Forkinit: Frankincense Sugar Body Scrub

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3/4 C raw sugar
1/4 C granulated sugar
1/8-1/4 C olive or coconut oil
5 drops Frankincense essential oil

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and store in air-tight container. Use to exfoliate skin. Will store up to 3 months.
