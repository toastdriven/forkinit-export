Forkinit: Seasoned Baked Tofu

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 lbs tofu, firm or extra-firm
1/3 C soy sauce
1 T toasted sesame oil
2 tsp garlic, crushed
1 T gingerroot

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400Â°F/200Â°C.
2. Remove the tofu from the package and rinse. If the tofu crumbles easily, press it to remove excess moisture.
3. Cut each block of tofu into 3/4"/2-centimeter cubes. Toss the tofu with soy sauce, toasted sesame oil, garlic, and ginger.
4. Spread out tofu on a baking sheet and bake for about 15 minutes. Tofu is now recipe-ready or may be served as is, sprinkled on top of salads, grain or vegetable dishes.

Makes 10 servings

Note: Other shapes may be cut and marinated for different presentations. Large slices can be used for sandwiches and triangles or smaller square or rectangular slices work well for plate presentations. Add a few drops of liquid smoke to marinate. For crisper tofu increase temperature or bake longer. To sauce the Seasoned Baked Tofu, use 1 or 2 batches of sauce per batch of tofu.
