Forkinit: Merry Mint Spray

By Moriah Lindsley
Infuse your home, car or office with this refreshing scent for extra holiday cheer!

Ingredients

4 drops peppermint essential oil
6 drops sweet orange essential oil
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
2 oz distilled water

Instructions

Place water, vanilla and essential oils in a small spray bottle. Shake contents before spritzing the air lightly with this seasonal fragrance.
