Forkinit: Pumpkin Orange Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 cup cooked pumpkin puree, fresh or canned
2 large seedless navel oranges, peeled
1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 cup coconut milk or non-dairy yogurt, optional

Instructions

Puree the pumpkin, oranges and cinnamon in a blender. If desired, add coconut milk or yogurt and process; serve immediately.

Yields: 2 servings
Prep time: 5 minutes
