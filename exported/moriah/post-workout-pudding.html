Forkinit: Post-Workout Pudding

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 ripe bananas
1 cup blueberries
1/2 cup soaked almonds
1/4 cup ground flaxseed
1 serving Chocolate Vega Sport® Performance Protein or Chocolate Vega® Clean Protein
1/4 cup raw cacao powder (or unsweetened chocolate powder)
1 tsp lemon juice
1/4 tsp sea salt
1/2-1 cup almond milk

Instructions

Before you work out, blend until smooth. Put in the refrigerator during your workout. When you return, enjoy!

Yields: 2 servings
