Forkinit: Mocha Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C powdered sugar
1/3 C unsweetened cocoa
1/4 C butter or margarine, softened (4 T butter flavored Crisco also works well)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp instant coffee, to taste
2-3 T milk or soy milk

Instructions

1. For the frosting, combine powdered sugar, cocoa, butter and vanilla in large bowl; beat until combined.
2. Dissolve 1 tsp instant coffee in 1 T milk and add to frosting.
3. Add additional milk, 1 T at a time, beating to desired consistency.
Makes about 1-1/4 C frosting.
