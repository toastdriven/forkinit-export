Forkinit: Homemade Tick Repellant

By Moriah Lindsley
Repellants for humans and pets!

Ingredients

Humans:

2 C distilled white vinegar
1 C water
20 drops essential oil (eucalyptus, peppermint or citrus work well against ticks)

Pets:

2 C distilled white vinegar
1 C water
2 spoonfuls vegetable or almond oil (another natural tick repellant)
20 drops citrus oil or peppermint oil, or fresh squeezed lemon juice (these also deter fleas)

Instructions

Humans:

Mix ingredients in spray bottle and spray onto clothing, skin and hair before going outdoors. Reapply every four hours.

Pets:

Mix ingredients in spray bottle and spray onto dry coat, avoiding sensitive areas. Apply 2-3 times/day when outdoors, once/day if indoors.
