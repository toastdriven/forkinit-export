Forkinit: Vegan Lemon Jello

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 tsp cornstarch
1 tsp water
Juice of 2 fresh lemons
1/4 cup pure maple syrup
Zest of 1/2 lemon, grated
1 1/2 cups water
2 tbsp agar-agar
Lemon slices for decoration

Instructions

Dissolve the cornstarch in the water in a small cup or bowl and set aside. Mix lemon juice with agave and lemon zest. Set aside. In a saucepan, bring 1 C water to a boil. Do not cover. Reduce the heat, stir agar-agar to blend and simmer for 3-5 minutes, until agar-agar is dissolved fully. Add the rest of the water. Stir in the lemon juice with agave mixture along with the cornstarch mixture. Pour into small serving cups. Refrigerate for 4 hours before serving. Decorate with lemon slices.
