Forkinit: Southern Barbecue Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
1/3 C molasses
1/4 C vinegar
1 clove garlic, minced
1 tsp chili powder
1/2 tsp ground black pepper
Dash salt

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in saucepan.
2. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes.
Makes about 1 C of barbecue sauce. For oven barbecued ribs, baste during last hours of baking.
