Ingredients

1 1/4 C all-purpose flour
2 tsp baking powder
2 T nutritional yeast
1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning
Pinch of salt
1/4 C cold margarine
2/3 C soy milk
1 T melted margarine
1 T garlic salt

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425Ã‚Â°F.
2. In a large bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, nutritional yeast, Italian seasoning, and salt. Using a pastry blender or 2 butter knives, cut in the cold margarine until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in the soy milk and mix until the flour is moistened.
3. On a lightly floured surface, knead the dough until smooth. Roll out into a 10x6" rectangle. Cut into 1" strips.
4. Place the strips 2" apart on an ungreased baking sheet. Brush with the melted margarine and sprinkle with the garlic salt.
5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, until lightly browned.
Makes approximately 1 dozen
