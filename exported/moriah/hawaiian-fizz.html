Forkinit: Hawaiian Fizz

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 pineapple rings, with skin
1/4" slice ginger root
1/2 pear
Sparkling water

Instructions

Juice pineapple. Push ginger through hopper with pear. Pour into glass and fill with sparkling water. Garnish if desired with pineapple.

Yields: 1 serving
