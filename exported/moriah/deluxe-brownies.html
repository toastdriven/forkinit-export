Forkinit: Deluxe Brownies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2/3 C vegan butter
5 squares (1 oz each) unsweetened chocolate, cut into pieces
1 3/4 C sugar
2 tsp vanilla
Egg Replacer equal to 3 eggs
1 C all-purpose flour
1 C chopped nuts

Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350Ã‚Â°F.
2. Heat vegan butter and chocolate over low heat, stirring constantly, until melted; cool slightly.
3. Beat sugar, vanilla and Egg Replacer in 3-quart bowl on high speed for 5 minutes.
4. Beat in chocolate mixture on low speed. Add flour; beat just until blended. Stir in nuts.
5. Spread in greased square pan, 9x9x2".
6. Bake just until brownies begin to pull away from sides of pan, 40 to 45 minutes; cool. Cut into about 2" squares.
Makes 16 brownies
