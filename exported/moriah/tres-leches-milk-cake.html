Forkinit: Tres Leches (Milk Cake)

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Cake:
1 1/2 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking powder
1/2 C unsalted butter
1 C white sugar
5 eggs
1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Milk Mixture:
2 C whole milk
1 (14 oz) can sweetened condensed milk
1 (12 oz) can evaporated milk

Whipped Topping:
1 1/2 C heavy whipping cream
1 C white sugar
1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350째F (175째C). Grease and flour one 9x13" baking pan.
Sift flour and baking powder together and set aside.
Cream butter or margarine and the 1 C sugar together until fluffy. Add eggs and the 1/2 tsp vanilla extract; beat well.
Add the flour mixture to the butter mixture 2 T at a time; mix until well blended. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake at 350째F (175째C) for 30 minutes. Pierce cake several times with a fork.
Combine the whole milk, condensed milk, and evaporated milk together. Pour over the top of the cooled cake.
Whip whipping cream, the remaining 1 C of sugar, and the remaining 1 tsp vanilla together until thick. Spread over the top of cake. Be sure and keep cake refrigerated, enjoy!

Yields: 1 cake
