Forkinit: Immune Boosting Green Juice

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 cups kale or spinach
1 teaspoon olive oil
1 pinch cayenne
4 inches ginger (or more, as much as you can handle)
2 cucumbers
2 lemons
2 limes
1 orange

Instructions

Juice the greens, ginger, cucumbers, lemon, lime and orange in a juicer.
Add the juice to your blender with the olive oil and cayenne, and blend.
Drink and HEAL!

Yields: ~16 oz juice
