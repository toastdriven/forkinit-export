Forkinit: The Mean Green Juice

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Kale
Cucumber
Celery
Green apple
Ginger
Lemon
Pineapple (optional, to make it sweeter)

Instructions

Blend in a high speed blender and strain through a nut milk bag, or put through a juicer, to make juice. Otherwise, just blend and enjoy as a smoothie.
