Forkinit: Chunky Apple Oatmeal Pancakes

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3/4 C whole wheat flour
1/8 C oats
2 T oat bran
1/2 tsp cinnamon
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 C raisins
1/2 C chopped apple
1 C apple juice

Instructions

1. Combine dry ingredients. Add raisins and apple.
2. Gently stir in juice until dry ingredients are completely moistened.
3. Pour batter by 1/4 C onto a nonstick skillet or griddle. Cook until bottom is brown and spatula slips easily underneath. Turn and brown other side. Makes eight plump pancakes.

Maple Cream

For a tasty and nourishing pancake topping, mix together equal parts of pure maple syrup and plain yogurt.
