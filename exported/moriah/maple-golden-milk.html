Forkinit: Maple Golden Milk

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 Traditional Medicinals Turmeric with Meadowsweet and Ginger tea bag
1 C boiling water
1/4 C hot almond milk, or milk of choice
1 T maple syrup
1/2 T vanilla extract
1/8 tsp turmeric powder
1/8 tsp pumpkin spice
1 tsp coconut oil
Cinnamon stick, for garnish

Instructions

Boil water and pour one cup onto tea bag.
Cover cup with saucer and allow to steep for 10-15 minutes to extract every bit of plant power.
Pour hot tea into blender and add the remaining ingredients. Blend until froth begins to form, then pour into the teacup.
Garnish with a cinnamon stick and a dash of pumpkin spice.

Yields: 1 serving
