Forkinit: Pumpkin Dip

By Moriah Lindsley
Easy dip; good with Gingersnaps!

Ingredients

15 oz pumpkin
8 oz cream cheese (vegan)
3/4 C powdered sugar
1 T Cinnamon Plus

Instructions

Mix together and put inside a cleaned out pumpkin. Serve with Gingersnaps.
