Forkinit: Celery Carrot Cumin Margarita

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Syrup:
2/3 c sugar
2/3 c water
2/3 C celery with some leaves, finely chopped

Drink Mix:
1 1/2 c tequila
1 c orange liqueur (such as Cointreau)
1 1/4 c fresh lime juice
2 C carrot juice

Glass Rim:
2 tbsp fresh lime juice
2 tbsp kosher salt
1 tsp ground cumin

Other:
Ice cubes
Celery sticks, for garnish

Instructions

For the syrup, add chopped celery to sugar and water. In a large jar with a tight-fitting lid, vigorously shake syrup ingredients until the sugar dissolves, about 1 minute. Strain the syrup into a pitcher.
Add all drink ingredients to the pitcher; stir.
To rim the glass, mix salt and cumin. Put lime juice on one plate and salt mix on another plate. Dip the rims of 8 glasses into the lime juice, then into the salt mix.
Fill the glasses with ice, then add the margarita. Garnish with celery sticks.

Yields: 8-10 margaritas
