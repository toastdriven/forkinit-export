Forkinit: The Mad Cowboy with Rodeo Ranch Dressing

By Moriah Lindsley
A vegan version of grilled chicken strips with ranch dressing.

Ingredients

For the "chicken:"
8 soy chicken "breasts" (available dried), or tofu
2 C barbecue sauce (without anchovies)
8 wooden bamboo skewers

Rodeo Ranch Dressing:
1 C vegan mayonnaise
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp black pepper
2 tsp chopped parsley
1/2-1 C soy milk
2 green onions, chopped

Instructions

For the "chicken:"
1. Reconstitute the soy chicken in boiling water according to the package directions, approximately 20 minutes. Slice into 1-inch strips, lengthwise, and spear on the skewers. Place in a baking dish and slather both sides with barbecue sauce; let sit for a couple of hours or overnight.
2. Grill until hot, approximately 5 to 10 minutes. Serve with the Rodeo Ranch Dressing.
Makes 8 servings

Rodeo Ranch Dressing:
1. Whisk together the vegan mayonnaise, garlic powder, onion powder, black pepper, and parsley.
2. Gradually add the soy milk and continue to whisk until you reach the desired consistency.
3. Top with the green onions.
Makes 4 servings
