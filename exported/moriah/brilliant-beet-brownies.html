Forkinit: Brilliant Beet Brownies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 medium beet, scrubbed and trimmed of the ends (~1 C once pureéd)
1/2 C honey (or agave nectar)
2 T coconut oil, melted
2 tsp vanilla extract
Egg Replacer equal to 2 eggs
1 C almond flour
1/2 C raw cacao powder
1 tsp baking powder
Pinch of sea salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350°F. Boil or steam the beet until easily pierced with a fork. Allow it to cool, then chop and transfer to a blender along with the honey, coconut oil and vanilla extract. Blend well; add Egg Replacer eggs and blend again until smooth. In a large bowl, sift together the remaining ingredients. Pour the beet mixture into the dry ingredients and mix well. Pour into a well-greased 8x8" pan and bake 35-45 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the middle comes out free of crumbs. Let cool completely before cutting.

Yields: 9 brownies
