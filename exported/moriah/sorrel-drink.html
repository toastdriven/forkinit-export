Forkinit: Sorrel Drink

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Sorrel Drink:
3 cups dried sorrel or red hibiscus flowers, or 5 Red Zinger tea bags
1 4" cinnamon stick
10 whole cloves
1/4 tsp crushed allspice
2 3" pieces peeled fresh ginger

Simple Syrup:
1 cup sugar
1 cup water

Instructions

To make Sorrel Drink: Bring all ingredients and 4 C water to a boil in saucepan. Reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer 10 minutes. Cool, strain, and chill.

To make Simple Syrup: Bring sugar and water to a boil in small saucepan. Stir to dissolve sugar, and simmer 1 minute. Cool.

Pour Sorrel Drink over ice. Serve with pitcher of Simple Syrup for guests to sweeten drinks to taste.

Yields: 1 quart
