Forkinit: Quick Stir-Fry Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 T soy sauce
1 tsp honey
1-2 tsp Chinese 5 spice
1/2 tsp granulated garlic
1/2 tsp minced ginger and/or 1 T Penzey's Sweet Ginger Bits
Cayenne pepper, to taste

Instructions

Combine all ingredients and mix well. Add to veggies while stir-frying and serve over noodles or rice.

Source: Adapted from Penzey's Spices magazine
