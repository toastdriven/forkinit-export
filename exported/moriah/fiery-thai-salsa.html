Forkinit: Fiery Thai Salsa

By Moriah Lindsley
Hot, sweet and spicy!

Ingredients

1 large ripe mango, peeled and seeded
1 small shallot, chopped
1/4 C fresh basil, coarsely chopped
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 T fresh lime juice
2 T Thai Kitchen Red Chili Dipping Sauce
salt and coarsely crushed black pepper to taste

Instructions

Coarsely chop mango. In a large bowl combine mango, shallot, basil, garlic and lime juice; mix well. Stir in red chili dipping sauce. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Makes 1 cup.
