Forkinit: Old Fashioned Organic Lemonade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

10 organic lemons
3/4 C WS Organic Blue Agave
4 C water
1 tray ice cubes
Mint leaves and lemon slices to garnish

Instructions

Cut lemons in half and juice. In large pitcher, combine lemon juice and Organic Blue Agave; stir well. Add water and ice cubes and stir again. Garnish with lemon slices and mint leaves. Adjust to taste and enjoy!

Yields: 1 pitcher
