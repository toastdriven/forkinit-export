Forkinit: Hinty Minty Brownies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 sticks butter (3/4 C)
1 C Hint of Mint Hot Chocolate Mix
1/2 C sugar
2 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
Egg replacer equal to 2 eggs
1 C flour

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 325Â°F and grease an 8x8 baking pan.
2. Melt butter in a small saucepan and add Hint of Mint Hot Chocolate Mix and sugar, stir to dissolve, then pour into a bowl and let cool.
3. Add the egg replacer, Vanilla Extract and sifted flour, mix well to a smooth batter. Pour into pan and bake for 40-45 minutes. Leave to cool and cut into squares and store in an air tight container.
