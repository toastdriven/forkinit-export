Forkinit: Natural Spa Treatment

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Handful of Epsom or sea salts
10 drops lavender essential oil
1/2 C baking soda

Instructions

Add this combo to a tub of hot water once a week and soak for 20 minutes. This combo draws out toxins, lowers stress-related hormones, and balances your pH levels.
