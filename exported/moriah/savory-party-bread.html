Forkinit: Savory Party Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 unsliced round loaf (1 lb) sourdough bread
1 lb Monterey Jack cheese, sliced (vegan)
1/2 C butter, melted (vegan)
1/2 C chopped green onions
2 to 3 tsp poppy seeds (optional)

Instructions

Cut the bread lengthwise and crosswise without cutting through the bottom crust. Insert cheese between cuts. Combine (vegan) butter, onions and poppy seeds (if using); drizzle over the bread. Wrap in foil; place on a baking sheet. Bake at 350Â°F for 15 minutes. Unwrap; bake 10 minutes longer or until the cheese is melted. Yield: 6-8 servings
