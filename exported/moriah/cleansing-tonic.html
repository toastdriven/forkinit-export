Forkinit: Cleansing Tonic

By Moriah Lindsley
Internal body cleanser

Ingredients

1/2" wedge green cabbage
2 large green apples (or 4 small), seeded and cored
6 carrots

Instructions

Cut ingredients into chunks. Push through hopper of juicing machine.
Note: It may be easier on the body to dilute using equal parts juice to water.
