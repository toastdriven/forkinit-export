Forkinit: Pizza On The Grill

By Moriah Lindsley
A great way to spice up an old favorite.

Ingredients

2 T vegetable oil
2 tsp sugar

2 3/4 to 3 C all-purpose flour

TOPPINGS:
Veggies, sauce, vegan cheese...be creative!

Instructions

1. In a mixing bowl, dissolve yeast in water. Add the oil, sugar, baking soda, salt and 2 C flour. Stir in enough remaining flour to form a soft dough.
2. Turn onto a floured surface; knead until smooth and elastic, about 6-8 minutes. Cover and let rest for 10 minutes.
3. On a floured surface, roll dough into a 13-inch circle. Transfer to a greased 12-inch pizza pan. Build up edges slightly.
4. Grill, covered, over medium heat for 5 minutes. Remove from grill. Spread on sauce, then top with veggies and vegan cheese.
5. Grill, covered, 5-10 minutes longer or until crust is golden and vegan cheese is melted.
Yield: 4 servings
