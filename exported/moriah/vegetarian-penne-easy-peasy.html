Forkinit: Vegetarian Penne Easy Peasy

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 cups whole grain mini penne pasta
1 can white beans
1 small can tomato sauce
1 can diced tomatoes with chiles
1 can diced tomatoes
finely diced sweet onion, garlic, broccoli, radish
2 cups vegetable broth & 1 cup water

Instructions

Stir all together at once, and press white rice option and walk away!! Thats it!! Stir twice during cooking. Thats its! Easy peasy!!
