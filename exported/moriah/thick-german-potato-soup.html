Forkinit: Thick German Potato Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
Good on a cold winter night!

Ingredients

1 tsp salt
6 C potatoes, sliced
1 C carrots, sliced
1 1/4 C celery, sliced
1/2 C onion, chopped
1 qt water
1 C milk (non-dairy)
1/2 tsp salt
Pepper & parsley
Butter (vegan)

Instructions

Heat first six ingredients to boiling. Simmer 45 minutes. Add rest of ingredients and beat until smooth. Serve with (vegan) butter.
