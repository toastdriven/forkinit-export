Forkinit: Grilled Seasoned Veggies

By Moriah Lindsley
A fantastic grilled treat for summer!

Ingredients

2 C sliced veggies of choice (mushrooms, summer squash, asparagus, bell peppers, etc.)
1/2 to 1 tsp Greek Seasoning
1 tsp olive oil

Instructions

1. Mix together olive oil and seasoning.
2. Brush onto sliced veggies.
3. Grill to desired tenderness.

Source: Maryanne Monrovia (Penzey's Spices Magazine)
