Forkinit: Pasta with Marinara

By Moriah Lindsley
Vegetarian pasta

Ingredients

Eggless pasta of choice
Meatless marinara sauce of choice
TVP, if desired
Vegan cheese and herbs to garnish

Instructions

1. Boil water and add pasta. Cook pasta to desired consistency.
2. If using TVP, heat water in microwave until boiling and add to dry TVP. Let sit 5 minutes.
3. In microwave-safe dish, heat marinara through.
4. Strain pasta.
5. Add TVP to marinara. Heat again.
6. Dish pasta onto plate. Cover with marinara, cheese and herbs. Serve hot.
