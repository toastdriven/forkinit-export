Forkinit: Million Dollar Potato Casserole

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 lbs frozen hashbrown potatoes
1/2 C vegan butter
1/2 C onion, chopped
1 1/2-2 C vegan cream of mushroom soup
1 pt vegan sour cream
2 C vegan cheddar shreds
1 tsp salt
1 tsp pepper
2 C cornflakes

Instructions

Mix butter and cornflakes together. Set aside. Mix all other ingredients together. Sprinkle cornflakes on top after mixture is spread in a 9x13" casserole dish.
Bake at 350Â°F for 1 hour.
