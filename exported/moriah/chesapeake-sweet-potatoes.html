Forkinit: Chesapeake Sweet Potatoes

By Moriah Lindsley
An easy way to dress up sweet potatoes.

Ingredients

1 lb sweet potatoes, cubed
1 T butter (vegan)
1 T olive oil
2 tsp Chesapeake Bay Seasoning
1/8 tsp Granulated Garlic
1/4-1/2 tsp salt

Instructions

2. Mix butter, oil, Chesapeake Bay Seasoning, Granulated Garlic and salt. Toss with sweet potatoes.
3. Spread on a nonstick tray and bake for 20 minutes or until done, stirring a few times during baking.
