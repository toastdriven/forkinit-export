Forkinit: Crock Pot Minestrone Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
A simple vegan minestrone soup.

Ingredients

1 C corn
1 lb cooked chickpeas
1/2 C minced onion
1 clove garlic, minced
1/2 C diced carrots
1/2 C diced celery
1 pkg frozen chopped spinach
10 oz can tomatoes
1 med potato, diced
2 T parsley
1 qt vegetable broth
1/2 C elbow macaroni

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients except macaroni in crock pot. Add water if vegetable broth does not completely cover.
2. Cook on high 1 hour, then switch to low for 6-8 hours. If you don't have time just cook on low 9-10 hours. I found it necessary to turn back up to high for the final hour to cook the potato and macaroni through.
3. One half hour before serving, add macaroni.

Yields: 6
