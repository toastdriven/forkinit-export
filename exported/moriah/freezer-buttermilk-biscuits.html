Forkinit: Freezer Buttermilk Biscuits

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 C all-purpose flour
1 T baking powder
1 T sugar
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
2/3 C vegan shortening
1 C buttermilk*

*To make vegan buttermilk, add 2 tsp lemon juice or vinegar to 1 C nondairy milk.

Instructions

1. Combine flour, baking powder, sugar, baking soda and salt in large bowl. Cut in shortening with pastry blender until mixture resembles fine crumbs.
2. Stir buttermilk into flour mixture until mixture forms a soft dough that leaves sides of bowl.
3. Turn dough out onto well-floured surface. Knead dough 10 times. Roll dough out into 8" square. Cut dough into 16 (2") squares.*
4. Line baking sheet with plastic wrap. Place squares on lined sheet. Freeze about 3 hours or until firm. Remove frozen squares from sheet and place in freezer container. Freeze up to 1 month.
