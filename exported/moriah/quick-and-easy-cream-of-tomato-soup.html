Forkinit: Quick and Easy Cream of Tomato Soup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4 T finely diced onion
1 T soy sauce
1 8 oz can tomato sauce
2 C rice milk
2 T cornstarch
Seasonings to taste

Instructions

Saute onion in soy sauce until onion is tender. Add 1 1/2 C of rice milk and 8 oz tomato sauce to onions, and bring to a boil. Mix 2 T cornstarch with remaining 1/2 C of rice milk. Add cornstarch/rice milk mixture to soup, stirring constantly until thickened. Season to taste with your favorite seasonings. (I used a little onion powder and some spicy Creole seasoning.)
