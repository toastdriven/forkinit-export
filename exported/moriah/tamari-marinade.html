Forkinit: Tamari Marinade

By Moriah Lindsley
Mix up this simple tamari marinade and bring new life to any meal.

Ingredients

4 T San-J Tamari
6 T white or red wine
1/8 tsp pepper
1/4 tsp basil
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
2 T olive oil
1/2 tsp sugar
2 tsp lemon juice

Instructions

Mix ingredients and marinate food for 30-90 minutes. Cook as desired. Discard unused marinade.
