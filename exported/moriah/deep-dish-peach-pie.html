Forkinit: Deep-Dish Peach Pie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Pastry for single crust pie
1 C sugar
2 T cornstarch
3 lbs peaches, seeded, pared and sliced (about 6 C)
2 T lemon juice concentrate
1 T margarine, melted (vegan)
1/4 tsp almond extract
2 T sliced almonds

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Ã‚Â°F.
2. Remove and reserve 1 T sugar. In small bowl, combine remaining sugar and cornstarch.
3. In large bowl, toss peaches with lemon juice concentrate; add sugar mixture, margarine and extract. Turn into 8" square baking dish.
4. Roll pastry to 9" square; cut slits near center. Place pastry over filling; turn under edges, seal and flute. Sprinkle with reserved 1 T sugar and almonds.
5. Bake 45 to 50 minutes or until golden brown. Cool on wire rack.
