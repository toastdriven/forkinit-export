Forkinit: Vegan Sweetened Condensed Milk

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 cups soy milk
1 3/4 cups brown sugar
2 Tbsp corn starch

Instructions

Measure the corn starch into a small glass or measuring cup.
Add 2-3 T of the soy milk and whisk with a small whisk or fork until smooth.
In a small saucepan, heat the remaining soy milk and brown sugar. Whisk in the cornstarch-soy milk mixture.
Bring to a boil, stirring constantly until thick.
