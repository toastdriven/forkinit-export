Forkinit: Sage Dressing

By Moriah Lindsley
A favorite from childhood, converted to vegan!

Ingredients

4 stalks celery
1 onion
1 lb tofu or seitan, chopped into pieces or crumbled
1 loaf dried bread, torn into chunks
3/4 pkg stuffing
Egg Replacer, equal to 4 eggs
1-2 C mushrooms, sliced
Sage, to taste
Poultry seasoning, to taste

Instructions

Sauté celery and onion in a little oil. Add tofu or seitan and heat through.
Soak bread and squeeze out excess water.
Add celery and onion, tofu or seitan, stuffing, Egg Replacer eggs, mushrooms and seasoning.
Place in baking dish and bake at 425°F for 30 minutes.
