Forkinit: Sweet Potato Cornbread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/3 C maple syrup
1 C mashed sweet potatoes
1/3 C canola oil
2/3 C soy milk
1 tsp salt
1 T baking powder
1 C organic cornmeal
1 C whole wheat flour (or any flour)
1/3 C minced pecans, optional

Instructions

Mix all ingredients and bake for 35 minutes at 350F in a lightly
oiled square glass pan (9x9).
