Forkinit: Chile Cheese Polenta

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 7-oz can diced green chilies or jalapenos, drained
1 C Westbrae Natural whole kernel corn, drained
4 C Rice Dream Original
1 C dry polenta or coarse ground corn meal
1/2 C shredded cheese (vegan)
3 T butter (vegan)
Hain Sea Salt and pepper

Instructions

1. In medium saucepan, bring chilies, corn and Rice Dream to a boil.
2. Slowly add polenta, whisking constantly until smooth, about 6 minutes.
3. Mix in cheese and butter.
4. Add salt and pepper to taste and serve.
