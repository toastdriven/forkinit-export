Forkinit: Grandma's Vegetable Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
This soup is so awesome and makes for a wonderful leftover when jarred.

Ingredients

Part 1:
1 pk (8 qts) tomatoes, peeled & sliced (approx. 48 tomatoes)
1 qt diced carrots (approx. 10 carrots)
1 can lima beans
3 green peppers, diced
1 can peas
3 bunches celery, diced
5 zucchini or onions, diced

Part 2:
1 T paprika
1/2 C salt
1 C sugar
2 C or 1 box soaked pearl barley
1/2 C (vegan) butter

Instructions

Part 1:
Cook each vegetable separately until almost tender. Mix all ingredients & cook 10 minutes.

Part 2:
Add "Part 2" ingredients. Fill ball jars & process.
