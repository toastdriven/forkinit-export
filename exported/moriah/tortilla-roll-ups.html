Forkinit: Tortilla Roll-Ups

By Moriah Lindsley
For a fun, Mexican appetizer!

Ingredients

1 tub Tofutti vegan cream cheese, room temperature
Taco seasoning, to taste
2-3 jalapeno peppers, finely chopped
1 jar (2 oz) diced pimientos, drained
4-6 whole wheat flour tortillas

Instructions

In a small mixing bowl, combine the vegan cream cheese, taco seasoning, jalapenos and pimientos; mix well. Spread over tortillas. Roll up tightly; wrap each in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Unwrap and cut each tortilla into 8 pieces.
