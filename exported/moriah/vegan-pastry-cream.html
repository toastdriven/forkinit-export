Forkinit: Vegan Pastry Cream

By Moriah Lindsley
Use as a filling for pies, tarts, pastries, or phyllo dough, or as a topping for desserts.

Ingredients

1/2 C flour
2 C soy milk (or other non-dairy milk of choice)
1/3 C sugar
1 pinch salt
1/4 C lemon juice
2 tsp grated lemons, zest of
1/2 tsp vanilla

Instructions

1. In a small bowl, place the flour and whisk in 1/2 C soy milk, and set aside.
2. In a small saucepan, place the remaining soy milk, sugar, and salt, and whisk to combine. Add the flour mixture to the liquid ingredients and whisk well to combine.
3. Cook the mixture over medium heat, while whisking constantly, for 5-6 minutes or until thickened. Add the remaining ingredients, whisk well to combine, and cook the mixture an additional minute. Remove the saucepan from the heat and transfer the mixture to a glass bowl.
4. Place a piece of waxed paper or plastic wrap directly on top of the pastry cream to prevent a skin from forming on the top. Place the pastry cream in the refrigerator for several hours to cool completely.

Source: The Vegan Chef, Beverly Lynn Bennett
