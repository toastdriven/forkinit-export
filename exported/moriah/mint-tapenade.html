Forkinit: Mint Tapenade

By Moriah Lindsley
Mint's refreshing kick complements the earthy olive flavor in this black olive spread.

Ingredients

1 1/2 C pitted black olives
3/4 C packed mint leaves, more for garnish
3 garlic cloves, peeled
1/4 C olive oil
Black pepper

Instructions

Put olives, mint and garlic in food processor and pulse until ingredients are chopped. Gradually add olive oil, until tapenade comes together in a smooth paste. Season to taste with pepper. Garnish with fresh mint leaves.
Dip fresh vegetables in tapenade or spread toasted slices of baguette with it.

Makes about 1 cup
