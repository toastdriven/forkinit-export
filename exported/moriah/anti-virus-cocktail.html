Forkinit: Anti-Virus Cocktail

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 apples
1 orange

Instructions

Cut the apples into narrow wedges. Peel the orange, leaving as much of the white pith as possible. Cut or break the orange into segments. Process the fruit a the juicer, or a Vitamix and strain.

Yields: 1 serving
