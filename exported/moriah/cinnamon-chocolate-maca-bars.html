Forkinit: Cinnamon Chocolate Maca Bars

By Moriah Lindsley
Boost your energy (and libido!) with these easy, tasty bars!

Ingredients

1 cup soaked cashews
1/2 cup maca
1 cup cacao powder
1/4 cup cacao butter
Dash of Kalahari salt
2 dates, pitted
1/8 cup raw honey
1 cup shredded coconut
1/2 cup coconut oil
3 tbsp cinnamon
1 tsp ginger
1 pinch cayenne

Instructions

Soak cashews overnight. Melt cacao butter and coconut oil over very low heat, or in a dehydrator. In a blender, grind the cashews first. Then add the remaining ingredients and blend on high. Flatten mixture into a small pan and freeze for 10 minutes. Remove from the freezer and cut into squares. Enjoy!
