Forkinit: Mummy Mini Pizzas

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

English muffins
Pizza sauce
Daiya cheese, block or shreds
Black olives, sliced

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Split English muffins and spread each half with about 2 tsp pizza sauce. Bake 8-10 minutes. While pizzas are baking, cut block cheese into strips, if using. Arrange strips, or shreds, on pizzas to look like mummies. Place 2 black olive slices on pizza for eyes. Return to oven and bake until cheese slightly melts.
