Forkinit: Passion Fruit Mocktail

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

120ml (4oz) passionfruit juice
Splash of lemon juice
Cranberry juice to taste (1/2-1 tsp)
Soda water to taste
Cherry to garnish
Ice

Instructions

Pour the passionfruit juice into a high ball glass packed with ice. Next add the lemon juice, cranberry juice and soda water.
Garnish with the cherry and serve.

Yields: 1 serving
