Forkinit: Memphis Vanilla Muffins

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C flour
3/4 C sugar
2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 large egg (or Egg Replacer)
1 C milk
1/3 C butter, melted
1 T vanilla

Instructions

1. Heat oven to 400Â°F.
2. Grease 12 muffin cups.
3. With wooden spoon, mix egg, milk, melted butter and vanilla well.
4. Stir in flour, sugar, baking powder and salt until just blended.
5. Spoon into cups and bake 18-20 minutes.
