Forkinit: Peanut Butter Crunch Bars

By Moriah Lindsley
Tasty bars for a yummy snack!

Ingredients

3 3/4 C powdered sugar
1/2 C butter
1 (16 oz.) jar Smucker's Natural Peanut Butter
3 C crispy rice cereal or corn flakes
1 (16 oz.) pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Melt butter over low heat in sauce pan.
2. In a mixing bowl, blend Peanut Butter and sugar. Add the melted butter and continue to blend.
3. Stir in cereal and spread evenly onto a 9" x 13" pan.
4. In a seperate saucepan, melt the chocolate chips over low heat.
5. Spread melted chocolate evenly over bars. Refrigerate to cool.
6. Cut into bars.

Makes approximately 30 bars.

Source: Smucker's Natural Peanut Butter
