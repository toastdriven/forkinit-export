Forkinit: Tofu Whipped Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C extra firm tofu, well drained
1/4 C honey
1 T safflower oil
1 T butter (or almond butter)
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 pinch salt

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a blender and process until smooth.
2. Refrigerate, covered, for several hours before serving.
