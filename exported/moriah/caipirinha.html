Forkinit: Caipirinha

By Moriah Lindsley
Brazil's national cocktail.

Ingredients

1/2 fresh lime, cut into 4 wedges
2 tsp brown sugar
1 2/3 oz Cachaça

Instructions

Place lime and sugar into Old Fashioned glass/low ball and muddle (mash the two ingredients together using a muddler or a wooden spoon). Fill the glass with crushed ice and add the Cachaça.

Yields: 1 drink
