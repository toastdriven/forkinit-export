Forkinit: Macadamia Nut Butter Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley
Dreamy Macadamia Butter takes the place of butter or shortening in this delicious, dairy free cookie.

Ingredients

8 oz jar Maranatha Macadamia Nut Butter
1 1/2 tsp pure natural vanilla
3/4 C raw sugar
1/2 C firmly packed light brown sugar
1 lg egg (Egg Replacer)
1 1/4 C pre-sifted organic all purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp kosher salt
10 oz Sunspire White Chocolate Baking Chips

Optional Ingredients: freshly grated coconut, chopped Macadamia Nuts

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
2. Combine Macadamia Butter, vanilla, sugars and egg (Egg Replacer).
3. Add flour, baking soda and salt, into wet mixture and combine. Note: consistency of dough is similar to a peanut butter cookie and will be crumbly.
4. Add Baking Chips & optional ingredients (or leave as toppers).
5. Form dough into quarter size balls and flatten slightly.
6. Place dough on ungreased cookie sheet.
7. Bake for 9-11 minutes or until light golden color.
8. Remove from pan and allow to cool on rack.
