Forkinit: Fancy Brussels Sprouts

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C water
1/4 C minced fresh parsley
Sugar substitute equivalent to 1 tsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt-free seasoning blend
2 pints fresh brussels sprouts, halved or 2 packages (10 oz each) frozen brussels sprouts, thawed
1 can (8 oz) water chestnuts, drained and diced
1 T margarine (vegan)

Instructions

In a saucepan over medium heat, bring water, parsley, sugar substitute and seasoning blend to a boil. Add brussels sprouts. Cover and simmer for 6-8 minutes or until tender; drain. Add water chestnuts and (vegan) margarine; heat through.
Yield: 6 servings

Diabetic Exchange: 2 1/2 vegetable
Per Serving: 74 cal, 2 g fat, 0 mg cholesterol, 34 mg sodium, 12 g carbs, 4 g protein, 6 g fiber
