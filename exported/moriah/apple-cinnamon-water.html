Forkinit: Apple Cinnamon Water

By Moriah Lindsley
Supports weight loss and increases energy

Ingredients

1 organic apple, thinly sliced
1 cinnamon stick
2 quarts water
Ice

Instructions

Place apple slices and cinnamon stick at the bottom of a large pitcher. Fill pitcher halfway with ice and then add water. Let sit for a couple hours. Enjoy!

Fill up 3-4 times before replacing the apple and cinnamon stick. Drink throughout the week.
