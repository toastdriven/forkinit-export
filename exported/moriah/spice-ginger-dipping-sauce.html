Forkinit: Spice Ginger Dipping Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 T water
1/4 tsp minced garlic
1/2-1 tsp granulated garlic
2 T soy sauce
1 tsp Penzey's Sweet Ginger Bits or crystallized ginger, chopped
3/4 C hot sauce

Instructions

In a small bowl, mix together first three ingredients. Let sit 10 minutes so flavor can develop. Add the rest of the ingredients and mix well. Great for kabobs, egg rolls, etc.
