Forkinit: Coconut Ice Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 14-oz cans coconut milk (approximately 5 C)
2 T agar-agar flakes
2-3 vanilla beans
1/8 tsp sea salt
1 T vanilla extract
1 C maple syrup
2 T melted coconut oil

Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan, use a wire whisk to combine the coconut milk, agar-agar, vanilla beans, and sea salt.
2. Bring to a simmer, cover, and cook over medium-low heat until the agar-agar is completely dissolved, stirring often.
3. Remove from the heat and discard the vanilla beans.
4. Stir in the vanilla extract and the maple syrup. Pour into a shallow pan and cool in the refrigerator for approximately 25 minutes, or until firm.
5. In a blender, combine small amounts of the milk mixture and the coconut oil until creamy. Repeat until the remaining milk mixture and oil have been blended.
6. Pour into a bowl and stir, adding additional maple syrup and vanilla if desired.
7. Pour into an ice-cream machine and churn until firm.

Makes 1 quart
