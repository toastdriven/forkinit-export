Forkinit: Fresh Fruit Marinade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C honey or agave
1/4 C water
1/4 C lime juice
1/4 C orange liqueur or orange juice

Instructions

In small saucepan combine honey/agave and water. Bring to a boil. Reduce heat; simmer 5 minutes. Stir in lime juice and orange liqueur. Cool completely. In medium bowl combine fruit. Pour marinade over the fruit and mix gently. Cover; refrigerate 1-2 hours to blend flavors. Makes 8 (1/2 C) servings.
