Forkinit: Shamrock Green Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 cups coconut milk, unsweetened
1 1/2 cups fresh spinach
1/2 cup fresh mint leaves
2 bananas
4 medjool dates, pitted
1 teaspoon vanilla
OPTIONAL: top with fresh cherry, coconut whipped cream, and/or cacao nibs

Instructions

Blend spinach, mint, and coconut milk until smooth.
Add remaining ingredients, and blend until smooth. Enjoy!
Note: Use a frozen fruit to make smoothie cold.

Yields: 2 smoothies
