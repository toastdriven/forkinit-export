Forkinit: Natural Tooth Whitener

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Tiny bit of toothpaste
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp hydrogen peroxide
1/2 tsp water

Instructions

Mix ingredients in a small cup thoroughly. Brush your teeth for two minutes. Do this once a week until you reach the results you want. Once your teeth are good and white, limit yourself to using the treatment once every month or two.
