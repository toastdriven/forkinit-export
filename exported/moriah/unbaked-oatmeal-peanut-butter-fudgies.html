Forkinit: Unbaked Oatmeal-Peanut Butter Fudgies

By Moriah Lindsley
For those PB fans out there!

Ingredients

2 C sugar
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 C cocoa powder
1/2 C soymilk
1/2 C vegan butter or margarine
1/2 C creamy peanut butter
4 C rolled oats

Instructions

In a large saucepan, combine the sugar, salt and cocoa powder. Add the soymilk and stir until the mixture is smooth. Add the margarine. Over medium-high heat, bring mixture to a boil. Boil 2 minutes, stirring often. Remove from heat and add the peanut butter, stirring until it is melted. Blend in the oats.
Drop mixture by rounded teaspoonfuls onto waxed paper. Cool. Store in a covered container in the refrigerator until ready to serve. Makes about 4 dozen.

Calories: 92
Fat: 4 grams
Carbs: 13 grams
Protein: 2 grams
Sodium: 44 mg
Fiber: 1 gram
