Forkinit: Fudgy Brownies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C vegan butter
1 pkg semisweet chocolate chips
1 2/3 C sugar
1 1/4 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp vanilla
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp salt
Egg Replacer equal to 3 eggs
1 C chopped nuts, if desired

Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350Ã‚Â°F.
2. Heat vegan butter and chocolate chips in 3-quart saucepan over low heat, stirring constantly, until melted.
3. Beat in remaining ingredients, except nuts, until smooth; stir in nuts. Spread in greased rectangular pan 13x9x2".
4. Bake until center is set, about 30 minutes; cool completely. Cut into bars, about 2x1 1/2".
Makes 36 brownies.
