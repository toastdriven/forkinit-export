Forkinit: Chocolate Chip Cookie in a Cup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 Tablespoon vegan butter
1 Tablespoon sugar
1 Tablespoon firmly packed dark brown sugar
3 drops of vanilla extract
Small pinch of kosher salt
Egg Replacer equal to 1 egg
Scant 1/4 C all purpose flour
2 Tablespoons semi sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Start by melting your butter in the microwave. Add sugars, vanilla and salt. Stir to combine. Add Egg Replacer egg. Stir to combine. Add flour, then stir again. Add the chocolate chips, and give a final stir. Cook in microwave 40-60 seconds, start checking for doneness at 40 seconds.

Serve warm.

Yields: 1 cookie
