Forkinit: Best Ever Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C granulated sugar
1 C brown sugar
3/4 C margarine, softened (vegan)
1/2 C peanut butter; we prefer Jiff
2 eggs (or Egg Replacer)
1.5 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
3 C all-purpose flour
12 oz package chocolate chips; we prefer Guittard milk-chocolate chips (or non-dairy chips)

Instructions

1. Mix sugars, margarine, peanut-butter, eggs and vanilla until smooth. Gradually add baking soda, salt, and flour, mixing until well blended. Add chocolate chips and mix until chips are evenly distributed.
