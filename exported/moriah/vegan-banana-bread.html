Forkinit: Vegan Banana Bread

By Moriah Lindsley
A healthier version of traditional banana bread, using quinoa flour and coconut sugar.

Ingredients

1/4 cup refined, organic coconut oil
3/4 cup coconut sugar (I use slightly less)
1 egg replacer
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 cup quinoa flour
1/4 cup flax seed meal
1 tsp cinnamon
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
5 ripe, mashed bananas
1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. With electric mixer mix coconut oil, coconut sugar and egg replacer (prepared per package directions).
Add quinoa flour and flax seed meal and mix.
Add baking soda, baking powder, salt cinnamon, vanilla and mashed bananas and mix.
Stir in chocolate chips by hand.
Pour batter into 9x5 pan, greased with coconut oil. Bake for about 40-50 minutes.

Yields: 1 loaf
