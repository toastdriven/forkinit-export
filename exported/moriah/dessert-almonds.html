Forkinit: Dessert Almonds

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/4 C peanut butter
4 oz chocolate baking bar (I use semi-sweet)
1 tsp cinnamon
2 C raw almonds
2 C oat bran

Instructions

Melt the chocolate and peanut butter together in a double boiler. Stir in cinnamon.

In a medium mixing bowl, stir the melted chocolate mixture with the almonds. Pour oat bran into a sealable plastic bag, or into a brown paper bag. Add almonds and toss to coat.

Spread almonds out on a sheet pan lined with wax or parchment paper and refrigerate until chocolate mixture has hardened. Enjoy!

Yields: 2 C almonds
