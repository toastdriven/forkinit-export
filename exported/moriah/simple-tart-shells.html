Forkinit: Simple Tart Shells

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 tsp sugar
8 T (1 stick) unsalted butter, cut in small pieces and chilled (vegan)
2-3 T ice water

Instructions

1. Briefly pulse flour, salt and sugar in a food processor to combine (or combine by hand in a large bowl). Add chilled butter and pulse (or cut in by hand with two table knives) until the mixture resembles coarse meal. Add ice water and pulse (or fork mix by hand) just until dough begins to come together. Do not over mix!
2. Turn out onto a smooth surface and form into a flattened disk. Wrap with plastic or parchment and refrigerate several hours or overnight. Roll out chilled dough on a lightly floured surface then transfer to tart pan.
Makes one 9" shell.
