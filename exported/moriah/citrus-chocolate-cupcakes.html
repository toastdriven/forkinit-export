Forkinit: Citrus Chocolate Cupcakes

By Moriah Lindsley
These cupcakes have a VERY mild citrus flavor. I would suggest adding some orange zest to them for a more prevalent citrusy flavor.

Ingredients

1 cup whole-wheat flour
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 C sugar
1/4 C unsweetened cocoa
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 C orange juice
1/3 C water
3 T oil
1 T vinegar
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 tsp orange extract (optional)
1/3 C mini chocolate chips

Instructions

1. In a medium bowl combine flours, sugar, cocoa and baking soda.
2. In a large bowl combine juice, water, oil, vinegar, vanilla, and orange extract (if desired).
3. Add flour mixture to the juice mixture; stir until just moistened.
4. Fold in chocolate chips.
5. Spray 12 medium muffin cups or use paper liners.
6. Fill cups half full with batter.
7. Bake at 375Â°F 13-16 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
8. Cool 5 minutes before removing from pan.
