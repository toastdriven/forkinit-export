Forkinit: Double Chocolate Chip Brownies

By Moriah Lindsley
This recipe can be converted into a vegan or gluten-free recipe.

Ingredients

1/2 C (1 stick) unsalted butter (or vegan butter)
1/4 C TROPICAL SOURCE Chocolate Chips
1 C turbinado or brown sugar
1/2 C all-purpose flour (or use gluten-free flour mix plus 1/4 T xanthum gum for texture)
2 eggs (or equivalent egg replacer)
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/4 tsp salt
1 tsp vanilla
1 C TROPICAL SOURCE Chocolate Chips
1/2 C chopped pecans or walnuts (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325Â°F. Grease 8" square baking pan. Melt the butter and 1/4 C of chips in double boiler (or melt in low oven). Combine with sugar. Sift together flour, powder and salt. Add to butter, chocolate and sugar mixture. Add vanilla and eggs. Blend thoroughly. Stir in remaining chips and nuts. Pour batter into greased pan. Bake 30 to 35 minutes, or until center is set. Do not overcook. Brownies should be moist and chewy. Cool completely before cutting into squares.
Makes 16 brownies.
