Forkinit: Key Lime Cheesecake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 pkgs Tofutti plain cream cheese
1 C sugar
1 tsp vanilla
1/4 C key lime juice
2 tsp lime zest (grated rind of lime)
2 T cornstarch
1 9" graham cracker pie crust
Kiwis and/or strawberries, sliced

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Combine the cream cheese, sugar, vanilla, lime juice, lime zest, and cornstarch in a blender and mix until smooth.
3. Pour into the graham cracker pie crust. Place on a cookie sheet and bake for 45 to 60 minutes or until golden brown.
4. Remove and let set until cool to the touch, then chill in the refrigerator overnight.
5. Top with sliced kiwis, strawberries, or both.
Makes 6 to 8 servings
