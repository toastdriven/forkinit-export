Forkinit: Thai-Style Pina Colada

By Moriah Lindsley
The ultimate tropical drink! Just find a palm tree and enjoy!

Ingredients

4 oz. Thai Kitchen Pure Coconut Milk (Regular or Lite)
4 oz. pineapple juice
4 oz. crushed ice
Optional: 2 oz. dark rum

Instructions

In a blender, combine coconut milk, pineapple juice, ice and rum. Blend until smooth. Serves 1.
