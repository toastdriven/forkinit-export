Forkinit: Vegan Vegetable Chowder with Quinoa

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

4-5 cups miso or 4-5 C vegetable broth
5 large mushrooms, sliced
1/2 cup corn
1/2 zucchini, sliced
1 cup celery leaves, packed
2 stalks celery, chopped
2 large carrots, sliced
3 tomatoes, diced
1/2 cup dried beans
1/4 cup quinoa, uncooked
1/4-1/2 C soymilk, unsweetened
2 bay leaves
Italian seasoning
garlic powder
salt
fresh ground pepper

Instructions

In a large pot, or crock pot, combine all ingredients except for soy milk.
Bring to a boil and simmer for about 2 hours (until beans are soft).
Pour in soymilk just before serving to desired creaminess.
*I like miso-broth, but use any broth you choose. Tomato juices makes a nice option, too. Use varied amount, if you would like a more ‘broth-y’ soup, or more of a stew. I used about 4-5 C.
