Forkinit: Light Marinade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C fresh lemon juice (3 lemons)
1/2 C extra-virgin olive oil
1 T minced garlic

Instructions

In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil and garlic. Place protein or vegetables and marinade into a resealable plastic bag. Seal bag and shake to coat. Marinate one hour. Grill 2-3 minutes per side.
