Forkinit: Homemade Mosquito Repellent

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

15 drops lavender oil
3-4 Tbsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup lemon juice
16 oz bottle fill bottle with water

Instructions

Put ingredients in 16 oz bottle filled with water; shake to combine ingredients and spray away.
