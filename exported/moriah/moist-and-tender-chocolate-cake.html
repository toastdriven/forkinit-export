Forkinit: Moist and Tender Chocolate Cake

By Moriah Lindsley
For chocolate lovers!

Ingredients

2 C all-purpose flour
2/3 C NESTLE TOLL HOUSE Baking Cocoa
1 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/2 tsp salt
3/4 C (1 1/2 sticks) butter or margarine, softened (vegan)
1 3/4 C granulated sugar
2 tsp vanilla extract
2 large eggs (or Egg Replacer)
1 C water
Chocolate Lover's Frosting

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Grease and flour two 9-inch-round baking pans or one 13x9-inch baking pan.
Combine flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt in medium bowl. Beat (vegan) butter, sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy. Add eggs (or Egg Replacer) one at a time, beating well after each addition. Gradually beat in flour mixture alternately with water. Spread into prepared pan(s).
Bake for 25 to 35 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool in pan(s) for 15 minutes; invert onto wire racks to cool completely.
Frost with Chocolate Lover's Frosting.
Makes 10 to 12 servings
