Forkinit: Raw Banana Ice Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

20 pitted dates, coarsely chopped
2 T raw honey (or agave nectar)
2 T extra virgin coconut oil
1 tsp vanilla extract
Pinch ground cinnamon, to taste
4 C sliced very ripe bananas
1/2 C raw peanuts, coarsely chopped
2 T cacao nibs

Instructions

1. Soak dates in lukewarm water to cover for 10 minutes. Drain dates and reserve soaking liquid.
2. In a food processor, blend dates with three to four tablespoons of soaking liquid, honey, coconut oil, vanilla and cinnamon until smooth.
3. Add bananas to food processor and blend until mostly smooth, leaving some small chunks of banana intact if desired. Transfer banana mixture to a stainless steel bowl and stir in peanuts and cacao nibs.
4. Freeze mixture, stirring occasionally, until it reaches a semi-solid state, 4 to 6 hours or overnight. Allow to thaw at room temperature 30 minutes to an hour before serving.

Nutrition Info
Per serving (About 1/2 cup/128g-wt.): 270 calories (80 from fat), 9g total fat, 3.5g saturated fat, 4g protein, 49g total carbohydrate (5g dietary fiber, 35g sugar), 0mg cholesterol, 0mg sodium
