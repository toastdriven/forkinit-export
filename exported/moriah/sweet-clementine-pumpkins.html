Forkinit: Sweet Clementine Pumpkins

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Clementines, peeled
Celery, cut into thin strips (~1/2" taller than clementines)

Instructions

Remove centers carefully from clementines without separating the sections. Poke celery through the center of each clementine to form a stem. Enjoy!
