Forkinit: High Raw Vegan Pumpkin Pie Pudding

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 cup cashews
6 soaked and pitted dates
2 cups pumpkin puree
1/4 cup almond milk
1 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Grind the cashews in a food processors fitted with the S blade till powdery.

Add dates, pumpkin, almond milk and cinnamon to the processor and process, stopping often to scrape the bowl, till creamy and smooth.

Serve.

Yields: 4 servings
