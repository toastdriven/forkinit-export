Forkinit: Easy Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 C Arrowhead Mills Unbleached White Flour
3 C Arrowhead Mills Stone Ground Whole Wheat Flour
2 T active dry yeast
2 1/2 C warm water (110°F)
3 T agave
2 tsp sea salt (optional)
3 T vegetable oil
Melted butter (optional)

Instructions

Dissolve yeast in water. Add all remaining ingredients except whole wheat flour. Beat two minutes. Add whole wheat flour, blend with spoon until smooth. Cover and let rise in warm place for 30 minutes. Stir batter 25 strokes. Pour evenly into two oiled 9x5" loaf pans. Smooth top and pat into shape with floured hands. Let rise again 40 minutes. Bake at 375°F for 45 minutes until brown. Remove from pans and cool. Brush top with melted butter, if desired.

Yields: 2 loaves
