Forkinit: Apple Pie Breakfast Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup old fashioned oats
1 cup quick oats
1/4 cup ground flax seed
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon each nutmeg and cloves
Egg Replacer equal to 2 eggs, beaten
1/2 cup unsweetened apple sauce
1/3 cup coconut sugar (or unprocessed cane sugar)
1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
1 cup diced apple pieces
1/2 cup toasted walnut pieces
1/2 cup raisins

Instructions

Preheat your oven to 350Â°F.
In a large bowl stir together old fashioned and quick oats, ground flax, salt and spices.
In a smaller bowl whisk together Egg Replacer eggs, apple sauce, coconut sugar and melted coconut oil. Add to the dry ingredients and stir to combine. Fold in the diced apple, walnuts and raisins.
Drop 1/4 C sized portions of batter onto a parchment lined baking sheet. Flatten the cookies slightly, as they won't spread in the oven.
Bake for 15-20 minutes until set. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet before transferring to an airtight container.

Note: Vegan cookies will be a little looser and might need a few extra minutes in the oven.

Yields: 12 cookies
