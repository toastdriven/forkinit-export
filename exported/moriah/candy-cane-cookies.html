Forkinit: Candy Cane Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley
Guests will have a merry time munching these mild mint cookies.

Ingredients

1/2 C butter (no substitutes), softened (vegan)
1/2 C shortening (vegan)
1 C sugar
1/4 C confectioners' sugar
1/2 C milk (non-dairy)
1 egg (Egg Replacer)
1 tsp peppermint extract
1 tsp vanilla extract
3-1/2 C all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
Green and red food coloring

Instructions

1. In a bowl, cream (vegan) butter, (vegan) shortening and sugars. Beat in (non-dairy) milk, egg (Egg Replacer) and extracts. Gradually add flour and salt. Set aside half of the dough. Divide remaining dough in half; add green food coloring to one portion and red food coloring to the other. Wrap dough separately in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until easy to handle.

2. Roll 1/2 teaspoonfuls of each color of dough into 3-in. ropes. Place each green rope next to a white rope; press together gently and twist. Repeat with red ropes and remaining white ropes. Place 2 in. apart on ungreased baking sheets. Curve one end, forming a cane. Bake at 350Â°F for 11-13 minutes or until set. Cool for 2 minutes; carefully remove to wire racks. Yield: about 6 dozen.
