Forkinit: Foods To Improve Your Mood

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Avocado
Raw Cacao
Spinach
Chia Seeds
Sunflower Seeds

Instructions

Avocado - Avocados have many benefits that go along with elevating your spirit.

Raw Cacao - High in magnesium and triggers the brain production of natural opioids to make you feel good!

Spinach - Spinach is so nutrient dense that it does wonders for your health, mood and the prevention of illness.

Chia Seeds - Potent vegetarian source of omega 3, packed full of essential amino acids.

Sunflower Seeds - Their richness in vitamin E, selenium and magnesium all contribute to improving your mood.
