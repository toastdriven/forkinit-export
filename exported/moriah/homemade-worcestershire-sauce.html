Forkinit: Homemade Worcestershire Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
This sauce is gluten free!

Ingredients

1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
2 T gluten free soy sauce
2 T water
1 T natural organic brown sugar or agave syrup
1/4 tsp ground ginger
1/4 tsp ground mustard
1/4 tsp onion powder
1/4 tsp garlic powder
1/8 tsp cinnamon
1/8 tsp black pepper

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Lower heat and simmer for 5 minutes until sugar has dissolved. Cool and store in the refrigerator.
