Forkinit: Florida Crystals Organic Mojito

By Moriah Lindsley
An alluring tropical beverage...with an organic twist!

Ingredients

4 fresh organic mint leaves (about 1 tsp)
1 T FLORIDA CRYSTALS Organic Cane Sugar
2 T fresh organic lime juice
1 1/2 oz light rum (optional)
Crushed ice
Splash of STEAZ Organic Green Tea Soda Key Lime, chilled
Organic lime slices, optional
Fresh organic mint sprigs, optional

Instructions

Place mint, organic cane sugar and lime in a tall glass. With the back of a wooden spoon, crush mint until organic cane is dissolved. Stir in rum. Fill glass with crushed ice. Top off glass with Steaz soda, to taste. Stir drink well. Garnish with lime slices and fresh mint sprigs, if desired.
Servings: 1
