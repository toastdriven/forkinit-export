Forkinit: Stuffed Tomatoes

By Moriah Lindsley
These tasty baked tomatoes are filled with creamy rice for an easy to make lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

2 T oil
1/4 C onions, chopped
1/4 C green pepper, chopped
1/4 C peas, boiled
1/2 C rice, boiled
2 T nondairy cheddar cheese, grated
1/4 C silken tofu, creamed
Salt and pepper, to taste
4 ripe tomatoes
4 T nondairy cheddar cheese, grated
