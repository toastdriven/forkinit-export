Forkinit: Butter Thins

By Moriah Lindsley
These light, delicate cookies are a family favorite. You can decorate them with pecan halves or candied cherry halves, but leave some plain for the kids.

Ingredients

1/2 C butter (vegan)
2/3 C sugar
1 egg (or Egg Replacer)
1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
3/4 C all-purpose flour
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
Pecan halves or candied cherry halves, optional

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Ã‚Â°F.
2. Cream butter, sugar, and Vanilla together. Add the egg and beat until the batter is light and fluffy.
3. Gently mix in the flour, salt and baking powder to make a soft dough.
4. Drop by the scant (barely full) tsp, 2" apart, on ungreased cookie sheets.
5. Bake for about 8 minutes. Use a thin, flexible spatula to remove the cookies from the pans, as they do not rise much and can be a tad difficult to remove from the pan. Using parchment paper or a cookie sheet that has no rim makes it easier. Remove the cookies right away or they will stick.
6. Top with pecan halves or candied cherries immediately after removing the cookies from the pan.
