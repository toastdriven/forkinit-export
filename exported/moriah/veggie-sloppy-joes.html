Forkinit: Veggie Sloppy Joes

By Moriah Lindsley
Delicious sloppy joes, vegetarian style!

Ingredients

1 cup TVP
Scant 1 cup water
1 can sloppy joe sauce
Vegan cheese
2-4 buns

Instructions

1. Boil water.
2. Add water to TVP; let sit for 5 min.
3. Cut slices of vegan cheese and place on buns.
4. Mix TVP with sloppy joe mix.
5. Serve mixture on buns.
