Forkinit: Ranch Mocktail

By Moriah Lindsley
From The Ranch at Live Oak Malibu

Ingredients

1 whole cantaloupe (or honeydew melon)
2 celery stalks
1 apple
1/4" piece of ginger
1 (750 ml) bottle of sparkling water
Salt, to taste

For the garnish:
4 citrus slices (optional)

Instructions

Put all ingredients except sparkling water and salt through a fruit and vegetable juicer. Mix the juice with the sparkling water; serve over ice. Garnish with a citrus slice (optional) and toast away!

Yields: 4 servings
