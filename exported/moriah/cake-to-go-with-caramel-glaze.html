Forkinit: Cake-to-go with Caramel Glaze

By Moriah Lindsley
Great for picnics or potluck dinners, this cake with a caramel glaze is simple, delicious, and best of all, easy to transport!

Ingredients

Basic Buttermilk Cake

Caramel Glaze:
3/4 C brown sugar
1 1/2 C coconut milk or vegan heavy cream
1 1/2 T vegan butter
Pinch of salt

Instructions

1. Make the batter as directed in the basic cake recipe. Scrape batter into a buttered 9x13x2" pan and spread to edges. Bake at 350Â°F for 25 to 30 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Cool cake completely in pan on a rack.
2. Place glaze ingredients in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir with a wooden spoon and bring to a simmer; then reduce heat slightly.
3. Simmer for 7 minutes, stirring frequently, until glaze is thickened. Cool until barely warm.
4. Pour about half the glaze over the top of the cake and spread to edges with a spatula. Drizzle remaining glaze over cut pieces of cake when serving.

Makes one 9x13x2" cake
