Forkinit: Thai Summer Breeze

By Moriah Lindsley
Try this delicious drink blended!

Ingredients

2 oz. Thai Kitchen Pure Coconut Milk (Regular or Lite)
2 oz. pineapple juice
2 oz. orange juice
Optional: 2 oz. dark rum
4 oz. crushed ice

Instructions

Combine coconut milk, pineapple juice, orange juice and rum; blend until frothy, and serve over crushed ice. Serves 1.
