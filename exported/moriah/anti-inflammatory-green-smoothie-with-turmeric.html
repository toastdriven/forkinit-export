Forkinit: Anti-Inflammatory Green Smoothie with Turmeric

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup frozen or fresh pineapple chunks
1 handful arugula
1 handful spinach and kale
1/2" fresh ginger root, peeled
1 1/2 cups coconut water and/or green tea
1/2 tsp ground turmeric powder or 1/2" fresh turmeric root
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
*optional scoop vegan and gluten-free protein powder
water and ice to blend

Instructions

Blend ingredients. Top it with chia seeds and a handful of walnuts. Enjoy!

Yields: 1 serving
