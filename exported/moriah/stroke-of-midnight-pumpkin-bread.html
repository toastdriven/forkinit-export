Forkinit: Stroke of Midnight Pumpkin Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/3 C vegan sugar
1/3 C shortening
1/2 C applesauce
1 C pumpkin
1 2/3 C flour (The author uses a combo of wheat and soy)
1/4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1/3 C water
dash of salt
dash of nutmeg, cloves, cinnamon, ginger (Whatever you like)

Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F.
2. Beat the vegan sugar, shortening and applesauce until 'light and fluffy' or well mixed. Stir in the pumpkin.
3. Sift together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt and spices. Add the batter and water, alternately.
4. Spoon into a greased and floured bread pan. Bake about 45 minutes - 1 hour or until done
Serves: 8 - 10
Preparation time: About 1 hr
