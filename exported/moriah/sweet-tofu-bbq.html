Forkinit: Sweet Tofu BBQ

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 lb firm tofu
1 C barbecue sauce (without anchovies)
1 C firmly packed brown sugar
2 tsp dried thyme
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp ground red pepper
6 oz frozen apple juice concentrate, thawed
3/4 tsp sea salt
1/2 tsp black pepper
Vegetable cooking spray

Instructions

1. Slice the tofu in half, widthwise. Wrap it in a towel and press between 2 heavy books for 30 minutes.
2. In a saucepan over medium heat, combine the barbecue sauce, brown sugar, thyme, chili powder, red pepper, and apple juice. Cook 5 minutes or until thoroughly heated. Set aside.
3. Unwrap the tofu and sprinkle with the salt and pepper. Marinate in the barbecue sauce for 1 hour.
4. Grill the tofu for 10 minutes, turning halfway, until hot and grill marks appear. Brush with the barbecue sauce.
Makes 4 servings
