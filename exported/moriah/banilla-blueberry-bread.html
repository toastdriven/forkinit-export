Forkinit: BaNilla Blueberry Bread

By Moriah Lindsley
A tasty sweet, breakfast bread!

Ingredients

1 1/3 C plus 1 tsp all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp salt
1/2 tsp baking soda
1/2 C blueberries, fresh or frozen
1/4 tsp baking powder
5 T butter, room temp. (vegan)
1/2 C sugar
2 eggs (or Egg Replacer)
1 C BaNilla lowfat yogurt (or vegan yogurt)
1 small banana, peeled, minced

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Combine all dry ingredients, and mix well. In a separate large bowl, combine (vegan) butter and sugar and beat on high for 2 minutes. Gradually incorporate dry ingredients, followed by the (vegan) yogurt, eggs (or Egg Replacer) and mashed banana. In a small bowl, toss frozen blueberries with one tsp of flour to coat, and gently fold into the batter. Pour batter into greased loaf pan and bake for 50-60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let loaf cool on a baking rack before removing from pan.
