Forkinit: Tropical Green Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup kale
1/2 cup cucumber
1/2 frozen banana
1/2 cup of mango
1/2 peeled lemon
1 kiwi
1 teaspoon greens
1 tsp maca powder
1 tsp coconut oil
1 scoop protein powder
1/2 cup water
1/2 cup coconut milk

Instructions

Place all ingredients into blender and blend. Add more liquids as desired.
