Forkinit: Athlete's Vegetable Juice

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 tomatoes
4-5 romaine lettuce leaves
1/3 English cucumber
1 stalk celery
1/3 of a small beet
2 medium carrots
1 clove garlic (optional)
Pinch sea salt


Read more: http://ohsheglows.com/2010/07/21/a-friend-in-the-mirror/#ixzz3xjYKu5gZ

Instructions

Juice the vegetables and pour into a large glass. Add a pinch of sea salt and stir well.

Yields: 1
