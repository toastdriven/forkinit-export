Forkinit: Lemon Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley
Yields enough to frost the tops and sides of two 8-inch or 9-inch cake layers.

Ingredients

1/3 C butter
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1/4 C fresh lemon juice
1/2 tsp finely shredded lemon peel
4 1/2 C sifted powdered sugar

Instructions

1. Beat butter in a mixing bowl until it is fluffy.
2. Add 2 C of the powdered sugar gradually, beating constantly.
3. Slowly beat in lemon juice, lemon peel and vanilla.
4. Slowly beat in the rest of the powdered sugar. If necessary, add a little more lemon juice if frosting is not spreadable.
5. Use immediately.

Tip: You can keep this frosting in the refrigerator up to six days and in the freezer up to six months.
