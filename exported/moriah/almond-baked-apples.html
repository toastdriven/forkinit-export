Forkinit: Almond Baked Apples

By Moriah Lindsley
A delectable combo for a warm treat any time of year.

Ingredients

1/2 C Maranatha Creamy Almond Butter
1/3 C raisins
1 C apple juice
1 1/2 tsp pure natural vanilla
1/2 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp nutmeg
4 lg baking apples (McIntosh, Fuji or Yellow Delicious)
1 C nonfat plain yogurt (soy)
1 T honey (or agave nectar)

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
2. Combine Maranatha Creamy Almond Butter, raisins, 3 T apple juice, vanilla and spices.
3. Core apples, avoid breaking through bottom of apple.
4. Fill each apple with almond butter filling.
5. Arrange apples in a shallow baking dish.
6. Pour remaining apple juice into pan.
7. Bake for 20 minutes.
8. Mix (soy) yogurt and honey (or agave nectar) to make a sauce and serve over warm apples or use as a dipping sauce.
