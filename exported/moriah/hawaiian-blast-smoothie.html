Forkinit: Hawaiian Blast Smoothie

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C ripe pineapple, cut up
1/2 C mango, cut up
1/2 C plain vegan yogurt
1/2 C non-dairy milk
2 tsp fresh lime juice

Instructions

For freshest flavor, use ripe fruit (the riper the fruit, the sweeter the smoothie). Process the fruit, yogurt and milk in a blender, food processor or with an immersion blender until smooth. Add some sweetener if desired.

Yields: 2 servings
