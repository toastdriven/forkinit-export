Forkinit: Healthier Banana Bread

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 C whole wheat flour
1 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp salt
1/2 C unsweetened applesauce
3/4 C honey
Egg replacer equal to 2 eggs
3 mashed overripe bananas

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Lightly grease a 9x5" loaf pan.
In a large bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt. In a separate bowl, mix together applesauce and honey. Stir in egg replacer "eggs" and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture; stir just to moisten.
Pour batter into prepared loaf pan. Bake 60-65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.

Yields: 1 loaf
