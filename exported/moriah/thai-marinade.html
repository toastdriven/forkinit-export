Forkinit: Thai Marinade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 T lemon juice
1 T orange juice
1/2 C soy sauce
1 tsp chopped fresh ginger
2 cloves crushed garlic
1 T chopped cilantro
1/4 tsp black pepper
1 bay leaf
2 T honey or agave

Instructions

Mix all marinade ingredients together. Pour over protein or vegetables. Marinate for 4-24 hours. Broil or grill as you prefer.
