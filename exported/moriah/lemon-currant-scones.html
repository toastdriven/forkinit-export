Forkinit: Lemon Currant Scones

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C all-purpose flour
6 T turbinado raw sugar, divided
1 1/2 tsp grated lemon peel
1 1/2 tsp non-aluminum baking powder
1/4 tsp baking soda
1/4 tsp sea salt
6 T unsalted (vegan) butter, cut into pieces
3/4 C chilled heavy cream, divided (or coconut milk)
2 T fresh lemon juice
1/2 C currants, softened in boiling water for 5 minutes, drained
Devonshire cream, honey and/or jam for the table

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
2. Mix flour, half the sugar, lemon peel, baking powder, baking soda and salt in food processor.
3. Add butter and process until mixture resembles coarse meal.
4. Add 1/2 C cream (or coconut milk) and lemon juice and blend until soft moist dough forms.
5. Stir in currants.
6. Drop dough by generous 1/4 Cupfuls onto a nonstick or parchment-lined baking sheet, spacing for 6 scones.
7. Brush tops generously with heavy cream (or coconut milk). Sprinkle with remaining sugar.
8. Bake about 25 minutes.
Makes 6
