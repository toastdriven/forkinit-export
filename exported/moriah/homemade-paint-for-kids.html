Forkinit: Homemade Paint for Kids

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C salt
1 C flour
1 C water
Food coloring

Instructions

Mix first three ingredients together and separate into containers or bowls for different colors. Add food coloring of choice to each container. Mix well. Store in squirt bottles or paint cups.
