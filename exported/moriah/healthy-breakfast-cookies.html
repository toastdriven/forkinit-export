Forkinit: Healthy Breakfast Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3/4 cup peanut butter
1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
1/4 cup honey (or agave), warmed
1 cup rolled oats
1 cup quick oats
1 ripe banana, mashed
3/4 cup dried fruit (I used raisins)
1/2 cup seeds (I used sunflower seeds)
1/2 cup nuts (I used cashew pieces)
1/4 cup ground flax
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon salt (only if using unsalted nuts above)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325Â°F. Combine peanut butter, coconut oil and honey until smooth.
Place all other ingredients into a large bowl. Mix with the peanut butter mixture until evenly distributed.
Drop about 1/4 C sized scoops of the mixture onto a cookie sheet and flatten (cookies won't spread while baking). Bake for about 15 minutes until edges are lightly browned.
Let cookies cool on baking sheet before moving to an air-tight storage container.

Yields: 12-14 large cookies
