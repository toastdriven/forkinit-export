Forkinit: Peanut Butter Cookie in a Cup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 Tablespoon vegan butter
1 Tablespoon peanut butter
1 Tablespoon firmly packed dark brown sugar
1 Tablespoon sugar
2-3 drops pure vanilla extract {a splash}
Small pinch of kosher salt
Egg Replacer equal to 1 egg
3 level T all-purpose flour
Crushed peanuts for topping

Instructions

Melt peanut butter and butter together in microwave for about 30 seconds. Add sugars, salt and vanilla and stir to combine. Add Egg Replacer egg and stir again. Add flour and give a final stir. Sprinkle peanuts on top and heat in microwave for around 45 seconds. Microwaves vary, so start checking for doneness at 35 seconds. Serve warm!

Yields: 1 cookie
