Forkinit: Chart House Squaw Bread with Agave Butter

By Moriah Lindsley
This is the bread recipe from the famous Chart House. It's hearty and delicious. Serve with the agave butter.

Ingredients

1 C white flour
2 C whole wheat flour
1/2 C sugar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp baking soda
1 1/2 C non-dairy milk
1/2 C molasses

Spread:
2 sticks Earth Balance Buttersticks
1/2 C agave

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325Â°F.
Sift the dry ingredients together.
Combine the liquids.
Blend all together with electric mixer until well blended.
Pour into 9x5" loaf pan. Bake 1 1/2 hours.
Mix the Buttersticks and agave together well. Store in the refrigerator.
Serve bread slices with the butter spread.

Yields: 1 loaf
