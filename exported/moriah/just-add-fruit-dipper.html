Forkinit: Just-Add-Fruit Dipper

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C unflavored non-dairy yogurt (almond or coconut)
2 T almond butter
1/2 to 1 serving Vega One Chocolate, French Vanilla or Natural flavor
Sprinkle of cinnamon, to taste
1 tsp maple syrup (optional)

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Stir until well mixed. Serve with fruit, such as apple slices or frozen berries.
