Forkinit: Whipped Topping Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley
Lighter and less sweet than traditional frosting. This was one of my favorites as a kid!

Ingredients

1 C non-dairy milk
1 (3 1/2 oz) pkg instant pudding mix, any flavor
1/4 C powdered sugar
8 oz dairy-free whipped topping

Instructions

Pour milk into bowl add pudding mix and sugar.
Beat with wire whisk for 2 minutes.
Gently fold in whipped topping.
