Forkinit: Peanut Butter Frosting

By Moriah Lindsley
A very rich frosting that tastes like peanut butter balls!

Ingredients

1/2 C natural peanut butter
1/2 C vegan butter
2-3 C icing sugar (confectioner's sugar)
Dash of sea salt
2-3 T non-dairy milk

Instructions

1. Place all ingredients in a bowl and beat until smooth. Chill for a half hour before icing the cake (make sure your cake is completely cooled). Should make enough for two round 8" or 9" layers or one 9x13" sheet cake.
