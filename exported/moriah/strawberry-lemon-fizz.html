Forkinit: Strawberry Lemon Fizz

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 Tbsp mashed strawberries
2 Tbsp whole sliced strawberries
3/4 cup sparkling mineral water
1/4 cup sparkling lemon soda
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 lemon slice
Sweeten to taste, if needed

Instructions

Add all the ingredients (chill liquids and fruit first). Stir well. Best when you let the juices sit and marinate in the fridge for at least twenty minutes or so. Could even be made a day ahead of time.

Yields: 1 serving
