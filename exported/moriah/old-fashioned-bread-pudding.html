Forkinit: Old-Fashioned Bread Pudding

By Moriah Lindsley
Old-fashioned taste, less fat and calories

Ingredients

2 C rice milk
Egg Replacer equivalent to 2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp sugar
4 slices stale bread, cut into 1-in cubes
2 T raisins
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Instructions

Combine rice milk, Egg Replacer, vanilla and sugar in a bowl. Add bread cubes and raisins; stir gently. Pour into a 1-qt baking dish coated with nonstick cooking spray. Sprinkle with cinnamon. Place in a shallow pan; add 1 in. of water. Bake at 350Â°F for 50 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean. Yield: 6 servings.
