Forkinit: Thin White Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
Good for soups!

Ingredients

1 T margarine or butter (vegan)
2 tsp Hodgson Mill Corn Starch
1 C milk (non-dairy)
1/2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp pepper

Instructions

Melt (vegan) butter in sauce pan. Blend in Hodgson Mill Pure Corn Starch, salt and pepper. Gradually add (non-dairy) milk. Heat to boiling; 2 minutes stirring constantly.
