Forkinit: Pomosa

By Moriah Lindsley
This colorful drink layers pomegranate juice, champagne, and orange Curacao in a sugar-rimmed champagne flute.

Ingredients

2 oz pomegranate juice
3 oz champagne
1 1/2 oz orange Curacao
1 T red sugar for rimming

Instructions

Dip the rim of the champagne flute into a little pomegranate juice and then into sugar. Let sugar dry. Pour pomegranate juice into a sugar-rimmed glass. Then pour champagne over the pomegranate juice and top the drink off with Orange Curacao.
