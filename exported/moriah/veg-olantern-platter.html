Forkinit: Veg O'Lantern Platter

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Bell peppers
Broccoli florets
Cauliflower florets
Carrot sticks
Other assorted veggies

Ghoulish Pumpkin Dip:
2 cloves garlic
1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed
1 (15 oz) can pure pumpkin
2 T almond or peanut butter
1 tsp ground cumin
Juice of 1 lemon
1 tsp olive oil
1/2 tsp salt
Dash cayenne pepper
Chopped black olives

Herb Yogurt Dip:
1 C vegan Greek-style yogurt
6 T herbs, chopped (parsley, chives, basil)
2 tsp lemon juice
Salt, to taste

Instructions

Cut the tops off bell peppers and carefully remove the center section of each pepper. Cut faces into the sides of peppers. Fill with vegetables. Serve with Ghoulish Pumpkin Dip or Herb Yogurt Dip.

Ghoulish Pumpkin Dip:
Puree all ingredients, except olives, in a food processor. Add ice water until desired consistency is achieved. Pour into shallow bowl and decorate with chopped olives.

Herb Yogurt Dip:
Combine ingredients in a serving bowl and mix well.
