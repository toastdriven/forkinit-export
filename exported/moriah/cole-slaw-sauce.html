Forkinit: Cole Slaw Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
A tasty, easy recipe for a delicious cole slaw sauce.

Ingredients

1 C oil
1 C sugar
1/2 to 1 C vinegar
1 tsp salt
1 tsp black pepper
1 tsp prepared mustard
1 tsp celery seed (optional)
1/2 C juice from sweet pickles (optional)

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients together in a saucepan and bring to a boil while stirring.
2. As soon as it comes to a boil, take the saucepan off the heat. If it is allowed to boil too long, the sugar will cause the sauce to turn into a thick syrup and will not coat the cole slaw well.
3. Continue stirring for a minute or two to cool it down a little.
4. Pour over shredded cabbage and carrots. For best results, let cole slaw sit for a day in the refrigerator before eating.
