Forkinit: Slutty Brownie Ice Cream Cake

By Moriah Lindsley
A decadent ice cream cake, perfect for summer birthdays or any other occasion!

Ingredients

Brownie Layer:
1 box brownie mix
Egg Replacer equal to 2 eggs
1/4 c water
1/2 c vegetable oil

Cookie Dough Layer:
1 stick vegan butter, melted
1/3 c brown sugar
1/4 c white sugar
2 tbsp non-dairy milk
1/2 tsp vanilla
1 1/4 c flour
1/4 tsp salt
1 c mini dairy-free chocolate chips

Sandwich Cookie Layer:
1 sleeve Oreos or Newman O's

Ice Cream Layer:
1 gallon vegan cookies 'n cream ice cream

Toppings:
Hot fudge/chocolate syrup
Crushed sandwich cookies

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325Â°F and grease a 9" springform pan with cooking spray.
In a large bowl, mix all brownie ingredients until well combined. Use half the batter and bake according to package directions. Let cool completely (or freeze for about 1 hour). (Use leftover batter however you'd like.)
Meanwhile, make cookie dough: In a large bowl, mix butter with brown and white sugar. Add milk and vanilla and stir to combine. Stir in flour and salt. When fully combined, fold in chocolate chips. Press cookie dough in an even layer into bottom of pan.
On top of cookie dough, add a single layer of sandwich cookies. Drizzle with hot fudge. On top of that, add first layer of slightly softened ice cream (1/2 gallon). Spread in an even layer. Cover in plastic wrap and freeze immediately, 1 hour.
Press brownie on top of the first frozen ice cream layer. Then spread remaining 1/2 gallon ice cream on top. Top with crushed sandwich cookies and a drizzle of hot fudge. Cover loosely with plastic wrap and immediately transfer back to freezer. Freeze until ice cream is hard, 6 hours or overnight.

Yields: 1 ice cream cake
