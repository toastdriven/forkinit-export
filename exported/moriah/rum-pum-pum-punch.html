Forkinit: Rum-Pum-Pum-Punch

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 1/2 C light rum
1/4 C coconut rum
2 1/2 C orange juice
2 1/2 C pineapple juice
1/4 C lime juice
3 T grenadine
Ice cubes

Instructions

Mix ingredients together in a punch bowl. Stir with a clean pair of drumsticks and get the party started. Makes 14 4-oz servings.

Yields: 14 servings
