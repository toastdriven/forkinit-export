Forkinit: Black Bean and Spinach Quesadillas

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/4 C chopped onion
1 (10 oz) pkg frozen spinach, thawed
1 (15 1/2 oz) can black beans, rinsed and drained
2 tsp lemon juice
1 T olive oil
1 tsp garlic powder
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1 tsp salt
4 (8") flour tortillas
2 C shredded Mexican cheese, divided (vegan)
Salsa (optional)
Sour cream (optional) (vegan)

Instructions

1. Saute onion, spinach, beans and lemon juice in hot oil in large skillet. Add garlic powder, cumin, chili powder and salt; stir well. Cook 5 to 7 minutes or until spinach is heated through; remove from heat.
2. Place 1 tortilla in lightly greased skillet. Layer with 1/4 C of the cheese, 1/3 C spinach mixture and an additional 1/4 C of the cheese. Fold in half; press lightly. Cook 1 to 2 minutes on each side or until cheese melts. Repeat with remaining tortillas, cheese and spinach mixture. Serve with sour cream and salsa, if desired.
