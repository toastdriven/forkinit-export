Forkinit: Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 C granulated sugar
1 C brown sugar
1 C vegan butter
Egg replacer equivalent to 2 eggs
1 1/2 tsp vanilla
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
3 C all-purpose flour
12 oz semi-sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

1. Place sugars, butter, egg replacer and vanilla in mixer bowl. Attach bowl and flat beater to mixer. Turn to Speed 2 and mix about 30 seconds. Stop and scrape bowl. Turn to Speed 4 and beat about 30 seconds. Stop and scrape bowl.
2. Turn to STIR Speed. Gradually add baking soda, salt and flour to sugar mixture and mix about 2 minutes. Turn to Speed 2 and mix about 30 seconds. Stop mixer and scrape bowl. Add chocolate chips. Turn to STIR Speed and mix about 15 seconds.
3. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto greased baking sheets, about 2" apart. Bake at 375Â°F for 10 to 12 minutes. Remove from baking sheets immediately and cool on wire racks.
