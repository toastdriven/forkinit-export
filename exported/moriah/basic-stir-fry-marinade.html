Forkinit: Basic Stir Fry Marinade

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 T soy sauce
2 T sherry, orange juice, brandy or vinegar
1-3 tsp Chinese Five Spice Powder
1-2 tsp agave nectar
1/2 tsp Granulated Garlic or 2 cloves
White Pepper, to taste

Instructions

Whisk ingredients together; use to coat 1 lb thinly sliced tofu or seitan. Cover and refrigerate to allow flavor to penetrate. Stir-fry with 4 C assorted raw vegetables, chopped.
