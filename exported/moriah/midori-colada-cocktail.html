Forkinit: Midori Colada Cocktail

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 oz Midori Melon Liqueur
1 oz rum
2 oz canned coconut milk
4 oz pineapple juice
1/2 oz lime juice
1/2 cup ice crushed

Instructions

Place all the ingredients into a blender and blend until ice is crushed.
Pour into a hurricane glass and garnish with a lime slice and a cherry.
