Forkinit: Vegan Apple Coffee Cake

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 tablespoons cinnamon
5 tablespoons sugar
5-6 C peeled and sliced apples
2 C sugar
1 C applesauce
4 tsp vanilla
1/2 C orange juice
1 pinch salt
4 egg substitute
3 C flour
1 T baking powder
1 tsp nutmeg

Instructions

1. Combine sugar and cinnamon and toss all of the mixture except 1 T with the apples.
2. Set aside.
3. Whisk together sugar, applesauce, vanilla, OJ, salt, and egg replacers.
4. Fold in flour, baking powder, and nutmeg.
5. Spoon about half of the mixture into a greased bundt pan.
6. Cover with half of the apple mixture.
7. Repeat with remaining batter and apples.
8. Sprinkle with reserved cinnamon-sugar.
9. Bake at 350Â°F for 60-70 minutes.
10. Let cool and invert onto a plate for serving.
