Forkinit: Soy Milk Whipped Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/4 C soy milk
2-4 T artificial sweetener
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 tsp cornstarch
1/2 C oil

Instructions

1. In a blender or food processor, blend together the milk, sweetener, vanilla, and cornstarch.
2. Slowly drizzle in the oil while the blender is running.
3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
4. Chill for 1 hour before using.
