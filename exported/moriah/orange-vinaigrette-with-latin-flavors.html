Forkinit: Orange Vinaigrette with Latin Flavors

By Moriah Lindsley
A spicy vinaigrette for salad, or marinate your favorite veggies and tofu for kabobs!

Ingredients

1/2 C frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
1/4 C extra-virgin olive oil
3 medium garlic cloves, crushed
3 T sherry vinegar or red wine vinegar
2 T water
1 tsp ground cumin
1 tsp paprika
3/4 tsp salt
1/2 tsp freshly ground pepper

Instructions

Place all ingredients in beaker or bowl; blend with hand blender until smooth. (Vinaigrette can be made up to 4 days ahead. Cover and refrigerate.) Stir or shake before using.
Yield: 1 cup

PER TABLESPOON: 45 cal., 3.5 g total fat (.5 g sat. fat), .5 g protein, 4 g carbs, 0 mg cholesterol, 110 mg sodium, 0 g fiber
