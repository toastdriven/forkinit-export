Forkinit: Homemade Ketchup

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 6-oz can no-salt-added tomato paste
Half an apple, diced (with or without skin)*
1/3 cup water
1 tablespoon lemon juice*
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a blender or Vitamix until smooth.

*Note: 1/3 C apple juice may be substituted for the apple and water. 1 T vinegar may be substituted for the lemon juice.

Yields: 1 C
Prep time: 5 minutes
