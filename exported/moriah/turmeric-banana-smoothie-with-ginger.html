Forkinit: Turmeric Banana Smoothie with Ginger

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 medium-sized bananas
1 1/2 cups vanilla almond milk, unsweetened
2 T hemp seeds
1/2 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp ginger, minced
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1 1/2 T honey (or agave)
1/2 tsp vanilla extract
6-8 ice cubes

Instructions

Add all ingredients to high speed blender. Blend 20-30 seconds or until smooth and creamy, top with additional hemp seeds and turmeric if desired.

Yields: 2 servings
