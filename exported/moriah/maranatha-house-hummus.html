Forkinit: Maranatha House Hummus

By Moriah Lindsley
A basic recipe you can customize with your favorite seasonings.

Ingredients

2 1/4 C cooked garbanzo beans, drained (save liquid)
1/4 C lemon juice
2 cloves garlic or to taste
1/4 C Maranatha Sesame Tahini, roasted
Sea salt, to taste

Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients in a food processor and blend thoroughly.
2. Add sea salt to taste.
3. Serve chilled with crackers, bread or your favorite vegetables.

<i>Zest up hummus with your favorite flavor(s):
Roasted peppers, eggplant, roasted garlic, fresh herbs or mild-hot chili peppers.</i>
