Forkinit: Chocolate Peanut Butter Cups/Balls

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

3 C peanut butter, plain or crunchy
1 1/2 lbs powdered sugar
2 sticks butter
14 oz pkg semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 tsp coconut oil

Instructions

Mix ingredients thoroughly and shape into balls. Melt coconut oil and chocolate chips over double boiler. Dip each ball into chocolate using a toothpick. If chocolate is too thick, add more coconut oil to thin it. Place balls on parchment paper. Cool in refrigerator.

Yields: 90
