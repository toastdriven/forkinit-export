Forkinit: Garden Pasta Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
A wonderful red wine sauce!

Ingredients

2 large onions, chopped
2 cloves garlic
1/3 C olive oil
15 large tomatoes, peeled and cut as desired
2 C red table wine (can sub. water or broth for wine)
1 1/2 tsp salt
crushed pepper to taste
2 tsp dried oregano
1 tsp basil
1 tsp rosemary

Instructions

Saute onions and garlic in a large pot until golden. Add other ingredients to pot, bring to boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 20 minutes. Or, after you saute onion and garlic, place all ingredients in crock pot and simmer 20 minutes.

Source: Kathy Bullack, Mt. Hood, Oregon
