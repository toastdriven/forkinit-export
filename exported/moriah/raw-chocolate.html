Forkinit: Raw Chocolate

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

8 oz raw carob powder
1 oz alcohol-free vanilla extract or contents of 1 vanilla bean
2 oz alcohol-free peppermint extract
16 oz extra virgin unprocessed coconut oil
36 oz raw cacao nibs
16 oz raw nut butter of choice
6-8 oz raw agave nectar

Instructions

Pour coconut oil into a bowl. If it's hard, warm it in the sun or in hot water first until it's a clear liquid. A little heat won't hurt it. Make sure it's clear like water.
Grind the cacao nibs into a fine powder in a coffee grinder.
Add the oil with all the other ingredients.
Stir/blend until all the lumps are gone. You can do this by hand, food processor or powerful blender like a Vitamix, but by hand is all you need.
Pour into shallow pans. The author likes to keep it at 1/2". Put in the freezer and in about an hour it is solid and ready.
Break into pieces and enjoy!

You can find all of these ingredients at rawfood.com or at your local health food store.
