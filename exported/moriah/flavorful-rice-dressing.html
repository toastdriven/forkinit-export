Forkinit: Flavorful Rice Dressing

By Moriah Lindsley
Another great stuffing recipe for Thanksgiving!

Ingredients

7 slices day-old bread, torn
1 C baked corn bread, crumbled
2/3 C hot water
1 T vegetable oil
1/2 C thinly sliced celery (1-2 stalks)
1/2 C chopped onion (1 medium)
1/2 C sliced fresh mushrooms
1 C firmly packed sliced fresh spinach
1 C cooked long grain rice
1/2 C cooked wild rice
1/2 C orange juice
Egg Replacer equal to 1 egg, beaten
2 tsp rubbed sage
1/2 tsp thyme
1/2 tsp salt
1/4 tsp sugar
1/4 tsp freshly ground pepper

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. In a large bowl, lightly toss the bread, corn bread and water. Set aside. In a skillet heat the oil over medium heat. Add the celery, onion and mushrooms and cook until tender, stirring constantly. Stir into the bread mixture. Add the remaining ingredients; mix well. Place in a greased 2-quart baking dish. Cover and bake for 30 minutes.

Yields: 6-8 servings
