Forkinit: Chocolate Yum-Yum Cake

By Moriah Lindsley
Substitute chocolate chips for the raisins for even more chocolatey yum-yuminess!

Ingredients

1/2 C butter or margarine (vegan)
2 squares (1 ounce each) unsweetened baking chocolate
1 C sugar
1/2 C raisins*
1 1/2 C water
1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
1/4 tsp ground cloves
Pinch salt
1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
1 3/4 C all-purpose flour
1 tsp baking soda

ICING:
1/2 C confectioners' sugar
1/4 tsp vanilla extract
1 to 2 tsp milk (non-dairy)

Instructions

In a large saucepan over low heat, melt butter and chocolate, stirring constantly. Add sugar, raisins, water, cinnamon and cloves; bring to a boil. Boil for 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from the heat; pour into a mixing bowl and cool for 15 minutes. Add salt and vanilla. (*If using chocolate chips instead of raisins, add now.) Combine flour and baking soda; add to chocolate mixture and mix well. Pour into a greased and floured 8-cup fluted tube pan. Bake at 350Â°F for 45 minutes or until a wooden pick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool in pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool. Combine icing ingredients; spoon over cooled cake.
Yield: 8-10 servings
