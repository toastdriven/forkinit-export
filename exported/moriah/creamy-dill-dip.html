Forkinit: Creamy Dill Dip

By Moriah Lindsley
Great for dipping veggies or chips!

Ingredients

1 C fat-free (vegan) mayonnaise
1 C (8 oz) light (vegan) sour cream
2 T dried parsley flakes
1 T dried minced onion
2 tsp dill weed
1-1/2 tsp salt-free seasoning blend
1 tsp sugar

Instructions

In a small bowl, combine all ingredients. Chill for at least 1 hour. Serve with fresh vegetables or crackers. Yield: 2 Cups
