Forkinit: Chili Soup

By Moriah Lindsley
Quick, easy and very filling with the spaghetti, this makes a perfect winter meal!

Ingredients

2 c diced onions
1 tsp garlic powder
1 1/2 lbs garbanzos or chili beans, lightly mashed OR vegan "hamburger" crumbles
1 16 oz can diced tomatoes
2 tsp salt
1/8 tsp allspice
Pinch cloves
3 c tomato or vegetable juice
1 can vegetable broth
1 16 oz can kidney or black beans
1 tsp red pepper
1/4 tsp cumin
1 c broken spaghetti or small shell pasta
Vegan Parma

Instructions

Saut√© "hamburger" crumbles (if using) & onions until onions are translucent. Add tomatoes & rest of ingredients except for pasta & cheese; bring to boil. Add pasta, reduce heat & simmer 15-20 min. Serve with Vegan Parma.
