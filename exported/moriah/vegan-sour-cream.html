Forkinit: Vegan Sour Cream

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1 cup of hemp seeds (or raw cashews – not salted or roasted! If you use cashews soak them for at least 4 hours; if you cannot get hemp seeds and are allergic to nuts, use sunflower seeds, and soak them for 8 hours)
1/4 teaspoon Himalayan salt
1 teaspoon of apple cider vinegar
the juice of one small lemon
1/2 teaspoon of dill
Add 1 tsp of water if it needs more liquid for a smooth ‘sour cream’ consistency

Instructions

Add all ingredients to a food processor like a VITAMIX and blend for 3 minutes or until completely smooth and creamy!

Yields: 1 C
