Forkinit: Nutritional Yeast Cheese Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

1/2 C nutritional yeast flakes
1/2 C flour
2 tsp salt
2 C water
1/4 C margarine or vegan butter
1/2 tsp garlic powder

Instructions

1. Mix dry ingredients in a saucepan; add water and cook over medium heat, whisking, until it thickens and bubbles.
2. Cook 1 minute.
3. Remove from heat and add margarine or vegan butter.
A dash of red food coloring or a dash of saffron will give it a more traditional color.
