Forkinit: Pumpkin Bars

By Moriah Lindsley
These are a fall favorite - the perfect reward for raking up a yard full of leaves on a brisk day.

Ingredients

Pumpkin Bars:
1/2 C butter, soft (vegan)
1 C brown sugar
2 eggs (Egg Replacer)
2/3 C canned pumpkin
1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
1 C flour
1/2 tsp baking powder
1/2 tsp baking soda
1 tsp Cinnamon
1/4 tsp Powdered Ginger
1/4 tsp Ground Nutmeg

Brown Butter Icing:
1/2 C butter (vegan)
3 C powdered sugar
1 tsp Pure Vanilla Extract
2 T milk

Instructions

1. Preheat oven 350Ã‚Â°F.
2. Cream the butter. Add the brown sugar, eggs, pumpkin, and Vanilla and mix well.
3. In a separate bowl, sift all of the dry ingredients together. Gradually add to the wet mixture and mix well.
4. Pour into a greased 9x13" pan and bake for about 15-20 minutes.
5. For Brown Butter Icing, in a heavy-bottomed saucepan, heat the butter over low heat until it is a light brown. Remove from heat and add the powdered sugar, Vanilla and milk. Whisk until smooth and well blended.
6. Let pumpkin bars cool and top with Brown Butter Icing.
