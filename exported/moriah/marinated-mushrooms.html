Forkinit: Marinated Mushrooms

By Moriah Lindsley
These flavorful mushrooms would be a perfect topping to any Italian pasta dish.

Ingredients

1 lb white or brown mushrooms, sliced
1 T canola or olive oil
1/2 tsp Shallot Salt
1 tsp Italian Herb Mix
1 to 2 tsp balsamic vinegar

Instructions

1. Saute sliced mushrooms in oil with the Shallot Salt and Italian Herb Mix.
2. Add balsamic vinegar and slow cook over medium heat for 5-10 minutes to develop the flavors.
