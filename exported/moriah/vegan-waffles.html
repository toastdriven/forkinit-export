Forkinit: Vegan Waffles

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2.5 cups flour
1 tsp vanilla extract
1/4 cup sweetener (use whatever you prefer such as honey, molasses, sugar, etc)
4 tsp baking powder
1 stick margarine (1/2 cup) melted
1 cup soy milk
1 cup water
dash of cinnamon

Instructions

Mix baking powder and flour first then add the rest. Make sure the mixture isn't too liquidly or the waffles will not be as firm. Brush waffle griddle with olive oil, then pour the mixture, spread with spoon.

Yields: 8 waffles
