Forkinit: Chris Tingom's Special Hot Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
Large batch of hot sauce made from freshly picked peppers.

Ingredients

68 freshly picked peppers (Anaheim, Cubanele)
10 T tomato paste
1 T BBQ sauce (Anchovie-free)
6 T honey (Agave Nectar or maple syrup)
7-11 C water
Dash of salt
1/2 C vinegar, for freshness

Instructions

1. Chop the ends off the peppers and clean out the inside, taking out all the seeds.
2. Place tomato paste into blender with some peppers, then add BBQ sauce and some water as needed; blend.
3. Place some more peppers into blender with water and blend till all peppers are a nice smooth liquid.
4. Place your hot sauce into a glass jar and put the honey (Agave or syrup) in, add as much as you prefer. Stir sauce.
5. Place in refrigerator for 3 weeks.
6. Place in another 3 weeks then serve. Stir occasionally as needed.

Source: Chris Tingom
