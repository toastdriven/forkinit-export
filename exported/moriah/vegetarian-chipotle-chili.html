Forkinit: Vegetarian Chipotle Chili

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 T 365 Organic Canola Oil
1/2 C diced 365 Organic Yellow Onion
1/2 C diced organic carrot
1 1/2 C 365 frozen Stoplight Pepper Strips
1 small pkg chili seasoning mix
1-3 T chipotle peppers in adobo sauce, minced
1 (28 oz) can 365 Organic Diced Tomatoes
1 (15 oz) can 365 Organic Kidney Beans, drained
1 (15 oz) can 365 Organic Black Beans, drained

Organic Toppings:
Chopped scallions
Sour cream (vegan)
Shredded cheese (vegan)

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat and saute onion and carrots about 3 minutes, then add peppers and continue cooking until warm.
2. Stir in seasoning mix and minced chipotles. Add tomatoes, beans and 1 C water.
3. Gently simmer over low heat, uncovered, 30 minutes.
4. Garnish individual bowls using vegan and gluten-free toppings, if desired. Serve with warm cornbread.
Serves 4
