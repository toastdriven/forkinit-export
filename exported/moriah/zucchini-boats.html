Forkinit: Zucchini Boats

By Moriah Lindsley
These flavorful stuffed zucchini boats are an old family favorite.

Ingredients

4 small or 2 large zucchini (1/2-1 zucchini/serving)
1 large onion per 4 boats
1 C shredded cheddar (vegan)
1/2 to 1 C bread crumbs
4 hard boiled eggs or 4 oz firm tofu
1/4 C (1/2 stick) butter (vegan)
Garlic, to taste
Salt and pepper, to taste
Parsley, to taste
Paprika, to garnish

4. Line empty zucchini boats onto a sheet pan or into a 13x9" pan.
5. Scoop vegetable mixture into boats and sprinkle with paprika.
6. Add a little water to the bottom of the pan and bake until golden brown and tender, about 25-30 minutes.
