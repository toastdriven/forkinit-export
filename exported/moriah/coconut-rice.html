Forkinit: Coconut Rice

By Moriah Lindsley
A tasty alternative to regular rice.

Ingredients

1 1/2 C 365 Long Grain White Rice
1 C coconut milk
1/2 C 365 Green Peas (frozen)

Instructions

Prepare rice according to package instructions, substituting 1 C coconut milk for 1 C of the water and adding peas halfway through cooking.
