Forkinit: Peanut Butter Pinwheels

By Moriah Lindsley
This easy-to-pack snack is perfect for a road trip, a day at the beach, or an after-school snack.

Ingredients

1 apple, cored and thinly sliced
1 T lemon juice
4 T peanut butter
2 large flour tortillas
1/4 C raisins
Toothpicks

Instructions

1. Toss the apple in the lemon juice until completely covered.
2. Spread the peanut butter onto the tortillas.
3. Evenly distribute the raisins and apples on the tortillas.
4. Roll the tortillas tightly and slice into 1-inch sections. Push a toothpick through each pinwheel to keep them from falling apart.
Makes 3 to 4 servings
