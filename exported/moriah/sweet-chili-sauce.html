Forkinit: Sweet Chili Sauce

By Moriah Lindsley
A sweet-and-hot sauce to use in place of salsa or ketchup.

Ingredients

1 T fresh red chili, minced
1/2 C rice vinegar
1 tsp salt
1 T sugar
1 tsp garlic, minced

Instructions

In a small pot, heat chili, vinegar, salt, sugar and garlic together over low heat. Stir constantly until mixture thickens to a syrupy consistency. Makes 5 servings.
