Forkinit: Classic Margarita

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

Syrup:
2/3 C sugar
2/3 C water

Alcohol:
1 1/2 C tequila
1 C orange liqueur (such as Cointreau)
1 C fresh lime juice

Glass Rim:
2 T fresh lime juice
2 T kosher salt

Other:
Ice cubes
Lime wedges, for garnish

Instructions

In a large jar with a tight-fitting lid, vigorously shake syrup ingredients until the sugar dissolves, about 1 minute. Pour the syrup into a pitcher.
Add drink ingredients to the pitcher; stir.
To rim the glass, put lime juice on one plate and salt on another plate. Dip the rims of 8 glasses into the lime juice, then into the salt.
Fill the glasses with ice, then add the margarita. Garnish with lime wedges.

Yields: 8-10 margaritas
