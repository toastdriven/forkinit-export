Forkinit: Tropical Rum Tonic

By Moriah Lindsley
The combination of rums adds spice notes and a coconut twist that intriguingly counterbalances the bitter and bracing flavor of tonic.

Ingredients

1 oz spiced or tropical flavored rum
1/2 oz coconut rum
3 oz chilled tonic water
Garnish: lime wedge and parasol, if desired

Instructions

Fill an Old-Fashioned glass with ice. Add rums. Top with tonic and stir gently. Garnish with lime and parasol, if desired.

Makes 1 cocktail
