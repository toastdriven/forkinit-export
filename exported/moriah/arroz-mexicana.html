Forkinit: Arroz Mexicana

By Moriah Lindsley
A light version of Mexican rice.

Ingredients

1 T vegetable oil
1 onion, chopped
2 cloves garlic, crushed
1/2 tsp dried oregano leaves, crushed
3/4 C uncooked rice
1 can (14 1/2 oz) Mexican style stewed tomatoes
1 green bell pepper, chopped

Instructions

1. In large skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add onion, garlic and oregano; cook and stir until onion is tender.
2. Stir in rice; cook until rice is golden, stirring frequently.
3. Drain tomatoes, reserving liquid; pour liquid into measuring cup. Add water to measure 1 1/2 C. Stir into rice mixture; bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat; cover and simmer 15 minutes or until rice is tender. Stir in tomatoes and bell pepper; cook 5 minutes.
5. Garnish with chopped parsley, if desired.
