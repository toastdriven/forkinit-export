Forkinit: Popcorn Cauliflower

By Moriah Lindsley

Ingredients

2 tsp salt
1 tsp paprika
1 tsp turmeric
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp onion powder
2 heads cauliflower, but into bite-sized pieces
Oil

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
Whisk salt, paprika, turmeric, onion powder, and garlic powder together in a bowl. Toss cauliflower with spice mixture. Add oil and toss again until coated.
Spread cauliflower on prepared baking sheet.
Roast in the preheated oven until cauliflower is tender, 30 to 35 minutes.
