Forkinit: Orange Cookies

By Mandy Stevens
my mom's orange cookies, really good

Ingredients

Cream together:
1 c. shortening
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
grated rind and juice of one orange
1 c. buttermilk (or 1 tsp. vinegar in 1 c. milk)

Sift together:
1 tsp. baking soda
3 Tbsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
4 c. + 2 Tbsp. flour

Icing:
1 1/2 c. powdered sugar
1 Tbsp. butter
2 tsp milk
1 tsp vanilla
rind & juice from an orange (whatever you didn't put in the cookies)

Instructions

Mix and let stand 15 minutes. Drop by spoonfuls on greased cookie sheet. Bake 12-15 minutes at 350 degrees. Ice while hot.
