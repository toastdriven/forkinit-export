Forkinit: Summer Salad for RTH recipe swap!

By Mandy Stevens
This is my go-to salad. I've not been able to eat a lot lately, so I'm making what food I have count. I eat some variation on this at least once a day.

Ingredients

organic baby spinach
half an organic tomato
a carrot
scallions
edamame, 5-8 pods (shelled)
1/4 cucumber
1/2 oz goat cheese

Instructions

Chop everything into bite size peices.
Toss everything in a bowl.
Use dressing to taste. I like Paul Newman's Organic Italian.
