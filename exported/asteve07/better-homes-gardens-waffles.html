Forkinit: Better Homes & Gardens Waffles

By Mandy Stevens
Good simple waffle recipe, makes about 4 irons worth (16 waffles) on small iron. Freezes beautifully, reheat in toaster.

Ingredients

1 3/4 c. sifted flour
3 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 beaten egg yolks
1 3/4 c. milk
1/2 c. vegetable oil
2 stiffly beaten egg whites

Instructions

Sift together dry ingredients.
Combine yolks, milk, and oil; stir into dry ingredients.
Fold in whites, leaving a few fluffs.
Bake.

Yields: 16 4-inch waffles or 3 10-inch waffles
Prep time: 5 minutes
Source: Better Homes & Gardens 1976
