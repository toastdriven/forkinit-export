Forkinit: Spiced Coffee

By Mandy Stevens
I like more than just plain coffee, and this gives me fancy flavored coffee at a fraction of the cost.

Ingredients

23 oz. can of coffee grounds--I use Folgers Simply Smooth
3 Tbsp. cinnamon
1 Tbsp. allspice
1 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
3 peppermint teabags

Instructions

Mix all spices into the coffee thoroughly. Prepare coffee as usual.

Yields: 1 can of coffee
Prep time: 5 minutes
