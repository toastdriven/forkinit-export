Forkinit: Bright Spring Soup

By Mandy Stevens
Something bright and cheery for a cool and cloudy early spring day.

Ingredients

1 small onion
3 cloves garlic
1 Tbsp. olive oil
1/2 small cabbage, chopped
1 stalk celery
1 can garbanzo beans, drained
1/2 parsnip, peeled & chopped
1/2 box veggie broth
parsley, sea salt, pepper, lemon juice to taste

Instructions

chop garlic and onion very finely (I use a food processor). Warm olive oil on medium, cook a little while. Add parsnip, cook a little while longer. Add in other ingredients slowly, including veggie broth. Cover and simmer, about 30 minutes or until veggies are tender. Serve with good bread.
