Forkinit: Cinnamon Lemon Cookies

By Mandy Stevens
"For tea time or dessert, homemade cookies are always a popular choice. These petite, crisp cookies are spiced with cinnamon and lemon peel."

Ingredients

1 c. sugar
1/2 c. butter
1 egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 1/2 c. all purpose flour
1 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
1 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. grated lemon peel (or a splash of lemon juice)
1/4 tsp. salt

Cinnamon sugar:
Stir together 1/4 c. sugar and 1 tsp. cinnamon

Instructions

Cream together sugar and butter. Beat in egg and vanilla. (Also add lemon juice if you are substituting that for lemon peel.)
Combine flour, cinnamon, baking powder, lemon peel and salt. Add to butter mixture. Blend well.
Cover and chill 2 hours until firm.
Shape dough into small balls, about 3/4" in diameter. Roll in cinnamon sugar to coat.
Set cookies 1" apart on lightly greased cookie sheets.
Bake at 350 degrees for 10 min. or until edges are lightly browned.
Cool slightly on pans, then remove to racks to cool completely.

Yields: 6 dozen cookies
Prep time: Prep time 20 minutes; chill time 2 hours; bake time 10 minutes.
