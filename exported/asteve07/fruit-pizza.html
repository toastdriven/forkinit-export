Forkinit: Fruit Pizza

By Mandy Stevens
A good breakfast/lunch option. Yum.

Ingredients

whole grain english muffin, toasted
peanut butter or almond butter
cut up fruit::
strawberries, peaches, apples, whatever's in season
honey

Instructions

Toast English muffin.
Spread with peanut/almond butter.
Top with fruit.
Drizzle with honey.

Yields: 1
Prep time: 5 minutes, plus chopping fruit
