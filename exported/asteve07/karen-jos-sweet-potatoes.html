Forkinit: Karen Jo's Sweet Potatoes

By Mandy Stevens

Ingredients

Simmer together for syrup (all spices to taste):
1 stick butter
brown sugar
maple syrup
cinnamon
nutmeg
some water

Instructions

Use small sweet potatoes. Boil in skins until fork tender. Run under cold water. Pull off skins. Slice longways. Lay sliced potatoes in 13x9 inch pan and add syrup. Bake in 350 degree oven about 20 minutes. Add marshmellows last few minutes, watch as they brown.

Source: Karen Jo White
