Forkinit: Potato Soup

By Mandy Stevens
Warm, filling, relatively inexpensive

Ingredients

4-6 potatoes, cut up in 1/2" chunks
half a bag of frozen carrots, or 3-4 carrots cut up, 1/2" chunks

2 c. chicken stock
1 1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. cream
1/2 onion
salt & parsley to taste

1 smoked sausage

Instructions

Boil potatoes & carrots until mostly tender, drain. In a large stock pot, simmer stock, milk, cream, onion & parsley. Add potatoes & carrots. Cut up a smoked sausage, add to soup. Simmer until onions are cooked, sausage is warm and potatoes & carrots are fully tender, about 10 minutes.

Yields: about 8-10
Prep time: about 30 minutes
Source: Mandy Stevens
