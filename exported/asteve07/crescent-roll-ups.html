Forkinit: Crescent roll-ups

By Mandy Stevens

Ingredients

1 can crescent rolls
1 bonelss skinless chicken breast
1 can cheddar cheese soup
1 can low sodium cream of chicken soup
1 c. sour cream
2 c. shredded cheese

Instructions

Bake chicken breasts until done. Chop chicken. Roll crescent rolls with 1/4 c. chopped chicken per roll. Mix soups and sour cream, pour over crescent rolls. Top with shredded cheese. Bake at 350 degrees, approx. 20 minutes.
