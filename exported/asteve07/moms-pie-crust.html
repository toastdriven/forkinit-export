Forkinit: Mom's Pie Crust

By Mandy Stevens
Pie crust recipe handed down from my Pennsylvania Dutch great-grandmother. Ingredients are public, but the method is secret. However, if you follow as written, you'll still end up with a good final product even without knowing the "how."

Ingredients

Dry:
2 c. flour
1 c. shortening
1/2 tsp. salt

Wet:
1 egg
6 Tbsp. water
1 Tbsp. vinegar

Instructions

Cut dry ingredients together until well blended. Mix wet ingredients together and add to dry. Seperate in two, roll out bottom, line greased pie plate. Prick with a fork to ensure no bubbling and brush edges with water so top and bottom do not seperate. Fill, place rolled out top on the top. Cut vent holes in the top. Bake until done (brown), about 13 minutes (unless otherwise directed by filling).

Yields: 1 pie
