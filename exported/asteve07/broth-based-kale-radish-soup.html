Forkinit: Broth-based Kale & Radish Soup

By Mandy Stevens
A nice warm soup for when the weather turns. Made up from what happened to be in my fridge. Pretty inexpensive to make.

Ingredients

1 box of reduced sodium chicken broth
2 oz white cooking wine
1 oz malt vinegar
1/2 tsp fresh ground pepper
1/2 tsp kosher salt
2-3 leaves of kale, shredded
2-3 radishes, quartered & chopped
1/2 can of garbanzo beans/chickpeas
1/2 onion, diced
1 potato, chopped into 1/4" chunks
Optional: half a smoked turkey sausage, quartered & chopped

Instructions

Bring all ingredients to a boil, reduce heat and simmer until fully cooked, 45 min-1 hr. Enjoy.

Yields: 4 servings, roughly 1 1/2 c. each
Prep time: 15 mintues prep, 1 hr cook
