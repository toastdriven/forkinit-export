Forkinit: Breakfast Casserole

By Mandy Stevens
Yummy on a cold winter morning. Can be prepped the night before.

Ingredients

4 eggs
1 c. Bisquik
1 c. milk
1 c. cheese
4 precooked sausage patties, thawed & cut into small pieces
1/4 c. bell pepper (I used red but whatever)
1/4 c. spinach

Instructions

1. Grease small baking dish (about 8x10 or equivalent). Preheat oven to 350*
2. Beat 4 eggs in small mixing bowl.
3. In larger bowl, combine milk & Bisquik til smooth. Add eggs, beat together until smooth.
4. Add in veggies, cheese, sausage one at a time, beat until well mixed.
5. Pour into baking dish. Bake for 35-40 minutes until fork comes out clean.

Yields: 3-4 servings
Prep time: 5 minutes prep, 40 minutes baking
