Forkinit: Feel Better Soup

By Mandy Stevens
A chicken soup devised during the Great Family Plague of 2014. Prep designed for minimal effort.

Ingredients

1 boneless skinless chicken breast
1 box (4 c.) chicken broth

A few shakes each of:
Poultry seasoning
Black pepper
parsley flakes
Salt

1/3-1/2 c. Long grain rice

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in small (1 qt) crockpot. Cook on high 3 hrs. Shred chicken & return to crock; mix well & serve.

Yields: 4 c
Prep time: 5 min prep; 3 hrs cooking
