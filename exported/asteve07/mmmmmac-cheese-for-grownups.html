Forkinit: Mmmmmac & Cheese (for grownups!)

By Mandy Stevens

Ingredients

2 c rotini pasta
1 jar alfredo sauce
1 roma tomato, chopped
small bunch of fresh basil, chopped
1 small onion, finely chopped (food processor)
2 cloves garlic, finely chopped (food processor)
2 Tbsp olive oil
Small amount of shredded parmesan
small amount of shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

sautee onion & garlic in olive oil over medium-high heat, turn heat off. Add in the chopped mushroom, let it cook over remaining heat.

In the meantime, cook rotini pasta according to package directions.

Drain pasta, mix in all ingredients but cheeses. Put in small baking dish, bake at 375* for 15 minutes. Add cheeses to top, bake until melts, about 3-5 minutes. Yum....
