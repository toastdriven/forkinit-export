Forkinit: Buttermilk Bread for bread machine

By Mandy Stevens
1 lb loaf mixed in bread machine

Ingredients

7/8 c. buttermilk, or 3 Tbsp. dry buttermilk powder & 7/8 c. water
2 c. bread flour
1 tsp. salt
1 Tbsp. butter or margarine
2 Tbsp. honey
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1 1/2 tsp. yeast

Instructions

Put ingredients in bread pan, select light crust, press start.

Yields: 1 loaf
Source: bread machine recipe book
