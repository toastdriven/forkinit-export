Forkinit: Sweet Potato Fries

By Mandy Stevens
Simple, tasty "fries" full of beta-carotene, which prevents infections, fights off disease and makes cuts heal faster.

Ingredients

2 large sweet potatoes
1 egg white
1/2 tsp. salt

Instructions

Heat oven to 450 degrees F. Coat baking sheet with vegetable cooking spray. Toss together potatoes, egg white, and salt. Spread potatoes in one layer on sheet, and bake 30 minutes without turning.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 10 minutes; cook 30 minutes
