Forkinit: Snickerdoodle Cookies

By Mandy Stevens
My Aunt Marcia's recipe, that I've been making since age 12 or so.

Ingredients

1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. shortening
1 1/2 c. sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
2 2/3 c. flour
2 tsp. cream of tartar (no substitution available)
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt

For topping:
2 Tbsp. sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon


Instructions

Beat butter & shortening together until light.
Add sugar & beat until fluffy.
Beat in eggs & vanilla.
Sift together: flour, cream of tartar, baking soda & salt.
Slowly stir dry ingredients into wet.

For Topping:
Combine 2 T. sugar & cinnamon.

Shape dough into 1" balls.
Roll in sugar/cinnamon mixture.
Place 2" apart on ungreased baking sheet.
Bake at 400* for 8-10 min.

Yields: 6 dozen
Prep time: ~20 min prep, 10 min bake (each batch)
Source: Marcia Whitehead
