Forkinit: Chicken Pot Pasta

By Mandy Stevens
For nights when I'm too tired to bother making pie crust. Omit pasta and put in pie crust if you're feeling up to it.

Ingredients

2 chicken breasts
2 cups whole grain pasta, such as rotini or penne or even wagon wheels (more or less according to taste)
1/2 bag frozen peas and carrots, or frozen vegetables of your choice
2 cans condensed cream of whatever soup--I use cream of chicken but have had good results with cream of celery--DO NOT reconstitute.

Instructions

Boil water for pasta. Add pasta to water, let that cook as directed while cooking the rest. Cut up chicken into 1 inch (or less) cubes and cook through in a skillet. When chicken is cooked through, drain any liquid and add frozen vegetables and soup. Heat everything through. Drain pasta, add to rest and enjoy.

Yields: variable according to amount of ingredients
Prep time: 20 min
