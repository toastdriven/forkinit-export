Forkinit: Chicken and Rice

By Mandy Stevens
Wheat-free one-dish dinner

Ingredients

Combine in a baking dish:
1 can cream of whatever soup
1 c. water

Add:
3/4 c. rice
sprinkling of poultry seasoning
1/2 bag of frozen veggies
sprinkling of black pepper

On top:
2 chicken breast cutlets
sprinkling of poultry seasoning
sprinkling of black pepper

Instructions

Cover with foil. Bake 375* for 45-50 minutes until chicken is done and rice is tender. Let stand for 10 minutes. Put chicken on plate, stir rice mixture and serve alongside.

Yields: 2-4
Prep time: 5 min prep, 45 min bake, 10 min to stand--1 hr total
