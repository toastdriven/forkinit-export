Forkinit: Gazpacho

By Mandy Stevens
Cold Veggie Soup for summer eating!

Ingredients

1c tomato, chopped
1/2 c cucumbers, chopped
1/2/ c green onion, finely chopped
1/4 c Parsley, snipped ( I usually just dash some dried in instead of fresh)
1T soy sauce
1/2 t celery seed
2T lemon juice
1/4t garlic powder
1 zucchini, thinly sliced
3 c tomato juice

Instructions

Combine and chill for 4 hours, serve.
