Forkinit: Bean Dip

By Mandy Stevens
good enough to eat....

Ingredients

2 small snack size bags chili cheese fritos
1 can vegetarian refried beans
2-3 oz. reduced fat sour cream
1 small tomato
2-3 oz. shredded cheese

Instructions

Crush fritos on the bottom of a small baking dish (or anything else with a lid).
Mix beans and sour cream, spread on top of fritos.
