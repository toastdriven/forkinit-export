We made this after having something similar at a local fusion restaurant. We were hooked, quite possibly for life. The recipe makes more spice mix than you'll need. This is very intentional.

Ingredients

4 ears Corn
2 Tbsp Parmesan *or* crumbled queso fresco

Wet ingredients:
1/2 c mayo
2 tsp lime juice

Spice mix:
1 Tbsp sugar
1/2 Tbsp chili powder
1/2 Tbsp garlic powder
3/4 tsp ground red pepper
3/4 tsp cumin
1/4 tsp fresh ground black pepper
1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

Mix spice mix and store covered in a cool, dry place. Mix wet ingredients in a separate bowl. Put this in the fridge until showtime.

Shuck, clean, and grill your corn. When it is cool enough to handle, stick something in one end as a handle - a chopstick, two toothpicks, or one of those corn handle things. Paint the wet ingredients on with a pastry brush, then roll in a mix of the cheese and 2 tsp spice mix. Leave the handle in, and serve.

Next time, we may add some cornmeal for texture.

Yields: 8
Prep time: 15 minutes
