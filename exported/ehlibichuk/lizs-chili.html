Forkinit: Liz's Chili

By Tastyyarns
There are a couple chilis in this house, and this one's the one I made. Mister and I crave it sometimes, his sometimes, and white chili still other times. Mine lacks the raw peppers and wine, instead using hot sausage and chipotles.

Ingredients

1 lb pork sausage (I like the hot stuff)
1 lb turkey sausage
1/3 can tomato paste (or less)
3 cloves garlic, minced
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 tablespoon adobo sauce
1 chipotle
1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
1 tablespoon white sugar
2 squares dark chocolate
Chili powder, to taste
Salt and pepper, to taste
Onion powder, to taste

Instructions

Easy-but method:
My crock pot reaches internal temps of 200+ when on high, so I dump everything in and cook until the entire chili measures 165 for safety (and a little more).

Safe-for-everyone method:
Cook the sausage and turkey (preferably in a dry teflon skillet). Drain. Add to crock pot. Add in all other ingredients, set on low and leave 8 hours

Fast method:
Cook the sausage and turkey. Drain. Put in pot with all other ingredients, bring to a boil. Cook 20 minutes.

Feel free to adjust vinegar and chipotle measurements to your liking, but if you leave out the chocolate it's not my chili any more. Or his chili, for that matter. You can make it less if you absolutely must.

Yields: 4-8
Prep time: 10-30 minutes
