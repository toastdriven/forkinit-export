Forkinit: Lasagna Soup

By Tastyyarns
It's been ages since I got this recipe, and I have no idea where. It doesn't matter - it's been heavily altered! Also, it's not soup. It's not even a little bit soup. But, that was the recipe name and it stuck, partly as a joke.

Ingredients

1 lb penne noodles
chicken stock - enough to cover the noodles in the pan
Marinara of some kind - 1 jar or can of your favorite sauce.
1 lb ground turkey sausage
1/2 c parmesan
1/2 c ricotta

Instructions

Make or acquire your marinara.
Cook turkey sausage. Add marinara.
Boil the noodles in chicken stock about half the listed time, add marinara. Cook twice the remaining amount of time, or until noodles are al dente. ((if your package says cook 8 minutes, cook the noodles 4 minutes in chicken stock and ~8 minutes in marinara-chicken stock mixture.))

Add the cheeses, stir until mixed, serve.

Yields: 3-4
Prep time: 20 minutes
