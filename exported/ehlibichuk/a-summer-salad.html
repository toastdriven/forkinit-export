Forkinit: A Summer Salad

By Tastyyarns
This is on my list of things to eat out of the serving bowl with a large spoon. It is very bad for you. It is also really easy and kind of obvious.

Ingredients

1 lb small shells
1 bag frozen peas, carrots, and corn
1/4 lb velveeta, cubed. VERY OPTIONAL (my mil does this and I don't like it at all. But it is the same recipe otherwise)
1/2 c Miracle Whip (YES WITH THE ZIP)
1/2 pack ranch dip powder
sugar, to taste
vinegar, to taste

Instructions

Obviously doubling the recipe is fabulous. :) Cook noodles. Microwave vegetables. Stir in remaining ingredients, refrigerate at least 1 hour. Eat.

PS: Vinegar and sugar quantities should be equivalent, no matter how much of them you add. Start with a teaspoon each.

Yields: Me. Or 8, with all the other picnic stuff.
Prep time: 10 minutes
Source: I reverse engineered my grandmother's recipe, though I suspect she actually used suddenly salad.
