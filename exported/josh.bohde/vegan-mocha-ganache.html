Forkinit: Vegan Mocha Ganache

By Josh Bohde

Ingredients

2 cups dark chocolate chips
½ cup soy milk
3 tablespoons cane sugar (preferably Rapadura)
Pinch of salt
1 teaspoon vanilla
¼ cup espresso or very strong coffee

Instructions

Melt chocolate chips in a double boiler. (If you don’t have a double boiler, put the chips in a small heatproof dish, set the dish inside a pot, and add enough water to the pot to reach halfway up the sides of the dish.) Warm slowly until chips are melted, being careful not to burn. Whisk in remaining ingredients. Remove from heat and allow ganache to cool slightly before pouring over cake.

Yields: 2½ cups
