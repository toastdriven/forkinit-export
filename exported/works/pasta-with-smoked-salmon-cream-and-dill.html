Forkinit: Pasta with Smoked Salmon, Cream and Dill

By Joshua Works
This dish is easy to prepare and absolutely delicious.

Ingredients

16 oz penne
3 tbsp butter
6 oz smoked salmon, chopped
1 cup whipping cream
1 tbsp tomato paste
3 teaspoons fresh dill, chopped
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper

Instructions

Cook pasta til al dente.

Melt butter in a heavy skillet. Add salmon and cook gently over medium heat. Stir in cream and tomato paste. Cook until heated through. Stir in dill and cayenne pepper. Mix sauce into pasta and serve in shallow bowls with additional dill.

Yields: 4 servings
