Forkinit: Buttermilk Flapjacks

By Joshua Works

Ingredients

2 cups flour
2 tbsp sugar
4 tsp baking powder
1 tsp baking soda
1 tsp fine salt
2 cups buttermilk
4 tbsp melted butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
2 beaten eggs

Instructions

Combine the dry ingredients in a bowl, whisk.
Combine the wet ingredients in a second bowl, whisk.
Add the wet ingredients to the dry and whisk until just combined. Fry in a pan with butter.
Top with maple syrup and devour.
