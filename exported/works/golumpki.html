Forkinit: Golumpki

By Joshua Works

Ingredients

Cabbage rolls:
2 lbs ground chuck
1/2 lb. ground sausage
1 1/2 cups rice, cooked
2 medium onions, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
3/4 pkg. Lipton Onion Soup Mix
1 beef bouillon cube, crushed
1/2 cup water
2 cabbage heads
Pepper, parsley and basil to taste

Sauce:
1 large can tomato soup
1/2 cup water
2 or 3 cups spaghetti sauce
Remainder of onion soup mix

Instructions

Cut out the hard core of the cabbage and immerse in water in a deep pot. Boil cabbage until leaves start coming apart. Remove leaves carefully and place in a large colander to drain and cool. When cool, cut off the large vein on each leaf.

Brown the sausage only.

Sauté onions and garlic in butter or margarine.

Combine ground chuck, sausage and rice. Add the onions, garlic, bouillon, onion soup, pepper, and 1/2 cup water. Mix well.

Spoon the mixture into a leaf. Season with salt & pepper before rolling up cabbage. Place seam down in a slightly-greased glass pan.

Combine sauce ingredients in a sauce pan and heat through.

Pour sauce over stuffed cabbage. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, parsley and basil. Cover in aluminum foil and bake in 350° oven for about 90 minutes.

Yields: About 25 cabbage rolls
