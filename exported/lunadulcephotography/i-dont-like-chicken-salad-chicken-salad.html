Forkinit: I don't like chicken salad chicken salad

By Jennifer Buehrer
A chicken salad without mayo

Ingredients

Shredded cooked chicken
Sliced fresh nectarines (you can use apples or peaches)
Thinly sliced red onion
Sliced raw almonds
Sliced celery
Curry (just to taste)
Salt (optional)
olive oil
Fruity Balsamic vinegar (I use Cherry vinegar http://www.fustinis.com but you could use original)
Honey
Lemon juice

Instructions

Stir Chicken, fruit, almonds, celery, curry, and salt in a bowl. It looks like I usually do one cup of chicken, one cup of nectarines, 1/4 cup of almonds, 1/4 cup of red onion, and a 1/2 cup of celery.

Dressing:
Make a dressing using olive oil, vinegar, honey, and lemon juice. Be careful not to use too much honey... it could get too sweet. Use just enough to keep it all together.
I use a salad dressing container to make a bunch. I use the remaining amount on other salads.

Coat chicken mix with dressing. Pile on a piece of Croissant and enjoy!
