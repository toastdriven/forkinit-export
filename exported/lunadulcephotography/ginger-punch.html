Forkinit: Ginger Punch

By Jennifer Buehrer
I look forward to this drink and a hammock every summer.

Ingredients

* 2 cups water
* 1/2 cup sugar
* 2 (1 inch) pieces crystallized ginger
* 4 cinnamon sticks
*
* 3 cups orange juice
* 1 cup pineapple juice
* 1/2 cup lemon juice
* 1 liter lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage

Instructions


1. In a sauce pan, combine water, sugar, crystallized ginger and cinnamon sticks. Bring to a boil and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
2. Pour syrup into a pitcher. Add orange, pineapple and lemon juice. Chill in refrigerator. When ready to serve, pour into a punch bowl and slowly add the lemon-lime soda.

*****I found it tastes best if you let it sit for a day or two.
