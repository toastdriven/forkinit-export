Forkinit: CHICKEN MUSHROOM SOUP

By Alex Cortelyou
A creamy mushroom soup with korean-spiced chicken

Ingredients

2 tbsp butter
2 tbsp olive oil
6 clove garlic (diced)
1 tbsp coarse salt
3 tbsp gochugaru (korean chili flakes)
1 tbsp smoked paprika (or regular)
1 tbsp ground black pepper
2 lb chicken thighs (cubed)
1 1/4 lb yellow onions (chopped)
1 1/4 lb fresh mushrooms (sliced)
2 cup chicken broth
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp fish sauce
1 tbsp dried dill weed
1 cup heavy cream
1 tbsp lemon juice
1/2 cup sour cream
fresh parsley (chopped)

Instructions

melt butter in olive oil over medium heat in deep saute pan
fry garlic, salt and spices until fragrant (about 3 minutes)
stir chicken into spice mixture and saute over medium-high for 10 minutes
set cooked chicken aside
add onion and saute for 10 minutes
add mushroom and saute for 10 minutes
stir in chicken broth, soy sauce, fish sauce and dill weed
bring back to temperature then reduce to medium-low, cover and simmer for 15 minutes
stir in heavy cream
bring back to temperature then reduce to medium-low, cover and simmer for 10 more minutes
stir in sour cream and lemon juice
heat through (about 3 minutes)
garnish with fresh parsley

Yields: 4
Prep time: 1 hour
