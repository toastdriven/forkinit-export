Forkinit: Korean Sticky Chicken

By Alex Cortelyou
Quick and delicious korean sticky sesame chicken

Ingredients

2 pound chicken thighs, sliced into large chunks
1/3 cup gochugaru
4 tbsp vegetable oil
4 cloves garlic, minced
1 tbsp ginger, minced
1/3 cup honey
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/4 cup rice vinegar
1 tbsp sesame seeds
2 green onions, finely chopped

Instructions

1. coat chicken with gochugaru, tenderize and/or let marinate
2. in a large pan over medium high heat, add oil and cook chicken for four minutes on each side
3. set chicken aside
4. in same pan over medium heat, cook garlic and ginger for one minute
5. add honey, soy sauce, and rice vinegar, bring to a boil and cook for 2-3 minutes until it thickened slightly
6. return chicken to the pan and cook until chicken is coated with the sauce on all sides
7. sprinkle sesame seeds, stir one last time to coat evenly and turn heat off
8. serve over rice and top with chopped green onions

Yields: 2-3 servings
Prep time: 15 minutes
