Forkinit: Blueberry Coffeecake

By Carol Parcell

Ingredients

1/2 cup flour
1/4 cup sugar
1/2 t. ground cinnamon
2 T. unsalted butter

For the cake:
1 egg
1 cup milk
1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
2 cups flour
1/2 cup sugar
2 1/2 t. baking powder
1/2 t. salt
1/2 t. ground mace
1 cup fresh or frozen blueberries

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350. Butter a 9 inch cake pan. To make topping, in small bowl, mix flour, sugar and cinnamon. Blend in butter, using your fingertips or a pastry blender until mixture is crumbly. Set aside. To make the cake, in large bowl, beat together egg, milk and butter. Sift flour, sugar, baking powder, salt and mace into a small bowl. Add to egg mixture. Beat until just blended. Gently stir in the blueberries. Spread batter evenly in prepared pan and sprinkle with the topping. Bake until a wood toothpick inserted in the center of the cake comes out clean, 40 to 45 minutes. Serve warm from the pan.

NOTE: Ground nutmeg may be substituted for the ground mace.

Yields: 6-8
