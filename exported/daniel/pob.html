A fruity drink

Ingredients

1 cup pineapple-orange-banana juice
1 oz dark rum (suggested Mount Gay dark)
1 oz light rum
Lime juice

Instructions

Mix all the ingredients to taste in a shaker of ice. Serve with umbrella.

Yields: 2
