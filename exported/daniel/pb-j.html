Forkinit: PB & J

By Daniel Lindsley
The classic!

Ingredients

2 pieces of bread
A jar of jelly
A jar of peanut butter

Instructions

1. Spread jelly on one side of one piece of bread.
2. Spread peanut butter on one side of the other piece of bread.
3. Put the two pieces of bread together, jelly-side facing peanut butter-side.

Yields: 1
