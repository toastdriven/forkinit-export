Forkinit: Vegan Pizza Burgers

By Daniel Lindsley
Easy, fast & a family favorite.

Ingredients

1 C TVP (Textured Vegetable Protein)
1 C water
16 oz can of pizza sauce
6 hamburger buns
12 slices of vegan cheese

Instructions

Boil the water.
Rehydrate the TVP with the water and let sit for 5 minutes.
Mix in the pizza sauce with the TVP.
Split all buns into halves.
Place a slice of cheese on each bun half.
Apply a liberal dose of mix on top of each bun half.
Heat pairs in the microwave for 20 seconds each.

Yields: 4
Prep time: 10 minutes
