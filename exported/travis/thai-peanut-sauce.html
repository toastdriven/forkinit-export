Forkinit: Thai peanut sauce

By Travis Cline

Ingredients

1-2 tbs Red Curry
1/2 cup peanut butter
1 tbs lime juice
1 tbs sugar
4-5 shakes of fish sauce
salt or soy sauce to taste

Instructions

Mix all of these then add warm water in small amounts until desired consistency is reached.
