Forkinit: Thousand Island Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven-cooked

Ingredients

1 whole chicken, cut up
1 8 oz. bottle thousand island dressing
1/2 jar apricot jam
1 pkg dry onion soup mix

Instructions

Mix ingredients together well & pour over chicken in baking dish. Bake @ 350 degrees 45-50 min.
