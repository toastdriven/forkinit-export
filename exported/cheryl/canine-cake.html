Forkinit: Canine Cake

By Cheryl Schnettler
Doggy birthday cake

Ingredients

1/2 c. all-purpose flour
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 c. shredded carrots
2 Tbs. peanut butter
2 Tbs. canola or vegetable oil
1/4 c. unsweetened applesauce

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Lightly grease 2 small ramekin or any 8 oz. oven-safe bowl. Combine flour & baking soda in bowl. Stir in carrots. Add remaining wet ingredients & mix well. Batter will be very thick, similar to cookie dough. Drop batter into prepared dishes & bake 25-35 min or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool completely.

Yields: 2 8 oz. cakes
Prep time: 15 min.
