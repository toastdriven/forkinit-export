Forkinit: Strawberry & Rhubarb Crumble

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 c. sliced fresh strawberries
1/2 c. sliced rhubarb
1/4 c. sugar, divided
1/3 c. flour
1/8 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
2 Tbs. unsalted butter, cut in small pieces
2 Tbs. nuts, chopped

Instructions

Place strawberries, rhubarb & 1 Tbs. sugar in 1 qt. baking dish. Stir & set aside for 10 min.
Combine remaining 3 Tb. sugar, flour, salt & cinnamon in bowl. cut in butter until mixture is crumbly.
Spoon flour topping over fruit. Sprinkle with nuts.
Bake @ 350 degrees for 30 min. or until crumb topping is golden brown & filling is bubbling slightly.

Yields: 2 servings
