Forkinit: Linguine with Basil Pesto

By Cheryl Schnettler
stove

Ingredients

3 garlic cloves
3/4 c. packed fresh basil
1/2 c. packed fresh parsley
1/2 extra virgin olive oil
1/3 c. finely grated Parmesan or Romano
1/4 c. chopped walnuts
1 Tbs. fresh lime juice
1/2 tsp. salt
12 oz. dried linguine, cooked & drained

Instructions

Place garlic in food processor, pulse to chop. Add next 7 ingredients; process until a paste forms. Toss with warm pasta.

Yields: 6 servings
