Forkinit: Easy Bistro Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients


2 tsp. oil
3 cups sliced fresh mushrooms
1 onion, chopped
1 can (15 oz.) stewed tomatoes, undrained
1/4 cup Italian Dressing
3 Tbsp. tomato paste
4 small boneless skinless chicken breast halves (1 lb.)
1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
2 slices cooked bacon, crumbled

Instructions

HEAT oil in large nonstick skillet on medium-high heat. Add mushrooms and onions; cook 5 min., stirring occasionally. Stir in tomatoes, dressing and tomato paste.

ADD chicken; cover. Simmer on medium-low heat 12 min. or until chicken is done (165Â°F).

TOP with cheese and bacon; simmer, uncovered, 5 min. or until cheese is melted.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
