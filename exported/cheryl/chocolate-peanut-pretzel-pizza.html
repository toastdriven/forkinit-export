Forkinit: Chocolate Peanut Pretzel Pizza

By Cheryl Schnettler
Refrigerator

Ingredients

1 8" Italian-style prepared pizza crust
1/4 c. graham cracker crumbs
1/4 c. powdered sugar
1/4 c. peanut butter
2 Tbs. butter, melted
1 c. semisweet chocolate chips, melted & divided
1/2 c. coarsely broken pretzels
1/4 c. dry roasted nuts

Instructions

Place pizza crust in9" pie plate.
In medium bowl, combine cracker crumbs, sugar, peanut butter & melted butter; mix until stiff then spread evenly over crust. Reserve 1 Tbs. melted chocolate, then spread remaining chocolate evenly over the peanut butter mixture. Sprinkle pretzels & nuts over top. Drizzle reserved melted chocolate over the top & chill for 30 min. or until firm. Cut into wedges to serve.
