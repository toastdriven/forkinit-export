Forkinit: Oatmeal Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1 c. butter
2 c. brown sugar, packed
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. vanilla
2 lge eggs
3 c. flour
1 tsp. cinnamon (I prefer 1 TB.)
4-41/2 c. old-fashioned or quick-cooking oats
11/2 c. chocolate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Melt the butter. Add brown sugar, baking soda, vanilla & eggs, beat until very creamy (2-3 min.) Add flour & cinnamon gradually, mix well. Add oatmeal & beat until combined & very thick. Fold in choc. chips. Make lge cookies with about 2 TB. dough & place on ungreased cokkie sheets. Bake 15-20 min. until they are as brown as you like them. Cool on pans 1 min., then remove to wire racks to cool. Store in sealed containers.

Yields: 2-3 doz.
Prep time: 15 min.
