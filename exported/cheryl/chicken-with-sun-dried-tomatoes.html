Forkinit: Chicken With Sun-Dried Tomatoes

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

3 Tbs. olive oil
4 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
1 shallot, finely chopped
1 can condensed cream of mushroom soup
3/4 c. water
1/4 c. thinly sliced sun-dried tomatoes
1 Tbs. red wine vinegar
2 Tbs. chopped fresh basil
4 c. cooked extra wide egg noodles
1/4 c. shredded Parmesan cheese

Instructions

Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat. Add chicken & cook 10 min. or until well browned on both sides; remove.
Add remaining oil to skillet; add shallot & cook 2 min. Stir in soup, water, tomatoes, vinegar & basil.
Return chicken to skillet & heat to boil. Reduce heat to low. Cook 5 min. or until chicken is cooked through. Serve over noodles. Sprinkle with cheese & added fresh basil.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
