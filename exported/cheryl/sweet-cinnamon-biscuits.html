Forkinit: Sweet Cinnamon Biscuits

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 c. all purpose flour
1 Tbs. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking soda
1/4 c. vegetable oil
3/4 c. buttermilk
1/2 c. butter, softened
3/4 c. sugar
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. cardamon

Instructions

Combine 1st 4 ingredients in bowl & mix well. Stir in oil. Add buttermilk & stir until just blended.
Knead dough on lightly floured surface until smooth. Roll into 15X8 rectangle.
Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Grease a 9 in. round baking pan lightly.
Spread butter over the dough. Combine sugar, cinnamon & cardamon & mix well. Sprinkle over butter. Roll up rectangle, jelly roll fashion, starting from one long side. Pinch seam to seal.
Cut roll into 1&1/2 in. slices. Arrange slices cut side up, in prepared pan. Bake until lightly browned, about 15-20 min. Serve hot.
