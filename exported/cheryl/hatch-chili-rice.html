Forkinit: Hatch Chili Rice

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 Hatch chili peppers
1 Tb. butter
1 medium onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
3 c. cooked long-grain white rice
1 c. light sour cream
1 c. low-fat small curd cottage cheese
2 tsp. minced fresh Jalapeno peppers
1/2 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper
8 oz. shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided

Instructions

Roast, steam & peel Hatch chilies. (To roast under broiler: arrange in single layer on foil-lined baking sheet. Broil 4-5 in. from heat for 4-5 min, turning frequently, until skins are blistered on all sides. To grill peppers, lay them directly on grill rack & roast 4-5 min, turning frequently.) Seed & chop peppers; set aside. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Melt butter in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion & garlic & cook until onion is soft & translucent, about 6 min. Remove from heat. Stir in chilies, cooked rice,sour cream, cottage cheese, Jalapeno, salt & pepper. Spread half rice mixture in greased 12X8 baking dish. Sprinkle with half of cheese. Top with remaining rice mixture & cheese. Bake 30-35 min. until golden.

Yields: 10 servings
