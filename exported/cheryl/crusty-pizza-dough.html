Forkinit: Crusty Pizza Dough

By Cheryl Schnettler
Breadmaker

Ingredients

For one 16 inch pizza:
1 c. plus 1&1/2 tsp. warm water
1&1/2 Tbs. olive oil
1/2 tsp. salt
3 c. all-purpose flour
1&1/2 tsp. dry yeast

Instructions

Put all ingredients in breadmaker as listed above. After dough is done, roll into ball & place in lightly oiled bowl. Cover with plastic wrap & refrigerate for 30 min. Using pizza dough roller or hands, spread dough in pizza pan.
