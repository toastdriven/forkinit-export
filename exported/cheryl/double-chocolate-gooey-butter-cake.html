Forkinit: Double Chocolate Gooey Butter Cake

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 c. (2 sticks) butter
1 pkg chocolate cake mix
3 lg eggs
1 8 oz. pkg cream cheese
3-4 Tbs. cocoa powder
1 lb. powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts

Instructions

Heat oven to 350 degrees & place rack in center of oven. Lightly butter a 13X9 in. baking dish. In a lg bowl, stir together cake mix, 1 egg & 1/2 c. butter until well combined; pat into prepared pan.
In a large mixer bowl, beat cream cheese on medium speed until smooth. Beat in remaining 2 eggs & cocoa powder until combined well. Reduce speed to low & add sugar; mix well. Gradually beat in remaining 1/2 c. butter & extract, beating until smooth. Fold in nuts & spread over cake mixture in pan.
Bake until set around edges but center is a little gooey, 40-50 min (don't overbake). Cool slightly before serving.

Yields: 20 servings
Prep time: 20 min.
