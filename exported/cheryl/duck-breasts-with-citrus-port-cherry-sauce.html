Forkinit: Duck Breasts with Citrus Port Cherry Sauce

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

4 duck breasts, boneless
Salt & pepper
2 small shallots, minced
1/4 c. ruby port wine
1/3 c. fresh-squeezed orange juice, plus 1 Tbs. orange zest
1/2 c. pitted frozen black cherries, thawed & roughly chopped

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Score the skin of the duck breasts with knife in 2 directions, making a crisscross. Season both sides of breasts.
Place duck in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, skin side down. Sear until skin is golden brown, about 10 min. Flip & sear other side for 3 min. Place breasts in baking dish skin side up & bake for 12 min. Remove from oven & allow to rest for 5 min.
Pour off excess fat from skillet, add shallots & saute over medium heat until translucent. Pour in wine & orange juice & scrape up any brown bits on bottom of pan. Add in orange zest & cherries & bring to boil. Simmer for 5 min. to reduce & thicken, mashing cherries to extract flavor.
Slice duck in 1/4 in. slices on diagonal & pour sauce over.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 40 min.
