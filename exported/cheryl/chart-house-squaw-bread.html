Forkinit: Chart House Squaw Bread

By Cheryl Schnettler
from famous Chart House

Ingredients

1 c. white flour
2 c. whole wheat flour
1/2 c. sugar
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. baking soda
1&1/2 c. milk
1/2 c. molasses

Optional spread:
2 sticks butter
1/2 c. honey

Instructions

Sift dry ingredients together. Combine liquids. Blend all together with electric mixer until well blended. Bake @ 325 degrees in loaf pan for 1&1/2 hrs. Best served warm.

Yields: 1 loaf
Prep time: 15 min.
