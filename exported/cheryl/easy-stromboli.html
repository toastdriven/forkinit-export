Forkinit: Easy Stromboli

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 can Pillsbury Pizza Crust
1/4 c. pizza sauce
1/2 lb. thinly sliced ham
1 c. shredded mozzarella
1/4 c. sliced olives
1/2 tsp. Italian seasoning
Fresh basil, chopped

Instructions

Unroll dough; place on greased cookie sheet. Press dough into 12X8 in. rectangle. Spread sauce over to within 2 in. of long sides & 1/2 in. of short sides.
Top with ham, cheese, olives & seasonings. Fold long sides of dough over filling; pinch to seal. Pinch short sides to seal.
Bake @ 400 degrees for 15-20 min. or until golden brown.

Yields: 6 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
