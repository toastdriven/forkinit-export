Forkinit: Cheesy Black Bean Quesadilla

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 Morningstar Farms Chipotle Black Bean Burgers, thawed & cut into 1/4 in. pieces
1/2 c. salsa
1/2 c. shredded Cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese
2 tsp. southwest or mexican seasoning
2 Tbs. chopped fresh cilantro
4 10-inch tortillas
1 Tbs. butter, melted
2 & 1/2 c. shredded lettuce

Instructions

In bowl, toss together 1st 5 ingredients.
Place 1/4 of mixture in center of each tortilla. Fold 2 of sides over, then roll, completely enclosing filling.
Place on baking sheet & brush with butter. Bake @ 375 degrees 10-15 min. or until brown on top.
Serve on shredded lettuce.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 25 min.
