Forkinit: Applesauce Oatmeal Muffins

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 c. all-purpose flour
1 c. sugar
4 tsp. baking powder
11/2 tsp. salt
11/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
Pinch cloves
2 c. applesauce
1/2 c. butter, melted
3 large eggs
11/2 c. plus 2 Tbs. oats
1 c. raisins

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Grease 18 muffin-pan cups. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder salt, cinnamon, nutmeg & cloves in large. Combine applesauce, butter & eggs; stir into dry ingredients just until moistened. Stir in 11/2 c. oats & the raisins. Spoon batter into prepared cups. Sprinle tops with remaining oats. Bake about 20-25 min.

Yields: 18 muffins
Prep time: 15 min.
