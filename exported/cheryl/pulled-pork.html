Forkinit: Pulled Pork

By Cheryl Schnettler
Crock Pot

Ingredients

Pork shoulder (ex. Boston Butt)
Liberal covering of:
Seasoning salt, pepper, minced garlic & brown sugar
8 oz. can tomato sauce
1 c. water
1 dash of Liquid Smoke
2-3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
BBQ sauce

Instructions

Place pork in crock pot, sprinkle seasonings on top. Pour tomato sauce over & around pork, followed by water. Add Liquid Smoke & Worcestershire sauce. Cover & cook on low for 8-9 hours.
Remove meat when done, shred (I use 2 forks) & add BBQ sauce to taste (I prefer Sweet Baby Ray's honey chipotle BBQ sauce).

Prep time: 5 min.
