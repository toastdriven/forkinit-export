Forkinit: Chewy Flourless Chocolate Cookies

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1&3/4 c. powdered sugar
1/2 c. cocoa
2 tsp. cornstarch
1/4 tsp. salt
2 egg whites
1 c. coarsely chopped nuts, toasted

Instructions

Preheat oven to 300 degrees.
Mix together 1st 4 ingredients. Gradually add egg whites, stirring with spoon until mixture formas a dough. (Should be thick enough to form into balls; if not add more powdered sugar & cocoa). Add nuts, mix well.
Form dough into 15 balls. Place on parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake 16-19 minutes, until glossy & crackled. Cool completely.

Yields: 15 cookies
