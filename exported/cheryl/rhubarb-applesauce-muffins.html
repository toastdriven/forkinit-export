Forkinit: Rhubarb Applesauce Muffins

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 c. all-purpose flour
1 c. whole wheat flour
2 tsp. baking powder
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs
1&1/3 c. brown sugar
1&1/4 c. applesauce
1/2 c. canola oil
2 c. rhubarb, cut into 1/4 in. pieces
Cinnamon & brown sugar for topping (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Combine 1st six ingredients & make well in center. Beat eggs. Stir in brown sugar, applesauce & oil. Pour egg mixture into well in flour mixture. Stir until batter is combined but still slightly lumpy.Fold in rhubarb.
Grease muffin tin, spoon about 2 Tbs. batter into each cup. Sprinkle with cinnamon & brown sugar, if desired.
Bake 15 min., or until muffins spring back when touched.

Yields: 18 muffins
