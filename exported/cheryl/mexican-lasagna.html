Forkinit: Mexican Lasagna

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 tsp. olive oil
1 green pepper, diced
1 lb. zucchini, diced (about 3 c.)
2 & 1/2 tsp. cumin
1 can diced tomatoes with chilies
1 can black beans, rinsed & drained
Hatch chilies, chopped
1 can corn
1 can enchilada sauce
1/2 c. chopped cilantro
5 whole-wheat flour tortillas
1&1/4 c. shredded cheddar
Sour cream

Instructions

Heat oven to 400 degrees. Spray a 3 qt. baking dish with baking dish with nonstick cooking spray. Heat oil in large skillet, saute green pepper & zucchini about 5 min. until crisp-tender. Stir in next 6 ingredients, bring to a simmer. Remove from heat & stir in cilantro. Place 1 tortilla in baking dish, top with 1&1/2 c. zucchini mixture; sprinkle with 1/4 c. cheese. repeat layers 4 times.
Cover with foil & bake 20-25 min. until bubbly around edges & cheese is melted. Cool 5 min. Cut in wedges & garnish with sour cream.

Yields: 6 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
