Forkinit: Orange-Chicken Rice Bowl

By Cheryl Schnettler

stove

Ingredients

1 lb. boneless, skinless chicken breasts, cut in strips
1/4 c. Asian Toasted Sesame Dressing
1/4 c. orange juice
3 c. fresh or frozen stir-fry vegetables
3 c. hot brown or white rice
1/4 c. chopped cashews or peanuts

Instructions

Heat nonstick wok or large skillet to medium-high heat; add chicken & stir-fry 5-7 min.
Stir in dressing & juice; bring to boil. Add vegetables. Cook 5-7 min. or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
Serve over rice, sprinkle with nuts.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 30 min.
