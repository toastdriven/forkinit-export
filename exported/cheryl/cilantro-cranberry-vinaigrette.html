Forkinit: Cilantro Cranberry Vinaigrette

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1/4 c. brown rice vinegar
2 Tbs. balsamic vinegar
1-2 garlic cloves, minced
1/2 Tbs. fresh lemon juice
1/3 c. fresh cilantro leaves, chopped
1/4 c. dried cranberries
2/3 c. extra virgin olive oil
Salt & pepper, to taste
Water (dilute vinaigrette to taste)

Instructions

In blender, combine 1st 6 ingredients. With blender still running, slowly add olive oil until well-blended. Add water, salt & pepper to taste.
