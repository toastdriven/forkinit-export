Forkinit: Blue Cheese & Green Onion Potato Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

3 lbs. new potatoes, quartered
2 tsp. salt, divided
1/3 c. sliced green onions
8 oz. sour cream
1/2 c. blue cheese dressing
1/2 tsp. pepper
1/2 c. crumbled blue cheese

Instructions

Bring potatoes, 1 tsp. salt & water to cover to a boil. Cook 10-15 min. or until just tender; drain.
Stir together green onions, next 3 ingredients & remaining salt in large bowl; add potatoes & blue cheese, stirring to coat. Serve immediately, or cover chill until ready to serve.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
