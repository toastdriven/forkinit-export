Forkinit: Fried Rice

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

Left over brown or white rice (cold)
3 Tbs. oil
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 lb. cooked ham, diced
2 c. fresh bean sprouts
1 c. sliced celery
10 oz. pkg. frozen peas
1/2 c. sliced green onions
3 Tbs. soy sauce

Instructions

In a wok fry rice & ham in oil, add vegetables & saute' until tender crisp. Make a well in middle, add eggs & scramble. Stir fry just till hot. Add soy sauce, heat another minute & serve.
