Forkinit: Chocolate Chai Tea

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1&1/3 c. boiling water
3 tea bags
1/4 c. sugar
2&2/3 Tbs. cocoa powder
5&1/2 c. milk
2 tsp. vanilla
1/3 tsp. cinnamon
1/3 tsp. ground ginger
1/3 tsp. ground cardamon
Heavy whipping cream (optional)

Instructions

Pour boiling water over tea bags in large saucepan. Cover & let stand 4-6 min. Remove tea bags. Stir in sugar & cocoa powder. Cook & stir over medium heat just until mixture comes to boiling;reduce heat. Stir in rest of ingredients except whipping cream. Heat through, but do not boil. Pour into mugs, dollop with whipped cream if desired.
