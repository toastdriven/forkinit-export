Forkinit: Healthy Monte Cristo

By Cheryl Schnettler
Griddle

Ingredients

2 tsp. honey mustard
2 slices whole wheat bread
1 slice reduced-fat sharp cheddar cheese
4 bell pepper slices
1 oz. smoked ham
1 oz. sliced turkey
1 oz. sliced swiss cheese

Instructions

Spread honey mustard on bread slices; layer remaining ingredients.
Preheat George Foreman grill; grill about 5 min. or until cheeses are melted & bread is browned.

Yields: 1 serving
