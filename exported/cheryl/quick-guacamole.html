Forkinit: Quick Guacamole

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

3-4 avocados, chopped
1-2 garlic cloves, minced
1/3 c. medium salsa
1/4 c. fresh cilantro, chopped
1 Tbs. lime juice
Salt to taste
Hot sauce to taste

Instructions

In bowl, mix all ingredients until blended.

Yields: About 2 cups
Prep time: 10 min.
