Forkinit: Apple Oat Crisp

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1/4 c. sugar
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
Pinch salt
4 lbs. tart apples (Granny Smith, Jonathan), peeled & cut into 8 wedges each
2 tsp. lemon juice

Oat topping:
2/3 c. all-purpose flour
2/3 c. packed brown sugar
1/2 c. butter, cut-up
2/3 c. rolled oats, uncooked
2/3 c. chopped pecans

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Combine dry ingredients in bowl. Add apples & lemon juice, toss to coat. Arrange in even layer in shallow 3 qt. baking dish. Sprinkle with oat topping.(Combine flour & brown sugar in bowl. With pastry blender, cut in butter until mixture resembles coarse crumbs). Bake 40 min. or until bubbly. Serve warm.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
