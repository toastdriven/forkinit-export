Forkinit: Zucchini Boats

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 large zucchini, cut in half
1 large onion, chopped
1/2 stick butter
2 hard-cooked eggs, mashed
1 c. cheddar cheese, shredded
1-2 garlic cloves, minced
salt & pepper to taste
1 tsp. dried parsley (or 2 tsp. fresh)
Cracker crumbs
Butter for cracker crumbs
Paprika

Instructions

Scoop out pulp of zucchini & chop. Melt butter in fry pan & saute' zucchini pulp & onion until tender. Remove from heat & add cheddar, eggs, garlic, salt & pepper & parsley. Add approximately 1/4 c. cracker crumbs to mixture. Scoop into zucchini boats & place boats in baking dish. Add enough butter to remaining crumbs to allow consistency for sprinkling on zucchini mixture. Add small amount of water to bottom of dish. Bake @ 350 degrees about 45 min. or until lightly browned & tender.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Source: family recipe
