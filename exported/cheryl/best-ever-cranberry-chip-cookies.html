Forkinit: Best Ever Cranberry Chip Cookies

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1/2 c. butter
3/4 c. sugar
3/4 c. packed brown sugar
1 tsp. vanilla
2 eggs
1/2 c. shortening
1 tsp. baking soda
2&1/4 c. flour
1/2 tsp. salt
2 c. semi-sweet chocolate chips
1 c. dried cranberries
1 c. chopped pecans

Instructions

Cream butter, shortening & sugars. Add eggs & vanilla, mix well. Add dry ingredients & blend well. Add chips, nuts & cranberries & blend. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Use medium cookie scoop & place on greased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 min. Do not overbake, cookies should be moist & chewy.

Yields: About 3 doz.
Prep time: 10 min.
