Forkinit: Philadelphia-Style Apple Cake

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

6 c. peeled & thinly sliced Granny Smith apples (about 3 large)
1&1/2 c. plus 5 Tbs. granulated sugar, divided
4 tsp. cinnamon
3 c. all-purpose flour
1 Tbs. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
4 eggs
1/2 c. brown sugar
1 c. vegetable oil
1/2 c. orange juice
2&1/2 tsp. vanilla

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease, sugar & flour a 10 inch Bundt or tube pan.
Combine apple slices with 5 Tbs. granulated sugar & cinnamon;set aside.
Combine flour, baking powder & salt in bowl & set aside.
Beat eggs with remaining granulated sugar & brown sugar. Add vegetable oil, orange juice & vanilla;beat well. Gradually blend in flour mixture & mix until well blended (about 1 min.)
Pour 1/3 of the batter into pan. Top with 1/2 the apple slices, draining off any liquid. Pour in 1/2 the remaining batter & top with remaining apple slices. Top with remaining batter, making sure the apples are covered.
Bake 55-60 min., until the top turns golden brown & knife inserted comes out clean. Let cool 10 min. in pan. Remove from pan & cool.

Yields: 16 servings
