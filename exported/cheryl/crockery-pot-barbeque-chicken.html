Forkinit: Crockery Pot Barbeque Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Crock Pot

Ingredients

4 boneless, skinless, chicken breasts
1 can condensed tomato soup
3/4 c. chopped onion
1/4 c. vinegar
3 tbsp. brown sugar
1 tbsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. sweet basil
1/4 tsp. thyme

Instructions

Place chicken in Crockery Pot. Combine all other ingredients and pour over chicken. Cover tightly and cook at low for 6 to 8 hours.

Yields: 4 servings
