Forkinit: Rueben Pie

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 deep dish pie crust shell
1 egg, beaten
1/3 c. evaporated milk
3/4 c. soft rye bread crumbs (about 1 slice)
3/4 c. chopped onion
1/4 tsp. salt
Dash pepper
1/2 tsp. prepared mustard
1 can (8 oz.) sauerkraut, drained
1/2 lb. ground chuck
1 can (12 oz.) corned beef, flaked
4 oz. grated swiss cheese

Instructions

In mixing bowl, combine egg, milk, bread crumbs, onions, salt, pepper, & mustard.Add sauerkraut, ground chuck & corned beef. Mix well. Place 1/2 of meat mixture into shell. Sprinkle 1/2 of cheese over mixture. Cover with remaining meat mixture. Top with cheese. Bake on preheated cookie sheet @ 400 degrees until crust is brown & bubbly, about 25-30 min.

Prep time: 15 min.
