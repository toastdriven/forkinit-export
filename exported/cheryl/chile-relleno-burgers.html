Forkinit: Chile Relleno Burgers

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

2 large poblano peppers, halved lengthwise & seeded
1 large tomato, cored & halved
1 small onion, quartered
3 cloves garlic, unpeeled
4 hamburger buns, split open
1 egg white, lightly beaten
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. cumin
1/2 tsp. chili powder
11/2 lbs. ground chuck
6 slices muenster

Instructions

Preheat broiler. Put poblanos cut-side down on foil-lined broiler pan. Add tomatoes, onions & garlic;broil until charred, 7-8 min. Cool slightly, then peel garlic. Chop garlic, onion & tomatoes;toss in bowl with salt to taste. Peel poblanos & cut each in half.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Brush bun tops with egg white. Mix oregano, cumin & chili powder, sprinkle on tops & bottoms of buns over a baking sheet. Bake 5 min.
Shape beef into four 3/4 in. thick patties;make an indentation in centers. Sprinkle with salt & cook until browned on bottom, about 4 min. Flip, then top each with some tomato mixture & cheese. Cover & cook 3-5 min. Serve on spiced buns with poblanos & remaining tomato mixture.

Yields: 4 servings
