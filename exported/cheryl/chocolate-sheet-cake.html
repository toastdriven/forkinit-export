Forkinit: Chocolate Sheet Cake

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1/2 c. butter
1/2 c. vegetable oil
4 Tbs. cocoa
1 c. water
2 c. all-purpose flour
2 c. sugar
1/2 c. buttermilk
2 eggs
1 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. vanilla

Frosting:
1/2 c. butter
4 Tbs. cocoa
1/3 c. buttermilk
3&3/4-4 c. powdered sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts

Instructions

Bring 1st 4 ingredients to boil in saucepan. Sift together flour & sugar. Pour chocolate sauce over dry ingredients & mix well. Add rest of ingredients & mix until combined. Pour into greased jelly roll pan. Bake @ 400 degrees for 30 min.
For frosting: Bring 1st 3 ingredients to a boil in saucepan. Remove from heat & add rest of ingredients. Frost cake while it is still warm.

This cake is better after being frozen. Can be eaten thawed or frozen.

Yields: sheet cake
