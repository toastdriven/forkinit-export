Forkinit: Smarty Pants Muffins

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 c. all-purpose flour
1 c. sugar
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1 c. chai tea concentrate
2 eggs, beaten
1/4 c. melted butter
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. nuts
1 c. cranberries

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Coat 12 muffin cups with spray.
Combine four, sugar, baking powder, salt, & cinnamon in mixing bowl.
Combine tea concentrate, eggs, butter & vanilla in separate bowl, whisk well.
Make a well in the center of dry ingredients. Pour wet ingredients into well & stir until just combined. Gently fold in nuts & cranberries.
Fill muffin cups 2/3 full. Bake 15-18 min., until centers spring back.

Yields: 12 muffins
