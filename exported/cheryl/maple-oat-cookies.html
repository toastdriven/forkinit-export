Forkinit: Maple Oat Cookies

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 c. rolled oats
3/4 c. plus 2 Tbs. flour
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. salt
1/2 c. pure maple syrup
1/2 c. canola oil
2 tsp. vanilla extract

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Place oats in medium saucepan & toast over medium-high heat, stirring frequently until oats are slightly golden & fragrant, about 3 min. Remove from heat & stir in next 4 ingredients. In separate bowl, whisk together syrup, oil & vanilla until combined, then add to oat mixture & stir to combine completely. Dough will be warm. Drop by rounded teaspoonfuls onto baking sheet. Gently flatten dough with wet fingers & bake until golden brown, about 15 min. Cool & store in airtight container.
