Forkinit: Doggie Biscuits

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 c. dry oats
2 c. whole wheat flour
1 c. all-natural peanut butter
1 c. water
1/4 c. olive oil
1 egg, beaten

Instructions

Mix together all ingredients. Sprinkle small amount of flour on flat surface. Roll mixture out to about 3/4 in. thick. Cut into desired shapes with cookie cutters; place cookies on lightly greased cookie sheet. Bake 20 min. on 1 side, then flip & bake an additional 20 min. Remove to cooling rack & let dry thoroughly. Biscuits are ready after about 2 hrs.
