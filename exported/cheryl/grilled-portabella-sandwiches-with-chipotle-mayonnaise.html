Forkinit: Grilled Portabella Sandwiches with Chipotle Mayonnaise

By Cheryl Schnettler
Grill

Ingredients

2 large portabella mushrooms, sliced 1/2 in. thick
2 Tbs. mayonnaise
1/4 tsp. ground chipotle pepper
1/2 c. olive oil
2 tsp. fresh garlic, minced
1 tsp. parsley
1 tsp. seasoned salt
2 hamburger buns
2 slices swiss cheese

Instructions

Preheat grill. In small bowl, mix together mayo & chipotle; set aside. In wide, shallow bowl, mix together olive oil, garlic, parsley & seasoned salt. Dunk each slice of mushroom in oil mixture, coating both sides. Place mushrooms on grill, cooking about 5 min. per side. Mushrooms should be browned with nice grill marks. Place buns on grill to lightly toast. Remove everything from grill. Spread 1 Tbs. chipotle mayo on each bun & top with mushroom slices. Add slice of cheese.

Yields: 2 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
