Forkinit: Texas Pecan & Avocado Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1 head lettuce
2 avocados, thinly sliced
2 bell peppers, thinly sliced
1/2 c. chopped toasted pecans
Tangy Dijon dressing

Dijon dressing:
1/3 c. olive oil
2 Tbs. lemon juice
2 Tbs. water
1 Tbs. sugar
2 tsp. Dijon mustard
1/8 tsp. salt
1/8 tsp. pepper

Instructions

Top lettuce leaves with avocados, pepper slices & pecans. For dressing, whisk together all ingredients. Drizzle desired amount over salad. Store rest in airtight container in refrigerator up to 1 week.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
