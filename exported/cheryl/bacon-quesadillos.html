Forkinit: Bacon Quesadillos

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients


4 flour tortillas, (6 inch)
1 cup KRAFT Shredded Colby & Monterey Jack Cheese
1/4 cup OSCAR MAYER Real Bacon Bits
2 green onions, thinly sliced (Can substitute 1 4 oz. can chopped green chilies, drained)

Instructions



HEAT oven to 400Â°F.

PLACE tortillas in single layer on baking sheet sprayed with cooking spray.

TOP half of each tortilla with 1/4 each of the remaining ingredients; fold in half. Press lightly to secure.

BAKE 8 to 10 min. or until edges are lightly browned.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 5 min.
