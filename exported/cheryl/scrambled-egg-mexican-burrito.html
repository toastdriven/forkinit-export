Forkinit: Scrambled Egg Mexican Burrito

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

3 eggs
1 Tbs. scallions, chopped
3 Tbs. cilantro, chopped
1/2 c. Jack cheese, shredded
Charred flour tortilla
1/4-1/2 c. medium or hot salsa
Hot sauce

Instructions

Scramble eggs, scallions & cilantro till just cooked. Place in large tortilla, sprinkle with cheese & fold securely. Place in hot skillet & char on both sides. Serve with salsa & hot sauce.

Yields: 1 serving
Prep time: 15 min.
