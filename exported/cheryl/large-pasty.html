Forkinit: Large Pasty

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

Pastry dough for 2 crusts:
1 c. shortening
2&1/2-3/4 c. flour
1 egg, beaten
2&1/2 tsp. vinegar
Water

Potatoes, diced
Carrots, diced
Rutabaga, diced
Onion, diced
1&1/2 lb. hamburger

Instructions

Dough:
Put egg in 1/2 c. measure cup, beat & add vinegar; fill cup to top with water. Cut flour into shortening till crumbly, add remaining ingredients. Blend well. Refrigerate for several min. before working dough. Can freeze unused dough.
Soak potatoes, carrots & rutabagas in bowl of water; then by handfuls, spread over dough (ok if water from veg. gets on crust.) Put scoops of raw hamburger over veg. Sprinkle with chopped onions & salt & pepper. Will have mound about 6 in. high.) Cover with 2nd half of dough, seal edges well. Bake @ 350 degrees 1&1/2 hrs.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 45 min.
