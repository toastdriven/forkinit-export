Forkinit: Cheryl's Favorite Jello Mold

By Cheryl Schnettler
Refrigerator

Ingredients

Bottom layer:
3 oz. pkg. orange-flavored jello
1 c. boiling water
1 c. orange juice
4 oz. cream cheese, softened
2 c. Cool Whip (do not use fat free)
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Top layer:
3 oz. pkg. lime-flavored jello
1 c. crushed pineapple, drained

Instructions

Bottom layer:
Dissolve orange jello in boiling water; stir in orange juice. Chill till partially set. Meanwhile blend cream cheese & Cool Whip, blend in nuts. Fold into chilled jello & pour into an 8X8 dish. Chill till almost firm.
Top layer:
Make lime jello according to pkg. directions; chill till partially set. Then add drained pineapple & mix well. Pour over cream cheese mixture & chill several hrs. before serving.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 20 min.
