Forkinit: Jalapeno Popper Dip

By Cheryl Schnettler
Microwave

Ingredients

5 jalapeno peppers
1 c. bacon bits
1 red bell pepper
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 c. mayo
1&1/2 c. shredded Cheddar
1/2 c. black beans
2 Tbs. dried ranch dressing mix
1 tsp. garlic powder
1 Tbs. olive oil

Instructions

Dice peppers & red pepper. Place in skillet with black beans, bacon bits & small amount of olive oil. Cook until softened, but not completely cooked through (still crunchy). In bowl, combine cream cheese, 1 c. of cheddar & rest of ingredients. Add cooked mixture & mix. Top with reserved 1/2 c. cheddar & warm in microwave. Serve with Tortilla chips.

Prep time: 15 min.
