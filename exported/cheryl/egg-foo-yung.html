Forkinit: Egg Foo Yung

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

8 oz. fresh bean sprouts
8 eggs, beaten
1 can sliced mushrooms
2 scallions, chopped
1/2 tsp. salt
3 Tbs. oil
1&1/2 c. chicken broth
2 Tbs. soy sauce
1/4 tsp. salt
2 Tbs. cornstarch
2 Tbs. cold water

Instructions

In bowl, combine bean sprouts, eggs, mushrooms, scallions & 1/2 tsp. salt. Mix well. Heat oil in deep skillet to medium-high. Pour 1/2 c. egg mixture into pan, make patty. Fry until golden brown, turning once. Add more oil if needed. In saucepan over medium heat, combine broth, soy sauce & salt. Bring to boil. Mix cornstarch & water, stir into broth mix. Cook until thickened, serve over patties.

Yields: 6 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
