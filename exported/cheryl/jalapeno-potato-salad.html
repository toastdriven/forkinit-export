Forkinit: Jalapeno Potato Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler
Salad

Ingredients

6 medium red potatoes, peeled & cubed
2 celery ribs, chopped
2 hard-cooked eggs, chopped
1/4 c. chopped onion
2 small jalapeno peppers, seeded & chopped
1/4 c. mayonnaise
3 tsp. spicy brown mustard
2 Tbs. southwest spicy sweet hot mustard
3 tsp. Tobasco
1/4 tsp. cumin
1/4 tsp. pepper

Instructions

Place potatoes in saucepan & cover with water. Bring to boil & reduce heat. Cover & cook 10-15 min. or until tender. Drain & cool to room temperature. Combine potatoes, celery, eggs, onion & jalapenos. In small bowl, combine mayonnaise, mustards, Tobasco, cumin & pepper. Pour over potato mixture & toss gently to coat. Cover & refrigerate overnight.

Yields: 12 servings
