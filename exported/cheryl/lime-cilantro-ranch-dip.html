Forkinit: Lime Cilantro Ranch Dip

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

* 1 (1-oz.) envelope Ranch dressing mix
* 1 1/2 cups light sour cream
* 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
* 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
* Quesadillas, tacos, or chili

Instructions

Whisk together first 4 ingredients. Cover and chill 30 minutes. Serve with quesadillas, tacos, or chili.

Prep time: 5 min.
