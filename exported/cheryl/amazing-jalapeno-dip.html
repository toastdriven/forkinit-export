Forkinit: Amazing Jalapeno Dip

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 diced jalapeno peppers, seeds removed
1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
1/2 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
1/2 cup mayonnaise
1/2 cup sour cream
1/2 diced Poblano pepper, seed removed

Instructions

Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F. Combine all ingredients in a bowl. Place in baking dish & bake for 30 minutes.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
