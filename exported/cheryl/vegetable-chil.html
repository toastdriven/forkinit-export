Forkinit: Vegetable Chil

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

3 Tbs. olive oil
1 small onion, diced
2 large stalks celery, sliced
1 small bell pepper, diced
1 ancho chili, diced
1 Tbs. ground ancho chili pepper
2 Tbs. cumin
2 tsp. Mexican oregano
1 tsp. ground chipotle pepper
2 small zucchini, sliced
1 can corn, rinsed & drained
1 can black beans, rinsed & drained
1 14 oz. can chopped tomatoes
2 Tbs. red wine or water
Grated cheddar, optional

Instructions

In large sauce pan, heat oil over medium-high heat. Add onion, celery & peppers; saute' until cooked through. Add seasonings, stir & cook a few minutes. Add rest of ingredients. Cover & simmer about 20 minutes. Serve with grated cheddar, if desired.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
