Forkinit: Heart-Smart Sweet Potato Muffins

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 c. whole wheat flour
1 c. all-purpose flour
1/2 c. packed brown sugar
2&1/2 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. cloves
1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. skim milk
2 egg whites
1 Tbs. oil
1 c. finely grated raw sweet potato
1/2 c. finely chopped dried pears
1/2 c. chopped nuts
1-2 Tbs. turbinado sugar (optional)

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Coat 12 muffin cups with cooking spray.
Combine 1st 7 ingredients in mixing bowl. Combine milk, egg whites & oil in separate bowl & whisk well.
Make a well in center of dry ingredients. Pour wet ingredients into well & stir just until combined. Gently fold in sweet potato, pears & nuts.
Fill muffin cups 2/3 c. full. Sprinkle with sugar, if desired. Bake 18-20 min. until centers spring back.

Yields: 12 muffins
