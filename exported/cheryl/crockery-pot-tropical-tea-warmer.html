Forkinit: Crockery Pot Tropical Tea Warmer

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

6 cups boiling water
1/3 cup sugar
1 1/2 cup orange juice
1 orange sliced (unpeeled)
6 tea bags
2 tbsp. honey
1 1/2 cup pineapple juice

Instructions

Warm your Crockery Pot first with some hot tap water. Then pour boiling water over the tea bags in the Crockery Pot. Cover and let stand 5 minutes. Remove the tea bags. Stir in the sugar, honey, orange juice, pineapple juice, and orange slices. Cover and heat on low for 2-3 hours; serve from pot.
