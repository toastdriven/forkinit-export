Forkinit: Cheedar Turkey & Black Bean Torta Sandwich

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients


3 tablespoons GREY POUPON Dijon Mustard
1 finely chopped canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce with 1 Tbsp. reserved adobo sauce
1 can (15 oz.) black beans, rinsed
1/4 cup KRAFT Real Mayo Mayonnaise
4 Bolillos (6 inch), split
20 slices OSCAR MAYER Deli Fresh Shaved Smoked Turkey Breast
4 KRAFT DELI DELUXE Process Sharp Cheddar Cheese Slices
1 Avocado, thinly sliced
24 drained pickled jalape√±o nacho slices

Instructions

MIX mustard, chipotle peppers and adobo sauce. Mash beans in medium bowl. Add half the mustard mixture; mix well.

SPREAD bottom halves of rolls with mayo, then bean mixture. Spread top halves with remaining mustard mixture.

FILL rolls with turkey, cheese, avocados and jalapeno slices.

For hot sandwich: Preheat oven to 375 degrees F. Fill rolls, except for avocado & nacho slices. Wrap in foil. Bake for 5 min., remove from oven & add avocado & jalapeno slices.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
