Forkinit: Waldorf Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

Maraschino cherries, halved
Bananas, sliced
Can pineapple chunks, drained
Can mandarin oranges, drained
Apples, cut in chunks
Red grapes, halved
Pecans or walnuts, coarsely chopped
Cool whip
Sour cream
Mayonnaise

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together, serve immediately.
