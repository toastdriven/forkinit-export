Forkinit: Asian Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

1 medium head cabbage
1 bunch green onions, sliced thin
3-5 Tbs. butter
3 3oz. pkgs Ramen noodles (any flavor)
2 oz. slivered almonds
4 Tbs. sesame seeds
1/4 c. vinegar
3/4 c. vegetable oil
2 Tbs. soy sauce
1/2 c. sugar

Instructions

Shred cabbage & add onions. Melt butter in skillet; crunch up noodles & add to melted butter. Add almonds & sesame seeds to noodles; stir until lightly browned, then remove from heat. Combine vinegar, oil, soy sauce & sugar in small sauce pan. Bring to a boil for 1 min, then remove from heat & cool. Combine cabbage & noodle mixture, pour dressing over & serve immediately. Refrigerate any extra dressing in airtight container.
