Forkinit: Granola

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

8 c. old-fashioned rolled oats
1 c. chopped almonds or walnuts
1 c. wheat germ
1 c. sunflower seeds
1/2 c. honey
1/2 c. vegetable oil
1/2 c. brown sugar
2 tsp. vanilla
11/2 c. raisins, diced prunes or dried cranberries
1 Tbs. cinnamon

Instructions

Combine 1st 4 ingredients. Combine honey, oil, sugar & vanilla in saucepan: heat but do not boil. Add to oats mixture, stirring until well-blended. Spread evenly onto 2 oiled cookie sheets. Bake @ 325 degrees 20-25 min., stirring occasionally. Stir in dried fruit. Cool. Store in air-tight container.

Yields: 3 lbs.
