Forkinit: Strawberry Jalapeno Appetizers

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

10 jalapeno peppers
8 oz. cream cheese
1/2 c. strawberry preserves
1 Tbs. cumin
1/2 c. finely chopped cilantro

Instructions

In bowl, mix cream cheese, preserves, cumin & cilantro together. Wearing gloves, cut tops of peppers off, carefully remove seeds & fiber with small spoon. Fill each pepper with mixture & serve.

Yields: 5-10 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
