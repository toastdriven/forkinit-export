Forkinit: Stuufed Cranberry Sauce

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1 8oz. container whipped cream cheese
2 Tbs. mayonnaise
1/2 c. finely chopped pecans
2 cans jellied cranberry sauce, chilled

Instructions

In bowl, beat together cream cheese & mayonnaise until creamy. Stir in nuts. Cut cranberry sauce into 7-8 round slices/can. Spread cream cheese mixture on half of slices & top with remaining slices to make sandwiches. Cover tightly with plastic wrap & chill up to 5 hrs.

Yields: 14-16 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
