Forkinit: Brownies Hepburn

By Cheryl Schnettler
An original recipe from the actress, Katherine Hepburn

Ingredients

2 squares unsweetened chocolate
1/4 lb. butter
1 c. sugar
2 eggs
1/2 tsp. vanilla
1 c. chopped nuts
1/4 c. flour
1/4 tsp. salt

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Melt chocolate & butter over low heat in heavy saucepan. Remove from heat & stir in sugar. Beat in eggs & vanilla. Quickly stir in nuts, flour & salt. spread in well-greased 8 X 8 in. pan. Bake 40-45 min. Cool completely before cutting.

Yields: 12 brownies
