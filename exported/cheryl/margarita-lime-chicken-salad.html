Forkinit: Margarita Lime Chicken Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

Romaine Lettuce
Grilled Chicken (best if marinated with Tequila lime seasoning.
Red and Yellow Bell Peppers cut in thin slices
Cherry Tomatoes
Grated Monterey Jack Cheese

Instructions

Make a bed of lettuce with the Romaine arrange peppers, chicken, tomatoes and sprinkle cheese on top. Make the Creamy Margarita Lime Dressing Mix according to directions and pour over salad.

Prep time: 10 min.
