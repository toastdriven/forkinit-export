Forkinit: Rueben Crock Pot Appetizer

By Cheryl Schnettler
Crockpot

Ingredients

1 qt. saurkraut, rinsed & drained
3 pkg. (2.5 oz. pkg) sliced corned beef
1 lb. shredded swiss cheese
1 c. mayonnaise
1/2 c. thousand island dressing

Instructions

Mix together & place in crock pot on low heat until heated through. Do not let boil. Serve on Pinah's rye chips.

Prep time: 15 min.
