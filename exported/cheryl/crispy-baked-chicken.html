Forkinit: Crispy Baked Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

3-3&1/2 lb. chicken, cut up
Moist coating:
1/2 c. sour cream
1 Tbs. lemon juice
1 tsp. salt
1 tsp. Worcestershire sauce
1/2 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. celery salt
1/2 tsp. dry mustard
Dry coating:
3/4 c. cornflake crumbs

Instructions

Mix moist coating together & toss chicken pieces in it thoroughly. Refrigerate for 30 min. to 24 hrs. Roll chicken in dry coating, coat thoroughly. Bake @ 350 degrees about 45 min. on foil-lined cookie sheet sprayed with cooking spray
