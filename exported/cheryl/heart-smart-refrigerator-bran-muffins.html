Forkinit: Heart-Smart Refrigerator Bran Muffins

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 c. All-Bran cereal
2/3 c. wheat bran
1&1/3 c. boiling water
2&2/3 c. all-purpose flour
1 Tbs. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
2 c. low-fat buttermilk
1/2 c. egg substitute
1 c. sugar
1/2 c. packed brown sugar
1/2 c. oil
1 tsp. vanilla

Instructions

Mix cereal & wheat bran in large bowl. Add boiling, stir well & set aside 1 hr.
Whisk together flour, soda, salt, cinnamon & nutmeg in separate bowl.
Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Coat muffin cups with cooking spray.
Add 1 c. buttermilk to bran mixture, stir well with whisk. Add remaining buttermilk, egg substitute, sugars, oil & vanilla, stirring until smooth.
Add flour mixture; stir until a thick batter forms.
Fill muffin cups 2/3 c. batter. Bake 20-25 min. until lightly browned & tops spring back.

Yields: 2 doz.
