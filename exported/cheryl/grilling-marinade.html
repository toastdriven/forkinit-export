Forkinit: Grilling Marinade

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

6 Tbs. ketchup
1/4 c. honey
1/4 c. Thai chili sauce
2 Tbs. minced garlic
2 Tbs. soy sauce
2 Tbs. olive oil
2 Tbs. white wine vinegar
4 tsp. Cajun seasonings, Chinese 5 spice or chili powder

Instructions

Mix all ingredients in small bowl. Refrigerate until ready to use.

Prep time: 5 min.
