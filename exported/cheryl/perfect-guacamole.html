Forkinit: Perfect Guacamole

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

4 extra large Avocados, peeled, pitted & diced (about 2 c.)
3/4 c. red onion, diced
3/4 c. Roma tomatoes, seeded & diced
1/2 c. fresh cilantro, chopped
2 Tbs. jalapeno or serrano chilies, seeded & minced
1 lime, juiced
1/4 tsp. garlic powder or 1 fresh clove, minced
Salt & pepper

Instructions

Blend all ingredients gently, leaving small chunks of avocado. Serve immediately.

Yields: 6-8 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
