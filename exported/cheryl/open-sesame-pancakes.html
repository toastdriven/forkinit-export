Forkinit: Open Sesame Pancakes

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

Pancake mix:
1 c. sesame seeds
1 c. old-fashioned oats
3 c. whole wheat flour
1&1/2 c. buckwheat flour
2&1/4 c. yellow or white cornmeal
1 c. sugar
1 12 oz. buttermilk powder
5 Tbs. baking powder
2 Tbs. baking soda
2 Tbs. salt

Pancakes:
2 eggs
1 c. water
2 c. pancake mix
4 tsp. oil

Instructions

To prepare mix, toast sesame seeds in large heavy skillet over medium-high heat. Stir constantly until light brown, about 4 min. Remove from heat & pour into bowl to cool.
Place oats in food processor & pulse until powdery. Add sesame seeds & pulse to grind. (Will be mostly powdery with a few whole seeds here & there. Don't overgrind or too much oil will be released).
Transfer oatmeal mixture to large bowl. Add flours, cornmeal, sugar & buttermilk powder. Stir well.
Sift baking powder, soda & salt over mixture, stir well. Transfer to zip-lock bags. to prevent rancidity, store in freezer.
To prepare pancakes, beat eggs & water in bowl. Whisk in pancake mix & oil; don't overbeat. Cook 2-3 min., flip & cook 1-2 min.

Yields: 4 (6 in.) pancakes
