Forkinit: Rum Chica Rum chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Grill

Ingredients

2/3 c. dark rum
1/3 c. orange juice
1 tsp. orange zest
3 Tbs. Worcestershire sauce
1 in. piece gingerroot, peeled & grated
2 Tbs. dark brown sugar
1 Tbs. fresh cilantro, chopped
2 lbs. boneless chicken breasts, trimmed of excess fat
1 orange, thinly sliced into rounds, for garnish

Instructions

Whisk together 1st 7 ingredients. Add chicken pieces, cover with plastic & refrigerate @ least 30 min. or up to 4 hrs.
Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
Place chicken on grill. Cook until chicken is browned on both sides, about 10 min. per side, basting with marinade a few times throughout. Serve sprinkle with cilantro & garnished orange slices.

Yields: 4 servings
