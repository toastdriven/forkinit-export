Forkinit: Avocado Salad Dressing

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1 large or 2 small avocados
1 c. low-fat plain yogurt
1-2 Tbs. fresh cilantro or parsley, chopped
2 Tbs. fresh lemon juice
Salt, to taste
Pepper, to taste

Instructions

Peel avocados & cut into chunks. combine remaining ingredients puree in food processor until smooth. Serve immediately or chill until ready to serve.
Use as a dressing for tossed salads or a dip for fresh veggies.
