Forkinit: All Day Chipotle Pork Chili

By Cheryl Schnettler
Crock Pot

Ingredients

1 Tbs. olive oil
1&1/2 lbs. pork tenderloin, cut in 3/4 in. cubes
1 c. chopped onion
2 cloves garlic, minced
2 Poblano peppers, seeded & cut in 1/2 in. pieces
2 c. beef broth
1 c. chunky salsa
1 16 oz. can black beans, rinsed & drained
1 pkg. chili seasoning mix
Sour cream (optional)
Fresh cilantro, chopped

Instructions

Heat olive oil in skillet. Add pork, onion & garlic; cook over medium-high heat 6-8 min. or until pork is browned. Place mixture in crock pot; add next 5 ingredients. cover & cook on low 8-10 hrs. or on high 5-6 hrs. Serve with sour cream & cilantro, if desired.

Yields: 6 servings
