Forkinit: Zucchini Nut Muffins

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 eggs, slightly beaten
1/2 c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/2 c. honey
1/2 c. melted butter
1 tsp. vanilla
13/4 c. flour
1 tsp. baking soda
2/3 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
11/2 tsp. cinnamon
1 c. granola
1/2 c. chopped nuts
2 c. shredded zucchini

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray muffin cups with nonstick cooking spray. In large bowl, beat eggs, brown sugar, honey, butter & vanilla together. In separate bowl, stir together flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder, nutmeg & cinnamon. Add dry ingredients to egg mixture, stirring just until moistened. Fold in granola, nuts & zucchini. Fill cups 3/4 full. Bake 20-25 min.

Yields: 12 muffins
Prep time: 15 min.
