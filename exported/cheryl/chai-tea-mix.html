Forkinit: Chai Tea Mix

By Cheryl Schnettler
The best!

Ingredients

1 c. nonfat dry milk powder
1 c. powdered non-dairy creamer
2 tsp. vanilla
1&1/2 c. sugar
1&1/2 c. unsweetened instant tea
4 tsp. ground ginger
4 tsp. ground cinnamon
2 tsp.
ground cloves
2 tsp. ground cardamon
2 tsp. ground nutmeg
2 tsp. allspice

Instructions

Add vanilla into sugar & let dry. Then break into small lumps.
In large bowl, combine milk powder, creamer, sugar & instant tea. Stir in all of spices.
In blender or food processor, blend 1 cup @ a time, until mixture is the consistency of fine powder. May need to repeat 1-2 times.
To serve: Stir in approx. 2 heaping Tbs. of mixture into 12 Oz. of hot water.

Yields: 36 servings
Prep time: 30 min.
