Forkinit: Pretzel Salad Dessert

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

Prepare part of top layer first (see instructions)

Top layer:
6 oz. strawberry jello
2 c. boiling water
1/2 c. cold water
1 pt. frozen strawberries, thawed

Crust:
6 Tbs. sugar
2 c. crushed pretzel rings
1 c. butter

1st layer:
8 oz. cream cheese (do not use light)
1 scant c. sugar
8 oz. cool whip

Instructions

Prepare jello first, so it can SLIGHTLY set before making crust.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Combine ingredients for crust, mix well & pat into 9X13 pan baking dish. Bake for 25 min. or until light brown. Cool.
1st layer: Combine cream cheese & sugar well, fold in cool whip. Spread over crust.
Top layer: Fold strawberries into partially set jello & pour over top. Chill until set.

Prep time: 20 min.
