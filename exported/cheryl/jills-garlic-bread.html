Forkinit: Jill's Garlic Bread

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1/2 lb. butter, softened
1/2 lb. Mozzarella cheese, shredded
1 c. grated Parmesan
1 Tbs. garlic powder
1 Tbs. olive oil
1 loaf French or Italian bread

Instructions

Mix 1st 5 ingredients together well in bowl. Cut bread in half lengthwise, spread each half with mixture. Place on cookie sheet. Bake uncovered @ 350 degrees F for 20 min. or until cheese mixture is lightly browned.

Prep time: 5 min.
