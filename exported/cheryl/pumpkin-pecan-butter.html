Forkinit: Pumpkin Pecan Butter

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

2 15-oz. canned pumpkin
1/4 c. water
2 c. granulated sugar
1/4 c. lemon juice
2 tsp. ground cinnamon
3/4 tsp. ground ginger
1/2 tsp. ground cloves
1/2 tsp. ground allspice
1/4 c. ground pecans

Instructions

In heavy saucepan, combine all ingredients except pecans. Heat & stir over medium-high heat until pockets of steam begin to escape from the thick mixture.
Reduce heat to medium-low for 20 min. Stir in pecans, cook 5-10 min. more or until mixture is thick & mounds on spoon. Remove from heat & cool about 1 hour. Cover & refrigerate up to 2 wks. Or freeze for up to 3 months.

Yields: About 4&1/2 cups
