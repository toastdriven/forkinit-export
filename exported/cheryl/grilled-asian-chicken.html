Forkinit: Grilled Asian Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Grill

Ingredients

1/4 c. soy sauce
4 tsp. sesame oil
2 Tbs. brown sugar
1 tsp. Chinese 5 spice
2 cloves garlic, crushed
4 chicken breasts, skinless & boneless
Peanut sauce
Hot sauce

Instructions

In microwave-safe bowl, combine 1st 5 ingredients. Heat in microwave 1 min., then stir. Heat again for 30 sec., watching closely not to boil.
Place chicken in shallow dish. Pour soy sauce mixture over & set aside to marinate for 15 min.
Preheat grill. drain marinade from chicken into small saucepan. Bring to boil & simmer over medium heat for 5 min. Set aside for basting.
Lightly oil grill grate. Cook chicken 6-8 min. per side or until juices are clear. Baste frequently with marinade.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
