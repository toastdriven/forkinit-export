Forkinit: Sherry Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

3 lb chicken, cut in large pieces
1/2. c. soy sauce
2 c. sherry
2 Tbs sugar
1 tsp salt
1/8 tsp MSG
1/8 tsp pepper
2 cloves garlic,crushed
1 thin slice ginger, finely chopped
2 Tbs oil for frying

Instructions

Heat skillet on moderate heat (300 degrees). Add oil & heat thoroughly. Brown chicken on all sides, drain off excess oil. Add sauce, bring to boil.Boil for 25 min. in covered pan. Reduce heat & simmer for 15 min.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Source: The Fine Art of Chinese Cooking
