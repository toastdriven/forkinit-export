Forkinit: Cilantro Orange Vinaigrette

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1/4 c. extra virgin olive oil
2 Tbs. balsamic vinegar
Juice & zest from 1 large orange
1/4 c. orange juice
1 c. cilantro leaves
1 Tbs. brown mustard

Instructions

Place all ingredients in blender & blend well, until cilantro is finely chopped & all ingrdients are well-mixed. Best if refrigerated several hours or overnight.
Great over Asian salad, especially.
