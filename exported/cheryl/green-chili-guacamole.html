Forkinit: Green Chili Guacamole

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

2 8oz. blocks cream cheese, softened
1 large clove garlic
1 large handful cilantro leaves
1&1/2 lbs. hatch chilies, roasted
2 Tbs. fajita seasoning

Instructions

Remove most of char from peppers. Remove seeds & stems. Put all ingredients in food processor & process until it resembles looks of guacamole (approx. 2 min.)
Serve with tortilla chips or crackers.
