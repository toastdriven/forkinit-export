Forkinit: Vienna Torte

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

6 egg yolks, room temperature
6 egg whites, room temperature
1 tsp. vanilla
1/3 c. water
1 c. sugar
2 Tbs. baking powder
1&1/3 c. all-purpose flour, sifted 3 times

Custard frosting:
1/3 c. cornstarch
2 c. milk
1/3 c. sugar
1 beaten egg
1 tsp. vanilla
1 c. butter
1 c. powdered sugar

Instructions

Beat egg yolks well. Add 3/4 c. sugar very gradually, add dry ingredients, alternating with water. In separate bowl, beat egg whites stiff with 1/4 c. sugar. Fold into yolk mixture. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Pour cake mixture into three ungreased 8 in. round pans & bake for 20 min. Invert pans immediately on rack.
Custard:
In saucepan, mix 1st 5 ingredients together. Cook until thick, stirring frequently. Remove from heat; beat with wooden spoon until until cool. Beat butter & powdered sugar together; add cooled custard & mix well.
Frost cake.
