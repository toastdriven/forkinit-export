Forkinit: Sweet Potato Casserole

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

3 c. mashed sweet potatoes
1 c. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
2 eggs, beaten
1/2 stick butter
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. cinnamon

Topping:
1/2 c. brown sugar
1/2 c. flour
1/2 stick butter
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together & sprinkle with nut topping. Spray 8X8 baking dish with cooking spray. Bake @ 350 degrees for 35 min.

Yields: 8-10 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
