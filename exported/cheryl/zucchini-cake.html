Forkinit: Zucchini Cake

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 C. flour
2 c. sugar
1 Tbs. cinnamon
2 tsp. baking soda
1 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
1 c. oil
2 tsp. vanilla
3 eggs
2 c. grated unpeeled zucchini
1&1/2 c. chopped nuts, divided

Instructions

In large bowl, combine 1st 9 ingredients. Beat @ medium speed with mixer until well mixed. Stir in zucchini & nuts. Pour into 13X9 pan; sprinkle with remaining 1/2 c. nuts. Bake @ 350 degrees for 45 min. or until pick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool on rack.

Yields: 18 bars
