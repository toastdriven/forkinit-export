Forkinit: Southwest Hash Browns

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

2 lb. pkg shredded hash brown potatoes
3 Tbs. butter
2 tsp. taco seasoning
1 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper

Instructions

In large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add potatoes. As they begin to brown, toss with seasonings. Cook about 10 min. or until golden brown.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 5 min.
