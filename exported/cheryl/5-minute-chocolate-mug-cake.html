Forkinit: 5 Minute Chocolate Mug Cake

By Cheryl Schnettler
Microwave

Ingredients

4 Tbs. flour
4 Tbs. sugar
2 Tbs. cocoa
1 egg
3 Tbs. milk
3 Tbs. oil
3 Tbs. chocolate chips
Splash of vanilla
1 large coffee mug

Instructions

Add dry ingredients to mug & mix well. Add egg & mix thoroughly. Pour in milk & oil, mix well. Add chocolate chips & vanilla, mix again.
Place mug in microwave & cook for 3 min. The cake will rise over top of mug, but don't be alarmed. Allow to cool somewhat & tip out onto plate.

Yields: 1 serving
