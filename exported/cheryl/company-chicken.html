Forkinit: Company chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
2 Tbs. olive oil
1/2 c. bread crumbs
1/8 c. Salad Elegant
1/8 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/2 tsp. basil
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 egg, beaten
1 Tbs. milk
1/3 c. flour

Instructions

Preheat oven to 425 degrees. Combine bread crumbs, salad elegant, garlic, oregano & basil in shallow bowl. In second shallow bowl, beat together egg & milk. Place flour in 3rd shallow bowl.
Lightly coat each breast with flour, shaking off excess. Dip in egg mixture, then in bread crumbs, making sure to evenly coat both sides.
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Place breasts in pan 2 @ a time & brown on each side, about 2 min. Remove chicken & place on baking rack that has been placed on baking sheet. Repeat for 2nd batch of chicken. Bake chicken @ 425 degrees for 15 min. or until chicken is cooked through.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 15 min.
