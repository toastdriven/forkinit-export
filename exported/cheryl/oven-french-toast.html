Forkinit: Oven French Toast

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1/4 c. butter, melted
8 thick slices French or Italian bread
4 eggs
2/3 c. orange juice
1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
1/4 tsp. nutmeg
1/2 c. chopped pecans

Syrup:
1/4 c. butter
1/2 c. orange juice
1/4 c. sugar

Instructions

Pour melted butter into 13X9 baking pan. Tilt to coat bottom evenly. Arrange bread slices in a single layer in pan.
Whisk eggs until well beaten. Add rest of ingredients, except pecans, whisk until well blended. Pour over bread. cover with Saran & refrigerate @ least 2 hrs. or overnight.
Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
Remove Saran & bake, uncovered, 20 min. Sprinkle pecans over top & bake 10 min. longer.
To prepare syrup, melt butter in small saucepan over medium heat. Add orange juice & sugar. Cook until thoroughly heated & the sugar is dissolved. Do not boil. Serve over french toast.

Yields: 8 servings
