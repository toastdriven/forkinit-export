Forkinit: Soutwest Corn on Cob

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

8 ears fresh corn
Juice of 1 lime
2 Tbs. sugar

Seasoned butter:
1/2 c. butter, softened
1 Tbs. Arizona Dreaming

Instructions

Place cleaned corn in large stock pot. Cover with water. Add lime juice & sugar. Bring to boil & boil for 2 min. Remove from heat & let sit 10 more min. While corn rests, combine butter & Arizona Dreaming in small bowl, mix thoroughly. Remove corn from water & serve with seasoned butter.

Yields: 4-8 servings
Prep time: 10 min.
