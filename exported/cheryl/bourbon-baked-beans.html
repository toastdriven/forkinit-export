Forkinit: Bourbon Baked Beans

By Cheryl Schnettler
Crock Pot

Ingredients

4 cans Boston baked beans (16 oz. each)
16 oz. crushed pineapple, drained
12 oz. chili sauce
1/2 cup strong brewed coffee
1/2 cup bourbon
1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
1 tbsp. molasses
3/4 tsp. dry mustard

Instructions

Put all ingredients in crock pot, cook on high 2 hrs.
