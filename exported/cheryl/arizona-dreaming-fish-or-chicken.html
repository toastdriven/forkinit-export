Forkinit: Arizona Dreaming Fish or Chicken

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

Boneless, skinless chicken breast
Fish filet ( ex. tilapia)
2 tsp. olive oil
1 tsp. Arizona Dreaming

Instructions

Heat oil in skillet over medium heat. Season meat or fish with Arizona Dreaming. Saute' until being careful not to burn.
