Forkinit: Pumpkin Bread

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1&1/2 c. brown sugar, packed
1 c. pumpkin puree, canned or cooked
2/3 c. vegetable oil
2 eggs
1/4 c. water
2 c. all-purpose flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. ground nutmeg
1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease 9X5 loaf pan. In large bowl combine 1st 5 ingredients; blend well. Combine next 6 ingredients; stir into pumpkin mixture & add nuts. Scrape loaf pan. Bake 50-60 min. or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Remove from pan; cool on rack.
