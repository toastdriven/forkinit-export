Forkinit: Easy Peach Crisp

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

2 cans (15 oz. each) sliced peaches in heavy syrup, drained
1/3 c. flour
1/3 c. firmly packed brown sugar
1/3 c. old-fashioned oats
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 c. butter

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375 degrees.
Place fruit in 1 qt. shallow baking dish.
Combine next 4 ingredients; mix in butter until crumbly & sprinkle over fruit.
Bake 30 min. or until golden brown. Serve with ice cream, if desired.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 5 min.
