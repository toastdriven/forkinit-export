Forkinit: Portobello-Rosemary Stuffing

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 pkg stuffing mix
1 bag fresh bread cubes
i box sliced portobello mushrooms
I can chicken broth
Chopped onions
olive oil
butter
white wine
rosemary

Instructions

Combine all ingredients, place in greased 13X9 dish. cover & bake @ 350 degrees F for 30 min. Remove cover & continue baking for 10-15 min. until golden brown.
