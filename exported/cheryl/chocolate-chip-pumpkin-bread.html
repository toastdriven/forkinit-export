Forkinit: Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

3 c. white sugar
1 15 oz. can pumpkin
1/2 c. oil
1/2 c. applesauce
2/3 c. water
4 eggs
3&1/2 c. all-purpose flour
1 Tbs. cinnamon
1 Tbs. nutmeg
2 tsp. baking soda
1&1/2 tsp. salt
1 c. miniature semisweet chocolate chips
1/2 c. chopped nuts

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease & flour 3 9X5 in. loaf pans.
In large bowl, combine 1st 6 ingredients. Beat until smooth. Blend in next 5 ingredients. fold in chips & nuts. Pour into loaf pans. Bake for 1 hr. or until knife inserted comes out clean. Cool on wire rack.

Yields: 3 loaves
Prep time: 30 min.
