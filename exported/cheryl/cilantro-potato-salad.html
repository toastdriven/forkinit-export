Forkinit: Cilantro Potato Salad

By Cheryl Schnettler
salad

Ingredients

3 lb. red potatoes
2 tsp. salt, divided
1 c. mayonnaise
1 can chopped green chilies
1/3 c. chopped fresh cilantro
3 green onions, chopped
2 Tbs. fresh lime juice (about 2 limes)
1 garlic clove, minced
4 slices maple bacon, cooked & crumbled
Cilantro for garnish

Instructions

Cook potatoes in 1 tsp. salt & water until fork tender. Drain & cool. Cut into quarters. Stir together mayonnaise & next 6 ingredients & remaining salt in large bowl; add potatoes & toss to coat. Cover & chill @ least 4 hrs. Stir in bacon just before serving & garnish with cilantro.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 20 min.
