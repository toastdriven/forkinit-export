Forkinit: BEAN DIP

By Cheryl Schnettler
Low fat, spicy

Ingredients

16 oz. can black beans, drained & rinsed
1/8 lge onion, finely chopped
1/4 c. pickled jalapeno peppers or slices
1/2 tsp. minced garlic soaked in 2 tsp water for 5 min.
1/4 tsp. ground cumin
2 tsp. chili powder (regular, medium or hot)
1 Tb. lime juice (juice of 1 lime)

Instructions

Put all ingredients in blender & blend until smooth.

Yields: about 2 c.
Prep time: 5 min.
