Forkinit: Gorgonzola Chive Sauce

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

1&1/2 c. heavy cream
2 oz. Gorgonzola cheese, crumbled
1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. pepper
1 Tbs. copped fresh chives

Instructions

Bring heavy cream to boil in small saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook @ a low boil, stirring occasionally, until mixture becomes thick & creamy, about 20 min. Remove pan from heat, add rest of ingredients & whisk rapidly until cheese melts. Use over steak or burgers.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 25 min.
