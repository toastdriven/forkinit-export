Forkinit: Bacon-Cheddar Mashed Potatoes

By Cheryl Schnettler
Stove

Ingredients

2 lbs. small potatoes
1 c. milk
1/2 stick butter
1 c. shredded cheddar cheese
4 slices bacon, cooked & crumbled
1 scallion, sliced

Instructions

Simmer potatoes in salted water until tender, about 20 min.;drain. Cool slightly, the3n peel. Heat milk with butter. Mash potatoes with hot milk & stir in cheese. Top with bacon & scallions.
