Forkinit: Basil Mayonnaise

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1 c. light mayonnaise
1 tsp. grated lemon rind
1 Tbs. fresh lemon juice
1 c. loosely packed fresh basil leaves

Garlic-Chili Mayonnaise:
Omit lemon rind & basil. Substitute fresh lime juice for lemon juice. Stir together mayonnaise, juice & 2 Tbs. Asian garlic-chili sauce. Makes 1 cup.

Instructions

Process all ingredients in food processor, stopping to scrape down sides. Store in airtight container in refrigerator up to 1 wk.

Yields: 1&1/2 cups
Prep time: 10 min.
