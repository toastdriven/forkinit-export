Forkinit: Dee's Dark Chocolate Granola

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

* cooking spray
* 1/2 cup brown sugar
* 2 tablespoons Nutella
* 2 tablespoons honey
* 2 tablespoons butter
* 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 2 cups oats (such as Quaker OatsÂ®)
* 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 cup chopped nuts
* 1/4 cup dark chocolate chips
* 1/4 cup sweetened dried cranberries

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line a 9x13-inch baking dish with aluminum foil and spray with nonstick cooking spray.
2. Melt together the brown sugar, Nutella, honey, butter, and vanilla extract in a small saucepan over low heat. Stir until well combined; remove from heat.
3. Combine the oats, cinnamon, and salt in a large bowl. Pour the brown sugar mixture into bowl; stir well to combine. Stir in the nuts. Spoon into the prepared pan; pat down and spread evenly.
4. Bake in preheated oven until browned, 15 to 20 minutes. Mix in chocolate chips & cranberries after removing pan from oven.

Yields: 8 servings
Prep time: 30 min.
