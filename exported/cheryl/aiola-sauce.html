Forkinit: Aiola Sauce

By Cheryl Schnettler
A Mexican mayo sauce
Use on hamburgers, etc.

Ingredients

2 cups mayonnaise
1 tablespoon tequila
2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
3 tablespoons pickled jalapenos
1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
Kosher salt and freshly ground pepper

Instructions

Pulse all of the ingredients in a food processor until combined. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.

Yields: 1 cup
Prep time: 5 min.
