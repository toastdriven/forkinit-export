Forkinit: Chicken Hawaiian

By Cheryl Schnettler

Ingredients

1 lg. can chunk chicken breast
1/2 c. celery, diced
1 medium apple, chopped
1 c. pineapple chunks
2 tsp. toasted sesame seeds
5 Tbs. mayonnaise

Instructions

Combine all ingredients & chill well.

Prep time: 10 min.
