Forkinit: Arizona Pork Tenderloin

By Cheryl Schnettler
Oven

Ingredients

1 lb. pork tenderloin
1 Tbs. Arizona Dreaming

Sauce:
1 Tbs. canola oil
1 onion, chopped
1-2 Tbs. Arizona Dreaming
1&1/2 c. water, divided
1&1/2 c. chopped fresh tomatoes
2 tsp. honey
1-2 Tbs. fresh cilantro, minced
1/3 c. sour cream

Instructions

Preheat oven to 325 degrees. Wash pork, pat dry. Season with Arizona Dreaming. Place in roasting pan & cook until desired doneness (approx. 40-50 min.). While pork is in oven, prepare sauce.
Heat oil in large skillet over medium heat. Add onion & cook about 3 min. until translucent. Add Arizona Dreaming & stir. Then add water, tomatoes & honey. Cook 20 min., stirring frequently to break up tomatoes. After 20 min., add remaining water & cilantro. Pour sauce into medium bowl & gently mix in sour cream. Pour sauce over meat or serve in dipping bowl.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 20 min.
