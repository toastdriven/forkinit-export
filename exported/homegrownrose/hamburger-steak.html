Forkinit: Hamburger Steak

By homegrownrose
Something a little different with a classic ingredient.

Ingredients

Optional Grilled Onions Topping:
1 onion, cut into thin slices
2 Tbs. butter

For the Hamburger Steaks:
1 lb Ground Beef
1/2 package Onion Soup Mix
1 Tbs. garlic powder
1 tsp. black pepper
1 egg
1/4 cup flour
2 Tbs oil (give or take)

For the Gravy:
1 packet white gravy mix
Water for gravy

Instructions

Optional Grilled Onions Topping:
1. Before cooking steaks, put onions & butter in the same pan for steaks, on med heat
2. Cook onions until lightly browned and soft. Remove from pan.

For the Hamburger Steaks:
1. Mix together ground beef, 1/2 package onion soup mix, garlic powder, black pepper and egg.
2. Form into thin patties and lightly dredge in flour.
3. Heat oil on med to med/hi heat in same pan onions were cooked in.
4. When oil is hot, brown patties on both sides, making sure it is cooked through.
5. Remove from pan.

For the Gravy:
1. Use the same pan that the onions & steaks were cooked in.
2. Mix gravy according to package directions
3. Use a metal whisk to stir gravy while it heats, stirring in bits from the pan.

To Serve:
Top with gravy, and then grilled onions. Enjoy! :)

Yields: 4 steaks
Prep time: 30 minutes
