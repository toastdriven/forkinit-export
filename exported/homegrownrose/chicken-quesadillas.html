Forkinit: Chicken Quesadillas

By homegrownrose
Simple, yet impressive, chicken quesadillas!

Ingredients

Flour Tortillas
Chicken Breasts (boneless skinless)
Colby-Jack Cheese
Cumin
Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

Boil Chicken Breasts until cooked through (plan on one per quesadilla).
When cooked, remove from water and shred / finely chop chicken.
Mix chicken with 1 Tbs cumin per breast and add salt & pepper to taste.
Warm up skillet on medium heat.
Place a layer of cheese, then chicken, then cheese between two tortillas and add to pan.
Cook until tortilla is browned on one side. Flip.
While second side is browning, press down with spatula to disperse melted cheese.
Serve with salsa and enjoy!

Yields: 4
Prep time: 20 minutes
