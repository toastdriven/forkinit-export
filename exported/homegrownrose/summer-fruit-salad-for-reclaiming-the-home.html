Forkinit: Summer FRUIT Salad for Reclaiming The Home

By homegrownrose
My grandma's "famous" stained glass fruit salad! Its refreshing, sweet, and colorful. Can't beat that!

Ingredients

1 Can of cherry pie filling
Bananas
Frozen or fresh Strawberries
Canned or fresh pineapple
3 cans mandarin oranges
1 bunch grapes

Instructions

Mix all ingredients and refrigerate. Best served after having chilled for 24 hours to marry flavors, but can be served immediately. Whipped cream topping, optional!

Yields: 12
Prep time: 10 minutes
