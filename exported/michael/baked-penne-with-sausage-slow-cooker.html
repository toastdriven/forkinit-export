Forkinit: Baked Penne with Sausage (Slow Cooker)

By Michael Metts
A ridiculously simple recipe that can feed an army. If you can get a hold of fresh Italian sausage, it's even better.

Ingredients

4 cups uncooked penne pasta
2 cups pasta sauce
2 fresh italian sausages, cut into pieces
1/2 medium onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
pinch of crushed red pepper
1/2 cup grated mozzarella or provolone cheese
1/2-3/4 cup water
Parmesan cheese, for sprinkling

Instructions

Spray the bottom and edges of a 4 quart crockpot insert with non-stick cooking spray.

Rinse pasta in a colander for added moisture. Pour pasta, sauce, sliced sausages, onion, garlic, crushed red pepper, cheese and water in the slow cooker. Stir thoroughly. Cook on low 5-6 hours (or on high for 3-4 hours).

Yields: 4
Prep time: 15 minutes plus 3-6 hours of cooking.
