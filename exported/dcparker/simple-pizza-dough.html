Forkinit: Simple Pizza Dough

By Daniel Parker
Pretty much the basic when it comes to pizza dough. Work in your own spices and variants.

Ingredients

4 cups bread flour / all purpose flour (white)
1 1/4 cups lukewarm water
2 1/4 teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
2 tablespoons olive oil, extra virgin is best
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon sugar or honey
1/4 cup more milk or water (milk adds flavor)

Instructions

In a liquid measuring cup pour in the warm water, sugar/honey, and yeast. Stir it up, it should foam a little, and set it aside for several minutes.

In another larger mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.

Wait a few more minutes for the yeast mixture to do its thing.

Now, mix the yeast mixture, along with the extra milk/water into the flour bowl, and add the olive oil. Stir together using a wooden spoon or just clean hands.

Sprinkle a layer of flour on an open clean surface, and some on your clean, dry hands; then drop the dough on the flour and fold it in half about 40 times - you should now have a pretty smooth elastic rubbery dough ball.

Drop it in a bowl, wash your hands, wet them with olive oil and rub over the top of the dough to keep it from drying out. Drape a towel over the bowl and let it sit for 30 minutes to an hour.

Lightly oil your pizza pan, or sprinkle flour on it, or cornmeal. Whatever you're in the mood for.

Take the dough out and flatten it out on your pizza pan. Feel free to try the old spinning trick you've always wished you could do - it's easier than you'd think. ;)

Just as a reminder for your memory, here are some typical things you'd put on the pizza dough at this point:

+ Pizza sauce or any pasta sauce you think would work
+ Shredded mozzarella cheese, parmesan cheese
+ Green bell pepper
+ Pepperoni
+ Italian sausage
+ Mushrooms
+ Ham
+ Onions

Baking should take about 12-14 minutes, but keep your eye on it.

Yields: 1 LARGE or 2 medium Pizzas
Prep time: 45 minutes
