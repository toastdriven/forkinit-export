Forkinit: Apple Cereal Muslix

By Daniel Parker
Another amazing breakfast, best prepared the night before.

Ingredients

5-6 chopped apples
7 c oats
4 c milk
1/2 c orange juice
2/3 c honey
1 tsp cinnamon
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 c raisins

Instructions

1. Mix the milk, honey, and orange juice
2. Mix everything else
3. Refrigerate overnight
4. Eat in the morning with a smile :)

Yields: 4
Prep time: 15 min
