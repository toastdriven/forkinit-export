Forkinit: Portugese Bean and Sausage Soup

By melgertz
This is a very good soup but caution on the peppers, most times half a pepper is enough but you can make it as hot as you like.

Ingredients

2 Cans kidney beans
2 Medium ripe tomatoes
1 Large onion
1-2 Seranno peppers (small but deadly)
1- 12oz. can Whole stewed tomatoes
1 tbsp chili powder
1 tbsp black pepper
6 Cups Beef stock or consomme
175 ml. Ketchup
2 tbsp Red wine
175g Garlic sausage
1 Bay leaf
Salt, sugar and hot sauce to taste.

Instructions

Dice tomatoes, onions,and pepper. Slice sausage. very lightly saute tomatoes, onions, peppers and sausage with chili powder, pepper and bay leaf. Deglaze with red wine and add stewed tomatoes, ketchup and stock.Bring to low boil and then simmer at least one hour.Add kidney beans, sugar and hot sauce to taste.
