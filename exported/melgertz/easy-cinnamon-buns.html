Forkinit: Easy Cinnamon Buns

By melgertz
You can also make regular buns by just cutting down a couple of teaspoons of sugar and forming buns. You can also cut this recipe in half. For wholewheat buns sub. 2 cups wholewheat flour for 2 cups white.

Ingredients

3 cups lukewarm water
8 tablespoons sugar (5-6 for reg buns )
6 tablespoons oil
2 tablespoons yeast
1 teaspoon salt
8 cups flour
cinnamon bun- melted butter, brown sugar, nuts and or rasins and cinnamon

Instructions

Mix yeast and 4 cups flour, set aside.
In a large bowl whip eggs, sugar, oil and water.
Add flour yeast mixture, blend well and add the remaining flour and salt.
Mix well should be able to form a nice not too sticky ball, add more flour if nesscary. Place in a clean bowl, cover and let rise for 15 min. Punch down (DO NOT KNEAD)
Let rise another 15 min.cobvered. Then punch down and either roll out to a rectangle for Cinnamon buns or form into buns. If making cinnamon buns roll out rectangle and brush with melted butter, sprinkle brown sugarnuts and or rasins and lots of cinnamon. Roll into jelly roll and cut about 1 inch slices. Place into greased aprox 3-4 rectangle pans cover and let rise 1 hour, brush with more butter and cook in a 350 degree oven.



Yields: aprox 28 rolls
