Forkinit: Indian Spiced Yogurt Chicken

By Margaret Keisling

Ingredients

4 whole chicken legs

1 tablespoon of cornstarch

1 1/4 cup of plain yogurt

1 tablespoon of chili paste (or powder)

1/2 a cup of chopped mint

1 tablespoon of cumin

Salt and pepper

...if you don’t have the spices above try curry powder or just cumin and ginger...

Instructions

Mix everything above in a large zip-lock bag including chicken. Use your favorite cut. I used quarters and left them overnight in the sauce Refrigerate until you’re ready to cook.

Bake at 400 degrees for 40 min or until the juice runs clear when you cut into the chicken. It’s even better on the grill. (I loved the seasonings on this one, but would definitely recommend the grill over the oven.)

Yields: 4 quarters
Prep time: 5 minutes plus bake/grill time
