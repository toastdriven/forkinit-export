Forkinit: German Potato Salad

By Margaret Keisling
My grandmother's recipe. Not the healthiest dish ever, but oh, so delicious!

Ingredients

10-12 potatoes- peeled, cubed and cooked.
1/2-3/4 lb bacon
2 eggs
1 c sugar
1/2 c vinegar
1 T mustard
Salt & pepper

Instructions

Dice bacon and fry until crispy
Beat eggs and add sugar, vinegar, mustard and salt and pepper
Add egg mixture to bacon and fat.
Stir constantly until it boils
Pour over potatoes and stir together.
Serve warm.

Yields: 6-8 servings
Prep time: About 30 minutes
