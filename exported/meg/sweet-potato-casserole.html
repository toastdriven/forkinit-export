Forkinit: Sweet Potato Casserole

By Margaret Keisling
Per Karina's request, here's my Aunt Toy's famous recipe. =)

Ingredients

3-4 c. mashed sweet potatoes
4 T. butter
2 eggs
1/2 c. (or less) sugar
1 c. milk
1 t. vanilla

Topping:
mini marshmallows or
1/2 c. brown sugar & 1/2 - 1 c. chopped nuts

Instructions

Preheat Oven to 375.
Combine Sweet potatoes, butter, eggs, sugar, milk and vanilla in mixing bowl until smoothly blended. If using sugar and nuts as topping, add before baking, if marshmallows, add during last 5 minutes.
Bake for 35 minutes, serve, and enjoy!

Prep time: aprx 30 min to prep, (if cooking potatoes), and 35 min to bake
Source: Aunt Toy Coxey
