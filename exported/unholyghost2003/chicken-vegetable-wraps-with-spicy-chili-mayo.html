Forkinit: Chicken Vegetable wraps with Spicy Chili Mayo

By unholyghost2003

Ingredients

To Make the Wraps:
1 package (6-8) Whole Grain Tortillas
1 Ripe Tomato
1 Ripe Avocado
2 cups chopped Lettuce
2 breasts cooked chicken
Spicy Chili Mayo

For the Spicy Chili Mayo:
2/3 cup Mayonnaise (must be REAL Mayonnaise, not Miracle Whip type "salad dressing")
Sriracha sauce

Instructions

To make Spicy Chili Mayo:
Take 2/3 cup mayo and stir in 1-2 T Sriracha sauce (or until DELICIOUS)

To Make Wraps:
Dice the tomato
Chop the lettuce
Slice the Avocado
Slice the chicken breasts
Put two or three chicken slices on a whole grain tortilla, top with avocado, tomato and lettuce. Drizzle with Spicy Mayo. Enjoy!

Yields: 6-8 wraps
Prep time: 20 min
Source: The Spicy Mayo is from the Bon Appetit Pork Meatball Banh Mi recipe http://www.bonappetit.com/recipes/2010/01/pork_meatball_banh_mi
