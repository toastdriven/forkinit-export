Forkinit: Vegetarian Tamale Pie

By Sean Bleier
I know this goes against the spirit of the site, but this recipe need not be forked because it is perfect ;)

Ingredients

Filling:
1 red bell pepper
1 medium sized yellow onion
2 cloves of garlic
3 cans vegetarian refried beans
4 stalks of celery
2 tbsp. tomato paste
1 tsp. salt
1 tbsp. water
1 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. garlic powder
1/2 tsp lime juice
1 cup frozen corn
2 tbsp. olive oil
1 cup chedder cheese
3/4 cup of monterey jack cheese

Crust:
2 1/2 cold water
1 1/2 cups corn meal
1 tsp salt
1/2 chili powder

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350° F.

Filling:
Sauté chopped onions and minced garlic cloves in olive oil until lightly browned.
Mix in refried beans, tomato paste, and water and cook for a 3-5 minutes until everything is consistently mixed together and heated.
Mix in chopped celery, minced red bell peppers, and frozen corn.

Crust:
Mix water, corn meal, salt, and chili powder in a saucepan and bring to a boil, immediately reduce heat to low and stir continuously until mixture is thickened.
Grease a 9 x 13 glass baking dish.
Place the corn meal mixture in the baking dish and spread evenly on the bottom and sides.

Assembly:
Place filling into baking dish and spread evenly.
Mix and sprinkle shredded cheese atop the filling.

Bake:
Heat the oven to 350 F.
Cook for 20 minutes until cheese is melted and crust is golden brown on the top.

Yields: depends, how hard core are you? (6-10)
