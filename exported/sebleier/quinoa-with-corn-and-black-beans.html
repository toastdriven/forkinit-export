Forkinit: Quinoa with corn and black beans

By Sean Bleier
Something I threw together the other night that was really good.

Ingredients

1 cup of Quinoa
1 cup of frozen corn
1 yellow onion
1 can of black beans
2 cups of water
1 cup grated cheese

Instructions

Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a medium saucepan and cook the quinoa for 10-15 minutes until all or most of the water is absorbed.
Dice the onion and Saut√© in a little olive oil in a frying pan until lightly browned.
Add corn and beans and cook for 5 minutes.
Mix the contents of the frying pan into the saucepan.
Add the cheese and stir the contents until mixed in.

Yields: 2-4
Prep time: 15-20
