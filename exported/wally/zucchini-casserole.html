Forkinit: zucchini casserole

By Wally Metts
Goes together quickly and makes a whole meal. We often double this for company, an easy meal for sharing.

Ingredients

1 lb. sausage
1 medium onion, thinly sliced
5 small zucchini, chopped
1 garlic clove, crushed (can use 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder)
1 8oz. can tomato sauce
8 oz. fresh mushrooms, sliced
1 t. sweet basil
1/2 t. oregano
1/2 t. salt
1/2 cup grated cheddar or parmesan cheese

Instructions

Pan fry crumbled sausage.
Drain and add zucchini, onion and garlic.
Cook five minutes.
Add tomato sauce, mushrooms and seasonings.
Simmer 25 minute and sprinkle with cheese.

Yields: 4-6
Prep time: 35-40 minutes
