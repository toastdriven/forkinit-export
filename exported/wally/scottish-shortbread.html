Forkinit: Scottish Shortbread

By Wally Metts
Quick and easy. Dense and sweet. Great for a simple dessert, served with tea or coffee.

Ingredients

1/2 cup butter
1 1/4 cup flour
1/2 cup sugar

Instructions

Melt butter. Stir in flour and sugar. Press into pan. Prick with a fork. Bake at 350 for 15 minutes. Cool and cut.
