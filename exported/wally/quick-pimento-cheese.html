Forkinit: quick pimento cheese

By Wally Metts
A quick and dirty version of this southern classic.

Ingredients

2 8 oz. bags shredded cheddar cheese
1 1/2 cup mayonnaise.
1 teaspoon horseradish sauce or spread.

Instructions

Premix the horseradish in the mayonnaise. Fold into cheese. Enjoy. Serve on crackers or bread. Traditionally white bread, with no crust. :)

Prep time: 2 minutes
