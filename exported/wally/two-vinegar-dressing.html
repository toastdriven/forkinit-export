Forkinit: two-vinegar dressing

By Wally Metts
We found this with a recipe for arugula salad with pomegranate and toasted pecans, but it has become our simple salad dressing for for almost any tossed salad we make. We've doubled the recipe and keep it on hand for a quick dinner salad any time.

Ingredients

1 tablespoon red wine vinegar
3 tablespoons aged balsamic vinegar
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
1/4 teaspoon fresh ground pepper
12 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil

Instructions

Whisk everything together in a metal bowl, and then drizzle in the oil while still whisking until it emulsifies.

(The original salad was 2 bunches of arugula, 1/3 cup of pecans and 1/2 cup pomegranate seeds.)

Yields: 3 or 4 family-sized salads
Prep time: 5 minutes
