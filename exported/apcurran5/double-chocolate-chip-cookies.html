Forkinit: Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Peggy Curran
Delicious, chocolaty, soft cookies any chocolate lover cannot resist!!! Very yummy warm with a glass of cold milk!!!

Ingredients


Ingredients

1. Cream together:
1 c. butter (2 sticks)
1 1/4-1 1/2 c. brown sugar (works with 3/4 c. honey)
1 tsp. vanilla
2. Add & mix:
1 egg
3. Add & mix:
½ c. cocoa powder
2 Tb. milk
4. Add & mix:
1 ¾ c. whole wheat flour (I use 2 1/3 c. spelt flour)
¼ tsp. salt
¼ tsp. baking powder
5. Add & mix:
1-2 c. dark chocolate chips (we definitely do 2 cups!)
1 c. walnuts, broken into 1/4 " size pieces

Instructions

Instructions

Drop onto cookies sheets and bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes ( a little longer for spelt flour).
Cool 5 minutes on cookies sheets, then place on counter top to cool.
Yield: 3 ½ dozen
