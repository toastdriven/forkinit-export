Forkinit: Salmon loaf

By Peggy Curran

Ingredients

1 can salmon
2 eggs
1/2 c plain yogurt (or plain Greek)
2 tbsp lemon juice
1 small can fried onions 2/3 in recipe 1/3 to top)

Instructions

Combine ingredients after drawing salmon (ok to leave in bones, just crush them)
bake at 325 about 45 minutes in a sprayed loaf pan
To make a larger serving, I triple for 2 loaf pans

Yields: 1small loaf oan or 2 pans if tripled
Prep time: 15-20
