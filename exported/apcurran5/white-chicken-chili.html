Forkinit: White chicken chili

By Peggy Curran

Ingredients

1 large jar of Randall's Northern beans
1 16 oz jar of hot salsa
2 small or 1 large can of chicken, or shredded chicken from a leftover chicken

Instructions

Put in a pit and heat
Serve with sour cream, shredded cheese and Fritos or crackers

Yields: 4 servings+
Prep time: 10 minutes
