Forkinit: Whole wheat bread (kitchen aid)

By Peggy Curran

Ingredients

2 1/8 cup hot water
1/4 c oil
1/4 c honey ( I use one half molasses)
1 1/4 tsp salt
1 tbsp soy lecithin
1/8 c vital gluten
400 mg vit c powder
1 heaping tbsp + 1 heaping tsp yeast
4-7 cups of whole wheat flour

Instructions

Combine 1st seven ingredients on low adding about 2 c of flour, add yeast, when well mixed switch to a dough hook. Add flour until dough stops stocking to bowl sides. let run until 7-9 minutes. Form 2 loaves put in well sprayed pans
Bake at warm oven setting (150-180) 25 minutes
Bake at 350 another 25-30 minutes til brown.
Butter or oil top of crust while hot.

Yields: 2 loaves
Prep time: 20 minutes
