Forkinit: Black bean and corn salsa

By Peggy Curran

Ingredients

1 16oz. Can of salsa
1 or 2 cans of black beans
1 can of whole corn

Instructions

Drain and rinse black beans, drain corn, stir together all 3 ingredients
Serve with Tirtilla chips, or use in tortilla wraps

Yields: 6 servings
Prep time: 5 minutes
