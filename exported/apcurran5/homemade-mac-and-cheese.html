Forkinit: Homemade Mac and cheese

By Peggy Curran

Ingredients

1/2 stick butter melted in bottom of a 13x9 pan
2 1/2 - 3 cups uncooked macaroni
12-16oz sharp cheddar cheese
(Stir together)
Pepper to taste
4 1/2 cups milk ( do not stir)

Instructions

Stir cheese and macaroni until well mixed, pepper to taste , cover with milk and bake (uncovered) at 325 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes
For a heartier version add 2 cans of tuna, or a pound of cooked ground meat or sausage.

Yields: 6-8 as main dish, 8-12 as side dish
Prep time: 10-15 minutes
