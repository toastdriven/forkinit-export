Forkinit: Foamy Pumpkin Bran Parfaits

By Audrey Feldroy

Ingredients

4 c cardboardy bran cereal (e.g. Fiber One)
2 c vanilla yogurt
1 c canned or cooked pumpkin puree, refrigerated
1/4 c light cream cheese
2 egg whites (use pasteurized if desired)
4 tbsp honey

Instructions

Using an electric mixer at high speed, whip egg whites and honey until stiff peaks form.

In a larger separate bowl, mix pumpkin puree and cream cheese at medium-high speed. Add egg white mixture to this bowl and lightly mix by hand, just until combined.

Layer bran cereal, yogurt, and pumpkin cheese mixture in clear glasses.
