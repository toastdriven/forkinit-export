Forkinit: Lemon Poppyseed Watermelon Cake

By Audrey Feldroy
This cake is very dense because we didn't have baking powder or baking soda. It's also low fat and low sugar. I would not make this if I were you. But if you are dieting and are used to weird foods and unsweet cakes, then try it.

Ingredients

2 lemons
1 tbsp poppyseeds
1 container vanilla probiotic yogurt
1/4 c brown sugar
1/2 c watermelon, seedless
1 c flour
1/4 c soy milk

Instructions

Mix them in the food processor, adding the poppyseeds at the last minute. Bake at 350 degrees for 30 min.

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 15 min
