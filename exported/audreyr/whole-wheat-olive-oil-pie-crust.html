Forkinit: Whole wheat olive oil pie crust

By Audrey Feldroy

Ingredients

2 1/4 c whole wheat flour
1/2 c olive oil
1 tsp salt
1/4 tsp black pepper (optional)

Instructions

Mix dry ingredients together. Add olive oil and mix by hand or in food processor.

Roll out dough between 2 sheets of wax paper.

Yields: 1 double 8-9" pie crust, or 2 single 8-9" crusts
Prep time: 5 min
