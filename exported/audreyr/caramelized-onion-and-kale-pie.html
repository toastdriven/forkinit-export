Forkinit: Caramelized Onion and Kale Pie

By Audrey Feldroy

Ingredients

1 bunch kale
1 tomato
1 onion
1 can tomato soup
Olive oil spray
1 tbsp olive oil
Salt
Pepper
Pie crust (preferably whole wheat)

Instructions

Wash kale and tear up. Spray with olive oil and bake at 400 for 5-10 min or until slightly crisped.

Slice onion and saute in olive oil until caramelized.

Layer ingredients in pie. Bake at 375 for 35-50 min.

Yields: 6 servings
Prep time: 30 min
