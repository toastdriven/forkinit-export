Forkinit: Spaghetti with Sweet Sausage-Onion Sauce

By Audrey Feldroy

Ingredients

3 tomatoes
1 Vidalia onion
1 can tomato soup
2 bratwursts
1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
Olive oil cooking spray
1/2 lb (1/2 box) vermicelli (thin spaghetti)
2 qts water
1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

Bring water and salt to a boil. Add pasta and cook 6 minutes or until al dente. Drain.

Chop onion and tomatoes.

Fry bratwursts in olive oil cooking spray until cooked through. Chop into bite-sized chunks.

Heat olive oil in large saucepan. Add onions and cook for 5 minutes or until softened. Add tomatoes and tomato soup. Simmer 1-2 minutes. Add bratwurst pieces. Simmer 5-10 min.

Arrange pasta on plates as nests, leaving room in the middle. Generously ladle sauce into centers of plates.

Yields: 2-4 servings
Prep time: 25 min
