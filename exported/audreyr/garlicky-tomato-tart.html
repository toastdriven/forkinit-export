Forkinit: Garlicky Tomato Tart

By Audrey Feldroy

Ingredients

2-3 tomatoes
2/3 c sharp cheddar
1/2 head garlic
Pate Brisee crust

Instructions

Flash bake crust at 425 for 6-10 min.

Chop garlic and saute in olive oil until slightly crisp. Slice tomatoes into thin circles (1/4"). Layer into pie and bake at 400 for 30 min or until edges are browned.

Yields: 6-8 servings
