Forkinit: Griddle Foccacia

By Audrey Feldroy
Foccacia dough is made in a breadmaker, then stretched on the griddle to finish.

Ingredients

3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
2 tsp active dry yeast
1 tsp salt
2 tbsp olive oil
1 cup water
1 tbsp McCormick Bruschetta seasoning mix

Instructions

Put all ingredients into bread machine.
Start dough cycle.
When dough is ready, brush olive oil on the griddle.
Stretch dough out onto the griddle.
Cook one side, then flip and cook other side.
Cut into rectangles and enjoy.

Yields: 8
Prep time: 5 min
