Forkinit: Pulled Pork Toast Squares Appetizer

By Audrey Feldroy

Ingredients

Pulled pork (store-bought or homemade)
1 onion
1 tbsp olive oil
1 c sharp cheddar, shredded
1 loaf wheat or spelt bread

Instructions

Slice onion and caramelize in olive oil.

Arrange pulled pork on bread. Top with caramelized onions and cheddar. Bake at 425 for 10 min or until edges of toast are browned. Cut into bite-sized pieces.
