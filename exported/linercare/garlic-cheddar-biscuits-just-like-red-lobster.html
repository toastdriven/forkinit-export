Forkinit: Garlic Cheddar Biscuits (just like Red Lobster)

By linercare
cheesy garlicy buttery goodness in a biscuit.

Ingredients

2 cups flour
1 tablespoon Baking Powder
1/3 teaspoon salt
1/3 cup shortening (or butter or margarine)
1 cup milk
1 cup cheddar cheese
1 clove garlic OR 1 teaspoon garlic powder/salt ~This can depend also on how much garlic you want to taste.
2 tablespoons butter
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Instructions

Set oven to 450.

Mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Using a pastry knife (or a fork like I generally do) cut in the shortening. After the shortening is mixed in (looks like little peas) pour in all the milk at once and mix. Add in cheese and garlic.

Drop mounds of batter on cooking sheet using teaspoon about an inch apart. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.

Soften butter till almost melted. Mix butter and garlic powder. Brush on biscuits after you take them out.

Yields: about 12 - depending on how large you make the mounds
Prep time: about 10 minutes
Source: partly from Better Homes & Garden cookbook, the rest from trial and error
