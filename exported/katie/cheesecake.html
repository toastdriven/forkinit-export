Forkinit: Cheesecake

By Katie Metts
A yummy baked cheesecake

Ingredients

2 egg yolks
1 1/2 cups sugar
1 pkg. cream cheese
1/2 c. butter, softened
2 tsp. vanilla
1 c. sifted flour
1 tsp. baking powder; sift with flour
3 egg whites, stiffly beaten
Orange rind
Strawberries or other berries/fruit for topping

Instructions

Beat the egg yolks until they thicken slightly.
Add 1/2 cup sugar and beat until thick and lemon-colored.
Add the cream cheese, butter and sugar, a small amount of each, alternately, beating constantly.
Beat in the vanilla and orange rind.
Fold in the flour mixture carefully until just combined.
Stir in 1/3 of the egg whites.
Fold in the remainder egg whites.

Place the batter in a well-buttered and floured 8" spring form cake pan.
Tp the on on the counter a few times to settle the batter.
Bake in a 350 degree oven for 1 hour or until the cake tests done.

Let cake cool in the pan for 15 minutes before removing from pan to cool further on a wire rack.
Slice strawberries or prepare your preferred topping.
When ready to serve, place cake upside down on a serving platter.
Top with fruit.

Yields: 10-12 pie-shaped pieces
Prep time: 1 hour
