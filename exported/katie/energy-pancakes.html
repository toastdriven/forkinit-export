Forkinit: Energy Pancakes

By Katie Metts
Very filling and substantial

Ingredients

1 egg
2 tablespoons honey (optional)
1 tablespoon baking powder
1/4 teaspoon vanilla
1/4 cup raisins
1/4 cup sesame seeds
1/4 cup bran or ground flax seed
2 1/2 cups milk
1 1/4 cups flour
2 tablespoons oil
3/4 cup rolled oats
1/4 cup sunflower seeds
1/4 cup wheat germ or ground flax seed
1 apple, shredded

Instructions

In a large bowl, combine egg, milk and honey. Sift flour with baking powder and add to egg mixture along with oil and vanilla and blend well.
Mix in oats, raisins, sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, wheat germ, bran and shredded apple. Refrigerate over night.
In morning spoon batter on hot griddle. Cook until browned, turn and cook other side.
Batter keeps about a week.
Serve with applesauce or fresh fruit.

Yields: Serves 4-6
