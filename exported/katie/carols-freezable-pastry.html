Forkinit: Carol's Freezable Pastry

By Katie Metts
Great recipe for excellent flaky crust. Freezes well to use at a later date.

Ingredients

5 cups flour
2 1/2 cups shortening/Crisco
1 beaten egg
2 tablespoons vinegar
1 tablespoon salt
Water

Instructions

Cut shortening into flour and salt until crumbly. Beat egg in a measuring cup, add vinegar, and fill cup with cold water. Add to flour. Mix and chill well. Roll out dough thinly on floured board to give a flakier crust.
This dough freezes well. Allow to thaw before rolling. It retains its flakiness and rolls with ease.

Yields: 3 double crusts for 9"pies
Prep time: 15 minutes
Source: Michigan Nurses Cook Cookbook, 1970
