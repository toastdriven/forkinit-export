Forkinit: Gazpacho With Goat Cheese

By Katie Metts

Ingredients

* 2 1/2 pounds beefsteak tomatoes, roughly chopped
* 2 small cloves garlic, peeled
* 1/2 small sweet onion, roughly chopped
* 2 red bell peppers, roughly chopped
* 1 pound Kirby cucumbers, peeled and roughly chopped
* 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
* kosher salt and pepper
* 3 ounces goat cheese, crumbled
* 1 cup fresh corn kernels (from 2 ears)
* 3 tablespoons fresh cilantro, roughly chopped
* 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
* crusty bread (optional)

Instructions

1. Working in batches, place the tomatoes, garlic, onion, bell peppers, and cucumbers in a food processor and pulse until almost pureed.
2. Transfer to a large bowl and stir in the lemon juice, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, and 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
3. Ladle the soup into individual bowls and top with the goat cheese, corn, and cilantro. Drizzle with the olive oil and serve with the crusty bread (if using).

Yields: 4-6 servings
Prep time: 20 minutes
