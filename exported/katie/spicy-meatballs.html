Forkinit: Spicy Meatballs

By Katie Metts
Good, simple, tasty, easy to freeze
My son, Christian, remarked that this recipe tasted like lasagna without the pasta.

Ingredients

2 pounds lean ground beef
2 pounds ground pork
1 medium onion, minced
2 slices soft bread, crusts removed and torn into pieces
5 cloves garlic, minced
2 eggs
1/2 cup chopped parsley
1/4 cup olive oil
1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
1 tablespoon kosher salt
1 1/2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
3 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper
1 1/2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
1 jar store-bought marinara sauce or Chunky Marinara Sauce

Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. In a large bowl, combine the first 10 ingredients, 1 cup of the Parmesan cheese, and 2 teaspoons of the pepper. Mix just to combine. Shape the mixture into meatballs. Each ball should consist of about 1/4 heaping cup of meat. Place on a baking pan and set aside.
2. In a medium bowl, combine the ricotta with the remaining Parmesan and the remaining pepper; set aside.
3. Bake the meatballs for 20-30 minutes in upper third of oven. Remove from oven, drain off the oil from the mean. Turn on broiler. Spoon 1 tablespoon of the ricotta mixture onto each meatball. Broil 3-5 minutes or until the ricotta just starts to brown. Serve the meatballs with jarred marinara sauce, or Chunky Marinara Sauce.

Yields: 20-24 meatballs; serves 8-10 people
Prep time: 20-30 minutes
