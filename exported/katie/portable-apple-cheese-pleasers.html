Forkinit: Portable Apple & Cheese Pleasers

By Katie Metts
High protein breakfast cookies, a favorite with children; low in sugar

Ingredients

3/4 cup all-purpose flour
2/3 cup butter or margarine, softened
1/3 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 1/2 cups oatmeal, old fashioned, uncooked
1 cup (4 oz) shredded Cheddar cheese
3/4 cup raisins or currants
1 cup chopped apple

Instructions

Combine flour, butter, sugar, eff, vanilla, cinnamon, baking powder and salt in a large bowl; mix well.
Add oats, cheese and raisins or currants; mix well Stir in apple.
Drop by heaping tablespoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet; bake in preheated moderate oven (375 degreses F) 15 minutes or until golden brown.
Store in tightly covered container in refrigerator or in loosely covered container at room temperature.

Yields: about 2 dozen
Source: Quaker Oats Wholegrain Cookbook
