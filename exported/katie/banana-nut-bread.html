Forkinit: Banana Nut Bread

By Katie Metts
A recipe I have enjoyed making for years

Ingredients

1/2 cup shortening or butter
1 1/3 cups sugar
2 large bananas
2 tablespoons milk
2 eggs
1 3/4 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon soda
1 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
1 teaspoon nutmeg
1 cup chopped nuts

Instructions

Cream butter and sugar.
Add bananas and milk; mix well with mixer.
Add eggs and blend.
Mix together flour, soda, baking powder, salt and nutmeg. Add to other ingredients.
Fold in nuts.
Put into baking loaf pan; bake at 350 degrees for 30 minutes, then 325 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until done. (Use toothpick to test.)

Yields: 1 loaf
