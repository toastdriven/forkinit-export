Forkinit: Chicken Salad

By Katie Metts
nicely seasoned; options and additions make it a delight to serve

Ingredients

4 cups bite-size pieces of cooked chicken
2 teaspoons grated onion or chopped scallions (optional)
1 cup thinly sliced celery
2/3 cup mayonnaise
1/4 heavy cream
2 tablespoons vinegar
1/8 teaspoon freshly ground pepper
Salt to taste
Romaine lettuce

Instructions

Put the chicken in a bowl and add the onion or scallions (if you like) and celery. Combine the mayonnaise, cream and vinegar and blend well. Add pepper and salt, and toss with the chicken until well mixed. Make a bed of the lettuce leaves and spoon the chicken salad over them.

Variations and Additions:
Sprinkle 1 cup chopped almonds over salad.
Instead of the celery, add 1 cup seedless grapes to chicken mixture.
Add 2 teaspoons curry powder to the mayonnaise.
Omit the celery and add 1/2 cup peeled, seeded, diced cucumber and 1/2 cup pineapple bits.
Add 1 cup diced, unpeeled red apple and 1/2 cup chopped walnuts.

Yields: Serves
Prep time: 30 minutes approximately
Source: Fannie Farmer Cookbook. 1990 edition
