Instructions

Stir in Cheddar and chives, then add buttermilk and 2 T. water; knead gently just until blended. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and pat into and 8: squar Cut into 2" squares then cut squares into triangles. Arrange scones on baking sheet, spacing 1" apart.
Brush tops with egg water mixture and bake until golden, about 25 minutes.
