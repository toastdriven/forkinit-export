Forkinit: Mushroom Salad

By Katie Metts

Ingredients

2 8-ounce packages, mushrooms, sliced
1 1/2 cups shredded Jarlsberg or Swiss cheese
1/2 cup parsley, fresh flat-leaf, chopped
3 tablespoons lemon juice
3 tablespoons olive oil
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/4 teaspoon pepper

Instructions

Toss together mushrooms, cheese and parsley.
In a small bowl, combine lemon juice, oil, salt and pepper. Whisk together well. Pour over mushroom mixture. Toss, coating mushroom mixture well. Chill for 1/2 hour if possible. (optional)
