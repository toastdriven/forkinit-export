Forkinit: Peanut Butter/Chocolate Spread (like Nutella!)

By Katie Metts
Another great snack designed with Diabetics and Candida dieters in mind. This goes great on Wasa crisp bread/crackers (low carbs). Also wonderful sweet dip with celery, bananas, apples, etc.

Kids and adults alike will enjoy this healthy spreadable desert!

Ingredients

1/2 cup all natural Peanut Butter

1 1/2 tablespoons Ghirardelli's 100% all natural baking cocoa powder

2 NOW brand Soy Lecithin soft gel capsules

less than 1/8 teaspoon Stevia

1 teaspoon Vanilla

1/2 cup plain/no sugar added Soy Milk

(for specific notes on preferred quality of ingredients refer to my Ghirardelli Fudgesicle recipe, also found on this website)

Instructions

1. Start by putting the 1/2 cup peanut butter into a medium size mixing bowl.
2. Add the cocoa powder and stevia, mix in thoroughly (using sturdy fork and/or small spatula).
3. Set 2 lecithin capsules in a small dish of HOT tap water; as lecithin begins seeping from capsule, add to the bowl by squeezing capsules. (This can get messy, but I haven't found another way to do this. To get lecithin off hands, I immediately squirt hand/dish soap on hands, rub vigorously, and rinse)
4. Add a teaspoon of vanilla, mix in.
5. Add the Soy Milk, and stir carefully until smoothly blended. It won't stir smoothly at first and has a tendency to fling contents out of bowl. :)
6. Spread on a cracker, veggie, or fruit. Or just eat with a spoon!
7. Enjoy!

*8. If you like coconut, you can also add non-sweetened coconut shavings to the amount that you like. That tastes wonderful as well. :)

Yields: 8 servings: serving size 2Tbsp or 1/8cup
Prep time: 10min
