Forkinit: Yogurt Glaze

By benspaulding

Ingredients

1 1/2 cups vanilla nonfat yogurt
3 tablespoons brown sugar
1 teaspoon lemon juice

Instructions

Stir until smooth and spread on whatever you want, though it is best if what you want is cool.

Yields: Some
Prep time: Not much
