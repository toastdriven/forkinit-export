Forkinit: Egg Salad

By benspaulding
A little mix that we love to have on toast for breakfast, lunch or dinner.

Ingredients

* Eggs (five does a good lunch for us)
* Salad dressing or Mayonnaise
* Mustard
* Salt & pepper

Instructions

* Boil eggs
* Remove shells
* Put eggs in a mixing bowl and begin to mash with a potato masher or fork
* Add salad dressing to desired consistency
* Add a little mustard
* Salt and pepper to taste
* Finish mashing and stirring mixture
* Refrigerate

Yields: As much as you make. More makes more salad. :)
