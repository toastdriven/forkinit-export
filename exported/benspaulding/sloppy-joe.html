Forkinit: Sloppy Joe

By benspaulding
This was actually known as “barbecue” in my family.

Ingredients

1 lb ground beef
1/2 tsp onion flakes
1 can tomato soup
1/3 cup brown sugar
1/3 cup ketchup

Instructions

Brown beef and onion flakes
Drain beef
Stir beef, soup, sugar and ketchup.

Add more or less brown sugar to taste.
