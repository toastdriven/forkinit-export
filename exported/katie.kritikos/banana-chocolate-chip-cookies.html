Forkinit: Banana chocolate chip cookies

By Katie
Very puffy cookies—and they're good for you because they have bananas in them, right?

Ingredients

8 tablespoons (1 stick) softened butter
1 banana
1 cup sugar
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/4 teaspoon baking powder
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 1/4 cup white all-purpose flour
1 cup chopped walnuts
1 cup chocolate chips

Instructions

Preheat oven to 375º F.
Mash bananas and beat with softened butter.
Add sugar and mix.
Add remaining ingredients except walnuts and chocolate chips and mix thoroughly.
Stir in walnuts and chocolate chips.
Drop batter onto cookie sheets—1.5 to 2 tablespoons per drop.
Bake at 375º for 12-14 minutes.

Yields: About 2 dozen
