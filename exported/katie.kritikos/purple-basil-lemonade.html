Forkinit: Purple basil lemonade

By Katie
It's pink!

Ingredients

4 cups water
1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (about 2-3 lemons)
1/2 cup loosely packed purple basil leaves
6 tablespoons sugar
ice

Instructions

Process the basil, sugar and 1/4 cup water in food processor or blender.
Combine remaining water and juice in a bowl. Add sugar mixture; stir until sugar dissolves.
Strain mixture through a sieve into a pitcher; discard solids.
Serve over ice.

Yields: 4 cups
