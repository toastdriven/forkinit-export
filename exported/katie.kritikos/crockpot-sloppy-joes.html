Forkinit: Crockpot sloppy joes

By Katie
These taste awesome and feed a large crowd. Really good with mac & cheese.

Ingredients

4-4.5 lbs ground beef
1 small to medium onion, chopped
1 medium to large green bell pepper, chopped
1 can (26-28 ounces) tomato sauce
2 cans (10.75 ounces) condensed tomato soup, undiluted
1 cup packed brown sugar
1/4 cup ketchup
3 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon dry mustard
1 tablespoon prepared mustard
1.5 teaspoons chili powder
1 teaspoon garlic powder
Toasted hamburger buns

Instructions

Brown meat in large skillet over medium-high heat, stirring to break up the meat. Drain.
Add onion and bell pepper; cook and stir 5-6 minutes over medium heat or until onion is translucent.
Transfer meat mixture to 4- or 5-quart slow cooker. Add remaining ingredients except buns; stir until well blended.
Cover; cook on low 4 to 6 hours or high 2 to 3 hours. Serve on buns.

Yields: 20
Prep time: 30 min to 1 hour prep time before slow cooking
