Forkinit: Mojito cookies

By Katie
Greenish sugar cookies with a fresh citrus kick

Ingredients

3 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking soda
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/3 cup mint leaves, not packed
zest of 1 lemon
zest of 1 lime
1 teaspoon lemon extract
1.5 teaspoons lime extract (or 1-2 more lime zests if you can't find lime extract)
1/2 cup butter
1 cup sugar
1 egg
1/2 cup milk

Instructions

Sift or whisk flour, baking soda and baking powder in a small bowl. Set aside.
In a food processor, combine mint, zests, extracts, butter and sugar. Mix until fluffy. Add egg and mix until fully incorporated. Remove mixture to a large bowl.
To the wet mixture, add the flour mixture alternately with the milk to make a soft dough. Wrap dough in plastic and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
Preheat oven to 450ยบ. Liberally flour surface and dough while working with it. Roll out dough to about 1/8 to 1/4 inch thick. Cut with cookie cutters.
Transfer to ungreased baking sheets. Bake 10-12 minutes, until starting to brown. Transfer cookies to wire rack soon after removing from oven to cool.

Yields: 4 dozen or so, depending on cookie cutter size
