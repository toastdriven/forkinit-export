Forkinit: E-Z FREEZER POPS

By Helen
Delicious

Ingredients

1c Unsweetened Greek Yogurt
1/2c Milk
1/4c Peanut Butter
3T Chopped Chocolate Chips
1 Chopped Banana

Instructions

Blend yogurt,milk and peanut butter in a blender until smooth.
Ster in chocolatechips and banana
Fill Freeze And Enjoy

Yields: This should fit 6 molds. (each mold holds about 1/2 cup)
