Forkinit: Vegetarian hot dogs

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

1 c. Cooked garbanzo beans
1-2 slices of bread
1 egg
1/4 t. Garlic, minced
1/4 t. Red peppers diced
1/2 t. Salt
Pinch of pepper

Instructions

Grind all ingredients in blender or food processor.
Shape mixture in about 10 patties or hot dogs.
Chill for an hour
Fry until browned and serve with ketchup and mustard
