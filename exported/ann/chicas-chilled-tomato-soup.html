Forkinit: chica's chilled tomato soup

By Ann Metts

Ingredients


2 tomatoes, diced
1 cucumber, diced
1 pepper (red, yellow, or green) seeded and diced
46oz. or 1 large can of tomato or vegetable juice

Instructions

Have your Sproutlet help mash up the diced tomato in a bowl. (lightly pulse in blender if desired)

Add diced cucumber and diced pepper to the bowl.

Pour in the tomato or vegetable juice and stir.

Chill for 20-30 minutes in the refrigerator.

If juice is already chilled, serve immediately.
