Forkinit: White chili

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

1T. oil
2 med. onions (chopped)
2 minced garlic cloves
3 cups chicken broth (I use chicken broth from boiling a whole chicken and I add chicken bouillon to it.)
2T. cilantro (I use more cilantro than this recipe calls for)
2T. lime juice
1t. cumin
1/2t. oregano
1/4t. red pepper sauce
1/4t. salt
11 oz. can corn (I use frozen corn)
15 to 16 oz. can great northern beans
15 to 16 oz. can butter beans
2 cups cooked and chopped chicken (I boil a whole chicken)
 

Instructions

Heat oil in pot. over medium heat cook onion and garlic. Stir in remaining ingredients except chicken.
Heat to boiling; reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes uncovered. Stir in chicken; simmer until hot.
serve with: shredded cheese, tortilla chips, chopped green onions, diced tomatoes, chopped cilantro, avocado, sour cream and whatever else you think would be yummy with this meal.
