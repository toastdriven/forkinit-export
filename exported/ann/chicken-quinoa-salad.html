Forkinit: Chicken Quinoa Salad

By Ann Metts

Ingredients



Suitable for Stages 1, 2 and 3 of the diet.
For 2 servings:
1 chicken breast
2/3 cup of cooked quinoa
2 cups of spinach
2 medium tomatoes
1/2 a cucumber
1 avocado
2 shallots
1 garlic clove
Juice of 1/2 lemon
2 tablespoons olive oil
Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

Cook the quinoa as directed. Chop up chicken, pan-fry until cooked, about 5 minutes. Now chop up the veggies, toss everything in a bowl and serve.
