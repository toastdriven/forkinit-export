Forkinit: bisquits

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

3 cups White Lily or Martha White flour (If using self-rising flour, omit baking powder.)

1 Tbsp baking powder

1 Tbsp sugar (add 1 more Tbsp if making biscuits for shortbread)

6 Tbsp butter, frozen and shaved with vegetable grater

1 ¼ cups + 1 Tbsp buttermilk or regular milk

Instructions

Preheat oven to 450 degrees.
Sift flour and sugar into a mixing bowl, and cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
Transfer the dough to a well-floured surface and knead lightly for about 20 strokes. Pat dough into a rectangle about ½” thick. Cut into 2” rounds and place on an ungreased baking sheet. Gather up the scraps and repeat the process to make more biscuits.
(3a.) If you prefer, spoon the batter on an ungreased sheet instead of kneading dough and patting into rectangle.

Bake until the tops and bottoms both are golden brown, about 10-12 minutes.

Yields: Makes about 12 biscuits.
