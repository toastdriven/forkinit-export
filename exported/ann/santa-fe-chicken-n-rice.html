Forkinit: Santa Fe chicken n rice

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

1/2-1 lb. cooked & diced chicken ($1-2)
1 1/2 cups rice
1 tsp cumin
1 tsp chili powder
1/4 tsp. Onion powder
1/4 tsp. Garlic powder
Salt
Pepper
2 cups chicken broth
1 15 oz. can diced tomatoes, Rotel if you like it spicy
1 15 oz. can black beans
1 15 oz. can corn
1 lb. oranges, cut into wedges

Instructions

Prepare rice according to the package directions, however, replace the water with chicken stock and add cumin, chili powder, onion powder, garlic powder,
Add all remaining ingredients, except orange slices, into the rice mixture. Heat until warmed through. Sprinkle with cheddar cheese if desired. Serve with orange slices on the side.
