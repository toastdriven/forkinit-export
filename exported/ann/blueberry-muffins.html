Forkinit: Blueberry muffins

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

2 ½ cups flour
2 tsp baking powder
½ tsp salt
½ tsp ground cinnamon
2 ripe bananas into chunks
2 eggs
3/4 cup soft brown sugar
1/3 cup softened butter
1 tsp vanilla extract
1 cup washed blueberries

Instructions

Makes 14.
Preheat oven to 375F

Sift together (2 ½ cups) flour, 2 tsp baking powder, ½ tsp salt, ½ tsp ground cinnamon.
Cut 2 ripe bananas into chunks, and puree in a blender.
Add 2 eggs, (3/4) cup soft brown sugar, (1/3 cup) softened butter and 1 tsp vanilla extract and blend again.
Pour this mixture into the flour mix, and stir well to combine. Gently fold through 1 cup washed blueberries. Spoon the mixture into greased muffin tins and bake for 25 minutes until risen and golden brown. Cool in the tin for 10 mins, then put on a wire rack to finish cooling.
