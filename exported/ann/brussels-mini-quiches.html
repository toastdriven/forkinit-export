Forkinit: brussel's mini quiches

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

3 eggs
3/4 c. skim milk
¼ c. shredded Parmesan cheese
Pinch of black pepper
Pinch of salt
½ cup chopped Brussels sprouts (fresh or frozen)
Cooking spray




Instructions

Preheat oven to 350° F (176° C).
Chop Brussels sprouts into bite-size pieces and place in microwave-safe dish with 1 tsp water.
Microwave for 2-3 minutes or until soft and set aside.
Combine eggs, milk, black pepper, and salt in mixing bowl.
Spray muffin tin with cooking spray.
Pour egg mixture into muffin tins, filling each tin 2/3 full. Top each with 2 tsp. of Parmesan cheese.
Sprinkle a few Brussels sprouts halves into each tin.
Place tin in oven and bake for 30 minutes.
