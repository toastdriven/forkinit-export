Forkinit: lentil cheese bake

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

1 3/4 c. lentils, rinsed
2 c. water
1 bay leaf
2 t. salt
1/2 t. pepper
2 large onions, chopped
2 garlic cloves, minced
2 c. canned tomatoes
2 chopped carrots
1 chopped green pepper
2 T. chopped parsley
enough cheddar cheese to cover

Instructions

preheat oven to 375 degrees.
combine all ingredients except cheese and place in a 9 by 13 baking dish. cover and bake for 40 minutes.
sprinkle cheese on top and bake uncovered for another 5 minutes.
