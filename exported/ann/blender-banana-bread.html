Forkinit: Blender banana bread

By Ann Metts

Ingredients


1 stick butter
1 cup sugar
2 eggs
1 1/2 cups mashed ripe bananas (3-4) (or I've also used 1/2 a yellow squash, shredded...really good!  You could probably use zucchini also i would imagine!)
1 Tbsp. lemon juice
2/3 cup quick cooking oats
1 1/3 cups flour
1 tsp. baking soda
1/4 tsp. salt
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions


Process first 5 ingredients.  Add dry ingredients (except nuts) and process just until mixed.  Stir in nuts.  Pour batter into greased 9x5x3 loaf pan.  (I like to sprinkle the top with cinnamon sugar for added taste!)  Bake in 350 degree oven, 45-55 minutes.
