Forkinit: Scallops stir fry over pasta

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

1 to 2 T. olive oil
1/2 lb. bacon, chopped
1 red pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
2 garlic cloves minced
1 bag of small scallops
1 bunch of spinach
spaghetti noodles

Instructions

Cook spaghetti noodles according to package

Heat skillet over medium/high heat. Add olive oil and bacon. Once bacon is browned, add red pepper, onion, and garlic. Once veggies are cooked through, add scallops and cook for about 3 to 5 minutes. Then add spinach. Once spinach wilts remove from heat and serve over spaghetti noodles.

Yields: 4 to 6
