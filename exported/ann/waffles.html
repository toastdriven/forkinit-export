Forkinit: waffles

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

2 cups AP flour

-1 tbsp baking powder

-1/2 t salt

-2 eggs

-1 ¾ cup milk (I usually use water since we don’t have milk on hand or rice milk if I have it for something I’ve cooked…used almond milk most recently.)

-6 tbsp oil

-2 tbsp sugar or sweetener of your choice (I usually use just 1 tbsp of honey and no one seems to complain about that!)


Instructions

-Let batter rest for 5 minutes after it is well mixed.

-Fill ½ cup measuring cup with batter, pour in the center of iron

-Waffles seem to do best if cooked between setting 3-4

-Green indicator light turns on when iron is ready to be used/waffle is finished cooking

Yields: 8
