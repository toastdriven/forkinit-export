Forkinit: oatmeal and cottage cheese pancakes

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

4 egg whites
1/4 cup low fat cottage cheese or fat-free cottage cheese (tofu can be used)
1/2 cup old-fashioned oatmeal, uncooked
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg

Instructions

1In a blender or a food processor, blend together all ingredients until smooth.
2Spray a medium sized skillet with cooking spray and set heat to medium.
3Add the pancake batter to skillet and cook until sides are lightly browned.
4Top with a low sugar syrup, trans free butter and/or a few sprinkles of a sugar substitute and serve with fresh fruit; blueberries or strawberries are allowed and would go nicely.
