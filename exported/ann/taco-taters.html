Forkinit: Taco ‘Taters

By Ann Metts

Ingredients


4 large or 6 medium potatoes
2 to 3 cups leftover taco meat
2 cups shredded cheddar or pepper jack cheese
Corn cobs, or other vegetable side dish
Butter



Instructions

Bake the potatoes, or cook them in the microwave if you are short on time.
Add the taco meat on top, then sprinkle cheese on top and microwave or run under the broiler to melt the cheese.

Yields: 4 servings
