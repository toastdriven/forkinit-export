Forkinit: baked sweet potato chips

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

sweet potato
vegetable cooking spray
favorite spice (I use garlic salt or something called crazy salt)


Instructions

Heat oven to 350. Peel sweet potato (works with yams too) and slice thin, as if for a thick potato chip. Cover a cookie sheet or baking pan with foil, sprayed lightly with vegetable spray. Lay sweet potato chips on the foil, rubbing each one slightly in the veggie spray. Then lightly spray the tops with veggie spray, sprinkly with your favorite spice. Bake for 20 minutes, turn, sprinkle that side, and bake for another 10 minutes.
