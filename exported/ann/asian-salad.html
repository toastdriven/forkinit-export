Forkinit: asian salad

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

1 cup of shredded or chopped cooked chicken

½ cup chopped Romaine lettuce

½ cup broccoli or cole slaw

½ cup edamame

¼ cup chopped cilantro

½ cup lightly salted peanuts

Instructions

Toss together in medium to large bowl the ingredients. Add peanut sauce to taste. Drizzle over salad ingredients and mix until they are lightly coated.

Note: Peanut sauces can be hot or mild. Choose the one your family will enjoy. If tastes vary, have both kinds on hand and have each person drizzle their own salad.
