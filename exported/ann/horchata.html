Forkinit: Horchata

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

3/4 cup - brown basmati rice
1/3 cup - almonds
1 cinnamon stick
1 t. Lime zest
1/4 cup - jaggery (or sugar)

Wet grinder or blender




Instructions

Grind the rice in a blender as smooth as possible.
Put ground rice, almonds, lime zest and cinnamon stick in a jar and pour 2 1/4 cups of hot water in the mix.
Let sit overnight (at least 6 hours)
Place mix in a blender for 3 to 5 minutes. Strain it through a sieve lined with cheesecloth or a clean kitchen cloth. Add Four more cups of water and sugar to taste. Chill in the fridge for a while and serve over ice.
