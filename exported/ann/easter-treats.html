Forkinit: Easter Treats

By Ann Metts
This has been an Easter tradition of my husbands since he was little.

Ingredients

3 tablespoons butter or margarine
1 package (10 oz., about 40) regular marshmallows
- OR -
4 cups miniature marshmallows
6 cups Rice KrispiesÂ® or off brand


Instructions

1. In large saucepan melt butter over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until completely melted. Remove from heat.
2. Add rice krispies cereal. Stir until well coated.

3. Lay out wax paper to form treats on. Coat your hands with butter and make easter sculptures out of the mixture. (bunnies, tombs, eggs, crosses, etc.)
