Forkinit: Caillou's Crunchy Carrot Salad

By Ann Metts

Ingredients

2 carrots
1 eating apple
1 cup raisins
1 cup cole slaw mix (bagged cabbage blend) without dressing
1 lemon
2 tsp. honey



Instructions

Peel the carrots and apple and cut into very fine strips.

Put carrots and apple into a mixing bowl. Add the raisins and cole slaw and mix gently.

Add the lemon juice and honey and mix again.

Refrigerate the salad until nice and cold.

Serve as a side salad, appetizer or even as a healthy sandwich filling.
