Forkinit: Holy Guacamole

By Angel Alchin

Ingredients

4 avocados, mashed
1 lime, juiced
1 large garlic clove, crushed
1 medium tomatillo, chopped
1 jalapeno, chopped
1 medium red pepper, chopped
1 medium onion, chopped
2 tbsp fresh cilantro chopped
1 tsp salt

Instructions

Mash the avocados and mix with the lime to reduce browning. Add the crushed garlic, tomatillo, jalapeno, red pepper, onion, cilantro and salt and mix well.

Prep time: 30 minutes
