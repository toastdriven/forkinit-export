Forkinit: Antibacterial Spray

By Madeleine McLaughlin

Ingredients

1/2 cup distilled water
1/2 cup vodka
25 drops orange oil
15 drops lavender oil
15 drops eucalyptus oil

Instructions

Mix well and store in a spray bottle. Shake prior to use.
