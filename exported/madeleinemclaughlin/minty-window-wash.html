Forkinit: Minty Window Wash

By Madeleine McLaughlin
Mild mint fragrance offers a clean, non-irritating pick me up. You could always leave out the essential oils or use different ones, but the mint does help to deter pests, which makes it ideal for a window or glass door that may otherwise be attractive to pests looking for an entrance into your home!

Ingredients

5 drops spearmint oil
4 drops peppermint oil
1/2 cup distilled vinegar
2T vodka
1/2 cup distilled water

Instructions

Combine everything in a spray bottle and shake well. Shake well before using.
