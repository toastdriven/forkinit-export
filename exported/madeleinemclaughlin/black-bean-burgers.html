Forkinit: Black Bean Burgers

By Madeleine McLaughlin

Ingredients

1 small onion
1 red pepper (use fire roasted jarred in the winter, when you canâ€™t get organic)
4 cloves garlic
1/4 t tumeric
1/2 t cumin
2 T. ketchup
1 cup cooked brown rice
1 can black beans
2 cup bread crumbs

Instructions

Saute onion, pepper, and garlic for about 8 minutes.
Then add spices and ketchup.
Process everything together in the food processor with rice and beans.
Transfer to a large bowl and add bread crumbs. Form into patties and fry. Season to taste.
