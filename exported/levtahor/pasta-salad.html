Forkinit: Pasta Salad

By Mary
Refreshing cucumbers, tomatoes, basil and mozzarella combo

Ingredients

1lb Pasta
8oz Mozzarella cubed
1c Cherry tomatoes
1 medium cucumber
basil

Dressing:
1 clove garlic
1 handful of fresh basil leaves
1T white rice vinegar
1T apple cider vinegar
1t lemon juice
Kosher salt and freshly ground

Instructions

Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling salted water until package directs for al dente. Drain well. Whisk together the diced garlic, chopped basil, vinegars, lemon juice, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper. Toss the dressing with the pasta. Cool.

Add the mozzarella, halved tomatoes, small diced cucumbers and basil to a large bowl. Stir in the pasta.

Yields: 6-8
Prep time: 30 minute
