Forkinit: Pumpkin Cheesecake Bars

By Mary

Ingredients

Crust:

7 gram crackers (crushed)
1/4 cup butter
2 Tbls sugar

Filling:

1 can pumpkin
1 1/2 cream cheese packages, warmed (12oz)
1/2 cup brown sugar
2 eggs
1 tsp vanilla
1 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Instructions

Spread crust in 9 by 13 baking dish. Mix filling together until smooth. Pour on crust. Bake for roughly 35 min or until set.
