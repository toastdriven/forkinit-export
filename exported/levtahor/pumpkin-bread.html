Forkinit: Pumpkin bread

By Mary
I altered the recipe of 2 large coffee can loaves so much that I thought I better note the changes.

Ingredients


3 c sugar
2 tsp molasses
1 c oil (I combined coconut, butter and lard)
2 eggs, beaten
3-4 c pumpkin
1/8-1/4 tsp nutmeg
1/4 tsp ginger
2 ½ tsp cinnamon
3 tsp baking soda
1 tsp salt
2 tsp vanilla
4 1/2 c flour
1 c chopped nuts (optional)
1 1/3 c raisins (Optional)

Instructions

Mix in order given. I mixed everything before the baking soda together. then sifted in the baking soda and added the remaining ingredients (except I forgot the raisins) and mixed the batter all together.

I greased a cake pan and a bread pan (because I didn't have 2 of either) and baked at 350⁰ for 1hr - 1hr 20min or until knife inserted in center comes out clean.

Cool on racks.

Refrigerate or freeze
Best after aging a few days (I wouldn't know about this... I ate mine still warm from the oven.)

Yields: 2 large loaves
