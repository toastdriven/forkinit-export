Forkinit: Baked Mac and Cheese

By Mary

Ingredients

2 1/2 cups shells or macaroni
3 tablespoons butter
3 tablespoons flour
1/2 tsp powdered mustard
2 cups milk
1/2 cup yellow onion, finely diced (optional)
1 bay leaf
1/2 teaspoon paprika
1 large egg
4 oz cream cheese
3 cups sharp cheddar, shredded (12 oz)
1 teaspoon kosher salt
Fresh black pepper
1 cup Ham, diced (optional)
1/2 C. breadcrumbs

Instructions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.

In a large pot of boiling, salted water cook the pasta to al dente.

While the pasta is cooking, in a separate pot, melt the butter. Whisk in the flour and mustard and keep it moving for about five minutes. Make sure it's free of lumps. Stir in the milk, onion, bay leaf, and paprika. Simmer for ten minutes and remove the bay leaf.

Temper in the egg. Stir in all of the cream cheese and 3/4 of the cheddar cheese. Season with salt and pepper. Fold the macaroni and ham into the mix and pour into a 2-quart casserole dish. Top with remaining cheese.
Bake for 20 minutes. Top with bread crumbs and bake 20 minutes more.

Yields: 6-8
Prep time: 1 hour 30 minutes
