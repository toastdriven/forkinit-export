Forkinit: Crockpot Pulled Pork

By Mary
Easy and quite yummy without using store bought BBQ sauce

Ingredients

1 large Vidalia onion, sliced thin
2 tablespoons sugar
2-4 tablespoons molasses
1 tablespoon paprika
2 teaspoon kosher salt
½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
1 (4-6 lb) boneless pork butt or shoulder
¾ cup cider vinegar
5 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
1 teaspoons crushed red pepper flakes
½ teaspoons dry mustard (or generous squirt of yellow mustard)
1 teaspoon garlic granules
¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper

Instructions

1. Rinse pork roast under cold water.

2. Place onions in crock-pot. Place the pork roast on top of the onions and evenly sprinkle all other ingredients over the roast.

3. Cover crockpot; cook on low for 10-12 hours.

4. Chop or shred meat and onions. If still very liquidy, cook uncovered for 20-50 minutes. For more flavor, serve with your favorite barbeque sauce.
