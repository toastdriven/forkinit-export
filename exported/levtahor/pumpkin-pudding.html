Forkinit: Pumpkin Pudding

By Mary
Like pumpkin pie, only crustless

Ingredients

3/4 cup milk
1/2 cup yogurt
1/4 teaspoon baking soda
1 teaspoon cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt
1/4 cup butter (warmed)
2 cups pumpkin
2 eggs
1/2 cup flour
1/2 teaspoon vanilla
1/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup brown sugar

Instructions

Preheat oven to 450.
Grease a 9x9 pan.
In large mixing bowl at medium speed, beat pumpkin until smooth. Add in milk, yogurt, sugars, flour, butter, eggs, vanilla, nutmeg, cinnamon, baking soda and salt to beat for 1 minute or until smooth and well blended.
Pour into pan.
Bake for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.

Prep time: 15 minutes
