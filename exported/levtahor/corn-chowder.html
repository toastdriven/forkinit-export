Forkinit: Corn Chowder

By Mary
I was wondering what kind of soup I could make if all I had for veggies was corn and potatoes and some other odds and ends from my garden. Loosely followed a recipe online... so loosely, I have to write up a new one. Sorry... I am guessing at amounts for this... put what LOOKS right. :)

Ingredients

1 quart chicken broth
4 cups corn
1 small onion, diced
2 leeks, sliced thinly
2 T chives, chopped
5 medium potatoes, diced small
8 oz of cream cheese
shredded cheddar
salt and pepper to taste



Instructions

In a large pot over medium heat, combine broth, corn, onion, leeks, chives and potatoes. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat, cover and simmer 15 to 20 minutes, until potatoes are just tender. (I used a potato masher to smash the potatoes a bit in order to thicken the broth.) Season with salt and pepper.
Stir in cubes of cream cheese until melted. Garnish with shredded cheddar and serve at once.

Yields: 6 servings
