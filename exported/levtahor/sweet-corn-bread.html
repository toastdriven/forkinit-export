Forkinit: Sweet Corn Bread

By Mary

Ingredients

1 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup yellow cornmeal
1/3 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon salt
1/8 teaspoon nutmeg
3 teaspoons baking powder
1 cup buttermilk
1/3 cup vegetable oil
1 egg

Instructions

Preheat oven to 400 degrees F. Spray or lightly grease a 9 inch round cake pan.
In a large bowl, combine flour, cornmeal, sugar, salt, nutmeg and baking powder. Stir in egg, buttermilk and vegetable oil until well combined. Pour batter into prepared pan.
Bake in preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.

Yields: 8
Prep time: 35 including baking time
