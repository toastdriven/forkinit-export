Forkinit: Pea Soup

By Mary

Ingredients

2 1/4 cup Split Peas
2 quarts water
1 Ham Bone
1 cup Ham
1 onion diced
2-3 stalks celery, chopped
2-3 carrots, chopped
1 potato diced
2 tsp salt to taste
1 tsp pepper
1 tsp Thyme
6 cloves garlic
8 sprigs of parsley
2 bay leaves (do not eat)

Instructions

Soak peas in water overnight or while cooking 2 quarts of water and ham bone together for 1 1/2 hours in large pot. Removed meat from ham bone and add all ingredients. Cook until peas are mush.

Yields: 6 servings
