Forkinit: Banana bread

By Mary

Ingredients

4 or 5 ripe bananas, well mashed
1/3 cup melted butter (5T)
2/3 cup brown sugar
1 egg
1 t vanilla
1 t baking soda
pinch of salt
1 1/2 cup flour
1 cup oatmeal

Instructions

350 degrees

Mix (with spoon) butter into mashed bananas. Mix in sugar, egg & vanilla. Sprinkle baking soda & salt over the batter and mix. Add four and oats (and/or nuts if desired) last.

Pour batter into 2 greased 4x8 loaf pans (I melt the butter in them while preheating the oven). Bake 45 minutes - 1 hour. Knife inserted should only have banana smear on it when removed. Cool.

Variation:
Banana muffins
bake 20 minutes
yields 1 1/2 dozen
