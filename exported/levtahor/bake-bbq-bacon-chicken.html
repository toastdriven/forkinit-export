Forkinit: Bake BBQ Bacon Chicken

By Mary
Tasty and juicy chicken and so easy to make.

Ingredients

4 chicken breasts (or thighs)
4 slices of bacon (more if desired)
8 oz BBQ sauce

Instructions

Preheat oven to 225 degrees.
Apply most of the BBQ sauce to chicken breasts in a baking dish. Slice bacon strips in half and lay over over the top of the chicken. Apply remaining BBQ sauce (to allow the bacon to get as crispy as possible). Bake covered for 2 hours. Set temperature to 350, uncover and bake for an additional 45 minutes.

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 15 minutes or less
