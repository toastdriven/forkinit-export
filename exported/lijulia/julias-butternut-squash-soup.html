Forkinit: Julia's Butternut Squash Soup

By lijulia

Ingredients

1-2 Tbsp canola oil
2-3 leeks (chopped)
2-3 cloves garlic (roughly chopped)
2 bay leaves
1 butternut squash (peeled, seeded, diced)
3 parsnips (chopped)
3/4 cup cococut milk
2 cups water (or more)
2 heaping tsps Better Than Boullion
coarse sea salt

Instructions

Sautee leeks, garlic and bay leaves in canola oil for 5 minues in open pressure cooker on low-medium heat.

Add remaining ingrediens, seal pressure cooker, turn burner to high. When steam starts releasing, cook for another 10 minutes at high heat. Turn off heat, allow pressure to escape naturally. Blend soup with immersion blender until perfectly smooth.

Add israeli cous cous, rice, grains, etc.
