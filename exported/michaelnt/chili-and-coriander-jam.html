Forkinit: Chili and Coriander Jam

By michaelnt

Ingredients

400g chopped tinned tomatoes
3 garlic cloves
2 red chillies, deseeded and finely chopped
1 tsp cumin seeds
1 tsp black mustard seeds
(1 tsp coriander seeds)
1 1/2 tbsp sunflower oil
1/2 tsp salt
40 caster sugar
1/2 tsp ground tumeric
2 1/2 tbsp red wine vingar
15 g chopped fresh coriander

Instructions

Put all the ingredients apart from the coriander into a saucepan, bring to the boil and then simmer for 35 minutes.

Remove from the heat and fold in the corriander
