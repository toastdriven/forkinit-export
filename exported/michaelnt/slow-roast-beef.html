Forkinit: Slow Roast Beef

By michaelnt

Ingredients


1Kg, Rib of Beef, off the bone and rolled

Instructions

Place a heavy frying pan on a very high heat and sear the outside of the beef. Transfer the frying pan and beef to an oven at 75C, check the temperature of the oven with a meat thermometer and adjust as necessary.

Cook the beef for four hours when it should be 53C, checking as you go and adjusting the temperature as needed.

Remove the pan from the oven, turn the oven to very high and when hot enough return the beef for 10 minutes. Leave to rest for forty minutes before serving, carved across the grain.
