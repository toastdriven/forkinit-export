Forkinit: Semi Dried Tomatoes

By michaelnt

Ingredients

1.5 Kg of Tomatoes
3 cloves of garlic
Dried Rosemary

Instructions

Oil a large roasting tray
Slice the tomatoes in half and remove the hard green stem and place in the roasting tray.
Very thinly slice the garlic and lay a sliver on each tomatoe
Sprinkle some dried Rosemary, salt and pepper on the tomatoes.
Place in an oven at 130C for 2.5 hours

Placed in a jar and cover with olive oil, and store in the fridge.
