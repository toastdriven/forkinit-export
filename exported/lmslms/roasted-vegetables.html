Forkinit: Roasted Vegetables

By fasola4mi
Perfect as a side dish, in an omelette, over pasta or polenta, in a veggie sandwich (with or without cheese), served warm or at room temperature.

Ingredients

choose any assortment of seasonal roasting veggies:
summer squash
eggplant
onion
mushrooms
cherry tomatoes
potatoes
sweet potatoes
parsnip
rutabaga
carrot
kohlrabi
winter squash

olive oil
Trader Joe's lemon pepper, or regular salt and pepper

Instructions

Wash and chop everything into bite-sized chunks. Toss with olive oil and salt and pepper. Roast in a single layer at 425, turning every 15 minutes. If using cherry tomatoes (and you should!), add them in the last 15 minutes.

Prep time: 15 minutes prep; up to 45 minutes to roast
Source: What We Eat When We Eat Alone- Deborah Madison
