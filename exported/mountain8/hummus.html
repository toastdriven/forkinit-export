Forkinit: Hummus

By wooliemama
A basic hummus recipe--can be tweaked in various ways to match taste.

Ingredients

1 19-oz can chickpeas
1/4 cup lemon juice (fresh is best)
1/4 cup tahini
2 cloves garlic
1 tsp. vegetable oil
1/2 tsp. ground cumin (or to taste)
1 tsp. ground coriander (or to taste)
freshly ground black pepper to taste
1/4 tsp. salt (optional)
1/4 cup to 1/2 cup water
1/2 cup finely chopped parsley

Instructions

Drain and rinse the chickpeas.
In a blender or a food processor fitted with a metal blade, combine chickpeas, lemon juice, tahini, garlic, oil, spices, and water (I generally start with 1/4 cup water and then add more if I want to make the hummus thinner). Blend, occasionally scraping the sides so that the whole mixture gets thoroughly blended into a smooth paste. Add more water very slowly if necessary to thin. Stir in parsley. Refrigerate before serving (overnight is best to allow flavors to fully blend).

Yields: 12
Prep time: 10 minutes
