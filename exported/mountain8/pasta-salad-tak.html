Forkinit: Pasta Salad (Tak)

By wooliemama
Nice mellow garlicky salad. Gets rave reviews from friends.

Ingredients

1/2 lb. uncooked pasta
3 Tbsp. olive oil
2 Tbsp. red wine vinegar
3/4 tsp. salt
2-3 cloves garlic
1 c. onions
1/4 c. grated parmesan
1 medium bell pepper
1 medium cucumber

Optional:
basil leaves, parsley, olives, cubed cheese, other vegetables, chicken sausage chunks

Instructions

Cook pasta until just al dente. Do not overcook!
Combine all ingredients in a large bowl.
Toss thoroughly.

Yields: 10
Prep time: 20 minutes
Source: I think it's an adaptation of a Moosewood recipe, via my friends the Takasakis
