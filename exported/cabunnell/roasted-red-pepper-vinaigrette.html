Forkinit: Roasted Red Pepper Vinaigrette

By Carol Ann
Yummy dressing for romaine salad - if you like garlic!

Ingredients

2 roasted red bell peppers, drained well, chopped
salt and pepper
1 clove garlic, chopped
2 T balsamic vinegar
3 T extra-virgin olive oil
drizzle of honey

Instructions

Place roasted red bell peppers in food processor bowl. Season them with salt and pepper. Add garlic and vinegar to food processor. Turn processor on and stream in EVOO and some honey. When dressing is smooth and combined, stop and adjust seasonings.

Less is more with this dressing. A little goes a long way, but the results are delectable!

Yields: 4 servings
Prep time: 5 minutes if you purchase roasted bell peppers in the jar.
