Forkinit: Lemon-Grilled Chicken

By Charles Leifer

Ingredients

Lemon juice
2 lbs chicken breast
1/2 lb bacon
Lemon pepper
Toothpicks

Instructions

Put chicken in ziploc and fill with lemon juice until chicken covered
Let sit for at least an hour
Remove chicken and lightly season with lemon pepper
Wrap with 2 pieces bacon, using toothpicks
Grill and enjoy!

Yields: 3-4
