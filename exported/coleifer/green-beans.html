Forkinit: Green beans

By Charles Leifer

Ingredients

A bag of fresh green beans
Soy sauce
Bacon

Instructions

Clean the beans
Cook bacon 2/3 of the way done in a skillet
Add soy sauce, water and beans to the skillet
Boil beans in bacon/soy/water mixture until soft

Prep time: 15 minutes
