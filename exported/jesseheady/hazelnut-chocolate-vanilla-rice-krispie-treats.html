Forkinit: Hazelnut Chocolate Vanilla Rice Krispie Treats

By Jesse Heady
Traditional Rice Krispee Treats with chocolate, hazelnut, and vanilla

Ingredients

5 cups Rice Krispies (or generic brand)
2/3 cup roasted and salted hazelnuts, finely chopped
3/4 stick salted butter (1/3 cup)
1/2 10 ounce bag of Jet-Puffed Snowman Mini French Vanilla marshmallows *
1/2 10 ounce bag regular Jet-Puffed marshmallows
1 large dollop of Nutella brand hazelnut spread (2 or so table spoons)

* Substitute 1 tsp vanilla extract and regular marshmallows in lieu of Jet-Puffed Snowman Mini French Vanilla marshmallows

Instructions

Spread butter inside 13 x 9 inch pan and large mixing bowl. Pour dry cereal and chopped hazelnuts into bowl. Set pan and bowl aside.

Melt butter in large sauce pan over low heat. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well-blended. Remove from heat and pour over dry mix. Mix thoroughly.

Using buttered spatula or waxed paper, press mixture evenly and firmly in buttered 13 x 9 inch pan. Cut into 2 x 2 inch squares when cool.

Prep time: 10 minutes
