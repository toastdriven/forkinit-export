Forkinit: Hummus

By CT Morrison
A quick version with peanut buter

Ingredients

1 can chick peas drained and rinsed, reserve liquid
2tbsp peanut butter
2 cloves garlic crushed
1/4 cup lemon juice
2 tbsp olive oil
1 or 2tsp pizza seasoning spice with (or) red pepper flakes and rosemarry

Instructions

pour all ingredients into a blender and blend. Add reserve liquid to thin out the hummus as desired
