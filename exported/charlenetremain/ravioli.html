Forkinit: Ravioli

By CT Morrison
I make this with my fresh Pasta Dough Recipe, gluten free!

Ingredients

Ground Meat Filling:
1 onion, diced small
1 lb ground beef or turkey
2 tsp garlic powder
Italian-type seasonings (oregano, basil, parsley, salt, pepper)
Brown together, remove from heat.
Add 1/4 c grated Parmesan and 1 egg.

Instructions

I put these together on a cookie sheet.
Brush tops of pasta with water to help them stick together, fill, fork edges together on both sides. Then freeze them for 1/2 hour. Lift off pan with a lifter and place in a sealed container or freezer bag without sticking together too bad.

To cook, boil for 7 minutes, drain and serve with tomatoe sauce and cheese.
