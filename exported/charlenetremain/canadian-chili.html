Forkinit: Canadian Chili

By CT Morrison
Our Favorite after sledding/skating meal

Ingredients

1 lb ground beef - cooked, drained and seasoned with salt & pepper

1 tblsp butter
one onion, chopped fine
1/2 green pepper chopped fine (optional)
6 mushrooms sliced

1L can tomato juice (low sodium)
2 cans beans in tomato sauce (pork 'n' beans)
1 can kidney beans
2 cups frozen corn
Chili powder (at least 1 tsp, but more if you like hot)
3-6 dashes of hot pepper sauce

Instructions

Saute in meat pan the butter and onion, peppers and mushrooms
Add all ingredients to a soup pot or crock pot and simmer untill the corn is completely cooked.

Yields: 6-8 servings
