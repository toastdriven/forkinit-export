Forkinit: Sweet and Sour Cabbage soup

By CT Morrison
Here’s a cabbage soup I invented. If you’ve ever had cabbage rolls, it’s very similar in taste

Ingredients

1/2 - 1 lb ground beef
1 med onion
S&P
1/3 of a head of cabbage chopped or grated
1/2 cup rice
2 cups water (or enough to slightly cover the cabbage)
1- 1L can of low salt tomato juice
2tsp Worcestershire sauce
4 Tbsp brown sugar
1 Tbsp lemon juice
1 Tbsp chicken soup base powder

Instructions

In a skillet, cook up: ground beef, chopped onion S&P
In a soup pot combine: cabbage chopped or grated, rice
and water (or enough to slightly cover the cabbage)
Bring to a boil and turn down until cabbage is tender, but not limp
Add:
meat mixture
Can of low salt tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar
lemon juice, chicken soup base powder

Cook on med low heat until rice is cooked through. Serve! If it looks too thick for your liking, add more water while cooking.

And apparently you can sub golden raisins for ground beef if you like meatless meals! I saw a recipe on recipezaar that used raisins for ground beef in soup.

Yields: 6-8 servings
