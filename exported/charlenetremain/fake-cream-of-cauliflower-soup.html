Forkinit: Fake Cream of Cauliflower Soup

By CT Morrison
I made this one up on the fly with my fridge door open ;o)

Ingredients


5 c water, bring to boil and add
1 small head of cauliflower chopped
2 carrots chopped
1 cup frozen corn
1 onion chopped fine
pepper to taste
1/2 cup of milk (I used rice milk, cause that's just what we use!)
2 slices processed cheese

Instructions

Boil carrots and cauliflower until almost tender and add corn and onion. Simmer until vegetables are tender. Pour into food processor (or mash if you are low on time)Place back into pot and add remaining ingredients.
Heat slowly to melt cheese and serve hot

Yields: Serves 4-6
