Forkinit: Easy pasta with veggie

By kmonahan

Ingredients

1 jar of any pasta sauce
1 lb any pasta
0.5lb of ground turkey/beef
1 green pepper, cut into 1/2" squares
1 onion, cut into strips
1 package (8oz) of sliced mushrooms, washed / 3 slices of deli ham cut into 1/4" squares / both

Instructions

Start boiling salted water (with ~1 tsp salt) for pasta, note the al Dente cooking time for pasta
At about 8 minutes before pasta is done to al Dente (adjust next time if you cook slowly/quickly), start cooking:

Stove to med-high and add small amount of EVOO
Add beef/Turkey and sprinkle with salt and pepper
When meat is cooked through, add mushroom
At 4 minutes before pasta, add onions
At 3 minutes before pasta, add green peppers
At 1 minute before pasta, pour entire jar into pan. Add a bit of tap water to the jar, close the lid and shake around to pour the rest out. It also makes the pasta not too dry.
Stir, switch the heat to lowest and simmer while you tend to your pasta.

When pasta is done, take what you need and cold wash the rest for storage.
Pour sauce on pasta and add ground black pepper and parm cheese.
