Forkinit: Stir-fried leafy greens

By kmonahan

Ingredients

1 lb leafy greens (spinach, watercress, bok choy, mustard green, broccoli rabe, etc)
3 cloves garlic thinly sliced or chopped
1/4-1/2 cup chicken broth
1/8-1/4 tsp salt

Instructions

Wash the veggies unless they are specifically pre-washed
Evoo + garlic until sizzles
Add greens and salt, stir a couple times
Add chicken broth, until very slightly soupy and enough to not boil away in 3 min
Cover and steam for 3 min at med heat
Test taste and add more salt if necessary
Serve

Adjust the '3 min' if a particular type of veggie is too soggy or too raw after 3 min.

This is a light side dish (maybe too light for you) so it's good to pair with something heavy or leftovery.
