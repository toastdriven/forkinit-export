Forkinit: Unkle Ken's Dead Chicken

By Matt Croydon

Ingredients

1 can cream of mushroom soup
1 can cream of celery soup
2 cans water
1 chicken boullion cube
garlic powder, to taste
onion powder, to taste
1 1/2 cup rice
4 chicken breast halves
mozzerella or cheddar cheese
croutons (optional)

Instructions

Combine ingredients in casserole pan.

Cover and cook for 1.5 hours.

10 minutes before done sprinkle cheese and optional croutons. Cover and return to oven.
