Forkinit: Awesome Seasoned Oyster Crackers

By Ed Brannin
Oyster crackers coated with a super-yummy seasoning!

Ingredients

20 oz. oyster crackers ("the cheap kind, not the good kind")
3/4 c vegatable oil (canola)
1 pkg. dried ranch dressing (powder)
1 tsp dill weed
1/2 tsp onion powder
1/2 tsp garlic powder
1/2 tsp lemon pepper seasoning

Instructions

Put the crackers in a large bowl.
Combine everything except the crackers in a separate dish.
Pour the spiced oil over the crackers.
Stir the mixture.
Stir again every 15 minutes, until reasonably dry.
