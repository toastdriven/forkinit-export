Forkinit: No-bake Peanut Butter Rice Crispy Treats

By Ed Brannin
Rice Crispy Treats with peanut-butter chips instead of marshmallows, plus a chocolate & chopped-peanut topping.

Ingredients

6.5 cups Rice Crispies
Peanut Butter Chips
Chocolate Chips
Butter
Light Corn Syrup
0.5c Honey-roasted Peanuts, chopped or crushed

Instructions

Melt the peanut butter chips and butter in a large mixing bowl.
Mix in the Rice Crispies.
Line a large baking dish with foil.
Pour the peanut-butter cereal into the large baking dish.
Put in the freezer for 10 minutes.

10 minutes later:
Melt the chocolate chips and corn syrup.
Pour the chocolate on the cereal and spread evenly.
Sprinkle peanuts over the top.
Put it back in the fridge for a while.

Before serving:
Remove the block of treats from their dish, and place on a large cutting board.
Cut and arrange the treats.
Enjoy!
