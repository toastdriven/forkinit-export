Forkinit: BBQ Baked Beans

By Ed Brannin
My mother-in-law's baked beans recipe.

Ingredients

42 oz. Plain pork and beans (2x 21-oz. cans)
1 green bell pepper, chopped
1 onion, chopped
3/4 c ketchup
2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
4 slices raw bacon, cut up (with scissors)
2 tsp chili powder
3 tsp brown sugar

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together in a microwave-safe 2.8 L dish.

Cover and microwave for a total of 25 minutes, mixing 3 times (every 8 minutes)
