Forkinit: Bacon Cheese Frittata

By Ed Brannin

Ingredients


* 6 eggs
* 1 cup milk
* 2 tablespoons butter or margarine, melted
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 teaspoon pepper
* 1/4 cup chopped green onions
* 5 bacon strips, cooked and crumbled
* 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese

Instructions

In a bowl, beat eggs, milk, butter, salt and pepper.
Pour into a greased 11-in. x 7-in. x 2-in. baking dish.
Sprinkle with onions, bacon and cheese.
Bake, uncovered, at 350 degrees F for 25-30 minutes or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean.





Yields: 6
Prep time: 10 min prep, 25 min cook, ready in 35 min.
