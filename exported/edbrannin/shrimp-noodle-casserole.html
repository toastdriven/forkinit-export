Forkinit: Shrimp & Noodle Casserole

By Ed Brannin

Ingredients

1lb cooked large or medium shrimp
1 8oz package of medium egg noodles
2 cans cream of mushroom soup
1c sour cream
1/2c green onions (sliced, including the tops)
1/4c green pepper (chopped)
1/2tsp dried dill weed
1/2tsp pepper
1/4tsp salt
1/2c shredded cheddar cheese

Instructions

* Cook noodles & drain.
* Combine everything except shrimp & cheese.
* Cut shrimp into thirds.
* Fold shrimp & cheese into noodle mixture.
* Put in a 13x9 baking dish.
* Cover with aluminum foil.
* Cook at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
* Take foil off and bake for another 10 minutes.
