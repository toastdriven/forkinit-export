Forkinit: Gooey Marshmallow Brownies

By Ed Brannin
Marshmallow-topped brownies that stay moist for days.

Ingredients

1 box Ghirardelli brownie mix
2 cups mini marshmallows
1/2 cup semi sweet chocolate chips

Instructions

Make brownies according to package instructions.
Remove from oven and sprinkle top first with marshmallows and then chocolate chips.
Return to oven for 2 to 5 minutes or until marshmallows just start to melt and brown.
Let cool completely before cutting into squares.

These brownies are super easy to make but they are really difficult to cut. The marshmallows stick to the knife – so be sure to use a liberal amount of cooking spray on your knife before cutting into squares. Be patient. It’s worth it.

Prep time: 10 minutes; 45 minutes cooking time
