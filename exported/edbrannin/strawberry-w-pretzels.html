Forkinit: Strawberry w/ Pretzels

By Ed Brannin

Ingredients

2 c Pretzels, coarsely crushed
3/4 c margarine, melted
1 c + 3 tbs sugar
1 pkg (8 oz.) cream cheese
1 container whipped topping
1 pkg (6 oz.) strawberry gelatin
1 c boiling water
1/2 c mini marshmallows
2 pkgs frozen strawberries

Instructions

Combine pretzels and margarine:
Mix and press into a 13x9 baking dish.
Bake at 400 degrees for 8 minutes, then cool.

In a large bowl with an electric mixer, beat sugar and cream cheese until blended.
Fold in whipped topping.
Spread over pretzels and refrigerate until chilled.

Make gelatin (add water).
Add marshmallows until melted
Add strawberries and juice.
Let stand for 10 minutes, then pour over cream cheese layer.

Refrigerate until chilled.
