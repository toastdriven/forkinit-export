Forkinit: Lasagna

By Pam Parker
Easy and delicious!!

Ingredients

1) Grease 9 x 13 pan.

2)Mix Sauce/Meat mixture in large pot or bowl:

1 1/2-2 jars spaghetti sauce (24 oz. each)
1/2 to 1 lb. browned ground beef
1/2 tsp. salt
1/2 tsp. oregano
1/4 tsp. garlic powder

6-8 lasagna noodles (uncooked)
24 oz. cottage cheese (small curd)
3 c. shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

3) Layer in 9 x 13 half each:

Noodles
Cottage Cheese
Mozzarella Cheese
Sauce/Meat Mixture

4) Repeat

5) Bake: 350 degrees for 1 1/2- 2 hours until noodles are done.

Yields: 9 x 13 pan
Prep time: About 30 minutes
