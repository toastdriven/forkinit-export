Forkinit: Chili

By Pam Parker
Easy crockpot recipe!

Ingredients

1) In large stockpot, brown together, then dump into crockpot:

1 pound hamburger
1 small onion-chopped
1/4-1/2 c. chopped green pepper (optional)

2) Dump into crockpot with meat:

3 cans (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes (or 1 qt. home-canned)
2 cans (15.5 oz.) chili beans--undrained
1 can (15 oz.) kidney beans (light or dark or other bean)--drained
2 1/2 tsp. chili powder
1 tsp. salt
dash black pepper
1 or 1 1/2 Tablespoons sugar

Instructions

3) Cook on low for 6-8 hours. Can cook on high for less time.

Can double or triple!!! Can decrease the meat also. I always make extra since it's so good leftover and easy too! It is also good served with rice, cheese, tortilla chips, and bread spread with peanut butter.

Yields: 6 servings?
Prep time: About 15 minutes
