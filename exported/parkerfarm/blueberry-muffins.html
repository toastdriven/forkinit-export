Forkinit: Blueberry Muffins

By Pam Parker
Delicious warm with butter and honey! Fresh or frozen blueberries can be used. When making 2 dozen, it's easier to use 2 different bowls and do 1 recipe in each bowl.

Ingredients

Put in mixing bowl and beat with fork:
1 egg

Stir in:
2/3 cups milk
1/4 cup oil

Mix and stir in just until moistened (will be lumpy):
2 cups flour (whole wheat preferred)
2/3 cups brown sugar or white sugar
2 1/2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt

Add:
1-1 1/2 cups blueberries (fresh or frozen) (batter will be really thick if you use frozen)

Instructions

Fill muffin cups and bake at 400 degrees from 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Yields: 12 muffins
Prep time: About 20 minutes
