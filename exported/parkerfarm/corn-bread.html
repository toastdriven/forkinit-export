Forkinit: Corn Bread

By Pam Parker
Good with Chili or Bean Soup!!! (or any soup!) This is smooth and yummy!!

Ingredients

1) Set oven to 425 degrees. Grease 9 x 13 pan. Combine in mixing bowl:

2 c. cornmeal
2 c. flour (whole wheat or white, or some of each)
2/3 c. sugar (white or brown, or another sweetener)
2 T. + 2 tsp. baking powder
1 1/2 tsp. salt

2) Add and mix:

2 c. milk
1/2 c. oil
4 eggs

Instructions

Pour into 9 x 13 pan and bake at 425 degrees for 25 minutes or until done.

Yields: 9 x 13 pan
Prep time: 10 minutes
