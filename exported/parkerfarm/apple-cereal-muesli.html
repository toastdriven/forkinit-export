Forkinit: Apple Cereal Muesli

By Pam Parker
A good, healthy breakfast alternative. It is eaten cold or room temperature.

Ingredients

Mix together in a large bowl and store in the refrigerator:

3 or 4 c. chopped apples (about 6 apples)
7 c. rolled oats (not quick--old fashioned/regular is better)
4 c. milk
1/2 c. orange juice
2/3 c. honey
1 tsp. cinnamon
1/4 tsp. salt
3/4 c. raisins
1/2 c. nuts (optional)

Instructions

Great as a snack too!

Yields: About 4-6 servings
Prep time: 13.76 minutes (I don't know!)
