Forkinit: Vegetable Beef Soup

By Pam Parker
Easy and uses common ingredients.

Ingredients

Brown together in large stock pot (drain if necessary):
1# hamburger
2 medium onions, diced
1/2 c. water

Add and simmer 10 minutes:
3 cans (14.5 oz.) tomatoes
6 c. water
3 1/2 c. carrots, diced 1/4" thick (about 5 medium)
3 envelopes onion soup mix
3 bay leaves
1 T. basil
1 T. salt
2 tsp. sugar
1 tsp. garlic powder

Add and simmer 10 minutes more:
4 c. potatoes, diced 1/2" thick (about 3 large or 4 medium)
2 c. celery, diced

Add and simmer about 1 hour to combine flavors:
1 bag (16 oz.) frozen corn
1 bag (16 oz.) frozen green beans
2 bags (16 oz.) frozen mixed vegetables

Instructions

Soup can be transferred to a crock pot to finish cooking or to keep warm. Great with homemade bread! Enjoy!!

Yields: 28 cups
Source: I combined 3 recipes from the internet, then tasted and revised it.
