Forkinit: Mexican Wedding Cake

By Pam Parker
Easy, moist, and delicious!!

Ingredients

Heat oven to 350 degrees.

Mix all and pour into ungreased 9 x 13 pan:

1 lg. can (20 oz.) crushed pineapple with liquid
2 eggs
2 c. sugar
2 c. flour
2 tsp. soda
1 c. chopped walnuts, optional

Bake at 350 for 35 minutes or more until done in middle. Cool.

Instructions

Frost with Cream Cheese Frosting:

1 stick butter
1-8 oz. pkg. cream cheese
2 c. powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Cream together in mixer until smooth and creamy.

Enjoy!!

Yields: 9 x 13 cake
Prep time: 20 minutes
