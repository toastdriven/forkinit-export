Forkinit: Baked Oatmeal

By Pam Parker
Delicious! Definitely a family favorite!! Most fruits can be added, like blueberries, strawberries, peaches, anything you like.

Ingredients

Place in 9 x 13 glass baking dish in 325 degree oven as it preheats:
1 stick butter (can use 1/2 stick then add 1/4 c. applesauce with other ingredients)

Beat together in bowl:
2 c. brown sugar (or maple syrup, or 1 c. honey)
6 eggs
3 c. milk

Add:
Melted butter from above (rotate butter in dish first to coat bottom and sides)

Add & mix well:
6 c. rolled oats (not quick)
4 tsp. baking powder
1 tsp. salt
2 tsp. cinnamon
2 apples chopped
1/4 c. raisins, optional
1/4-1/2 c. nuts, optional

Instructions

Pour into buttered dish and bake at 325 degrees for 55 minutes or more until golden brown. (For a metal dish, bake at 350.) Serve warm with milk.

Notes: Can be made the night before. Put in refrigerator and bake in the morning. Can use some oil instead of butter, but does affect the taste. Can decrease salt. Other options for the brown sugar are 1/2 c. honey plus 2 Tbsp. molasses OR 2/3 c. sugar plus 2 Tbsp. molasses.
