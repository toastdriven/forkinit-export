Forkinit: Parker's Chocolate Frosting

By Pam Parker
This is our "famous" family birthday cake frosting. It is made simply with common ingredients, but is also delicious!

Ingredients

Beat until soft:
1 stick butter

Mix together and add 1/2 of mixture to butter and beat until mixed:
4 c. powdered sugar
3/4 c. baking cocoa

Add 1/2 to mixture above and beat until mixed:
1/2 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla

Instructions

Add remaining powdered sugar and milk mixtures. Beat until smooth. Add more milk if it is too thick.
