Forkinit: Granola

By Pam Parker
Great for breakfast or a snack! Easy to make and good for you!

Ingredients

1 stick butter
1 1/2 c. brown sugar (or fructose or white sugar)
1 T. cinnamon
1 T. vanilla (can try adding 1 T. maple)

10 c. rolled oats

1 package (12 oz. or 2 cups) raisins
1/2 c. coconut

Instructions

Put butter in large open pan like a roaster or 9 x 13 pan. Melt butter in 325 degree oven while measuring brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, and maple. When melted, add to butter and stir well. Then add oats and stir well. (hands work well for mixing) Bake 30-40 minutes at 325 degrees until golden brown, stirring every 10-15 minutes. Cool in pan. Add raisins and coconut. Store in airtight containers or ziploc bags.

Prep time: 10 minutes
