Forkinit: Fruit Jello

By Pam Parker
Easy and refreshing!

Ingredients

1. Drain fruit liquid into 4 cup liquid measuring cup. Set aside fruit in refrigerator. Add water to make 3 cups liquid and set aside:

6 cans fruit cocktail


2. Dissolve in large bowl (12 cup) with a cover:

3--3 oz. packages cherry jello
3 cups boiling water


3. Add fruit liquid and fruit.

4. Put in refrigerator to chill until set or overnight.

Instructions

Enjoy!

Yields: About 12 cups
Prep time: 10-15 minutes
