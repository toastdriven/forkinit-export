Forkinit: Chocolate Chip Oatmeal Cookies (or Butterscotch Chip)

By Pam Parker
A family favorite!!

Ingredients

1) Cream together in mixing bowl:

2 sticks butter
1 c. brown sugar


2) Add and mix:

2 eggs
1 tsp. vanilla
1 tsp. water


3) Add and mix:

1 1/2 c. whole wheat flour
1 tsp. soda
1/2 tsp. salt


4) Add and mix:

2 c. oats--old fashioned
2 c. chocolate or butterscotch chips
1/2 c. nuts, chopped (optional)

Instructions

Bake: 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes until they are done around the edges. Let them cool another 10 minutes on the cookie sheet and they will get solid in the middle. Enjoy!!!

Yields: 45 cookies
Prep time: About 45 minutes
Source: My Mom--Phyllis Showler (I reduced the sugar and salt)
