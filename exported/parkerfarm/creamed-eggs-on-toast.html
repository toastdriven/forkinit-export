Forkinit: Creamed Eggs on Toast

By Pam Parker
This is our Easter day tradition as it is a great way to use hard-boiled eggs. It is yummy too!

Ingredients

1. Heat in saucepan until melted:
1 stick butter

2. Blend into butter and cook over low heat stirring constantly until mixture is smooth and bubbly:
1/2 c. flour
1 1/2 tsp. dry mustard
3/4 tsp. salt
3/8 tsp. pepper

3. Remove from heat. Stir in milk:
6 c. milk

4. Stir in gently and cook on low heat until heated through, stirring often so it won't burn:
12 egg (hard-boiled and cut into chunks, suggest 8ths lengthwise, then slice)

5. Serve over toast and top with parmesan cheese.
Bread--toasted
Parmesan Cheese

Instructions

Serve it any time of the day! Enjoy!

(Could add 2-3 c. cubed ham.)

Yields: 6-8 servings
Source: Parker tradition
