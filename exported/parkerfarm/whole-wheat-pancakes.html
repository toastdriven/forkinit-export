Forkinit: Whole Wheat Pancakes

By Pam Parker
Light and fluffy!! Yum!

Ingredients

Beat Lightly:

3 eggs

Add and beat together:

3 c. milk
2 T. oil

Stir in until smooth:

3 c. whole wheat flour
2 T. baking powder
1 tsp. salt

Instructions

Cook on HOT griddle. Yummy with blueberries or other fruit in them. (With apples add cinnamon.) Enjoy!!
