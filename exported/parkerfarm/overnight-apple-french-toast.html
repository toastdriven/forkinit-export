Forkinit: Overnight Apple French Toast

By Pam Parker
A family favorite!!!!

Ingredients

Mix in small sauce pan and cook until thick. Pour into 9" x 13" pan:

1 c. brown sugar
1 stick butter
1 T. cornstarch
1 T. water


Arrange on top of sauce:

2 large apples, slice 1/4" thick


Mix together in flat-bottomed dish:

3 eggs
1 c. milk
1 tsp. vanilla


Dip each slice into egg mixture until soaked and place over apples:

About 9 slices bread


Sprinkle over bread:

1/2 tsp. cinnamon
1/8 tsp. cloves

Instructions

Cover and put in refrigerator overnight. (Or can bake it immediately)

Bake: 350 degrees for 35-40 minutes, uncovered.

Invert each slice as you serve it so apples will be on the top. Can use syrup if desired.

Yields: 9 single servings
Prep time: About 30 minutes
