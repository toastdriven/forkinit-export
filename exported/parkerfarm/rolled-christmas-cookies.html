Forkinit: Rolled Christmas Cookies

By Pam Parker
A yearly Parker tradition! The anise flavoring gives these cookies a yummy flavor. A nice twist and better than sugar cookies.

Ingredients

Cream together:
2 c. butter or shortening
3 c. brown sugar

Add & Mix:
5 eggs
1 tsp. soda in 2 Tb. water
4 tsp. anise flavoring
1 1/2 tsp. vanilla

Mix in just enough flour to make dough the right consistency for rolling:
Almost 8 c. flour

Instructions

Roll dough 1/4" thick and cut out shapes with cookie cutters. Bake at 375 degrees for 8-10 minutes. Middles will still be soft. Cool for 10 minutes on cookie sheets, then remove and finish cooling on counter. Frost with frosting below and decorate.

Frosting:

Beat in mixer to soften:
3 sticks butter or 1 1/2 c. shortening

Add a little at a time and mix:
32 oz. powdered sugar (2 # bag)
2 tsp. vanilla
Water--start with 2 Tbsp.
