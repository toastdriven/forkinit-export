Forkinit: Ham and Noodle Casserole

By Pam Parker
Ben's favorite birthday meal!

Ingredients

1-8 oz. pkg. noodles, cooked and drained
2 c. frozen peas (optional) (run under hot water to thaw)
1 c. diced ham
1 c. Velveeta cheese, diced
1 can cream of mushroom soup
About 1/2 c. milk

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together and put in large casserole dish. Bake covered at 350 degrees for 1 hour.
