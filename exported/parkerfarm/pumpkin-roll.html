Forkinit: Pumpkin Roll

By Pam Parker
This is a family favorite that we started doing around Christmas time and became an "anytime" treat!

Ingredients

3 eggs
1 c. white sugar
2/3 c. canned pumpkin
2 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. baking powder
3/4 c. flour
1 1/2 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. allspice
1/2 tsp. nutmeg
1/8 tsp. cloves

Instructions

Grease jelly roll pan REALLY GOOD! Mix above ingredients together. Pour batter onto pan and shake gently to spread to sides. Bake 12-15 minutes at 350. Take out and cool about 2 minutes. Spread out smooth tea towel and sprinkle with powdered sugar. Loosen pumpkin roll with spatula or knife. Flip over onto towel. Let sit 5 minutes to cool. Roll up with towel in it.

Cream thoroughly:
8 oz. cream cheese, softened
1 c. powdered sugar
4 Tb. butter
1 tsp. vanilla

Open roll and spread filling evenly with rubber spatula or spoon. Roll back up and put on plastic wrap and wrap tightly. Refrigerate. To freeze double wrap with foil.

Prep time: About 45 minutes
