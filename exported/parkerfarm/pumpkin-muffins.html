Forkinit: Pumpkin Muffins

By Pam Parker
Light and Moist. Good any time of the year. Add cream cheese frosting and you have pumpkin cupcakes!

Ingredients

Mix together in bowl:
4 eggs
1 cup oil
1 1/2 cups water
2 tsp. vanilla
3 cups brown sugar
15 oz. canned pumpkin

Add to above and mix:
3 1/2 cups flour (whole wheat is preferred)
2 tsp. baking soda
2 tsp. salt

Add and mix:
3 tsp. cinnamon
1 tsp. nutmeg
1 tsp. ginger
1/2 tsp. cloves

Instructions

Fill muffin cups 3/4 cup full. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean. Remove from pans to cool.

Yields: 2 dozen
Prep time: About 15 minutes
