Forkinit: Congo Bars

By Pam Parker
Quick and easy cookie bars!

Ingredients

1)Cream together in mixing bowl:

2 sticks butter
2 1/2 c. brown sugar
(Optional: 1 c. peanut butter + 1 extra egg)

2)Add:

3 Eggs

3)Then add:

2 3/4 c. flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
1 tsp. vanilla

4)Spread in pan and then top with whatever you want:

Chocolate or Butterscotch Chips, Nuts, Coconut, etc.

Instructions

5) Bake: 350 degrees--20-25 minutes. Cool and cut into bars.

Prep time: 15 minutes
