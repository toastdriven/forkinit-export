Forkinit: Strawberry Summer Salad

By Pam Parker
This yummy salad was made by Lindy and served at Emily's shower! Delicious!!

Ingredients

Combine in Large Bowl:

1 head Romaine Lettuce--torn
1-6 oz. container Feta Cheese
3 cups (or more) sliced strawberries
About 1/2 cup Walnuts (optional--she didn't put these is)


Just before serving, pour over top and mix:

Raspberry Vinaigrette Dressing (she got at Krogers)

Instructions

Eat it right away as it will get soggy! Enjoy!!
