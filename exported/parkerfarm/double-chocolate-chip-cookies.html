Forkinit: Double Chocolate Chip Cookies

By Pam Parker
Delicious, chocolatey, soft cookies any chocolate lover cannot resist!!! Very yummy warm with a glass of cold milk!!!

Ingredients

1. Cream together:
1 c. butter (2 sticks)
1 1/4-1 1/2 c. brown sugar (works with 3/4 c. honey)
1 tsp. vanilla

2. Add & mix:
1 egg

3. Add & mix:
1/2 c. cocoa powder
2 Tb. milk

4. Add & mix:
1 3/4 c. whole wheat flour (I use 2 1/3 c. spelt flour)
1/4 tsp. salt
1/4 tsp. baking powder

5. Add & mix:
1-2 c. dark chocolate chips (we definitely do 2 cups!)
1 c. walnuts, broken into 1/4 " size pieces



Instructions

Drop onto cookies sheets and bake at 350 degrees for 10-12 minutes ( a little longer for spelt flour).

Cool 5 minutes on cookies sheets, then place on counter top to cool.

Yield: 3 1/2 dozen

Yields: 3 1/2 dozen medium sized cookies
Prep time: 45 minutes
