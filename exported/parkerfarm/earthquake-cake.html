Forkinit: Earthquake Cake

By Pam Parker
Very unique and delicious! Prepare and bake; no frosting needed!

Ingredients

Grease 9 x 13 pan. Sprinkle with:

1 cup coconut
1 cup chopped nuts


Mix according to box directions and pour over coconut & nuts:

1 chocolate cake mix + ingredients listed on box

Cream together then drop by large globs onto cake mixture and stir slightly to marble:

1 stick butter
1 pkg. cream cheese (8 oz.)
3 1/2 c. powdered sugar
1 tsp. vanilla

Instructions

Bake at 350 degrees for 45-50 minutes or until done. Serve alone or with ice cream.

Yields: 9 x 13 cake
Prep time: About 25 minutes
