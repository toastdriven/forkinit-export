Forkinit: Myra St. John's CORN CASSEROLE

By jessica kibert

Ingredients

2 EGGS BEATEN
1/4 C. MARG, MELTED
1 CAN (8 OZ) WHOLE CORN
1 CAN (8 OZ) CREAM CORN
1 CONTAINER (8 OZ) SOUR CREAM
1 PACKAGE (8 0Z BOX) JIFFY CORN MUFFIN MIX

Instructions


MIX ALL INGRED. TOGETHER.
PUT INTO A 1 1/2 QT. CASSEROLE DISH
BAKE @ 350 FOR 35 MIN

I FREEZE MY OWN CORN, SO I JUST USE 1 PINT
OF CORN OUT OF MY FREEZER.
