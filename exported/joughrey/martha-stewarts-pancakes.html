Forkinit: Martha Stewart's Pancakes

By joughrey
This was in the February 1998 issue of Martha Stewart Living. The article claimed that you'd never burn the first pancake again with this batter. We were skeptical. But in the last 12 years, well, we haven't burned any pancakes.

Ingredients

1 cup flour
2 tsp. baking powder
1/2 tsp. salt
2 TBSP sugar
1 egg, slightly beaten
1 cup milk
2 TBSP butter, melted

Instructions

Mix all ingredients together. Pour onto griddle (375 degrees). Flip.

Yields: nine 4" pancakes
Source: Martha Stewart Living magazine (Feb 98)
