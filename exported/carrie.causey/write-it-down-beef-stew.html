Forkinit: WRITE IT DOWN Beef Stew

By Carrie

Ingredients

1.5 - 2 lbs stew beef, in cubes
1/3C flour
1/2t salt
1/2t pepper
1/2t garlic powder
1/2t onion powder
2T oil

1 can tomato soup
2 cans water
2 beef bullion cubes
2 onions, chopped

1 can fire-roasted diced tomatoes
1 zucchini, cubed
1C baby carrots, halved
1 red bell pepper, cubed
1/2 lb button mushrooms, halved
2 potatoes, cubes
1 heaped T minced garlic

Instructions

1. Mix together flour, salt, pepper, onion and garlic powder. Dredge the beef cubes in the flour, a few at a time, and brown in a dutch oven in the oil until all pieces have been added, and meat is browned. Add tomato soup, water, bullion & onions. Bake for 1 hour at 350.
2. Stir in remaining ingredients and return to oven. Bake at 350 for 1.5-2 hours or until potatoes are tender OR reduce heat to 200 and bake for 4 hours or until tender.

Yields: 8-10
Prep time: 15 min
